# Pets Guide

Pets are companions in Stardew Valley that provide friendship, daily gifts, and add life to your farm. The 1.6 update expanded the pet system with turtles and additional pet options.

## TL;DR

Choose a cat or dog at character creation. Marnie brings your first pet on the first sunny Wednesday or Friday in Spring after earning 1,000g. Pets have friendship levels (max 1000). At max friendship, pets have a 20% chance daily to drop gifts. Additional pets can be bought from Marnie starting Year 2 or after maxing your first pet's friendship.

## Getting Your First Pet

### Character Creation

During character creation, you choose between:
- **Cat** (multiple styles available)
- **Dog** (multiple styles available)

This choice determines which pet Marnie will bring to your farm.

### Adoption Event

Marnie will visit your farm with your pet on:
- **When:** First sunny Wednesday or Friday morning in Spring
- **Time:** 6:00 AM - 9:30 AM (must exit farmhouse)
- **Condition:** Earned at least 1,000g (or forced by Spring 20)

**Note:** If you skip the cutscene, the pet is not adopted.

### Default Pet Names

| Player Gender | Cat Default | Dog Default |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|
| Male | Dudley | Yogi |
| Female | Miso | Snooch |

Names cannot be changed after adoption.

## Additional Pets (1.6)

After reaching maximum friendship with your first pet **OR** reaching Year 2 without a pet, you can adopt more pets from Marnie's Ranch.

### Pet Prices

| Pet | Price |
|-----|-------|
| Cat | 40,000g |
| Dog | 40,000g |
| Turtle (Green) | 60,000g |
| Turtle (Exotic) | 500,000g |

### Requirements for Additional Pets

1. Purchase a **Pet License** from Marnie
2. Build a **Pet Bowl** at the Carpenter's Shop for each new pet
3. Place the Pet Bowl on your farm

**Warning:** Pets without their own Pet Bowl lose 10 friendship points each night.

### Removing Pets

If you no longer want a pet, use **Butterfly Powder** on it.

## Pet Friendship

### Friendship Mechanics

- **Maximum:** 1000 points
- **Petting:** +12 points per day
- **Water Bowl:** +6 points (next morning, if not raining)
- **Rainy Days:** Bowl automatically fills

### Friendship Levels

Every 200 points = 1 friendship level (5 levels total)

| Level | Points | Status |
|-------|--------|--------|
| Level 1 | 0-199 | New friend |
| Level 2 | 200-399 | Getting to know you |
| Level 3 | 400-599 | Good friends |
| Level 4 | 600-799 | Close friends |
| Level 5 | 800-999 | Best friends |
| Max | 1000 | "<Name> loves you" |

### Grandpa's Evaluation

Having 999+ friendship points with your pet grants **1 point** in Grandpa's Evaluation.

## Pet Care Tips

### Daily Routine

1. **Pet your pet** every day (+12 friendship)
2. **Fill the water bowl** with your watering can (+6 friendship)
3. Check for **gifts** at max friendship

### Pet Bowl Locations

The Pet Bowl is placed automatically on your farm:

| Farm Type | Bowl Location |
|-----------|--------------|
| Standard | Top left, next to house |
| Forest | Top left, next to house |
| Wilderness | Top left, next to house |
| Hill-top | Top left, next to house |
| Riverland | Top left, next to house |
| Four Corners | Intersection of sections |
| Beach | Right above right shore |
| Meadowlands | Top right, next to house |

### Important Notes

- **Sprinklers cannot fill** the Pet Bowl
- **Rain automatically fills** the bowl
- **Ignoring pets** does not decrease friendship (unlike NPCs)
- **Slingshots** do not decrease pet friendship
- **Hats** can be placed on cats and dogs (but not turtles)

## Horses

While not traditional pets, horses are important companions:

### Getting a Horse

- Purchase a **Stable** from Robin (10,000g + Hardwood + Iron Bars)
- The horse comes automatically with the Stable
- Can be named on first ride

### Horse Speed

- Base speed: +1.4 (much faster than walking)
- **Carrot buff:** Feed a carrot for +0.4 speed for the day
- **Horse: The Book:** Permanent +0.5 speed
- Speed buffs stack

### Horse Flute

- Warp your horse to your location
- Works outdoors (including Ginger Island and Desert)
- Obtained from the Bookseller
