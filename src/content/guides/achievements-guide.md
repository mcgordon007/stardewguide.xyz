# Stardew Valley Achievements Guide

Stardew Valley features 45+ Steam achievements (as of version 1.6) that track your progress and mastery of various game aspects. This comprehensive guide covers every achievement, how to unlock them, and strategies to complete your collection.

## Achievement Overview

Achievements in Stardew Valley are earned through natural gameplay progression, though some require specific actions or planning. Many achievements unlock rewards in the form of hats that can be purchased from the abandoned house in Cindersap Forest (the "Hat Mouse").

## Detailed Achievement Strategies

### Money-Making Achievements

**Path to 10,000,000g (Legend):**

| Stage | Amount | Recommended Strategy |
|-------|--------|---------------------|
| Greenhorn | 15,000g | Sell spring crops and forage |
| Cowpoke | 50,000g | Expand to summer crops, start fishing |
| Homesteader | 250,000g | Kegs and Preserves Jars for artisan goods |
| Millionaire | 1,000,000g | Ancient Fruit wine, Starfruit with casks |
| Legend | 10,000,000g | Massive Ancient Fruit operation, truffles, fish ponds |

**Best Money-Making Methods:**

1. **Ancient Fruit Wine** - Plant in Greenhouse, process in Kegs, age in Casks
2. **Starfruit Wine** - Summer crop, high base value, excellent with casks
3. **Truffle Oil** - Pigs in barns, process truffles in Oil Maker
4. **Crystalarium Diamonds** - Passive income, place multiple machines
5. **Fish Ponds** - Roe production, especially Sturgeon for Caviar

## Secret Achievements Explained

### Movie Theater Invitation
- **Requirement:** Complete either the Community Center or Joja Warehouse project
- **How to Unlock:** After completion, the movie theater is built (or repaired). Invite any villager to a movie.
- **Note:** This achievement was added in version 1.4

### Summit of the Mountain
- **Requirement:** Achieve 100% Perfection
- **How to Unlock:** Complete all Perfection requirements, then visit the summit west of the Railroad.
- **Reward:** Access to the true ending cutscene

### Strange Capsule
- **Requirement:** Witness the Strange Capsule random event
- **How to Unlock:** After Year 1, there's a 1% chance each night of this event occurring (if you have an empty tile on your farm).
- **Note:** The capsule will crack open after a few days, leaving footprints leading to the forest.

### Owl Statue
- **Requirement:** Witness the Owl Statue random event
- **How to Unlock:** Rare decoration event that places a stone owl statue on your farm.
- **Note:** Purely cosmetic, no gameplay effect

### Green Rain
- **Requirement:** Experience the Green Rain weather event
- **How to Unlock:** Very rare weather occurrence that tints the rain green.
- **Note:** Extremely rare, may take many in-game years to witness

## Tips and Tricks

### General Achievement Tips

1. **Plan Ahead:** Many achievements require long-term planning (shipping every item, cooking all recipes)

2. **Check the Wiki:** For specific item locations and availability

3. **Use Multiple Saves:** Some achievements are mutually exclusive (Community Center vs. Joja)

4. **Track Progress:** Use the in-game collections tab to monitor shipping and cooking progress

5. **Don't Rush:** Many achievements come naturally through gameplay

### Time-Saving Strategies

- **Friendship:** Focus on 8 villagers for "The Beloved Farmer" rather than trying to max everyone
- **Cooking:** Watch Queen of Sauce every Monday and Sunday without fail
- **Crafting:** Keep a list of uncrafted items and their requirements
- **Fishing:** Use tackle and bait to catch fish faster
- **Mining:** Use bombs and staircases to descend quickly

### Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Missing Queen of Sauce episodes (check Wednesday reruns)
- Forgetting to ship one of each item
- Not befriending the Dwarf (required for some artifacts)
- Selling unique items before donating to museum
- Not checking the Traveling Cart for rare items
