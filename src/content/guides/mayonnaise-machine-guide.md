# Stardew Valley Mayonnaise Machine Guide

## Overview

The **Mayonnaise Machine** is one of the earliest artisan equipment available in Stardew Valley. It processes **Eggs** into **Mayonnaise**, a valuable artisan good that is also used in several cooking recipes and loved by some villagers.

## Unlock Requirements

- **Farming Level 2** required to unlock the crafting recipe

## Crafting Materials

To craft a Mayonnaise Machine, you will need:

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Wood | 15 |
| Stone | 15 |
| Copper Bar | 1 |
| Earth Crystal | 1 |

## How to Use

1. Place the Mayonnaise Machine on your farm or inside a coop
2. Interact with it while holding an **Egg** or **Duck Egg**
3. Processing time: **3 hours** (180 minutes) of in-game time

## Output Products

### Mayonnaise (from Eggs)
- **White/Brown Egg** → **Mayonnaise** (190g)
- **Large White/Brown Egg** → **Mayonnaise** (237g)
- **Duck Egg** → **Duck Mayonnaise** (375g)
- **Void Egg** → **Void Mayonnaise** (275g)
- **Dinosaur Egg** → **Dinosaur Mayonnaise** (800g)
- **Golden Egg** → **3 Gold-Quality Mayonnaise** (varies)

## Quality Mechanics

- Regular eggs produce regular mayonnaise
- Large eggs produce gold-quality mayonnaise (higher value)
- The quality of the egg does not affect mayonnaise quality for most eggs
- Ostrich Eggs produce 10 mayonnaise of the same quality as the egg

## Tips & Strategy

- The Artisan profession (+40% sell price) makes mayonnaise highly profitable
- Mayonnaise is a **loved gift** for Mayor Lewis
- Duck Mayonnaise is particularly valuable and profitable
- Place machines inside the coop for maximum efficiency
- Dinosaur Mayonnaise (800g) is one of the highest-value single-process items
