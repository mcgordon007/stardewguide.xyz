# Complete Guide to All Big Craftables in Stardew Valley

Big Craftables are large items in Stardew Valley that serve functional purposes on your farm. Unlike regular craftables (tools, equipment, and consumables), Big Craftables are placed as furniture-like objects on your farm or in buildings. They include essential farming equipment like Bee Houses and Furnaces, as well as decorative items and powerful teleportation devices. This guide covers every Big Craftable in the game with their recipes, materials, and effects.

## What Are Big Craftables?

### Overview

Big Craftables are 2x2 tile items that can be placed on your farm, in buildings, or in your house. They are crafted at the workbench or purchased from vendors. Big Craftables serve a wide range of purposes, from farming equipment to decorative furniture.

### Categories of Big Craftables

1. **Farming Equipment**: Bee Houses, Casks, Lightning Rods, Seed Makers
2. **Storage**: Chests, Mini-Fridges
3. **Processing**: Furnaces, Charcoal Kilns, Kegs, Preserves Jars (technically crafted but placed as big craftables)
4. **Teleportation**: Obelisks (Earth, Water, Desert, Island)
5. **Decorative**: Furniture, lamps, signs, and seasonal decorations
6. **Special**: Statue of Endless Fortune, Statue of Perfection, Hopper, Auto-Grabber

## Farming Equipment Big Craftables

### Bee House

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| Recipe Source | Farming Level 3 |
| Materials | 40 Wood, 8 Coal, 1 Gold Bar, 1 Maple Syrup |
| Effect | Produces Honey every 4 days (seasonal variety) |
| Placement | Outdoors only |
| Seasonal Honey | Wild Honey (Spring), Tulip Honey (Tulip nearby), Blue Jazz Honey (Blue Jazz nearby), etc. |

**Bee House Tips**:
- Place flowers near Bee Houses to produce flavored honey worth more.
- The flower must be within 5 tiles of the Bee House.
- Bee Houses do not produce honey in winter.
- Flavored honey sells for significantly more than Wild Honey.

### Cask

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| Recipe Source | Unlocked when the farmhouse is upgraded to the third level (nursery) |
| Materials | 50 Wood, 1 Copper Bar, 1 Iron Bar, 1 Oak Resin |
| Effect | Ages cheese, wine, and mead to increase quality over time |
| Placement | Farmhouse cellar or any building (with cellar upgrade) |
| Aging Time | Varies by item (14-56 days for iridium quality) |

**Cask Aging Times**:

| Item | Silver | Gold | Iridium |
|------|--------|------|---------|
| Cheese | 7 days | 14 days | 21 days |
| Goat Cheese | 3.5 days | 7 days | 10.5 days |
| Wine | 14 days | 28 days | 56 days |
| Mead | 14 days | 28 days | 56 days |
| Pale Ale | 14 days | 28 days | 56 days |
| Beer | 14 days | 28 days | 56 days |

**Cask Tips**:
- The cellar has space for 125 Casks (5 rows of 25).
- Only place items in Casks that benefit significantly from quality increases.
- Ancient Fruit Wine and Starfruit Wine are the most profitable items to age.

### Lightning Rod

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| Recipe Source | Foraging Level 6 |
| Materials | 1 Copper Bar, 1 Iron Bar, 1 Gold Bar |
| Effect | Protects farm from lightning strikes during storms |
| Placement | Outdoors on farm |
| Radius | Protects the entire farm from one rod |

**Lightning Rod Tips**:
- A single Lightning Rod protects the entire farm from lightning damage.
- Lightning strikes during storms can destroy crops and scare livestock.
- The Lightning Rod produces a Battery Pack when it absorbs a strike.
- Multiple Lightning Rods increase the chance of Battery Pack production.

### Seed Maker

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| Recipe Source | Farming Level 9 |
| Materials | 25 Wood, 10 Coal, 1 Gold Bar |
| Effect | Converts crops into seeds (1-3 seeds per crop) |
| Placement | Anywhere on farm or in buildings |
| Special | Can produce Rare Seeds from certain crops |

**Seed Maker Tips**:
- The Seed Maker can produce 1-3 seeds of the same crop type.
- There is a small chance (about 2%) of producing a Rare Seed instead.
- Ancient Fruit and Starfruit are the most profitable crops to process.
- Place Seed Makers near your storage for efficient processing.

## Advanced Big Craftable Strategies

### Automating Your Farm

The ultimate goal of Big Craftable placement is farm automation:

**Processing Chain Example**:
1. **Harvest crops** -> Place in Seed Maker -> Get seeds for next season.
2. **Harvest crops** -> Place in Keg -> Get wine.
3. **Wine** -> Place in Cask -> Get iridium-quality wine.
4. **Animal products** -> Place in Mayonnaise Machine / Cheese Press -> Get artisan goods.
5. **Artisan goods** -> Place in Shipping Bin -> Get gold.

**Hopper Integration**:
- Place Hoppers next to Kegs and Preserves Jars.
- Hoppers automatically load nearby items into machines.
- This eliminates the need to manually load each machine.

### Farm Layout Optimization

**Central Processing Area**:
- Place all processing machines in a central location.
- Surround with chests for raw materials and finished products.
- Place Hoppers between chests and machines for automation.
- Keep the Shipping Bin nearby for easy access.

**Outdoor Production Area**:
- Place Bee Houses in a dedicated area with flower fields.
- Position Lightning Rods to cover the entire farm.
- Place Obelisks near the farmhouse for easy access.
- Arrange Scarecrows to maximize crop coverage.

### Seasonal Big Craftable Management

| Season | Focus | Big Craftables to Prioritize |
|--------|-------|------------------------------|
| Spring | Crop processing | Preserves Jars, Kegs |
| Summer | Honey production | Bee Houses (near flowers) |
| Fall | Wine aging | Casks in cellar |
| Winter | Planning | Build new craftables, reorganize |

**Can Big Craftables be moved after placement?**
Yes, you can pick up and relocate Big Craftables by using the pick-up tool (right-click or hit with a tool). They return to your inventory and can be placed elsewhere.

**Do Big Craftables work in winter?**
Most Big Craftables continue to work in winter. Exceptions include Bee Houses (no honey production) and outdoor crop-based processors (no crops to process).

**Can I place Big Craftables outside my farm?**
Most Big Craftables can only be placed on your farm or inside buildings. Some decorative Big Craftables can be placed in your house.

**What is the most profitable Big Craftable setup?**
A cellar full of Casks aging Ancient Fruit Wine is generally considered the most profitable Big Craftable setup, followed by Kegs producing wine.
