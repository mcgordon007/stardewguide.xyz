# Stardew Valley Clint Guide: Complete NPC Overview

Clint is the local blacksmith of Pelican Town, providing essential services for tool upgrades, geode processing, and metal crafting. He's a key NPC for progressing your mining and farming capabilities. This comprehensive guide covers everything about Clint, from his schedule to his services to his gift preferences.

## Basic Information

### Character Overview

**Name**: Clint
**Role**: Blacksmith
**Location**: Pelican Town (Blacksmith Shop)
**Family**: None (single, lives alone)
**Birthday**: Winter 26
**Marriageable**: No

### Personality

Clint is a somewhat shy and introverted blacksmith who takes pride in his work. He's skilled at metalworking but struggles with social interactions, particularly romantic ones. He harbors feelings for Emily but finds it difficult to express them. Clint is generally friendly but can seem gruff or awkward at times. He's dedicated to his craft and provides valuable services to the community.

## Blacksmith Shop

### Location

Clint's Blacksmith Shop is located in Pelican Town, near the bridge to the mountains. It's easily accessible from town.

### Shop Hours

| Day | Hours |
|-----|-------|
| Monday | 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM |
| Tuesday | 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM |
| Wednesday | 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM |
| Thursday | 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM |
| Friday | 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM |
| Saturday | 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM |
| Sunday | 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM |

**Note**: Clint closes at 4:00 PM (earlier than other shops). He visits the Saloon on Friday afternoons.

### Services Available

Clint provides three main services:

1. **Tool Upgrades**: Improve your tools for better efficiency
2. **Geode Processing**: Crack open geodes for minerals and gems
3. **Furnace Purchase**: Buy furnaces for smelting

## Tool Upgrades

### Upgrade Progression

Each tool can be upgraded four times, improving efficiency and capabilities.

**Upgrade Levels**:
1. Basic (starter)
2. Copper
3. Steel (Iron)
4. Gold
5. Iridium

### Upgrade Costs and Materials

| Tool | Copper | Steel | Gold | Iridium |
|------|--------|-------|------|---------|
| Pickaxe | 2,000g + 5 Copper Bars | 5,000g + 5 Iron Bars | 10,000g + 5 Gold Bars | 25,000g + 5 Iridium Bars |
| Axe | 2,000g + 5 Copper Bars | 5,000g + 5 Iron Bars | 10,000g + 5 Gold Bars | 25,000g + 5 Iridium Bars |

| Watering Can | 2,000g + 5 Copper Bars | 5,000g + 5 Iron Bars | 10,000g + 5 Gold Bars | 25,000g + 5 Iridium Bars |
| Trash Can | 500g + 5 Copper Bars | 1,000g + 5 Iron Bars | 2,000g + 5 Gold Bars | 5,000g + 5 Iridium Bars |

Each tool upgrade takes 2 days:
- Day 1: Give tool to Clint

## Geode Processing

### Geode Types

Clint can process all types of geodes:

| Geode Type | Cost | Possible Contents |
|------------|------|-------------------|
| Geode | 25g | Copper ore, coal, minerals, gems |
| Frozen Geode | 25g | Iron ore, frozen minerals, gems |
| Magma Geode | 25g | Gold ore, fire minerals, gems |
| Omni Geode | 25g | Any mineral, gems, artifacts |
| Artifact Trove | 25g | Artifacts, rare items |

### Geode Sources

| Geode | Source |
|-------|--------|
| Geode | Mines levels 1-40, fishing |
| Frozen Geode | Mines levels 41-80, fishing |
| Magma Geode | Mines levels 81-120, fishing |
| Omni Geode | Skull Cavern, fishing, Krobus |
| Artifact Trove | Skull Cavern, special drops |

### Processing Tips

1. **Batch processing**: Bring multiple geodes at once
2. **Check contents**: Minerals may be duplicates
3. **Save rare minerals**: For museum donation
4. **Sell duplicates**: Extra minerals for gold

## Furnace

### Purchase

Clint sells furnaces for crafting metal bars.

**Cost**: 100g
**Materials**: Copper Ore (25), Stone (25)

### Crafting with Furnace

Furnaces smelt ore into bars:

| Bar | Ore Needed | Time |
|-----|------------|------|
| Copper Bar | 5 Copper Ore | 30 minutes |
| Iron Bar | 5 Iron Ore | 1 hour |
| Gold Bar | 5 Gold Ore | 5 hours |
| Iridium Bar | 5 Iridium Ore | 8 hours |

## Heart Events

### 3 Hearts

**Trigger**: Enter the Blacksmith Shop during business hours

**Event**: Clint discusses his work and his feelings for Emily. He may share his struggles with expressing his emotions.

### 6 Hearts

**Trigger**: Enter the Blacksmith Shop or see Clint near Emily

**Event**: Clint's feelings for Emily become more apparent. He may ask for advice or express his frustrations about romance.

### Notes on Heart Events

- Clint's events focus on his feelings for Emily
- They reveal his shy personality
- He struggles with romance
- No major gameplay rewards

## Tips for Interacting with Clint

### Gift Strategy

**Best Gifts**:
- Any gem (ruby, emerald, etc.)
- Iridium Bar (loved, valuable)
- Minerals from geodes

**Easy Daily Gifts**:
- Amethyst (common geode drop)
- Topaz (common geode drop)
- Coffee (easy purchase)

### Service Strategy

**Tool Upgrades**:
- Upgrade pickaxe first (mining efficiency)
- Upgrade axe second (wood gathering)
- Upgrade hoe and watering can later
- Plan upgrades around seasons

**Geode Processing**:
- Process geodes regularly
- Donate minerals to museum
- Sell duplicates for gold
- Keep rare minerals

### Friendship Benefits

While Clint isn't marriageable, friendship provides:
- Better dialogue
- Understanding of his character
- Insight into Emily relationship
- Community connection
