# Stardew Valley Auto-Petter Guide

## Introduction

**Auto-Petter** is an automation device added in Stardew Valley 1.5. When placed inside a Barn or Coop, it automatically pets all animals daily, helping maintain and improve animal friendship.

## Acquisition

Auto-Petter has the following acquisition methods:

### Main Acquisition Methods
- **Joja Warehouse**: Available for purchase at Joja Warehouse for 50,000g after completing the Joja Community Development Form
- **Skull Cavern Chests**: Chance to obtain in Treasure Rooms in Skull Cavern

### Other Acquisition Methods
- **Golden Coconut**: Very low chance from opening Golden Coconuts on Ginger Island
- **Fish Pond**: Certain fish pond outputs (such as Lava Eel pond) have a very small chance to produce

## Usage

1. Place the Auto-Petter inside a Barn or Coop
2. Only one Auto-Petter is needed per building
3. Automatically pets all animals in that building every day
4. Animals gain the same friendship as manual petting

## How It Works

- Automatically provides one pet per day for all animals in the building
- Provides the same friendship as player manual petting
- Can stack with player manual petting (only one effective pet per day maximum)
- Animals do not gain extra friendship (manual + auto does not double)

## Notes

- Only one Auto-Petter is needed per Barn/Coop
- Does not affect animal product collection (needs Auto-Grabber)
- Still effective on animals with max friendship, but does not exceed cap
- Easier to obtain on Joja route

## Strategy Tips

- For large animal farms, Auto-Petter can save a lot of time
- Combined with Auto-Grabber for fully automated animal farming
- If taking Joja route, can purchase in large quantities
- If taking Community Center route, need to rely on Skull Cavern chests

## Difference from Auto-Grabber

- **Auto-Petter**: Maintains animal friendship
- **Auto-Grabber**: Collects animal products

Both used together can achieve fully automated animal farm management.
