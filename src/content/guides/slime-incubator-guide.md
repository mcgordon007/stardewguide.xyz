# Slime Incubator Guide

## Unlock Requirements

The Slime Incubator is unlocked at **Combat Level 8**.

## Crafting Materials

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Iridium Bar | 2 |
| Slime | 100 |

## Function

The Slime Incubator is used to hatch Slime Eggs:

- Place a Slime Egg into the incubator, and after approximately **1-3 days** of game time, it will hatch into a Slime of the corresponding color.
- Hatchable egg types:
  - Green Slime Egg
  - Blue Slime Egg
  - Red Slime Egg
  - Purple Slime Egg
  - Tiger Slime Egg

## Usage Tips

- Mainly used for breeding Slimes inside the Slime Hutch.
- Different colored Slime Eggs produce different colored Slimes.
- Slimes drop Slime and Petrified Slime when killed, which can be recycled to craft more incubators.
- Purple and Tiger Slime Eggs are of high value; consider selling them instead of hatching.
- Hatched Slimes will produce Slime Balls, which can be collected for resources.

## Related Building

Use in conjunction with the **Slime Hutch** for better results:
- The Slime Hutch is built by Robin for 10,000g + 500 Stone + 10 Refined Quartz + 1 Iridium Bar.
- Can hold up to 20 Slimes.
- Slimes will produce Slime Balls, which contain Slime and Petrified Slime.
