# Stardew Valley Special Orders Guide: Complete Overview

The Special Orders board is a quest system introduced in Stardew Valley 1.5/1.6 that provides specific, named challenges and rewards. This comprehensive guide covers all available special orders, their rewards, and strategies for completing them efficiently.

## Special Orders Overview

### What are Special Orders

Special Orders are:
- **Specific, named quests** posted on the Special Orders board (not generic "catch 10 fish" type orders)
- Available after certain game progress
- Provide gold and item rewards upon completion
- Some are repeatable, some are one-time
- Each order has a unique name and specific objectives

### Board Location

The Special Orders board is located:
- **Outside Lewis's house** in the town center (NOT inside Pierre's General Store)
- On the left side of Lewis's house, near the community bulletin board area
- Visible after unlocking
- Check regularly for new orders

### Unlock Requirements

**When Available**:
- After completing Community Center OR
- After purchasing Joja Membership
- Board appears outside Lewis's house in the town center
- New orders appear regularly

### Order Types

**One-Time Orders**:
- Complete once
- Never repeat
- Special rewards
- Story-related

**Repeatable Orders**:
- Can complete multiple times
- Appear regularly
- Ongoing income source
- Resource collection

## Order Mechanics

### Accepting Orders

**How to Accept**:
1. Approach Special Orders board outside Lewis's house
2. Read available named orders
3. Accept desired order
4. Track progress in quest log

### Order Limits

**Maximum Active**:
- Can accept multiple orders at once
- No strict limit
- Manage multiple quests
- Complete in any order

### Order Duration

**Time Limits**:
- Most orders have time limits
- Typically 7 days to 2 seasons
- Check deadline carefully
- Plan completion timing

### Order Rewards

**Reward Types**:
- Gold rewards
- Item rewards
- Friendship increases
- Special unlocks (e.g., Qi Gems)

## Complete Special Orders List (1.6)

Special Orders in Stardew Valley 1.6 are specific, named quests with unique objectives. Below are the major Special Orders available in the game:

### Qi's Special Orders

These orders are given by Mr. Qi and typically reward Qi Gems.

| Order Name | Request | Reward | Time Limit | Notes |
|-------------|---------|--------|------------|-------|
| **Qi's Kindness** | Give 10 specific items to Mr. Qi | Qi Gems | 7 days | Items vary; check the order details for the specific items requested |
| **Dangerous Mines** | Reach a specific mine level | Qi Gems | 7 days | Requires descending to a deep mine level |
| **The Bottom** | Reach the bottom of the mines (Level 120) | Qi Gems | 7 days | One-time order; unlocks Skull Key access |
| **Cryptic Note** | Solve a cryptic puzzle | Qi Gems | Varies | Requires deciphering clues; unique each time |

### Town Special Orders

These orders involve helping villagers or completing town-related tasks.

| Order Name | Request | Reward | Time Limit | Notes |
|-------------|---------|--------|------------|-------|
| **Robin's Project** | Collect wood and/or stone for Robin | Gold, friendship | 7 days | Helps Robin with construction projects |
| **Pam Is Thirsty** | Give Pam a specific drink | Gold, friendship | 7 days | Check the order for the specific beverage |
| **Enchanted Grove** | Plant specific trees | Gold | 14 days | Requires planting designated tree types |
| **Biggest Catch** | Catch a specific fish | Gold | 7 days | Target the specified fish species |
| **Avid Collector** | Collect specific items for Gunther (Museum) | Gold, friendship | 14 days | Donate or deliver specific artifacts/minerals |
| **Solve The Puzzle** | Unlock a specific area or solve a puzzle | Special reward | Varies | May involve Secret Notes or other mysteries |

### Repeatable Special Orders

Some Special Orders can repeat, offering ongoing challenges:

| Order Name | Request | Reward | Repeatable |
|-------------|---------|--------|------------|
| **Dangerous Mines** | Reach a specific mine level | Qi Gems | Yes |
| Various gathering orders | Collect specific items | Gold | Yes |

## Reward Details

### Gold Rewards

**Standard Rewards**:
- Easy orders: 500-1,000g
- Medium orders: 1,000-2,000g
- Hard orders: 2,000-5,000g
- Special orders: Variable

### Qi Gem Rewards

**Qi's Challenges**:
- Qi Gems are used to purchase items from Mr. Qi's Walnut Room
- Can buy powerful items like the Iridium Band recipe, auto-petter, and more
- Essential for endgame progression

### Item Rewards

**Possible Item Rewards**:
- Special equipment
- Rare seeds
- Decorations
- Crafting recipes

### Friendship Rewards

**Friendship Increases**:
- Orders involving specific villagers increase friendship with that villager
- Pam Is Thirsty increases friendship with Pam
- Robin's Project increases friendship with Robin
- Avid Collector increases friendship with Gunther

## Order Strategy Tips

### Accepting Strategy

**Best Practices**:
1. Check the board outside Lewis's house every few days
2. Accept orders that align with your current activities
3. Consider time limits carefully
4. Plan completion order

**Overlap Strategy**:
- Accept mining orders when planning a Skull Cavern run
- Accept fishing orders when doing daily fishing
- Accept farming orders during active growing seasons
- Maximize efficiency by combining orders with daily activities

### Completion Strategy

**Efficient Completion**:
- Track progress daily
- Focus on deadline orders first
- Complete easy orders quickly
- Stack similar activities

**Time Management**:
- Check deadlines daily
- Prioritize urgent orders
- Don't accept impossible orders
- Plan around seasons
