# Stardew Valley Caroline Guide: Complete NPC Overview

Caroline is the wife of Pierre and mother of Abigail, known for her cheerful personality and her mysterious sunroom activities. As one of the more complex non-marriageable NPCs, Caroline's storyline includes surprising revelations and a rich family dynamic. This comprehensive guide covers everything about Caroline, from her daily schedule to her gift preferences to her intriguing sunroom secret.

## Basic Information

### Character Overview

**Name**: Caroline
**Role**: Homemaker / Pierre's Wife
**Location**: Pierre's General Store (upstairs residence)
**Family**: Pierre (husband), Abigail (daughter)
**Birthday**: Winter 7
**Marriageable**: No

### Personality

Caroline is an upbeat, energetic, and somewhat mysterious woman who is deeply involved in the Pelican Town community. She maintains a close friendship with Jodi, attends aerobics classes regularly, and takes pride in her home and garden. However, beneath her cheerful exterior lies a more complex personality with interests that she keeps hidden from her husband. Caroline is open-minded and curious, which sometimes puts her at odds with Pierre's more conventional worldview.

## Gift Preferences

Caroline's gift preferences reflect her interests in nature, tea, and the finer things in life.

### Loved Gifts (80 points)

These gifts give the maximum friendship boost:

- **Green Tea** - Caroline's absolute favorite. Can be brewed from Tea Leaves using a Keg. Tea Leaves grow on Tea Saplings, which can be purchased from Pierre's store.
- **Summer Spangle** - A beautiful flower that grows in Summer. Plant Summer Spangle seeds in Summer for a loved gift.
- **Mead** - A honey-based alcoholic beverage made from Honey and a Keg. Caroline enjoys this refined drink.
- **Rabbit's Foot** - A universal loved gift for many NPCs. Can be obtained from Rabbits in a Deluxe Coop.

### Liked Gifts (45 points)

Good alternatives when loved gifts are unavailable:

- **All cooked foods** (except disliked ones)
- **All fruits** (except disliked ones)
- **All vegetables** (except disliked ones)

- **Flowers** (except Poppy and Tulip)

### Birthday Gift (Winter 7)

Caroline's birthday is Winter 7. Giving a loved gift on this day provides 640 friendship points. Green Tea is the most thematic and practical choice. If you have been growing Tea Saplings, you should have a steady supply of Green Tea by Winter. Rabbit's Foot is also an excellent choice if available.

## Heart Events

Caroline's heart events reveal her complex personality, her relationship with her family, and her intriguing secret activities.

### Two Hearts - The Garden

This event triggers when you enter Pierre's store and go upstairs. Caroline is tending to her plants and talks about her love for gardening. She mentions that she finds peace in growing things and that her garden is her sanctuary. This event establishes Caroline's connection to nature.

### Four Hearts - The Tea

Visit Pierre's store between 9:00 AM and 3:00 PM. Caroline offers you a cup of tea and talks about how she discovered her love for tea cultivation. She mentions that she grows her own Tea Leaves in the sunroom and that Pierre does not know about all of her gardening activities. This event hints at Caroline's secret and her desire for personal space.

### Six Hearts - The Sunroom Secret

This is one of the most revealing events in the game. Visit Pierre's store and go to the sunroom between 8:00 AM and 10:00 AM. You will find Caroline in the sunroom, which is filled with unusual plants. She is startled to see you and explains that she has been cultivating rare plants that Pierre would not approve of. She asks you to keep her secret, and you agree. This event reveals Caroline's independent streak and her desire to have interests outside of her role as Pierre's wife.

### Eight Hearts - Abigail

Visit Pierre's store when Abigail is also home. Caroline and Abigail have a conversation about Abigail's interests in the supernatural and adventure. Caroline is supportive but worried, while Abigail is frustrated by her mother's concern. The player witnesses a genuine mother-daughter moment that shows both their love and their differences. This event deepens the understanding of Caroline's relationship with Abigail.

### Ten Hearts - The Wizard's Tower

This is the most surprising and controversial of Caroline's events. Visit the mountain area between 10:00 AM and 4:00 PM on a non-rainy day. You will see Caroline leaving the Wizard's Tower. She is embarrassed and explains that she has been visiting the Wizard to learn about magical plants and alternative gardening techniques. She swears you to secrecy and says that Pierre must never know. This event has led to widespread speculation in the community about the true nature of Caroline's relationship with the Wizard and its connection to Abigail's unusual hair color and magical interests.

## The Sunroom Secret

One of the most discussed aspects of Caroline's character is her sunroom and its connection to the Wizard's Tower.

### The Sunroom

Caroline's sunroom is a small greenhouse-like room attached to the upstairs of Pierre's General Store. It is filled with plants that are not typical of Pierre's store inventory, suggesting that Caroline has interests in botany that go beyond conventional farming.

### The Wizard's Connection

Caroline's visits to the Wizard's Tower have sparked significant fan discussion. The most popular theory is that Abigail may actually be the Wizard's biological daughter, which would explain her purple hair, her interest in the supernatural, and the Wizard's seemingly unusual interest in her. However, the game never explicitly confirms this theory.

### What the Game Actually Shows

- Caroline visits the Wizard to learn about plants and magic.
- She keeps these visits secret from Pierre.
- Abigail has natural purple hair and magical inclinations.
- The Wizard has a portrait of a woman in his tower that some players believe resembles Caroline.

### Community Interpretation

The Stardew Valley community has extensively debated this storyline, with some players viewing it as a hint at infidelity and others seeing it as a simple interest in botany and magic. Regardless of interpretation, it adds a layer of complexity to Caroline's character and the family dynamics in Pierre's household.

## Family Dynamics

### Relationship with Pierre

Caroline and Pierre have a seemingly stable marriage, but there are undercurrents of tension. Pierre is focused on his business and sometimes dismissive of Caroline's interests, while Caroline seeks fulfillment beyond her role as a storekeeper's wife. Their relationship is not unhappy, but it is not without its challenges.

### Relationship with Abigail

Caroline is a caring but sometimes overprotective mother. She worries about Abigail's safety, particularly her adventures in the mines and her interest in the supernatural. Despite their differences, Caroline loves Abigail deeply and supports her in her own way.

### Friendship with Jodi

Caroline and Jodi are best friends, bonded by their shared experiences as mothers and their weekly aerobics classes. Their friendship provides Caroline with emotional support and a sense of community.

## Friendship Tips and Strategies

### Maximizing Friendship with Caroline

1. **Grow Tea Saplings**: Purchase Tea Saplings from Pierre's store and plant them. Once mature, they produce Tea Leaves daily, which can be brewed into Green Tea using a Keg.
2. **Summer Spangle**: Plant Summer Spangle seeds in Summer for beautiful loved gifts.
3. **Birthday priority**: Winter 7 is Caroline's birthday. Save Green Tea or Rabbit's Foot for this day.
4. **Visit the sunroom**: Going to Pierre's upstairs area gives you access to Caroline in the sunroom during her scheduled times.
5. **Fish Taco and Tropical Curry**: Cook these dishes for additional loved gifts. Fish Tacos are easy with Summer/Fall Tilapia, and Tropical Curry uses Ginger Island ingredients.

### Gift Planning

- **Spring**: Focus on Tea Leaves if your Tea Saplings are mature. Otherwise, use saved items.
- **Summer**: Summer Spangle is available. Plant plenty for weekly gifts.
- **Fall**: Use preserved Green Tea or cook Fish Tacos with Tilapia.
- **Winter**: Green Tea and Fish Tacos are your best options during Winter.
