# Quest Guide

Stardew Valley's storyline unfolds through a series of quests that introduce you to the game's core systems: farming, fishing, mining, socializing, and the Community Center. This guide covers every main storyline quest in chronological order, with objectives, rewards, and tips.

## TL;DR

The main quest chain follows this order: **Introductions** (greet NPCs) → **Getting Started** (harvest parsnip) → **To The Beach** (get fishing rod) → **How to Win Friends** (give a gift) → **Raising Animals / Advancement** (build coop/craft scarecrow) → **Explore The Mine** (reach floor 5) → **Deeper In The Mine** (reach floor 40) → **To The Bottom** (floor 120, get Skull Key) → **Rat Problem** (discover Community Center) → **Meet The Wizard** (get potion) → **Archaeology** (donate to museum). Side quests unlock throughout via mail.

## The Introduction Chain (Day 1)

These quests teach you the basics of the game and unlock core mechanics.

### 1. Introductions

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Trigger** | Automatic on Day 1 (envelope in farmhouse) |
| **Objective** | Greet 28 villagers |
| **Reward** | 100 friendship points with every known NPC |

Talk to every villager at least once. The wizard, Krobus, Dwarf, Sandy, Kent (not in town Year 1 Spring), and Leo (not on Ginger Island yet) don't count toward the 28. Once complete, the "How to Win Friends" quest triggers.

### 2. Getting Started

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Trigger** | Automatic on Day 1 (envelope in farmhouse) |
| **Objective** | Till soil, plant Parsnip Seeds, water daily, harvest |
| **Reward** | 100g |

**Meadowlands Farm variant**: If you chose Meadowlands Farm, this quest is replaced with harvesting an egg from your starting chickens instead.

## Early Game Quests (Spring Week 1-2)

### 3. To The Beach

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Trigger** | Mail on Spring 2 |
| **Objective** | Visit the beach before 5:00 PM |
| **Reward** | **Bamboo Pole** (basic fishing rod) |

This is critical -- visit the beach on Spring 2 to get your first fishing rod from Willy. Don't miss the 5:00 PM deadline.

## Mail Quests (Seasonal)

These quests arrive via mail at fixed dates. Complete them for gold and friendship rewards.

| Quest | Trigger | Objective | Reward |
|-------|---------|-----------|--------|
| **Robin's Lost Axe** | Spring 11 | Find axe near Sewer Pipe in Cindersap Forest | 250g, +1 heart |
| **Jodi's Request** | Spring 19 | Bring Jodi a Cauliflower | 350g, +1 heart |
| **Mayor's "Shorts"** | Summer 3 | Find Lewis's purple shorts in Marnie's bedroom (2:00 PM, when Marnie is upstairs) | 750g, +1 heart |
| **Pam Is Thirsty** | Summer 14 | Bring Pam a Pale Ale | 350g, +1 heart |
| **Crop Research** | Summer 20 | Bring Demetrius a Melon | 550g, +1 heart |
| **Knee Therapy** | Summer 25 | Bring George a Hot Pepper | 200g, +1 heart |
| **Cow's Delight** | Fall 3 | Bring Marnie one Amaranth | 500g, +1 heart |
| **Blackberry Basket** | Fall 8 | Find Linus's basket in the Backwoods near tunnel entrance | +1 heart |
| **A Dark Reagent** | Winter 12 | Bring the Wizard a Void Essence | 1,000g, +1 heart |
| **Robin's Request** | Winter 21 | Bring Robin 10 Hardwood | 500g, +1 heart |

### Tips for Mail Quests

- **Mayor's Shorts**: Enter Marnie's Ranch on a Wednesday or Saturday between 1:30 PM and 6:00 PM (Marnie goes upstairs, Lewis is visible). The shorts are in her bedroom. This is the highest gold reward of any mail quest
- **Jodi's Cauliflower**: Plant extra Cauliflower in Spring to ensure you have one for this quest
- **Crop Research Melon**: Plant Melons in Summer for this quest
- **Blackberry Basket**: The basket is in the Backwoods, northwest of the tunnel entrance. Easy to find during Blackberry Season (Fall 8-11)

## Qi's Quest Chain (Skull Cavern)

After entering the Skull Cavern, Mr. Qi's quest line begins. These quests unlock the Casino and special items.

| Quest | Trigger | Objective | Reward |
|-------|---------|-----------|--------|
| **Qi's Challenge** | Day after entering Skull Cavern | Reach floor 25 in Skull Cavern | 10,000g |
| **The Mysterious Qi (1)** | Put Battery Pack in tunnel lock-box | Place Rainbow Shell in train platform box | Continues |
| **The Mysterious Qi (2)** | Complete step 1 | Place 10 Beets in Mayor Lewis's fridge | Continues |
| **The Mysterious Qi (3)** | Complete step 2 | Put Solar Essence in sand dragon's mouth (Caldera Desert) | Continues |
| **The Mysterious Qi (4)** | Complete step 3 | Inspect lumber pile beside your house | **Club Card** (Casino access) |

### Qi's Quest Tips

- **Rainbow Shell**: Beach forageable in Summer only. Save one for this quest
- **Beets**: Buy seeds from the Oasis Shop or Traveling Cart. Plant in Summer/Fall
- **Solar Essence**: Dropped by Ghosts (Mines 80+, Skull Cavern) or found in Omni Geodes
- **Battery Pack**: From Lightning Rod strikes or breaking Lightning Crystals in the Volcano Dungeon

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I miss any quests permanently?

Most quests are time-gated but not missable. The "To The Beach" quest has a deadline (5:00 PM on Spring 2), so don't miss it. Mail quests with delivery deadlines must be completed before the deadline expires.

### Do quests carry over between years?

Yes. If you don't complete a mail quest in time, it simply expires with no penalty. The quest won't reappear, but the rewards aren't critical for progression.

### What happens if I choose the Joja route?

If you buy a Joja Membership (5,000g), the Community Center becomes a Joja Mart warehouse. The "Rat Problem" and "Meet The Wizard" quests become irrelevant. You pay to unlock each area instead of completing bundles.

### Is there a main "ending" to the game?

Stardew Valley doesn't have a traditional ending. After completing Perfection (all achievements), the Summit area unlocks with a final scene. You can continue playing indefinitely.
