# Stardew Valley Trout Derby Guide

The **Trout Derby** is a fishing mini-festival introduced in version 1.6. It takes place during Summer and offers players a chance to win valuable prizes by catching Rainbow Trout with special Golden Tags.

---

## When and Where

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Dates** | Summer 20th and 21st |
| **Time** | 6:10 AM to 2:00 AM (both days) |
| **Location** | Cindersap Forest, by the river near Marnie's Ranch |
| **Season** | Summer |

Unlike most festivals, the Trout Derby does **not** close shops or homes in Pelican Town. Time continues to pass normally, and you still need to feed your animals.

---

## How It Works

### Golden Tags

During the festival, every Rainbow Trout catch (from anywhere except Fish Ponds) has a **33% chance** to give you a **Golden Tag**.

- This chance is **multiplied** by the number of Rainbow Trout caught at once:
  - 1 Trout = 33% chance
  - 2 Trout = 66% chance
  - 3 Trout = 99% chance
- Only **1 Golden Tag** can be obtained per catch, even if multiple trout are caught
- If you get a Fishing Treasure Chest, the Golden Tag comes with the chest
- Golden Tags **carry over** from day to day and can be saved for subsequent years

---

## Prize Booth Rewards

Exchange Golden Tags at the Derby Booth for prizes. The first prize is always:

### First Prize (Guaranteed)
- **Tent Kit** (1)

### Subsequent Prizes (In Order)
After the Tent Kit, prizes are given in this repeating sequence:

| Order | Prize |
|-------|-------|
| 1 | Bucket Hat (1) |
| 2 | Crab Pot (1) |
| 3 | Mystery Box (3) |
| 4 | Diamond (1) |
| 5 | Mounted Trout (1) |
| 6 | Deluxe Bait (20) |
| 7 | Triple Shot Espresso (2) |
| 8 | Quality Sprinkler (1) |
| 9 | Warp Totem: Farm (3) |
| 10 | Omni Geode (3) |

After receiving all 10 prizes, the cycle repeats starting from Bucket Hat again.

**Note:** Each Golden Tag must be exchanged individually, even if you have multiple tags.

## Optimal Strategies

### Best Bait and Tackle

1. **Challenge Bait** (Best option)
   - Catches 3 fish at once when you get a perfect catch
   - With 3 Rainbow Trout, Golden Tag chance becomes 99%
   - If you can consistently get perfect catches, this is the most efficient

2. **Rainbow Trout Bait**
   - Significantly increases Rainbow Trout spawns
   - Craft at a Bait Maker using Rainbow Trout
   - You can bring the Bait Maker to the festival to craft more on-site

3. **Training Rod** (Last resort)
   - Only useful if you have low fishing level and no bait/tackle
   - Makes catching easier but limits fish quality

### Best Tackle

- **Cork Bobber** - Increases fishing bar size, making perfect catches easier
- Any fishing buff food also helps maintain perfect catches

### General Tips

1. **Fish Close to Shore** - Being close to shore with no luck buffs and low fishing level actually improves Rainbow Trout chances
2. **Bring a Bait Maker** - Set it up at the festival to convert caught trout into more Rainbow Trout Bait
3. **Save Golden Tags** - They carry over between years, so you can save up for future derbies
4. **Check the Sign** - A few days before the festival, a sign appears at the derby location with event details

## Festival Characters

- **Willy** is present at the festival, making his shop unavailable during the event (but you can still give him gifts)
- Various fishermen and fisherwomen from outside Pelican Town attend with unique dialogue

## Fun Secret

If you consume **Mayonnaise** to recover energy during the festival, nearby fishers will react with humorous dialogue:
- "Gross..."
- "Did you just...?"
