# Giant Stump Guide

## Overview

The **Giant Stump** is a new location introduced in **Stardew Valley 1.6**. It appears in **Cindersap Forest** after a **strong wind storm** event. The stump becomes home to a family of raccoons who offer the player a series of quests with unique rewards.

## How to Unlock

1. Progress through the game until a **strong wind storm** occurs (random event).
2. After the storm, check **Cindersap Forest** for the newly fallen Giant Stump.
3. Approach the stump to meet the raccoons and begin their questline.

## Raccoon Quests

The raccoon family will ask for various items over multiple quests:

### Quest 1
- Typically requests common forageables or crops.
- Reward: Friendship and small items.

### Quest 2
- Requests slightly rarer items.
- Reward: Better items, possibly seeds or forageables.

### Quest 3
- May request crafted items or specific fish.
- Reward: Unique items or resources.

### Quest 4+
- Later quests may request more difficult-to-obtain items.
- Rewards include rare seeds, forageables, and other valuable items.

## Rewards

Completing raccoon quests provides:
- Unique items not obtainable elsewhere.
- Rare seeds and forageables.
- Resources like hardwood and moss.
- Progress toward 100% completion.

## Tips

- The strong wind storm is a random event; check the forest regularly in later years.
- Keep a variety of items in storage to quickly fulfill raccoon requests.
- The quests are optional but provide valuable rewards.
- The Giant Stump remains in the forest permanently after appearing.
