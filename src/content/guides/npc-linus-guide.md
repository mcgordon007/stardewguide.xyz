# Stardew Valley Linus Guide: Complete NPC Overview

Linus is a homeless man who lives in a tent near the mountains in Stardew Valley. He's a kind, misunderstood character who teaches survival skills and represents the marginalized members of society. This comprehensive guide covers everything about Linus, from his schedule to his basket quest to his gift preferences.

## Basic Information

### Character Overview

**Name**: Linus
**Role**: Homeless Man, Survival Expert
**Location**: Mountains (Tent near Railroad)
**Family**: None (lives alone)
**Birthday**: Winter 3
**Marriageable**: No

### Personality

Linus is a gentle, misunderstood man who lives a simple life in his tent. He's often judged by villagers who don't understand his lifestyle, but he's content with his choices. Linus is knowledgeable about survival skills, foraging, and living off the land. He's grateful for kindness and friendship, often sharing his wisdom with those who approach him respectfully.

## Basket Quest

### Quest Overview

**Quest Name**: Linus's Basket
**Trigger**: Find basket in Backwoods
**Reward**: Friendship with Linus

### Quest Steps

1. **Find the Basket**: The basket appears in the Backwoods area (north of your farm)
2. **Pick Up Basket**: Interact with the basket to collect it
3. **Return to Linus**: Bring basket to Linus at his tent
4. **Receive Thanks**: Linus expresses gratitude

### Quest Details

**Basket Location**: 
- Backwoods area (north of farm)
- Near the tunnel entrance
- Visible on the ground

**When Available**:
- After reaching certain friendship level
- Basket appears randomly
- Check Backwoods regularly

### Quest Rewards

- Friendship increase with Linus
- Special dialogue
- Understanding of Linus's life
- No gold reward

## Heart Events

### 3 Hearts

**Trigger**: Visit Linus at his tent

**Event**: Linus shares his philosophy about living off the land. He explains why he chooses this lifestyle and his contentment with simplicity.

### 4 Hearts - Basket Quest

**Trigger**: Find and return Linus's basket

**Event**: Linus is grateful for your kindness. He shares more about his life and expresses appreciation for your friendship.

### 6 Hearts

**Trigger**: Visit Linus at his tent or see him in town

**Event**: Linus teaches you survival skills. He may show you how to forage effectively or share tips about living outdoors.

### 8 Hearts - Survival Skills

**Trigger**: Visit Linus at his tent

**Event**: Linus invites you to learn more about his lifestyle. He demonstrates how to cook outdoors using foraged ingredients.

**Reward**: Linus teaches you how to make Wild Seeds and shares foraging wisdom.
