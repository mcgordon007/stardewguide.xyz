# Harvey Complete Guide

Harvey is a marriageable male NPC in Stardew Valley, living at Harvey's Clinic in Pelican Town. He is the town doctor, passionate about the health of the townspeople. He is a kind, respected bachelor.

## Basic Information

- **Birthday**: Winter 14
- **Address**: Harvey's Clinic (Pelican Town, second-floor apartment)
- **Family**: None (lives alone)
- **Marriage**: Yes
- **Clinic Check Days**: Checks villagers on Tuesday and Thursday

## Schedule

### Spring
- **Regular Schedule**: 7:00 AM stands in front of room refrigerator; 8:30 AM works at Clinic front desk; 12:00 PM goes to park west of town square; 5:30 PM returns to Clinic standing right of front desk; 6:40 PM goes upstairs to room reading in front of bookshelf; 10:00 PM sleeps.
- **Rainy Day**: 8:00 AM in room; 8:40 AM works at Clinic front desk; 12:00 PM goes upstairs to room sitting in front of radio; 4:00 PM moves to reading in front of bookshelf; 5:30 PM goes to Stardrop Saloon; 10:00 PM returns home to sleep.
- **Tuesday and Thursday**: 7:30 AM in Clinic examination room; 12:50 PM moves to waiting room; 1:30 PM returns to examination room; 4:30 PM works at Clinic front desk; 6:00 PM goes upstairs to room reading in front of bookshelf; 11:00 PM sleeps.
- **Friday**: 7:00 AM stands in front of room refrigerator; 8:30 AM works at Clinic front desk; 12:00 PM goes to Pierre's General Store; 3:00 PM returns to Clinic room; 10:00 PM sleeps.
- **Saturday**: 8:30 AM leaves home for Museum; 3:00 PM leaves Museum to return to Clinic room.

## Heart Events

### 2 Heart Event
Enter Harvey's Clinic, you will see him working at the front desk. He will talk to you about his work and his concern for the townspeople's health.

### 4 Heart Event
Enter Harvey's Clinic between 9:00 AM and 4:00 PM, you will see him examining a villager. He will show you his medical skills and talk about his love for medicine.

### 6 Heart Event
Enter Harvey's Clinic between 11:00 AM and 4:00 PM, you will see him in his room. He will open up to you, talking about his dream of becoming a pilot.

### 8 Heart Event
Enter Harvey's Clinic between 9:00 AM and 4:00 PM, you will see him in his room. He will invite you to ride a hot air balloon together, and on the balloon he will confess to you.

### 10 Heart Event
Go to town in the evening (8:00 PM to midnight), Harvey will invite you to watch a movie together. After the movie, he will confess to you.

### 14 Heart Event
Post-marriage event. Harvey will invite you to participate in a special event, showing how much he values your relationship.

## Marriage

After marrying Harvey, he will move into your farmhouse. He will set up a medical workstation in the farmhouse, sometimes help fill the pet's water bowl or check your health. He will also occasionally give you gifts, such as Coffee or Super Meal.

## Relationships

- **Maru**: Harvey's colleague at the Clinic, they work together. If the player doesn't invite them to the Flower Dance, Harvey will dance with Maru.

## Tips

- Among Harvey's favorite gifts, Coffee can be purchased from Stardrop Saloon or brewed with a Keg (requires Coffee Bean).
- Pickles can be made with a Preserves Jar (requires any vegetable).
- Super Meal requires learning the cooking recipe (from Kent at 7 hearts friendship).
- Truffle Oil can be made with an Oil Maker (requires Truffle, need to raise pigs).
- Wine can be brewed with a Keg (requires any fruit).
- His birthday is Winter 14, gift effects are doubled on this day (8x friendship).
- Harvey performs villager checkups at the Clinic on Tuesday and Thursday, he usually won't leave the Clinic on these days.
