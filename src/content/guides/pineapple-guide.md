# Pineapple Guide

Pineapples are a tropical crop introduced in Stardew Valley 1.5. They grow from Pineapple Seeds and offer excellent profitability, especially on Ginger Island where they can be grown year-round. This guide covers everything you need to know about farming Pineapples.

## What Are Pineapples?

Pineapples are a fruit crop that grows from **Pineapple Seeds**. Unlike regular fruit trees, Pineapples are planted like crops and continue to produce fruit after the initial harvest.

## How to Get Pineapple Seeds

### Sources

| Source | Price/Requirement | Notes |
|--------|-------------------|-------|
| Island Trader | 1 Magma Cap | Available on Ginger Island |
| Tiger Slimes | Random drop | Found in Tiger Slime Grove on Ginger Island |
| Golden Coconuts | Random drop | Crack open Golden Coconuts at the Blacksmith |

## Growing Pineapples

### Requirements

- **Growth Time:** 14 days to first harvest
- **Regrowth:** 7 days after each harvest
- **Season:** Summer (in Stardew Valley) or All Seasons (on Ginger Island or in Greenhouse)
- **XP:** 30 Farming XP per harvest

### Growth Stages

| Stage | Duration |
|-------|----------|
| Stage 1 | 1 day |
| Stage 2 | 3 days |
| Stage 3 | 3 days |
| Stage 4 | 4 days |
| Stage 5 | 3 days |
| **Total to Harvest** | **14 days** |
| **Regrowth** | **7 days** |

### Profitability

Pineapples are one of the most profitable crops in the game, especially when grown on Ginger Island:

- **Base Profit:** 300g every 7 days after initial 14-day growth
- **Gold per Day:** Approximately 42.86g/day (after first harvest)
- **With Artisan:** Wine sells for 1,260g (regular quality)

### Gifting

- **Like:** Demetrius, Elliott, Harvey, Jodi, Kent, Leah, Linus, Pam, Robin, Sandy, Shane
- **Neutral:** Most other villagers
- **Dislike:** Abigail, Haley, Jas, Vincent

### Other Uses

- **Tailoring:** Used with Cloth to create Relaxed Fit Pants
- **Dyeing:** Yellow dye for clothing
- **Fish Pond:** Lionfish may request 1 Pineapple to increase pond capacity from 3 to 5
- **Special Orders:** Caroline may request 100 Pineapples in a Special Order

## Tips for Pineapple Farming

1. **Ginger Island Best:** Plant on Ginger Island for year-round production with no season restrictions
2. **Speed-Gro:** Use Deluxe Speed-Gro to reduce initial growth to 10 days
3. **Artisan Profession:** Essential for maximizing profits - Pineapple Wine is extremely valuable
4. **Greenhouse:** Alternative location for all-season growing
5. **Mass Production:** With regrowth every 7 days, a large Pineapple farm generates massive income
