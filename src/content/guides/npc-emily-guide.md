## Heart Events

### 2 Heart Event
- **Trigger**: Enter Emily and Haley's house
- **Plot**: Emily is sewing, she shows you her work and talks about her love for fashion.
- **Choice Effect**: Appreciating her work increases friendship.

### 4 Heart Event
- **Trigger**: Enter her house when Emily is home
- **Plot**: Emily is practicing yoga, she invites you to join.
- **Choice Effect**: Participating in her yoga practice increases friendship.

### 6 Heart Event
- **Trigger**: Enter Stardrop Saloon
- **Plot**: Emily is working at the Saloon, she shows you a special drink she mixed.
- **Choice Effect**: Tasting her drink increases friendship.

### 8 Heart Event
- **Trigger**: After receiving Emily's letter, enter town in the evening
- **Plot**: Emily invites you to a special fashion show, she designed an outfit for you.
- **Choice Effect**: Attending her fashion show increases friendship.

### 10 Heart Event
- **Trigger**: After receiving Emily's letter, enter the forest in the evening
- **Plot**: Emily is performing a mysterious ritual in the forest, she shows you her spiritual world.
- **Choice Effect**: Accepting her spiritual world increases friendship.
- **Note**: Need to purchase Mermaid's Pendant to propose

### 14 Heart Event (Post-Marriage)
- **Trigger**: After marriage, enter the farmhouse
- **Plot**: Emily wants to establish a sewing studio on the farm, she asks for your opinion.
- **Choice Effect**: Supporting her fashion career increases friendship.

## Marriage Information

### Proposal Requirements
1. Reach 10 hearts friendship with Emily
2. Complete 10 heart event
3. Have at least 1 farmhouse upgrade
4. Purchase Mermaid's Pendant from the Old Mariner (3000g)

### Married Life
- **Room**: Adds a fashion studio-style room on the right side of the farmhouse
- **Help**: Occasionally waters crops, feeds animals, repairs fences
- **Gifts**: Sometimes gives you clothing she made, food, or other items
- **Children**: Can have or adopt up to two children

### Post-Marriage Schedule
- Spends most time inside the farmhouse
- Sometimes works at Stardrop Saloon on Tuesday and Thursday
- Sometimes goes to the forest to gather materials

## Trivia
- Emily is a creative fashion designer
- She works as a waitress at Stardrop Saloon
- She lives with her sister Haley
- She is very interested in mysticism and spiritual practices
- Her favorite colors are purple and blue
