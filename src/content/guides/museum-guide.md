# Stardew Valley Museum Guide

![Artifacts](/images/artifacts-collection.jpg)

The Museum in Stardew Valley, run by Gunther, is located in the southeast corner of Pelican Town. Donating artifacts and minerals to the Museum is a major gameplay objective that rewards players with valuable items, seeds, and the final Stardrop. This comprehensive guide covers all 95 donations, where to find them, and the rewards you'll earn.

## Museum Overview

### Location and Hours
- **Location:** Southeast Pelican Town (east of the Blacksmith)
- **Hours:** 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM
- **Closed:** During festivals

### Gunther's Services
- Accepts donations of artifacts and minerals
- Rewards players for milestones
- Provides information about donated items
- Sells rare items after certain milestones

## Where to Find Artifacts and Minerals

### Artifact Spots (Worm Tiles)

Artifact spots appear as wiggling worms/twigs on the ground. Hoe them to find artifacts.

| Location | Best Artifacts | Spawn Rate |
|----------|----------------|------------|
| The Farm | Chicken Statue, Rusty Spur | Medium |
| Pelican Town | Chewing Stick, Rusty Spoon, Chipped Amphora | High |
| Cindersap Forest | Prehistoric Tool, Arrowhead | Medium |
| Mountain | Ancient Sword, Prehistoric Tool | Medium |
| Beach | Dried Starfish, Glass Shards, Nautilus Fossil | High |
| Railroad | Prehistoric Tibia | Low |
| Desert | Golden Mask, Golden Relic, Palm Fossil | Medium |

### Geode Sources

| Geode Type | Source | Minerals Inside |
|------------|--------|-----------------|
| Geode | Mines (levels 1-39), fishing, duggies | Regular minerals |
| Frozen Geode | Mines (levels 40-79), fishing | Frozen minerals |
| Magma Geode | Mines (levels 80-120), Skull Cavern | Magma minerals |
| Omni Geode | All mine levels, Skull Cavern, fishing, monsters | Any mineral |

**Best Omni Geode Farming:**
- Skull Cavern (iridium nodes drop them)
- Magma Sprites in Volcano Dungeon
- Carbon Ghosts in Skull Cavern
- Purchase from Oasis (Wednesday)

### Fishing Treasure Chests

Many artifacts can be found in treasure chests while fishing:
- Equip the Treasure Hunter tackle
- Eat food that increases treasure chance (Dish O' The Sea)
- Fish in locations with high artifact spawn rates

## Rare and Difficult Items

### Most Difficult to Find

| Item | Why It's Hard | Best Strategy |
|------|---------------|---------------|
| Dinosaur Egg | Very rare spawn | Check artifact spots daily, kill Pepper Rex |
| Prismatic Shard | Extremely rare | Mine iridium nodes, kill monsters in Skull Cavern |
| Strange Dolls | Rare mine drops | Fish in mines, check level 100+ artifact spots |
| Golden Mask/Relic | Desert only | Check Calico Desert daily |
| Dwarf Scroll IV | Rare drop | Kill monsters in lower mine levels |

### Tips for Finding Rare Items

1. **Check Artifact Spots Daily:** They disappear if not dug up
2. **Fish with Treasure Hunter:** Increases chest spawn rate
3. **Process Omni Geodes:** Clint can process them for rare minerals
4. **Kill All Monsters:** Many drop artifacts or geodes
5. **Visit the Desert:** Unique artifacts only found there
6. **Use the Statue of Endless Fortune:** Can produce minerals

## Museum Layout and Organization

### Donation Organization

The Museum has designated spots for each item type:
- **Left Side:** Artifacts (arranged by type)
- **Right Side:** Minerals (arranged by type)
- **Back Room:** Special displays (unlocked at milestones)

### Duplicate Handling

**What to do with duplicates:**
- **Sell:** Most can be sold for gold
- **Gift:** Some villagers like specific artifacts/minerals
- **Craft:** Ancient Seeds can be crafted into plantable seeds
- **Keep:** Dinosaur Eggs for hatching, Prismatic Shards for weapons

**Cannot be donated twice:**
Once an item is donated, Gunther will not accept another of the same type.

## Strategies for Completing the Collection

### Early Game (Year 1)

1. **Check for artifact spots daily** on your farm and in town
2. **Process all geodes** immediately to build collection
3. **Fish regularly** for treasure chests
4. **Explore the mines** thoroughly for monster drops
5. **Save rare items** like Dinosaur Eggs (don't sell!)

### Mid Game (Year 2)

1. **Focus on missing items** - check collection tab
2. **Farm Skull Cavern** for Omni Geodes and rare drops
3. **Check the Traveling Cart** for rare items
4. **Befriend the Dwarf** (donate 4 items first) for shop access
5. **Visit the Desert regularly** for Golden artifacts

### End Game

1. **Process hundreds of Omni Geodes** if missing minerals
2. **Farm specific monsters** for remaining artifacts
3. **Fish extensively** with treasure hunter
4. **Check all artifact spots** across the entire map daily

### Collection Tracking

Use the Collections tab in your inventory to track:
- Items donated (shown with Gunther's icon)
- Items found but not donated
- Items never found (blank spaces)

## Special Notes

### Dwarf Translation

Donating all 4 Dwarf Scrolls allows you to:
- Understand the Dwarf's speech
- Purchase items from the Dwarf's shop
- Learn about Dwarvish history

### Ancient Seeds

The Ancient Seed artifact is special:
- Donate to Museum to receive plantable seed recipe
- Craft Ancient Seeds into plantable Ancient Fruit seeds
- Ancient Fruit grows in all seasons (except Winter)
- One of the most profitable crops in the game

### Prismatic Shard Uses

While valuable to donate, Prismatic Shards have other uses:
- Galaxy Sword (best weapon) at Calico Desert
- Wedding Ring (multiplayer) recipe
- Dyeing clothes
- Gifting (loved by most villagers)
- Selling for 2,000g

### Dinosaur Eggs

- Can be donated to Museum
- Can be placed in incubator (Big Coop required)
- Hatches into a dinosaur that lays more eggs
- Dinosaur Mayonnaise sells for 800g

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Selling the first Dinosaur Egg** - Always incubate first
2. **Not checking artifact spots** - They disappear after a day
3. **Selling Prismatic Shards early** - Save for Galaxy Sword
4. **Ignoring geodes** - Process them regularly
5. **Not befriending the Dwarf** - His shop has unique items
6. **Missing the Sewers** - Rusty Key unlocks at 60 donations

## Conclusion

Completing the Museum collection is one of Stardew Valley's most rewarding challenges. The 95 donations require patience, exploration, and a bit of luck. With the strategies outlined in this guide, you'll be well on your way to earning that final Stardrop and the prestigious Stardew Hero Trophy. Remember to check those artifact spots daily and process every geode you find!

Happy collecting!
