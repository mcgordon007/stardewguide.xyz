# Complete Guide to Krobus's Sewers Shop in Stardew Valley

Krobus is a shadow creature who lives in the Sewers beneath Pelican Town and operates a small shop that sells unique items not available anywhere else. His shop is the only place to buy certain essential items, including a Stardrop and various shadow-themed materials. This guide covers Krobus's complete inventory, prices, and strategies for making the most of his shop.

## How to Access the Sewers

### Unlocking the Sewers

Before you can visit Krobus's shop, you need to unlock the Sewers:

1. **Complete the Community Center**: Finish all bundles in the Community Center, OR purchase a Joja Membership and complete all Joja Community Development projects.
2. **Receive the Rusty Key**: After completing the above, Mayor Lewis will mail you the Rusty Key the next day.
3. **Enter the Sewers**: Use the Rusty Key on the sewer cover located in the south of Pelican Town, near the bridge to the beach.

### Sewer Navigation

- **Entrance**: The sewer cover is located just south of the bridge connecting Pelican Town to the beach area.
- **Interior**: The Sewers are a small area with waterways, a few forageable items, and Krobus's shop.
- **Safety**: There are no enemies in the Sewers, making it a safe area to visit.

## Krobus's Shop Location

### Where to Find the Shop

Krobus's shop is located in the lower-right corner of the Sewers:

- **Visual**: Look for a small counter or stand where Krobus stands.
- **Hours**: The shop is open whenever Krobus is present in the Sewers.
- **Krobus's Schedule**: Krobus is in the Sewers most days, but he occasionally visits other locations. Check his schedule to ensure he is available.

## Item Details and Uses

### Void Essence (40g)

Void Essence is a crafting material dropped by Shadow Brutes and Shadow Shamans in the mines. Buying from Krobus is a convenient alternative to farming:

**Uses**:
- Crafting: Dark Ring (1 Void Essence + 5 Iron Bar)
- Crafting: Void Ghost Pendant (1 Void Essence + 1 Battery Pack)
- Crafting: Thorns enchantment at the forge
- Gift: Loved by Krobus, liked by the Wizard

### Solar Essence (40g)

Solar Essence is dropped by Serpents in the Skull Cavern and other locations:

**Uses**:
- Crafting: Iridium Band (5 Solar Essence + 5 Void Essence + 1 Galaxy Soul)
- Crafting: Infinity Band (at the forge)
- Gift: Liked by several NPCs
- Consumable: Can be eaten for a small energy boost

### Void Egg (200g)

The Void Egg is one of the most interesting items in Krobus's shop:

**Uses**:
- **Hatching**: Place in an incubator to hatch a Void Chicken, which produces Void Eggs daily.
- **Cooking**: Used in certain cooking recipes.
- **Gift**: Loved by Krobus.
- **Mayonnaise**: Can be placed in a Mayonnaise Machine to produce Void Mayonnaise.

**Void Chicken Details**:

| Egg Production | Every day (like regular chickens) |
| Egg Type | Void Egg |
| Void Mayonnaise Value | 275g (Artisan profession: 385g) |

## The Stardrop: Investment Analysis

### ROI Calculation

The Stardrop costs 20,000g and provides +34 max energy permanently:

- **Extra Energy Value**: 34 energy per day x 365 days = 12,410 extra energy per year.
- **Energy-to-Gold Ratio**: If each energy unit is worth ~5g in productivity, that is ~62,050g in value per year.
- **Payback Period**: Less than 4 months of gameplay.

**Does Krobus's shop restock every day?**
Limited-stock items restock daily. Always-available items like Void Essence and Solar Essence are always in stock.

**Can I buy multiple Stardrops from Krobus?**
No, the Stardrop is a one-time purchase. After buying it, it will never appear in his shop again.

**Is the Void Chicken worth raising?**
Yes, Void Chickens produce Void Eggs daily, which can be made into Void Mayonnaise (275g base). With the Artisan profession, Void Mayonnaise sells for 385g, making it a profitable investment.

**Does Krobus leave the Sewers?**
Krobus occasionally visits other locations, but he is in the Sewers most days. If he is not there, check back the next day.

**Can I sell items to Krobus?**
No, Krobus's shop is buy-only. You cannot sell items to him.
