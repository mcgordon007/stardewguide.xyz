# Complete Ginger Island Guide

![Ginger Island](/images/location-ginger-island.jpg)

Ginger Island is a tropical paradise introduced in Stardew Valley's 1.5 update. This expansive new area offers year-round farming, unique forageables, dangerous dungeons, and the ultimate endgame challenge: collecting all 130 Golden Walnuts. This comprehensive guide covers everything you need to know about unlocking, exploring, and mastering Ginger Island.

## Unlocking Ginger Island

Ginger Island is not accessible from the start of the game. You must complete several steps to unlock travel to this tropical paradise.

### Requirements to Unlock

1. **Complete the Community Center** OR **Complete the Joja Warehouse**
2. **Receive Willy's Letter:** The day after completion, Willy sends you a letter
3. **Visit Willy's Fish Shop:** Go to the back room
4. **Repair Willy's Boat:** Collect the required materials

### Boat Repair Materials

| Material | Amount | How to Obtain |
|----------|--------|---------------|
| **Hardwood** | 200 | Chop Large Stumps and Logs (Steel/Gold Axe required) |
| **Battery Pack** | 5 | Lightning Rod during storms, or Solar Panel |
| **Iridium Bars** | 5 | Smelt Iridium Ore (from Skull Cavern) |

### Boat Repair Process

1. Enter Willy's back room (unlocked after receiving his letter)
2. Click on the broken boat
3. Provide materials one section at a time:
   - First: 200 Hardwood (hull repair)
   - Second: 5 Battery Packs (electrical)
   - Third: 5 Iridium Bars (anchor and reinforcement)
4. Wait one day for repairs to complete
5. Boat is available to use from then on

### Using the Boat

**Location:** Willy's Fish Shop back room
**Cost:** Free
**Schedule:** Available any time after repair
**Travel Time:** Instant

**Important:** The boat can be used unlimited times once repaired. There is no cost to travel to or from Ginger Island.

### Best Island Farm Strategy

1. **Plant Ancient Fruit:** Year-round growing means infinite Ancient Fruit harvests
2. **Grow Starfruit:** Best profit per day when processed into wine
3. **Plant Trees:** Banana and Mango trees produce year-round
4. **Use Deluxe Speed-Gro:** Maximize harvest frequency
5. **Set up Kegs/Jars:** Process crops on-site for maximum profit

## Optimal Golden Walnut Collection Route

### Day 1: Island South and East

1. Arrive at Ginger Island
2. Collect visible walnuts on the docks (3)
3. Head south to solve the Mermaid Puzzle (5)
4. Explore Island East, kill Tiger Slimes (up to 5)
5. Find the Gem Bird Shrine (5 when completed)
6. Place a banana at the Banana Shrine (3)
7. **Day 1 Total:** ~21 walnuts

### Day 2: Island West

1. Unlock the Island Farm (10 walnuts)
2. Talk to the Gourmand Frog
3. Plant Melon, Wheat, and Garlic on the island
4. Search the shipwreck area (5)
5. Fish and forage for hidden walnuts (5)
6. **Day 2 Total:** ~10 walnuts (plus unlocks)

### Day 3: Island North and Dig Site

1. Unlock the bridge to the Dig Site (10 walnuts)
2. Dig up all artifact spots (10)
3. Explore the Volcano entrance area (3)
4. Search hot springs area (2)
5. **Day 3 Total:** ~15 walnuts

### Day 4: Volcano Dungeon

1. Enter the Volcano Dungeon
2. Collect chest walnuts on each floor (10)
3. Kill monsters for drops (5)
4. Reach the Forge on Level 10
5. **Day 4 Total:** ~15 walnuts

### Day 5+: Cleanup and Pirate Cove

1. Visit Pirate Cove at night for darts (up to 3)
2. Fish in all areas for remaining walnuts
3. Kill remaining Tiger Slimes
4. Find hidden foraging walnuts
5. Complete Gourmand Frog quests (15)

**Total Collection Time:** 5-7 days for all 130 walnuts

## Tips for Ginger Island

1. **Bring Seeds:** Plant crops immediately upon unlocking the farm
2. **Check Weather:** Gem birds only appear on rainy days
3. **Visit at Night:** Pirate Cove only open after 8 PM
4. **Use Warp Totems:** Save time traveling to and from the island
5. **Prepare for Combat:** Tiger Slimes are tougher than normal slimes
6. **Save Golden Walnuts:** Prioritize unlocking the farmhouse (20) and Qi Room (100)
7. **Fish Everywhere:** Many walnuts come from fishing
8. **Dig Everything:** Artifact spots contain walnuts
9. **Grow Taro:** Needed for various recipes and quests
10. **Explore Thoroughly:** Some walnuts are well-hidden
