# Stardew Valley Lightning Rod Guide

## Overview

The **Lightning Rod** is a unique crafting item that protects your farm from lightning strikes during storms while generating **Battery Packs**. Battery Packs are valuable crafting materials used in many late-game recipes and are essential for completing several bundles and quests.

## Unlock Requirements

- **Foraging Level 6** required to unlock the crafting recipe

## Crafting Materials

To craft a Lightning Rod, you will need:

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Iron Bar | 1 |
| Refined Quartz | 1 |
| Bat Wing | 5 |

## How to Use

1. Place Lightning Rods anywhere on your farm
2. During a **thunderstorm** (random event in Spring, Summer, and Fall), lightning rods have a chance to be struck
3. When struck, the rod will begin processing and create a **Battery Pack** the next morning
4. The rod will show a charging animation while processing

## Battery Pack Production

- A Lightning Rod produces **1 Battery Pack** per lightning strike
- Processing completes the morning after being struck
- Multiple rods increase your chances of collecting Battery Packs
- Rods that are already processing cannot be struck again until finished

## Battery Pack Uses

Battery Packs are used in numerous crafting recipes including:
- Iridium Sprinkler
- Crystalarium
- Slime Egg-Press
- Wood Lamp-post and Iron Lamp-post
- Various quest items and bundle requirements

## Tips & Strategy

- Place multiple Lightning Rods across your farm to maximize collection chances
- Lightning Rods also protect your crops and trees from being destroyed by lightning
- Storms are most common in **Summer**, so prepare rods before then
- Battery Packs sell for 500g each, but are generally more valuable for crafting
- The Weather Report on TV can warn you about upcoming storms

## Storm Mechanics

- Lightning strikes are random during thunderstorms
- Without Lightning Rods, lightning can destroy crops and trees
- Each rod can only process one strike at a time
- Having more rods than strikes per storm ensures maximum protection
