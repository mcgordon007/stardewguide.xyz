# Stardew Valley Cheese Press Guide

## Overview

The **Cheese Press** is an artisan equipment that processes **Milk** into **Cheese** and **Goat Milk** into **Goat Cheese**. Cheese is a valuable artisan good that also restores health and energy, making it useful for mining and combat.

## Unlock Requirements

- **Farming Level 6** required to unlock the crafting recipe

## Crafting Materials

To craft a Cheese Press, you will need:

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Wood | 45 |
| Stone | 45 |
| Hardwood | 10 |
| Copper Bar | 1 |

## How to Use

1. Place the Cheese Press on your farm or inside a shed
2. Interact with it while holding **Milk** or **Goat Milk**
3. Processing time: **200 minutes** of in-game time (approximately 3.3 hours)

## Output Products

### Cheese (from Milk)
- **Regular Milk** → Cheese (230g)
- **Large Milk** → Cheese (345g)
- Base value: Milk price × 2

### Goat Cheese (from Goat Milk)
- **Regular Goat Milk** → Goat Cheese (400g)
- **Large Goat Milk** → Goat Cheese (600g)
- Base value: Goat Milk price × 2

## Quality and Aging

Cheese and Goat Cheese can be placed in a **Cask** to increase their quality:

| Quality Stage | Time in Cask | Value Multiplier |
|---------------|--------------|------------------|
| Silver | 7 days | ×1.25 |
| Gold | 14 days | ×1.5 |
| Iridium | 28 days | ×2 |

## Tips & Strategy

- The Artisan profession (+40% sell price) significantly boosts cheese profits
- Cheese is an excellent food source, restoring 125 energy and 50 health
- Goat Cheese is one of the most profitable items to age in Casks in the Cellar
- Build multiple Cheese Presses if you have several cows or goats
