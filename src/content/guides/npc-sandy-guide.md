# Stardew Valley Sandy Guide: Complete NPC Overview

Sandy is the cheerful and somewhat mysterious shopkeeper who runs the Oasis shop in the Calico Desert. As one of the few NPCs who lives outside of Pelican Town, Sandy has a unique perspective on the world and provides essential services for players who have unlocked the desert. This comprehensive guide covers everything about Sandy, from her shop inventory to her gift preferences to the requirements for accessing the desert.

## Basic Information

### Character Overview

**Name**: Sandy
**Role**: Desert Shopkeeper
**Location**: Oasis Shop (Calico Desert)
**Family**: None known
**Birthday**: Fall 15
**Marriageable**: No

### Personality

Sandy is a friendly, upbeat, and somewhat lonely woman who has chosen to live in the desert running her shop. She is always happy to see visitors and enjoys chatting with the player about life in the desert. Despite her cheerful demeanor, there are hints of loneliness in her dialogue, suggesting that she does not get many visitors. Sandy has a quirky sense of humor and a love for desert plants and fruits. She is one of the few NPCs who seems genuinely excited when the player visits, making her a joy to interact with.

## How to Access Sandy

Before you can meet Sandy, you must first unlock access to the Calico Desert.

### Unlocking the Desert

1. **Complete the Vault bundles** in the Community Center (requires 5,000g, 2,500g, 5,000g, and 10,000g) OR purchase the Bus Stop repair from Joja Mart for 40,000g.
2. **Wait for Pam to repair the bus**. Once the bus is fixed, you can ride it to the desert for 50g per trip (free after the first ride each day).
3. **Enter the Oasis shop** to meet Sandy for the first time.

### Bus Schedule

- The bus departs from the Bus Stop in Pelican Town.
- The bus runs from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
- The cost is 50g per ride (first ride of the day is free once unlocked).
- The bus will not run on festival days or when Pam is unavailable.

## Daily Schedule

Sandy has one of the simplest schedules in the game because she never leaves the Oasis shop.

### Standard Schedule

| Time | Location | Activity |
|------|----------|----------|
| 8:00 AM | Oasis Shop | Opening the shop |
| 10:00 AM | Oasis Shop | Serving customers |
| 2:00 PM | Oasis Shop | Organizing inventory |
| 5:00 PM | Oasis Shop | Closing time (shop closes) |
| 6:00 PM | Back room of Oasis | Personal time |
| 10:00 PM | Back room of Oasis | Going to sleep |

### Special Schedule Notes

- **Sandy is always in the Oasis shop** during business hours (8:00 AM to 5:00 PM).
- **She does not leave the desert**, making her one of the most location-dependent NPCs in the game.
- **Rainy days do not affect her schedule** since it never rains in the desert.
- **Festival Days**: Sandy does not attend town festivals since she lives in the desert.

## Gift Preferences

Sandy's gift preferences reflect her desert lifestyle and her love for tropical and desert plants.

### Loved Gifts (80 points)

These gifts give the maximum friendship boost:

- **Cactus Fruit** - A desert plant that Sandy loves. Can be foraged from cacti in the desert. They regrow every few days.
- **Coconut** - A tropical fruit that Sandy enjoys. Can be foraged from Palm Trees on Ginger Island or purchased from Sandy's own shop on occasion.
- **Mango Sticky Rice** - A tropical dessert that Sandy loves. Made with Mango and Rice.


### Liked Gifts (45 points)

Good alternatives when loved gifts are unavailable:

- **All cooked foods** (except disliked ones)
- **All fruits** (except disliked ones)
- **All vegetables** (except disliked ones)
- **Milk** (any quality)
- **Flowers** (except Poppy and Tulip)
- **Chocolate**
- **Chocolate Cake**
- **Cookies**
- **Spaghetti**
- **Sandfish**
- **Scorpion Carp**

### Neutral Gifts (20 points)

- **Most basic items**
- **Bread**
- **Eggs**

### Disliked Gifts (-20 points)

Avoid giving these items to Sandy:

- **All gems**
- **All minerals and ores**
- **Monster Loot**
- **Mayonnaise**
- **Duck Mayonnaise**
- **Truffle**
- **Truffle Oil**
- **Honey**
- **Coffee**
- **Tea**

### Hated Gifts (-40 points)

Never give these items to Sandy:

- **Clay**
- **Coal**
- **Dried Spices**
- **Fiber**
- **Garlic**
- **Green Tea**
- **Holly**
- **Seaweed**

### Birthday Gift (Fall 15)

Sandy's birthday is Fall 15. Giving a loved gift on this day provides 640 friendship points. Cactus Fruit is the easiest and most thematic loved gift since it can be foraged right in the desert where Sandy lives. Mango Sticky Rice is also an excellent choice if you have Mangoes.

## Heart Events

Sandy's heart events are limited compared to other NPCs, reflecting her isolated location.

### Two Hearts - First Visit

When you first visit the Oasis, Sandy is excited to meet you. She talks about how rare it is to get visitors and how happy she is to have a new customer. This event establishes Sandy's friendly personality and her relative isolation.

### Four Hearts - Desert Life

Visit the Oasis during business hours. Sandy talks about what it is like living in the desert and how she came to run the shop. She mentions that the quiet life suits her but that she sometimes misses the bustle of Pelican Town. This event provides background on Sandy's character.

### Six Hearts - Homesickness

Sandy opens up about feeling homesick occasionally. She talks about missing the changing seasons and the green landscapes of the valley. However, she also expresses her love for the desert and its unique beauty. This event adds emotional depth to Sandy's character.

### Eight Hearts - The Package

Sandy mentions that she sometimes receives packages from Pelican Town and how excited she gets when they arrive. She talks about the small connections she maintains with the outside world and how much she appreciates the player's regular visits.

### Ten Hearts - True Friend

Visit the Oasis on a sunny day. Sandy expresses genuine gratitude for the player's friendship. She talks about how the player has become one of the most important parts of her life in the desert and how she looks forward to each visit. This event is a heartwarming conclusion to Sandy's storyline.

## Friendship Tips and Strategies

### Maximizing Friendship with Sandy

1. **Visit regularly**: Since Sandy lives in the desert, you need to make a conscious effort to visit her regularly. Take the bus at least once a week.
2. **Forage Cactus Fruit**: Cactus Fruit grows on cacti throughout the desert and regrows every few days. Collect them during each visit for free loved gifts.
3. **Birthday priority**: Fall 15 is Sandy's birthday. Save Cactus Fruit or Mango Sticky Rice for this day.
5. **Mango Sticky Rice**: Cook Mango Sticky Rice using Mango and Rice for a loved gift.

### Gift Planning

- **Spring**: Visit the desert for Cactus Fruit.
- **Summer**: Focus on Cactus Fruit foraging during desert visits.
- **Fall**: Forage Cactus Fruit and cook Mango Sticky Rice.
- **Winter**: Use saved Cactus Fruit or Mango Sticky Rice.

### Cost-Effective Strategy

The most cost-effective approach is to forage Cactus Fruit during every desert visit. Since Cactus Fruit regrows and is free, you can give Sandy a loved gift every time you visit without spending any gold. Combined with a loved gift on her birthday, you can build friendship efficiently.

## Conclusion

Sandy is a charming and underrated character in Stardew Valley. Her isolation in the desert makes each visit feel special, and her cheerful personality makes befriending her a rewarding experience. By visiting regularly, foraging Cactus Fruit for loved gifts, and experiencing her heart events, you can build a meaningful friendship with this desert shopkeeper. Sandy reminds us that even in the most remote corners of the world, a friendly face can make all the difference.
