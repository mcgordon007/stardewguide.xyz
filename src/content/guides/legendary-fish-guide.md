# Stardew Valley Legendary Fish Guide

Legendary Fish are the rarest and most challenging fish to catch in Stardew Valley. There are **5 original Legendary Fish** that can only be caught once per save file (or once per player in multiplayer), plus **5 Legendary Fish II** (Extended Family) that appear during Mr. Qi's special quest.

---

## Original Legendary Fish (5)

These fish can only be caught **once per save file** (once per player in multiplayer). They appear with an orange crown in the fishing minigame.

### 1. Crimsonfish

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Location** | Ocean at The Beach (far east end, past the pier) |
| **Season** | Summer |
| **Time** | Any |
| **Weather** | Any |
| **Fishing Level** | 5+ |
| **Difficulty** | 95 (Mixed behavior) |
| **Sell Price** | 1,500g |

**Requirements:**
- Fishing level 5 (naturally or with buffs)
- Stand at the far east end of the beach (repair the small bridge with 300 wood or purchase the second community upgrade)
- Bobber must be at least 3 tiles away from land in all directions
- Each cast has an 18% chance (25% with Curiosity Lure)

---

### 2. Angler

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Location** | Pelican Town river (northernmost end, north of JojaMart) |
| **Season** | Fall |
| **Time** | Any |
| **Weather** | Any |
| **Fishing Level** | 3+ |
| **Difficulty** | 85 (Smooth behavior) |
| **Sell Price** | 900g |

**Requirements:**
- Fishing level 3 (naturally or with buffs)
- Stand at the northernmost end of the river (within 15 tiles of the map edge)
- No distance-from-land requirement for the bobber
- Each cast has a 20% chance (25% with Curiosity Lure)

### 3. Legend

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Location** | Mountain Lake |
| **Season** | Spring |
| **Time** | Any |
| **Weather** | Rain only |
| **Fishing Level** | 10+ |
| **Difficulty** | 110 (Mixed behavior) |
| **Sell Price** | 5,000g |

**Requirements:**
- Fishing level 10 (naturally or with buffs)
- Bobber must be at least 4 tiles away from land in all directions
- The area near the large submerged log works well
- Each cast has a 10% chance (20% with Curiosity Lure)

## Legendary Fish II - Extended Family (5)

These fish appear during Mr. Qi's **"Extended Family"** special quest. They have the same locations and catching requirements as their original counterparts but can be caught in any season while the quest is active. You must catch all 5 within 3 days to complete the quest and earn 20 Qi Gems.

### 1. Son of Crimsonfish
- Same location and requirements as Crimsonfish
- Can be caught in any season during the quest
- Multiple can be caught per quest

### 2. Ms. Angler
- Same location and requirements as Angler
- Can be caught in any season during the quest
- Multiple can be caught per quest

### 3. Legend II
- Same location and requirements as Legend
- Can be caught in any season during the quest
- Multiple can be caught per quest

### 4. Glacierfish Jr.
- Same location and requirements as Glacierfish
- Can be caught in any season during the quest
- Multiple can be caught per quest

### 5. Radioactive Carp
- Same location and requirements as Mutant Carp
- Can be caught in any season during the quest
- Multiple can be caught per quest

## General Tips for Catching Legendary Fish

1. **Use Curiosity Lure** - Increases hook chance for most legendary fish (except Glacierfish)
2. **Eat Fishing Buff Foods** - Dish o' The Sea (+3 Fishing), Seafoam Pudding (+4 Fishing)
3. **Use Trap Bobber or Cork Bobber** - Makes the minigame easier
4. **Practice Perfect Catches** - Perfect catches increase quality and XP
5. **Use Challenge Bait** - Can yield multiple legendary fish per catch (if you haven't caught it yet)
6. **Use Magic Bait** - Allows catching regardless of season, time, or weather

---

## Fish Pond Notes

All legendary fish can be placed in Fish Ponds, but:
- Pond capacity is always **1 fish** (cannot be increased)
- Only produce that specific fish's Roe
- Legend fish roe is extremely valuable (Legend Roe sells for 2,530g base)
