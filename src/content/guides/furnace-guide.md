# Stardew Valley Furnace Guide

## Overview

The **Furnace** is a crucial crafting station used to smelt **Ore** into **Metal Bars**. Metal bars are essential for crafting tools, buildings, and various equipment throughout the game. It is one of the first crafting recipes players obtain.

## Unlock Requirements

- Collect **1 Copper Ore** from mining
- Clint the blacksmith will visit your farm the next morning and give you the recipe
- This typically happens early in the game, usually within the first few days of mining

## Crafting Materials

To craft a Furnace, you will need:

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Copper Ore | 20 |
| Stone | 25 |

## How to Use

1. Place the Furnace on your farm
2. Interact with it while holding the appropriate ore and coal
3. Each smelting operation requires **1 Coal** as fuel
4. Processing time varies by metal type

## Smelting Recipes

| Input | Output | Time | Coal Needed |
|-------|--------|------|-------------|
| 5 Copper Ore + 1 Coal | 1 Copper Bar | 30 in-game minutes | 1 |
| 5 Iron Ore + 1 Coal | 1 Iron Bar | 2 in-game hours | 1 |
| 5 Gold Ore + 1 Coal | 1 Gold Bar | 5 in-game hours | 1 |
| 5 Iridium Ore + 1 Coal | 1 Iridium Bar | 8 in-game hours | 1 |

## Special Recipes

| Input | Output | Time |
|-------|--------|------|
| 2 Copper Bars + 1 Iron Bar + 1 Coal | 1 Steel Bar | 1.5 in-game hours |
| 1 Quartz + 1 Coal | 1 Refined Quartz | 1.5 in-game hours |
| 1 Fire Quartz + 1 Coal | 3 Refined Quartz | 1.5 in-game hours |
| 3 Copper Ore + 1 Coal | 1 Copper Bar (Blacksmith Profession) | 30 minutes |

## Tips & Strategy

- Build multiple furnaces to process large amounts of ore efficiently
- Place furnaces near your farm entrance for easy access after mining trips
- The Blacksmith profession (Mining Level 10) makes bars worth 50% more
- Keep a stockpile of coal, as it is required for every smelting operation
- Refined Quartz is used in many crafting recipes and loved by several villagers
