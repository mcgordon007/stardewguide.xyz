# Stardew Valley Pierre Guide: Complete NPC Overview

Pierre is the owner of Pierre's General Store, the primary shop for seeds, fertilizers, and various supplies in Stardew Valley. He plays a central role in the game's economy and the Community Center storyline. This comprehensive guide covers everything you need to know about Pierre, from his schedule to his shop inventory to his gift preferences.

## Basic Information

### Character Overview

**Name**: Pierre
**Role**: General Store Owner
**Location**: Pelican Town (General Store)
**Family**: Caroline (wife), Abigail (daughter)
**Birthday**: Spring 26
**Marriageable**: No

### Personality

Pierre is a businessman focused on his store's success. He's generally friendly but can be competitive, especially regarding Joja Mart. He cares about his family and the community, though his primary concern is often his business. He's supportive of the Community Center restoration and opposes Joja's corporate presence.

## Community Center Relationship

### Pierre's Role

Pierre is directly connected to the Community Center storyline:
- He opposes Joja Mart's presence
- He benefits from Community Center completion
- His store expands after completion

### Wednesday Closure

Before Community Center completion:
- Pierre's store closes on Wednesdays
- This forces players to use Joja Mart
- Pierre dislikes this competition

After Community Center completion:
- Pierre's store opens on Wednesdays
- He no longer loses business to Joja
- Pierre expresses gratitude

### Joja Route

If you choose the Joja route:
- Pierre's store remains closed on Wednesdays
- He expresses disappointment
- His business suffers

## Heart Events

### 3 Hearts

**Trigger**: Enter Pierre's store when he's working

**Event**: Pierre discusses his business concerns and the competition with Joja Mart. He expresses his desire for the Community Center to be restored.

### 6 Hearts

**Trigger**: Enter Pierre's store during business hours

**Event**: Pierre shares more about his family life and his relationship with Caroline and Abigail. He may hint at family tensions.

### Notes on Heart Events

- Pierre's heart events are relatively simple
- They focus on his business and family
- They provide insight into his character
- No major rewards from events

---

## Family Relationships

### Caroline (Wife)

Caroline lives with Pierre and works in the store occasionally. She's a kind woman who enjoys gardening and spends time in the sunroom.

### Abigail (Daughter)

Abigail is Pierre's daughter who lives at home. She's rebellious and adventurous, often exploring the mines. Pierre worries about her but supports her interests.

### Family Dynamics

- Pierre cares about his family
- Abigail sometimes conflicts with Pierre
- Caroline mediates family tensions
- Pierre wants Abigail to be successful

## Tips for Interacting with Pierre

### Gift Strategy

**Best Gifts**:
- Fried Calamari (requires squid, best loved gift)
- Wine (good value gift)

**Avoid**:
- Joja products (he hates them)
- Bug meat (disliked)
- Low-quality items

### Shop Strategy

**Best Purchases**:
- Seeds for current season
- Backpack upgrades (essential)
- Fertilizers for quality crops
- Cooking ingredients

**Timing**:
- Visit early in the day
- Remember Wednesday closure
- Plan purchases around seasons

### Friendship Benefits

While Pierre isn't marriageable, friendship provides:
- Better dialogue
- Understanding of his character
- Story progression
- Community Center support
