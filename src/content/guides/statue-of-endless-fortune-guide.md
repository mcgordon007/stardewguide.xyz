# Stardew Valley Statue of Endless Fortune Guide

## Overview

The **Statue of Endless Fortune** is a decorative furniture item that also functions as a daily production machine. It is one of the most expensive purchases in the game but provides a steady stream of valuable resources.

## How to Obtain

The Statue of Endless Fortune can be purchased from the **Casino** for **1,000,000g**.

### Unlocking the Casino
1. Complete the **Community Center** or the **Joja Warehouse**.
2. Wait for the **"The Mysterious Qi"** questline to trigger.
3. Follow the quest steps to gain access to the Casino in the **Calico Desert**.
4. The Casino is located in the back room of **Oasis**, behind a locked door.

### Default Production (No Birthday)

If no villager has a birthday on a given day, the statue produces one of the following:
- **Gold Bar**
- **Iridium Bar**
- **Diamond**
- **Prismatic Shard** (very rare)

## Placement

- The Statue of Endless Fortune can be placed anywhere on your farm or in your farmhouse.
- It does **not** need to be placed in a specific location to function.
- It produces items every day, including during festivals and seasons.

## Profitability Analysis

At **1,000,000g**, the statue is a long-term investment:
- Daily production value varies significantly.
- Days with villager birthdays often produce loved gifts, which can be given to that villager for major friendship boosts.
- Default production days yield valuable crafting and selling materials.
- It will take many in-game years to recoup the cost purely through selling produced items.
- The real value lies in the **convenience** of daily resources and **birthday gifts**.

## Tips

- Check the calendar daily to see whose birthday it is and plan accordingly.
- Save produced loved gifts for villager birthdays to maximize friendship gains.
- Gold and Iridium Bars are essential for late-game crafting and building.
- Consider purchasing multiple statues if you have excess Qi Coins or money.
