# Stardew Valley Solar Panel Guide

## Introduction

The **Solar Panel** is a device introduced in Stardew Valley 1.5 that uses sunlight to automatically produce Battery Packs, an important automated resource production device in the mid-to-late game.

## Unlock Requirements

- Complete **Caroline's special order** "Island Ingredients" to obtain the recipe.
- Requires unlocking Ginger Island to receive this order.

## Crafting Materials

- Refined Quartz x10
- Iron Bar x5
- Gold Bar x5

## Function

- Produces **1 Battery Pack** every **7 sunny days**.
- Only accumulates progress on sunny days; rainy/thundery/snowy days do not count.
- Displays a completion animation when producing, and can be collected directly.
- Does not require any input materials; fully automated.

## Battery Pack Uses

Battery Packs are an important resource in the game with a wide range of uses:

1. **Crafting Iridium Sprinkler**
2. **Crafting Crystalarium**
3. **Crafting Slime Egg-Press**
4. **Building Obelisks**
5. **Completing various quests and building upgrades**

## Usage Tips

1. Place Solar Panels on Ginger Island, as it is mostly sunny there, resulting in higher efficiency.
2. Build multiple Solar Panels in batches for a steady supply of Battery Packs.
3. Use in conjunction with Lightning Rods to obtain Battery Packs on rainy days as well.

## Notes

- Solar Panels do not work indoors.
- Winter has fewer sunny days, so production speed will decrease.
- Each Solar Panel calculates time independently.
