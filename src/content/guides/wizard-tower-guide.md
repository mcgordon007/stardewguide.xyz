# Complete Wizard's Tower Guide

![Wizard Tower](/images/location-wizard-tower.jpg)

The Wizard's Tower is a mysterious building located in the western forest of Cindersap Forest. Home to the enigmatic Wizard Rasmodius, this tower holds secrets of ancient magic, powerful buildings for your farm, and connections to the supernatural elements of Stardew Valley. This comprehensive guide covers everything you need to know about the Wizard, his tower, and the magical services he provides.

## Wizard's Tower Location and Access

**Location:** Western edge of Cindersap Forest
**Access:** Walk west from the town entrance, past the lake
**Hours:** 6:00 AM - 11:00 PM (unlocked after 4-heart event)
**First Visit:** Spring 5, Year 1 (cutscene trigger)

### First Visit

On Spring 5, Year 1, walking into Pelican Town from the Bus Stop direction between 9:00 AM and 4:00 PM triggers a cutscene where the Wizard introduces himself and explains the Junimos and Community Center bundles (if not on Joja route).

### Tower Layout

| Floor | Contents |
|-------|----------|
| **Ground Floor** | Wizard's main room, bookshelves, cauldron |
| **Basement** | Shrine of Illusions (unlocked later) |
| **Outside** | Teleportation circle, magical plants |

## The Wizard (M. Rasmodius)

The Wizard is a reclusive but friendly NPC who lives in the tower. He is one of the villagers you can befriend and plays a key role in several quests.

### Wizard's Schedule

The Wizard rarely leaves his tower:

| Time | Location |
|------|----------|
| All Day | Wizard's Tower (6:00 AM - 11:00 PM) |
| Exceptions | Certain festival days and cutscenes |

### Wizard's Loved Gifts

| Gift | Source |
|------|--------|
| **Purple Mushroom** | Foraging in Mines (levels 80-120) or Secret Woods |
| **Super Cucumber** | Fishing in ocean (Summer/Winter, 6:00 PM - 2:00 AM) |
| **Solar Essence** | Monster drops in Mines |
| **Void Essence** | Monster drops in Mines (levels 80-120) |

### Wizard's Liked Gifts

- Quartz
- Fire Quartz
- Frozen Tear
- All Mushrooms (except Red)

### Wizard's Heart Events

| Hearts | Trigger | Effect |
|--------|---------|--------|
| 0 | Spring 5, enter town | Introduction cutscene |
| 4 | Enter Wizard's Tower | Invitation to basement |
| 8 | Mail from Wizard | Recipe for Stardrop Tea |

## Magic Buildings

After reaching 4 hearts with the Wizard, you can purchase magical buildings for your farm from the book in his tower.

### How to Purchase

1. Reach 4 hearts with the Wizard
2. Enter the Wizard's Tower
3. Click on the magic book on the table
4. Select the building you want to purchase

### Junimo Hut

**Cost:** 200 Stone, 9 Starfruit, 100 Fiber
**Function:** Junimos harvest crops within a 17x17 area

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Range** | 17x17 area centered on the hut |
| **Capacity** | 36 crops per day |
| **Season** | Works in all seasons except Winter |
| **Flowers** | Junimos also harvest flowers |

**Important Notes:**
- Junimos stop working when it rains
- They do not harvest crops in Winter
- They do not harvest giant crops
- They store harvested crops in the hut

### Gold Clock

**Cost:** 10,000,000g
**Function:** Prevents debris from appearing on your farm and stops fence decay

| Feature | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| **Debris Prevention** | No more weeds, stones, or branches spawn |
| **Fence Decay** | Fences never break |
| **Status Symbol** | Ultimate endgame purchase |

### Earth Obelisk

**Cost:** 500,000g + 10 Iridium Bars + 10 Earth Crystals
**Function:** Warp to the mountains from your farm

### Water Obelisk

**Cost:** 500,000g + 10 Iridium Bars + 10 Clams
**Function:** Warp to the beach from your farm

### Island Obelisk

**Cost:** 1,000,000g + 10 Iridium Bars + 10 Dragon Teeth
**Function:** Warp to Ginger Island from your farm

### Obelisk Strategy

| Priority | Obelisk | Reason |
|----------|---------|--------|
| 1 | Island | Ginger Island is far and visited frequently |
| 2 | Earth | Mountains contain mines and Robin's shop |
| 3 | Water | Beach is less frequently visited |
| 4 | Gold Clock | Ultimate luxury item |

## Book of Summoning (Pet Change)

After reaching 4 hearts with the Wizard, you can change your pet's appearance using the Shrine of Illusions in the basement.

### How to Use

1. Enter the Wizard's Tower basement (unlocked at 4 hearts)
2. Click on the Shrine of Illusions
3. Select the pet change option
4. Choose your new pet appearance

**Cost:** 500g per change

## The Shrine of Illusions

Located in the Wizard's Tower basement, this shrine allows you to customize your character's appearance.

### How to Access

- Reach 4 hearts with the Wizard
- Enter the tower and go to the basement
- The shrine is in the center of the room

### Customization Options

| Feature | Cost | Options |
|---------|------|---------|
| **Hair Style** | 500g | All available hairstyles |
| **Hair Color** | 500g | Full color palette |
| **Eye Color** | 500g | Full color palette |
| **Skin Tone** | 500g | All available tones |
| **Shirt** | 500g | All unlocked shirts |
| **Pants** | 500g | All unlocked pants |

## Dark Talisman Quest

The Dark Talisman quest is triggered after completing the Community Center and unlocks access to the Witch's Swamp.

### Quest Trigger

**Requirements:**
1. Complete the Community Center OR Joja Warehouse
2. Railroad must be unlocked
3. Receive a letter from the Wizard

### Quest Steps

| Step | Action | Location |
|------|--------|----------|
| 1 | Talk to Wizard | Wizard's Tower |
| 2 | Go to Railroad | North of Spa |
| 3 | Enter cave | Northeast corner of Railroad |
| 4 | Retrieve Dark Talisman | End of cave dungeon |
| 5 | Return to Wizard | Wizard's Tower |

### Cave Dungeon

The cave dungeon contains monsters and requires combat to navigate:

| Monster | Strategy |
|---------|----------|
| **Skeletons** | Standard combat |
| **Ghosts** | Hit them when they materialize |
| **Bats** | Quick attacks |

### Witch's Hut Shrines

Once you have the Dark Talisman, you can access the Witch's Hut which contains three shrines:

| Shrine | Function | Cost |
|--------|----------|------|
| **Shrine of Illusions** | Change appearance | 500g |
| **Dark Shrine of Memory** | Erase divorced spouse's memory | 30,000g |
| **Dark Shrine of Night Terrors** | Toggle monster spawns on farm | Free |

### Void Mayonnaise

To enter the Witch's Hut, you must give Void Mayonnaise to the Henchman guarding the door.

**How to Obtain Void Mayonnaise:**
- Place a Void Egg in a Mayonnaise Machine
- Void Eggs can be purchased from Krobus in the Sewers
- Or obtained from Void Chickens

## The Wizard and the Witch

### Backstory

The Wizard was once married to the Witch, but they separated due to "dark magic disagreements." The Witch now lives in the Witch's Swamp and occasionally flies over your farm, leaving strange effects.

### The Witch's Activities

| Event | Effect |
|-------|--------|
| **Flying Over Farm** | Random occurrence at night |
| **Hexing Slimes** | Can turn slimes black |
| **Cauldron Visits** | Rare cutscene if you're friends with Wizard |

### Abigail Connection (Speculation)

There are hints in the game that Abigail might be the Wizard's daughter:
- Abigail has purple hair (like the Wizard)
- Her mother mentions she has "someone else's" eyes
- The Wizard mentions having a daughter

## Wizard's Tower Strategy

### Early Game

1. **Visit on Spring 5:** Trigger introduction cutscene
2. **Give Gifts Weekly:** Purple Mushrooms are easiest loved gift
3. **Check Schedule:** Wizard is always home during tower hours
4. **Save Resources:** Start saving for magic buildings

### Mid Game

1. **Reach 4 Hearts:** Unlock basement and magic buildings
2. **Purchase Junimo Hut:** Automate crop harvesting
3. **Complete Dark Talisman Quest:** Access Witch's Hut
4. **Customize Appearance:** Use Shrine of Illusions

### Late Game

1. **Purchase Obelisks:** Fast travel around the valley
2. **Save for Gold Clock:** Ultimate farm upgrade
3. **Max Friendship:** Learn all Wizard's recipes
4. **Explore Witch's Hut:** Use all three shrines

## Tips and Tricks

1. **Purple Mushrooms:** Check the Mines (levels 80-120) and Secret Woods for loved gifts
2. **Junimo Hut Placement:** Center it to maximize 17x17 coverage
3. **Appearance Changes:** Save before changing appearance if unsure
4. **Void Mayonnaise:** Keep one on hand for Witch's Hut access
5. **Dark Shrine:** Use Dark Shrine of Night Terrors to toggle monsters if needed

## Summary

The Wizard's Tower offers:
- **Unique NPC:** Befriend the mysterious Wizard
- **Magic Buildings:** Junimo Hut, Obelisks, and Gold Clock
- **Customization:** Change appearance at Shrine of Illusions
- **Quest Content:** Dark Talisman quest and Witch's Hut
- **Endgame Goals:** Purchase all magic buildings

The Wizard and his tower add a mystical element to Stardew Valley. From the practical Junimo Hut to the luxurious Gold Clock, the magical buildings available here can transform your farming experience. Take time to befriend the Wizard and explore all the secrets his tower has to offer.
