# Stardew Valley Secret Notes Guide

Secret Notes are collectible items in Stardew Valley that contain hidden messages, puzzles, and clues to secret content. Introduced in version 1.3, these 25 notes add depth to the game's lore and provide valuable rewards for those who solve their mysteries.

## How to Obtain Secret Notes

### Unlocking Secret Notes

Secret Notes become available after completing the "Winter Mystery" quest:
1. Reach Winter 1 of Year 1 or later
2. Walk to the Bus Stop between 6:00 AM and 4:00 PM
3. A cutscene triggers with a "Shadow Guy" (Krobus)
4. Follow his footprints to the playground
5. Interact with the bush to receive the Magnifying Glass

### Sources of Secret Notes

Once you have the Magnifying Glass, Secret Notes can be found through:

| Source | Chance | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Hoeing Artifact Spots (Worms) | ~3.5% | Most reliable source |
| Killing Monsters | ~1.5% | Any monster can drop |
| Chopping Trees | ~0.5% | Any tree type |
| Fishing Treasure Chests | ~1% | Use Treasure Hunter tackle |
| Breaking Rocks | ~0.5% | Mines and Skull Cavern |

**Note:** You cannot find duplicate Secret Notes. Once collected, that note is removed from the drop pool.

### Secret Note #10 - Rarecrow Location (SOLUTION)

**Content:** A drawing of a person standing at the bus stop with numbers: 1-5-4-8-6-7-2-3

**Solution:** 
1. Go to the Bus Stop
2. Use your hoe on the road at the exact spot shown
3. Dig in the pattern indicated by the numbers

**Reward:** Solid Gold Lewis Statue (Rarecrow #8)

**Note:** This Rarecrow is unique and cannot be obtained any other way.

### Secret Note #11 - Leah's Sculpture

**Content:** Leah's notes about her sculpture ideas and artistic vision.

### Secret Note #13 - Junimo Plush Location (SOLUTION)

**Content:** A drawing of a bush in the playground with a hidden item.

**Solution:**
1. Go to the playground in Pelican Town (near the Community Center)
2. Interact with the large bush north of the playground equipment
3. Receive the Junimo Plush furniture item

**Reward:** Junimo Plush (decorative furniture)

### Secret Note #14 - Elliott's Novel

**Content:** Elliott's notes for his novel, including character ideas.

**Reward:** None (lore only)

**Significance:** Shows Elliott's writing process and literary aspirations.

### Secret Note #15 - Maru's Inventions

**Content:** Maru's notes about her inventions and experiments.

**Reward:** None (lore only)

**Significance:** Reveals Maru's scientific mind and creativity.

### Secret Note #16 - Dwarf's Treasure Hunt (SOLUTION)

**Content:** A map showing the location of a hidden treasure near the railroad.

**Solution:**
1. Go to the Railroad area (unlocked after Earthquake in Summer Year 1)
2. Walk to the north end near the train platform
3. Use your hoe on the ground at the marked location
4. Receive: 1 Gold Bar, 5 Gold Ore, and a Rare Disc

**Reward:** Gold Bar, Gold Ore x5, Rare Disc

### Secret Note #17 - Joja Cola Recipe

**Content:** A recipe for making Joja Cola at home.

**Reward:** None (recipe is a joke)

**Significance:** Humorous note about the artificial nature of Joja Cola.

### Secret Note #18 - Ancient Doll Mystery

**Content:** A disturbing note about the Ancient Doll artifact.

**Reward:** None (lore only)

**Significance:** Adds creepy lore to the Ancient Doll artifact.

### Secret Note #19 - Casino Location (SOLUTION)

**Content:** Directions to a secret club in the desert.

**Solution:**
1. Go to the Calico Desert (unlock via Bus repair or Desert Obelisk)
2. Enter the Oasis shop
3. Walk to the back and interact with the curtain
4. Complete Qi's quest line to gain access to the Casino

**Reward:** Access to the Casino (Blackjack and Slot Machines)

**Note:** You need the Club Card from Qi's quest to enter.

### Secret Note #20 - Train Schedule

**Content:** A schedule showing when trains pass through Pelican Town.

**Reward:** Knowledge of train times

**Train Schedule:**
- Trains can pass on any day between 9:00 AM and 6:00 PM
- The schedule shows various types of trains
- Being present when a train passes can yield resources

### Secret Note #22 - Trash Bear's Location (SOLUTION)

**Content:** A drawing showing where to find the Trash Bear.

**Solution:**
1. Go to Cindersap Forest
2. Look for the Trash Bear near the pond (south of the Wizard's Tower)
3. Appears in Year 3+ if certain conditions are met

**Reward:** Trash Bear cleans up trash around town in exchange for items

### Secret Note #23 - Mermaid Puzzle (SOLUTION)

**Content:** Musical notes and a drawing of Ginger Island.

**Solution:**
1. Go to Ginger Island (unlock via Willy's boat)
2. Visit the Mermaid's area on the beach (rainy days only)
3. Place Flute Blocks on the pedestals
4. Tune them to match the notes: 1-10-8-4-2

**Reward:** 5 Golden Walnuts, Pearl

### Secret Note #24 - Island Gem Birds (SOLUTION)

**Content:** A map of Ginger Island showing four gem bird locations.

**Solution:**
1. On rainy days on Ginger Island, gem-colored birds appear
2. Find all four birds (North, South, East, West areas)
3. Note the gem each bird drops
4. Place the gems on the pedestals in the jungle shrine

**Reward:** 5 Golden Walnuts

**Bird Locations and Gems:**
- North: Amethyst or Topaz
- South: Aquamarine or Jade
- East: Emerald or Ruby
- West: Diamond

### Secret Note #25 - Qi's Challenge (SOLUTION)

**Content:** A cryptic message about finding Qi's room.

**Solution:**
1. Collect 100 Golden Walnuts on Ginger Island
2. Enter Qi's Walnut Room (north of the Volcano)
3. Access special challenges and the Perfection Tracker

**Reward:** Access to Qi's Walnut Room, Qi Gems, special challenges

## Tips for Collecting Secret Notes

### Efficient Farming Methods

1. **Artifact Spot Method (Best)**
   - Check all artifact spots (worm tiles) daily
   - Focus on high-spawn areas: Town, Beach, Railroad
   - Use the hoe on every spot you see

2. **Monster Farming**
   - Farm low-level mines (5-29) for easy kills
   - Use the Burglar's Ring for better drop rates
   - Kill everything while doing other activities

3. **Tree Chopping**
   - Chop trees regularly for wood
   - Secret Notes can drop from any tree type
   - Plant tree farms for efficient chopping

4. **Fishing Method**
   - Use Treasure Hunter tackle
   - Eat Dish O' The Sea for treasure chance
   - Fish in high-traffic areas

### Daily Routine for Secret Notes

**Morning:**
- Check farm for artifact spots
- Check Pelican Town for artifact spots

**Afternoon:**
- Visit Beach and Railroad for artifact spots
- Chop any mature trees

**Evening:**
- Mine for monsters and rocks
- Fish with treasure tackle

## Secret Notes and Game Completion

### Perfection

Secret Notes are not required for Perfection, but they:
- Provide Golden Walnuts (Notes #23, #24)
- Unlock the Casino (Note #19)
- Give access to Qi's Walnut Room (Note #25)
- Provide unique items (Rarecrow, Junimo Plush)

### Achievements

While no achievement specifically requires Secret Notes, they help with:
- "A Complete Collection" (Museum items from rewards)
- "Treasure Trove" (Museum donations)
- "Mystery of the Stardrops" (indirectly through rewards)

## Common Questions

### Can I miss any Secret Notes?
No, once unlocked, Secret Notes can be found at any time. There are no time-limited notes.

### Do I need to keep Secret Notes?
No, after reading them, the information is added to your collection tab. You can safely discard them.

### Can I find Secret Notes in Winter?
Yes, though artifact spots are less common in Winter, they still appear.

### What if I can't find a specific note?
Keep trying! The drop is random. Focus on artifact spots for the best chance.

### Do Secret Notes respawn?
No, once you find a note, you cannot find it again. There are exactly 25 unique notes.
