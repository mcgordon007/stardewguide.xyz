# Complete Guide to the Feast of the Winter Star and Secret Santa in Stardew Valley

The Feast of the Winter Star is the final festival of the year in Stardew Valley, held on Winter 25. It features a Secret Santa gift exchange where you give a gift to another villager and receive one in return. This guide covers how the Secret Santa system works, the best gifts to give and receive, and strategies for maximizing friendship during this special event.

## Overview of the Feast of the Winter Star

### Event Details

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| Date | Winter 25 |
| Time | 9:00 AM to 2:00 AM |
| Location | Pelican Town (near the large tree in the center) |
| Entry | Free, automatic invitation |
| Weather | Always clear (forced by the game) |
| Required | None (all players can attend) |

### What Happens at the Feast

The Feast of the Winter Star is a social event centered around gift-giving:

1. **Arrival**: Enter Pelican Town after 9:00 AM to attend the feast.
2. **Secret Santa Assignment**: You will be assigned a Secret Santa recipient (another villager).
3. **Gift Exchange**: You give a gift to your assigned villager, and another villager gives you a gift.
4. **Socializing**: After the exchange, you can socialize with other villagers.

## Secret Santa Mechanics

### How the Assignment Works

On the morning of Winter 25 (or when you first enter the festival), you will learn who your Secret Santa recipient is:

- **Random Assignment**: The game randomly assigns you a villager as your recipient. You cannot choose who you give a gift to.
- **Notification**: You will receive a notification telling you who your recipient is.
- **Preparation**: You should prepare a gift for your recipient before attending the feast.

### Who Can Be Your Recipient

Your Secret Santa recipient can be any of the villagers in Pelican Town, including:

- All marriage candidates
- Non-marriageable NPCs (Gus, Clint, Wizard, etc.)
- Elderly NPCs (George, Evelyn)
- Children (Jas, Vincent)

Your recipient is randomly selected from the pool of all villagers.

### Who Can Be Your Secret Santa

Similarly, your Secret Santa (the person giving you a gift) is randomly assigned from the villager pool. You do not know who your Secret Santa is until they give you the gift.

## Gift Giving Rules

### What You Can Give

During the Secret Santa exchange, you can give any giftable item to your assigned recipient:

| Gift Type | Allowed | Notes |
|-----------|---------|-------|
| Crops | Yes | Any crop, any quality |
| Animal Products | Yes | Milk, eggs, wool, etc. |
| Foraged Items | Yes | Any forageable item |
| Cooked Dishes | Yes | Any cooked food |
| Artisan Goods | Yes | Wine, cheese, etc. |
| Minerals | Yes | Gems, ores, etc. |
| Furniture | No | Cannot gift furniture |
| Tools | No | Cannot gift tools |
| Trash Items | No | Cannot gift trash/specific items |

### Gift Quality Matters

The quality of your gift affects the friendship bonus:

| Gift Quality | Friendship Bonus |
|-------------|-----------------|
| Loved gift | +80 friendship points |
| Liked gift | +45 friendship points |
| Neutral gift | +20 friendship points |
| Disliked gift | -20 friendship points |
| Hated gift | -40 friendship points |

### Birthday Bonus Does NOT Apply

The Secret Santa gift exchange does NOT provide the 8x birthday bonus. The friendship bonus is calculated normally based on the recipient's preferences. However, since this is a special event, giving a loved gift is still highly recommended.

You do not gain friendship points from receiving a Secret Santa gift. The friendship bonus only applies to the gift you give.

Keep a stash of universally-loved gifts year-round:

### What to Do If You Forget to Prepare

If you arrive at the feast without a prepared gift:

- **Check Your Inventory**: You may have something suitable in your backpack.
- **Forage**: Check the area around the feast for forageable items.
- **Buy Last-Minute**: Some vendors may be near the feast area.
- **Give What You Have**: Even a neutral gift is better than not participating.

## Secret Santa Event Walkthrough

### Step-by-Step Guide

Here is exactly what happens during the Feast of the Winter Star:

1. **Wake Up**: On Winter 25, you receive a letter reminding you about the feast.
2. **Enter Town**: Go to Pelican Town between 9:00 AM and 2:00 AM.
3. **Arrival Scene**: A cutscene shows the town gathered around the large decorated tree.
4. **Learn Your Recipient**: Mayor Lewis announces the Secret Santa assignments. You learn who you are giving a gift to.
5. **Give Your Gift**: Walk up to your assigned recipient and give them your prepared gift.
6. **Receive Your Gift**: Your Secret Santa approaches you and gives you a gift.
7. **Socialize**: After the exchange, you can talk to other NPCs.
8. **Leave**: You can leave the feast at any time by walking to the exit.

### Event Timing

| Time | Event |
|------|-------|
| 9:00 AM | Feast begins, can enter town |
| 9:00 AM - 10:00 AM | NPCs gather, assignments announced |
| 10:00 AM | Gift exchange begins |
| 10:00 AM - 2:00 AM | Socializing and free time |
| 2:00 AM | Day ends if still at feast |

## The Feast of the Winter Star: Social Guide

### NPC Behavior During the Feast

During the feast, NPCs behave differently than on normal days:

- **All NPCs Attend**: Every NPC in Pelican Town attends the feast (except those who are sick).
- **Festive Attire**: Some NPCs wear special winter clothing.
- **Social Interactions**: NPCs interact with each other during the feast.
- **Gift Reactions**: NPCs have unique reactions to receiving Secret Santa gifts.

### Photo Opportunities

The Feast of the Winter Star is one of the most visually appealing events:

- **Decorated Tree**: The large tree in the center of town is beautifully decorated.
- **Snow**: The ground is covered in snow, creating a picturesque scene.
- **NPC Gatherings**: NPCs cluster in groups, creating photo-worthy moments.
- **Gift Exchange**: The gift exchange itself is a memorable moment.

## Secret Santa in Multiplayer

### Multiplayer Secret Santa Rules

In multiplayer, the Secret Santa event works slightly differently:

- **Individual Assignments**: Each player receives their own Secret Santa assignment.
- **No Overlap**: The game ensures no two players are assigned the same recipient.
- **Separate Exchanges**: Each player gives and receives gifts independently.
- **Shared Experience**: All players participate in the same feast event.

### Coordinating Gifts

In multiplayer, coordinate with other players:

- **Share Gift Stash**: Pool resources to ensure everyone has a good gift.
- **Avoid Duplicates**: Make sure no two players give the same NPC the same gift type.
- **Communication**: Discuss gift strategies before the feast.


**Can I choose my Secret Santa recipient?**
No, the recipient is randomly assigned by the game. You cannot choose or change your assignment.

**Does the Secret Santa gift count toward the daily gift limit?**
The Secret Santa gift is a special event gift and does not count toward your normal daily gift limit. You can still give another gift to the same NPC on the same day.

**What happens if I don't attend the feast?**
If you do not attend the feast, you miss the Secret Santa exchange entirely. The event does not happen without your participation.

**Can I give a wrapped gift?**
No, there is no gift-wrapping mechanic in Stardew Valley. You simply give the item directly.

**Does the Secret Santa event affect my relationship with other NPCs?**
No, the Secret Santa exchange only affects your relationship with the specific recipient. Other NPCs are not affected.
