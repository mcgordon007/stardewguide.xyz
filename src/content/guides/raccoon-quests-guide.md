# Stardew Valley Raccoon Quests Guide - Giant Stump & Rewards

The Raccoon questline is a new feature introduced in Stardew Valley 1.6. After a strong wind event reveals a Giant Stump on your farm, you can repair it to attract a family of raccoons who will request various items in exchange for unique rewards.

## How to Start the Raccoon Questline

### Trigger: Strong Wind Event

The questline begins after a **random strong wind event** occurs on your farm. This event:

- Happens randomly after you have progressed sufficiently in the game
- Reveals a Giant Stump somewhere on your farm
- The stump is too large to chop with a regular axe

### Repairing the Giant Stump

To repair the Giant Stump and attract the raccoons:

| Material | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Hardwood | 100 |

Once repaired, a raccoon family will move in, and the questline begins.

## Raccoon Requests and Rewards

The raccoon will visit your farm and make a series of requests. Each request must be completed to unlock the next one.

### Day 1 Request

| Request | Amount |
|---------|--------|
| Crab Pot Fish (any) | 5 |
| Smoked Fish (any) | 1 |

**Reward:** 25 random seeds

### Day 2 Request

| Request | Amount |
|---------|--------|
| Dried Fruit (any) | 1 |
| Jelly (any) | 1 |

**Reward:** Raccoon Journal

### Day 3 Request

| Request | Amount |
|---------|--------|

**Reward:** Raccoon Hat

### Day 4 Request

| Request | Amount |
|---------|--------|
| Pickles (any) | 1 |
| Juice (any) | 1 |

**Reward:** 5 Fairy Dust

### Day 5 Request

| Request | Amount |
|---------|--------|
| 2 random items from the list below | Varies |

**Possible random items:**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Bat Wing | 20 |
| Bug Meat | 20 |
| Coral | 4 |
| Diamond | 1 |
| Various other items | Varies |

**Reward:** Jungle Tank (furniture)

### Day 6+ Requests

After completing the first 5 requests, the raccoon will continue to make random requests that repeat one of the previous 5 patterns. These ongoing requests provide:

- Fairy Dust
- Book of Stars
- Random seeds
- Other miscellaneous rewards

## Good Neighbors Achievement

Completing **9 raccoon requests** in total unlocks the **"Good Neighbors"** achievement.

- Track your progress in the in-game achievements menu
- Continue fulfilling raccoon requests after Day 5 to reach 9 total completions

## Raccoon Wife''s Shop

After progressing through the questline, the raccoon''s wife will open a small shop where you can exchange items for seeds and special items.

### Shop Inventory

The shop offers various seeds and special items in exchange for requested goods. Check back regularly as inventory may change.

## Tips for Completing Raccoon Requests

### Preparation

- **Crab Pot Fish:** Set up crab pots in advance near the farm or in various water locations
- **Smoked Fish:** Build a Fish Smoker (recipe from Willy after catching certain fish)
- **Dried Fruit:** Use a Dehydrator (recipe from Pierre or obtained through other means)
- **Jelly:** Place fruit in Preserves Jars
- **Dried Mushroom:** Use a Dehydrator with mushrooms
- **Pickles:** Place vegetables in Preserves Jars
- **Juice:** Place vegetables in Kegs

### Efficient Completion

1. **Stockpile processed goods** - Keep a supply of smoked fish, jelly, pickles, and juice in chests
2. **Maintain crab pots** - Check crab pots daily for a steady supply of crab pot fish
3. **Use the dehydrator** - Process excess fruit and mushrooms when not in use for other purposes
4. **Plan ahead** - The requests follow a pattern, so you can prepare for upcoming needs

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I complete multiple requests in one day?**
No, raccoon requests are on a set schedule. You must wait for the raccoon to appear with each new request.

**What happens if I don''t have the requested items?**
The raccoon will wait indefinitely. There is no time limit to complete each request.

**Can the Giant Stump appear anywhere on the farm?**
Yes, the stump spawns in a random location on your farm after the wind event.

**Is the Raccoon Hat purely cosmetic?**
Yes, the Raccoon Hat is a wearable cosmetic item that can be equipped in the hat slot.

**What is Fairy Dust used for?**
Fairy Dust instantly completes any machine''s processing when applied. It is extremely valuable for speeding up Kegs, Preserves Jars, and other processors.

## Summary

The Raccoon questline is a fun and rewarding addition in Stardew Valley 1.6. Prepare processed goods in advance to complete requests efficiently and enjoy this charming side quest.
