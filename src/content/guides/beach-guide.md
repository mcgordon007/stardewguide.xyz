# Complete Beach Guide

![Beach](/images/location-beach.jpg)

The Beach is a beautiful coastal area located south of Pelican Town. It offers unique foraging opportunities, excellent ocean fishing, and is home to several important NPCs and locations. This comprehensive guide covers everything you need to know about the Beach, from seasonal forageables to the mysterious Old Mariner.

## Beach Location and Access

**Location:** South of Pelican Town, accessible via the southern exit
**Alternative Access:** Repair the bridge on your farm (300 Wood) for a direct shortcut
**Hours:** Always accessible
**Special:** Some areas require bridge repair to access

### Beach Layout

| Area | Features | Access |
|------|----------|--------|
| **Main Beach** | Sandy area, Willy's Shop, Elliott's Cabin | Always |
| **Tide Pools** | Forageables, unique items | Always |
| **Eastern Beach** | Additional foraging, hidden items | Bridge repair (300 Wood) |
| **Pier** | Enhanced fishing spot | Always |
| **Rocky Outcrop** | Special fishing location | Always |

## Tide Pool Foraging

The tide pools are located on the eastern side of the beach and contain unique forageable items. These pools fill and empty with the tide, revealing different items.

### Tide Pool Mechanics

- Items spawn randomly in tide pool areas
- Forageables respawn daily
- Some rare items only appear during specific weather conditions
- Tide pools are a reliable source of shells and coral

### Tide Pool Exclusive Items

| Item | Rarity | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| **Sea Urchin** | Uncommon | Spawns on rocks near water |
| **Coral** | Common | Found in shallow pools |
| **Rainbow Shell** | Rare | Any season, very low spawn rate |

## The Old Mariner

**Location:** Tide pools on rainy days (east side of beach)
**Condition:** Must have repaired the bridge (300 Wood)
**Appearance:** Only appears on rainy days

The Old Mariner is a mysterious figure who sells the Mermaid's Pendant, required for marriage.

### Mermaid's Pendant

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Cost** | 5,000g |
| **Requirement** | 10-heart relationship with eligible bachelor/bachelorette, upgraded farmhouse |
| **Use** | Propose marriage |
| **Stock** | One per day (if conditions met) |

**Important Notes:**
- The Old Mariner will not appear unless you have repaired the bridge
- He only appears on rainy days
- You must have a 10-heart relationship with a marriage candidate
- You must have upgraded your farmhouse at least once
- The Pendant can only be purchased once per day

### Wedding Ring (Multiplayer)

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Cost** | 10,000g |
| **Requirement** | Multiplayer mode |
| **Use** | Propose to another player |
| **Recipe** | Can also be crafted (5 Iridium Bars, 1 Prismatic Shard) |

## Beach Bridge Repair

**Location:** Eastern side of beach
**Cost:** 300 Wood
**Benefits:**
- Access to eastern beach area
- Access to tide pools
- Access to legendary fish (Crimsonfish)
- Access to the Old Mariner
- Additional foraging area

**How to Repair:**
1. Walk to the broken bridge on the eastern beach
2. Click on the bridge with Wood in your inventory
3. Select "Yes" to repair

## Special Beach Events

### The Luau

**Date:** Summer 11
**Time:** 9:00 AM - 2:00 PM
**Location:** Beach

The Governor visits Pelican Town for the annual Luau. Your contribution to the community soup affects the event outcome.

**Soup Ingredients and Reactions:**

| Ingredient Quality | Governor's Reaction | Friendship Impact |
|-------------------|---------------------|-------------------|
| Trash (Sap, Holly, etc.) | Disgusted | -50 friendship with all villagers |
| Silver/Gold quality crop | Pleased | +120 friendship with all villagers |
| Iridium quality crop / Loved item | Ecstatic | +255 friendship with all villagers |
| Milk / Common items | Neutral | No change |

### Dance of the Moonlight Jellies

**Date:** Summer 28
**Time:** 10:00 PM - 12:00 AM
**Location:** Beach

A beautiful nighttime festival where glowing jellyfish migrate past the beach.

**Features:**
- Watch the jellyfish migration with the townspeople
- No gameplay mechanics, purely aesthetic
- One of the most visually stunning events in the game

### Elliott's 8-Heart Event

**Trigger:** 8 hearts with Elliott, sunny day, 7:00 AM - 10:00 PM
**Location:** Beach

Elliott reads you a passage from his novel on the beach. This event is required to reach 10 hearts and propose.

## Beach Tips and Tricks

1. **Check Daily:** Beach forageables respawn every day
2. **Winter Priority:** Nautilus Shells are most common in Winter and needed for bundles
3. **Rainy Days:** Visit the tide pools on rainy days for the Old Mariner
4. **Early Game:** Sell beach forageables for quick early gold
5. **Gifting:** Save Rainbow Shells and Nautilus Shells for villager gifts
6. **Fishing Spot:** The pier provides slightly better fishing odds
7. **Crab Pots:** Place crab pots along the beach for passive income
8. **Bundle Planning:** Save one of each shell type for Community Center bundles
9. **Elliott Friendship:** Visit Elliott at his cabin to build friendship
10. **Bridge Priority:** Repair the bridge as soon as possible for full beach access
