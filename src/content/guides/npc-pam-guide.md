# Stardew Valley Pam Guide: Complete NPC Overview

Pam is one of the most colorful characters in Pelican Town. As the town's bus driver and a devoted (if somewhat rough-around-the-edges) mother, she brings a unique personality to the valley. This comprehensive guide covers everything about Pam, from her daily schedule to her gift preferences to her role in unlocking the desert.

## Basic Information

### Character Overview

**Name**: Pam
**Role**: Bus Driver
**Location**: Pelican Town (Trailer, west of the river)
**Family**: Penny (daughter)
**Birthday**: Spring 18
**Marriageable**: No

### Personality

Pam is a blunt, no-nonsense woman who enjoys the simpler pleasures in life, particularly a cold beer at the end of a long day. She can come across as crude or lazy, but beneath her tough exterior is a deeply caring mother who would do anything for her daughter Penny. Pam struggles with alcohol and financial difficulties, living in a rundown trailer, but she maintains an optimistic attitude and a strong sense of independence. Her humor is often self-deprecating, and she speaks her mind without filter, which can be both refreshing and off-putting to other villagers.

## Daily Schedule

Pam has a relatively predictable schedule that revolves around her job as a bus driver and her evenings at the Saloon. Note that the bus does not run until you complete the Vault room in the Community Center or purchase the Bus Stop from Joja Mart.

| Time | Location | Activity |
|------|----------|----------|
| 6:30 AM | Trailer | Waking up |
| 8:00 AM | Bus Stop | Waiting at the bus stop |
| 10:00 AM | Bus Stop | Driving the bus (if repaired) |
| 10:10 AM | Desert (Oasis) | Waiting at the Oasis |
| 4:00 PM | Bus Stop | Returning from the desert |
| 4:30 PM | Trailer | Brief stop at home |
| 5:00 PM | Stardrop Saloon | Drinking at the bar |
| 11:00 PM | Trailer | Going home to sleep |

### Pre-Bus Repair Schedule (Year 1)

Before the bus is repaired, Pam spends most of her day at the trailer or wandering near her home:

| Time | Location | Activity |
|------|----------|----------|
| 8:00 AM | Trailer | Waking up and lounging |
| 10:00 AM | Near Trailer / Town | Wandering around |

### Special Schedule Notes

- **Pam almost always goes to the Saloon at 5:00 PM**, making this the most reliable time to find her.
- **Festival Days**: Pam usually attends festivals but may arrive late or leave early to visit the Saloon.
- **Winter**: Her schedule does not change significantly in winter, though she spends more time indoors.

## Gift Preferences

Pam has straightforward gift preferences that reflect her personality. She loves simple pleasures and practical items.

### Loved Gifts (80 points)

These gifts give the maximum friendship boost:

- **Beer** - Pam's absolute favorite. Easy to obtain from the Saloon for 400g or craft at the Keg using Wheat and Hops.
- **Cactus Fruit** - A desert plant that Pam enjoys. Can be foraged in the Desert once the bus is repaired.
- **Glazed Yams** - A sweet dish that Pam enjoys. Made from Yam and Sugar.\n- **Mead** - A honey-based alcoholic drink. Craft at the Keg using Honey.\n- **Pale Ale** - A hoppy beer. Craft at the Keg using Hops.\n- **Piña Colada** - A tropical cocktail. Made with Coconut and Fruit.
- **Parsnip** - A simple root vegetable that Pam has a soft spot for. Available from Spring crops.

### Liked Gifts (45 points)

### Birthday Gift (Spring 18)

Pam's birthday is Spring 18. Giving a loved gift on this day provides 640 friendship points. Beer is the easiest and most thematic loved gift to give her. Stock up on Beer from the Saloon or brew your own using Kegs, and save several for her birthday and weekly gifts.

## Heart Events

Pam's heart events reveal her struggles with poverty, her love for her daughter, and her complicated relationship with alcohol.

### Two Hearts - The Trailer

This event triggers when you enter Pam's trailer. You will see Pam sitting on the couch looking tired. She talks about how the trailer is falling apart and mentions that she wishes she could give Penny a better place to live. Despite her complaints, she shrugs it off with her usual humor. This event establishes Pam's living situation and her underlying guilt about Penny's upbringing.

### Four Hearts - Penny's Teaching

Visit the Community Center area or the library between 3:00 PM and 5:00 PM. You will overhear Pam talking about Penny's teaching efforts. Pam expresses pride in her daughter but also admits she does not understand why Penny spends so much time with the children when she could be doing something more profitable. This event shows the generational divide between Pam and Penny.

### Six Hearts - The Bus Stop

Go to the Bus Stop between 10:00 AM and 4:00 PM. Pam is standing near the broken bus, looking at it wistfully. She talks about how much she loved being a bus driver and how losing the job was devastating. She mentions that she hopes someone will fix the bus someday so she can feel useful again. This event directly ties into the bus repair quest and adds emotional weight to the Community Center or Joja Mart storyline.

### Eight Hearts - The Saloon Confession

Visit the Stardrop Saloon between 5:00 PM and 10:00 PM. Pam has had a few too many drinks and opens up about her life. She talks about how she used to have a good job and a nice house before things fell apart. She mentions that Penny is the reason she keeps going and that she wishes she could be a better role model. This is one of the more emotional events in the game and shows Pam's vulnerability beneath her tough exterior.

### Ten Hearts - Mother and Daughter

Visit Pam's trailer when both Pam and Penny are home. You will witness a rare tender moment between the two. Penny is helping Pam clean the trailer, and they share a genuine conversation about their hopes for the future. Pam thanks Penny for everything she does, and Penny tells Pam that she is proud of her. This event is a beautiful resolution to their storyline and shows that despite their differences, they love each other deeply.

## Bus Repair Quest

One of Pam's most important roles in the game is tied to the bus repair. Repairing the bus unlocks access to the Desert, which is essential for many late-game activities.

### Community Center Route

Complete the Vault bundle by gathering the following items:
- 5,000g
- 2,500g
- 5,000g
- 10,000g

Once all Vault bundles are complete, the Junimos will repair the bus, and Pam will resume her job as a bus driver.

### Joja Mart Route

If you chose the Joja Mart route, you can purchase the Bus Stop repair from Morris for 40,000g. Once purchased, the bus will be repaired, and Pam will start driving again.

### Why Repair the Bus?

- **Desert Access**: The Desert contains the Oasis shop (run by Sandy), the Skull Cavern entrance, and various foraging items.
- **Skull Cavern**: Essential for reaching the bottom of the mines and obtaining Iridium Ore.
- **Sandy's Shop**: Sells unique items including Rare Seeds, Starfruit, and other desert-specific goods.
- **Pam's Happiness**: Repairing the bus gives Pam her job back, which improves her mood and dialogue.

## Friendship Tips and Strategies

### Maximizing Friendship with Pam

1. **Beer is king**: Pam loves Beer, and it is easy to obtain. Buy it from Gus at the Saloon or brew it yourself using Kegs. Give her one Beer per week for consistent friendship gains.
2. **Birthday priority**: Spring 18 is Pam's birthday. Save your best gifts for this day. A loved gift gives 640 points.
3. **Daily conversations**: Talk to Pam every day for small but consistent friendship boosts.
4. **Saloon visits**: Since Pam is at the Saloon every evening from 5:00 PM, this is the most convenient time to give her gifts and chat.
5. **Cactus Fruit**: Once you unlock the Desert, forage Cactus Fruit for free loved gifts.

### Gift Acquisition Tips

- **Beer**: Purchase from the Saloon for 400g each, or craft at the Keg using Wheat (which grows in any season) and Hops (Summer only). Brewing your own Beer is much more cost-effective in the long run.
- **Parsnip**: Plant Parsnips in Spring. They are one of the first crops available and cost only 20g each from Pierre's.
- **Cactus Fruit**: Forage in the Desert after repairing the bus. Cactus Fruit regrows every few days.

### Cost-Effective Strategy

The most cost-effective approach to befriending Pam is to give her Beer every week. At 400g per Beer from the Saloon, this costs 1,600g per month for maximum weekly friendship gains. If you brew your own Beer, the cost drops significantly. Combine this with daily conversations and a loved gift on her birthday, and you will reach maximum friendship quickly.

## Conclusion

Pam is a complex and deeply human character in Stardew Valley. Behind her rough exterior and love of beer lies a woman who cares deeply about her daughter and takes pride in her work. Her story arc, from losing her job as a bus driver to getting it back through the player's efforts, is one of the most satisfying in the game. By understanding her schedule, giving her loved gifts (especially Beer), and experiencing her heart events, you can build a meaningful friendship with this unforgettable character. Pam reminds us that everyone has a story worth hearing, and sometimes the people who seem the toughest are the ones who need friendship the most.
