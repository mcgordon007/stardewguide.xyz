# Cinder Shard Guide

Cinder Shards are a valuable resource introduced in Stardew Valley 1.5. Found primarily in the Volcano Dungeon on Ginger Island, they are essential for forging, enchanting, and crafting endgame items.

## What Are Cinder Shards?

Cinder Shards are warm, glowing stones obtained from the Volcano Dungeon. They are a key resource for the Forge system and several important crafting recipes.

## How to Get Cinder Shards

### Volcano Dungeon Sources

| Source | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Cinder Shard Nodes | Mineable nodes throughout the Volcano Dungeon |
| Common/Rare Chests | Found on dungeon floors |
| Magma Sprites | Enemies that drop Cinder Shards |
| Magma Sparkers | Enemies that drop Cinder Shards |
| Magma Duggies | Enemies that drop Cinder Shards |
| False Magma Caps | Mushroom enemies that drop Cinder Shards |

### Other Sources

| Source | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Stingray Fish Pond | Population 7+ produces Cinder Shards |
| Skull Cavern | Treasure rooms (after Volcano Shortcut unlocked) |

### Sell Price

- **Base Price:** 50g each

## Uses for Cinder Shards

### Forging (Primary Use)

Cinder Shards are used at the Forge on Floor 10 of the Volcano Dungeon:

| Action | Cinder Shards Required |
|--------|------------------------|
| Combine two rings | 20 |
| Enchant a tool/weapon | 20 |
| Add Galaxy Soul to Galaxy weapon | 20 |
| Forge a tool (1st time) | 10 |
| Forge a tool (2nd time) | 15 |
| Forge a tool (3rd time) | 20 |

### Tailoring

- Used in the Sewing Machine to create **Sunglasses**
- Orange dye for clothing

### Gifting

- **Dislike:** Almost all villagers
- **Hate:** Sam

**Note:** Cinder Shards are generally not good gifts. Most villagers dislike or hate them.

## Volcano Dungeon Strategy

### Floor Layout

The Volcano Dungeon has 10 floors:
- Floors 1-9: Randomly generated dungeon floors with enemies and obstacles
- Floor 10: The Forge and Dwarf Shop

### Tips for Farming Cinder Shards

1. **Bring Bombs:** Useful for clearing rocks and finding nodes quickly
2. **Combat Gear:** Magma enemies can be tough - bring good weapons and food
3. **Watering Can:** Some floors have lava rivers that need to be cooled with water
4. **Check All Chests:** Common and Rare chests can contain Cinder Shards
5. **Stingray Pond:** Set up a Stingray Fish Pond for passive Cinder Shard generation

### Volcano Shortcut

After reaching Floor 10 for the first time, you unlock a shortcut that allows quick access to the Forge. This also enables Cinder Shards to appear in Skull Cavern treasure rooms.

## Tips for Efficient Collection

1. **Daily Runs:** Visit the Volcano Dungeon daily for consistent Cinder Shard income
2. **Fish Pond:** A Stingray pond with 7+ population provides passive shards
3. **Save for Forging:** You'll need many shards for ring combinations and enchantments
4. **Prioritize:** Decide whether to spend on forging, trading, or crafting
