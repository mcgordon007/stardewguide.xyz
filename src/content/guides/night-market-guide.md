# Complete Guide to the Night Market in Stardew Valley

The Night Market is one of the most anticipated events in Stardew Valley's calendar. Held annually on Winter 15-17, this three-day festival transforms the beach into a bustling nighttime marketplace with unique shops, entertainment, and exclusive items. This guide covers everything you need to know about making the most of your Night Market visits.

## Overview

### When and Where

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| Dates | Winter 15, 16, and 17 |
| Time | 5:00 PM to 2:00 AM |
| Location | The Beach (south of Pelican Town) |
| Entry | Free, no ticket or item required |
| Weather | Always clear, regardless of forecast |

### How to Access

The Night Market is accessible from the beach area. Simply go to the beach after 5:00 PM on any of the three nights. The market occupies the pier area and extends onto the beach itself.

### Important Notes

- **Time Limit**: The market closes at 2:00 AM. If you are still at the market at 2:00 AM, you will pass out from exhaustion and wake up in your bed the next morning, losing some gold.
- **No Farming Time**: Since the market opens at 5:00 PM, you have the entire day for farming and other activities before heading to the beach.
- **All Three Nights**: The market is open all three nights with the same shops and attractions. You do not need to visit all three nights, but each night offers the same experience.

During the submarine ride, you explore an underwater area where you can find:

## The Mermaid Show

### Overview

The Mermaid Show is a musical performance that takes place on a special stage at the Night Market. It is one of the most memorable events in the game.

### When and Where

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| Time | Approximately 6:30 PM |
| Location | The mermaid stage on the left side of the beach |
| Duration | The show lasts for several minutes |
| Cost | Free |

### The Mermaid Song

During the show, the mermaid sings a sequence of musical notes. If you pay close attention, you can learn the song. The notes are displayed as colored orbs that float above the mermaid. The song sequence is:

1. **Note 1**: The first note plays.
2. **Note 2**: The second note follows.
3. **Note 3**: The third note plays.
4. **Note 4**: The fourth note plays.
5. **Note 5**: The fifth note completes the song.

### The Mermaid's Pearl

If you correctly replicate the mermaid's song on the shells near the stage after the show, you will receive the **Mermaid's Pendant** (if you are not already married) or a special reward. To play the song:

1. Wait for the mermaid show to end.
2. Approach the five shells arranged in a row near the stage.
3. Click each shell in the correct order to replicate the song.
4. If correct, a pearl or pendant will appear.

### Mermaid Song Notes

The exact note sequence changes each year, so pay attention during the show. The five shells correspond to five different musical notes, and you must play them in the exact order the mermaid sang them.

## NPCs at the Night Market

### Who Attends

Many Pelican Town residents visit the Night Market during the three days. This is an excellent opportunity for socializing:

- **Gift Giving**: You can give gifts to NPCs who are present at the market.
- **Friendship Building**: Talking to NPCs at the market counts as your daily interaction.
- **Birthday Opportunities**: If an NPC's birthday falls on Winter 15-17, you can give them their birthday gift at the Night Market.

### NPC Locations

Different NPCs can be found at various locations around the Night Market:

- **Near the Shops**: Some NPCs browse the vendor boats.
- **On the Beach**: Others walk along the shoreline.
- **At the Food Stalls**: Several NPCs gather near the food vendors.
- **Watching the Show**: NPCs gather to watch the mermaid show.

## Tips for Maximizing the Night Market

## The Mermaid Show: Complete Guide

### Mermaid Song Patterns

The mermaid's song follows a specific pattern that changes each year. Here is how to decode it:

1. **Watch the Show**: Pay close attention to the colored orbs that appear during the mermaid's performance.
2. **Count the Notes**: There are exactly 5 notes in the song.
3. **Note the Colors**: Each note corresponds to a specific colored shell.
4. **Remember the Order**: The order of the colors is the key to replicating the song.

### Shell Layout

The five shells near the mermaid stage are arranged in a row:

| Shell Position | Note |
|---------------|------|
| Shell 1 (leftmost) | First note |
| Shell 2 | Second note |
| Shell 3 | Third note |
| Shell 4 | Fourth note |
| Shell 5 (rightmost) | Fifth note |

### Rewards for Correct Song

If you correctly replicate the mermaid's song:

- **Not Married**: You receive a Mermaid's Pendant (if you have a 10-heart relationship with a marriage candidate).
- **Already Married**: You receive a special reward or message.
- **Friendship**: No direct friendship bonus, but the event is memorable.

The Night Market is not directly tracked in the Perfection system, but it contributes indirectly:

- **Furniture Collection**: Night Market furniture items count toward your furniture collection.

**Can I fish at the Night Market?**
No, regular fishing is not available during the Night Market. The beach area is occupied by market stalls and NPCs.

**Does the Night Market happen every year?**
Yes, the Night Market occurs every year on Winter 15-17 without exception.

**Can I bring a spouse to the Night Market?**
Your spouse may attend the Night Market on their own, but you cannot formally invite them as a companion like you can with the Movie Theater.

**What happens if it rains on Winter 15-17?**
The Night Market is always held regardless of weather. Even if the forecast predicts rain or snow, the Night Market will proceed as normal.

**Are Night Market items available anywhere else?**
Most Night Market items are exclusive to the event. However, some items like Starfruit and Rabbit's Foot can be obtained through other means (farming and rabbit farming, respectively).
