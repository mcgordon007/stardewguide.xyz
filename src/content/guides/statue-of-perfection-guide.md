# Stardew Valley Statue of Perfection Guide

## Overview

The **Statue of Perfection** is a unique furniture item that produces **Iridium Ore** daily. It is obtained through **Grandpa's Evaluation**, a progress check that occurs at the start of Year 3.

## How to Obtain

The Statue of Perfection is awarded based on your performance in **Grandpa's Evaluation**.

### Grandpa's Evaluation

At the beginning of **Year 3 (Spring 1)**, the ghost of your grandfather evaluates your farm progress. You are scored on various criteria, and if you achieve **4 or more lit candles** out of 4, you will receive the Statue of Perfection.

### Evaluation Criteria (Points System)

| Criteria | Points |
|----------|--------|
| Total Earnings (1,000,000g+) | 7 points |
| Skill Levels (all at 10) | 2 points |
| Friendship (married with upgraded house) | 1 point |
| Friendship (5 hearts with 10 villagers) | 1 point |
| Friendship (5 hearts with 8 villagers) | 1 point |
| Friendship (4 hearts with 4 villagers) | 1 point |
| Community Center Completion | 1 point |
| Community Center Completion (with ceremony attended) | 2 points |
| Skull Key obtained | 1 point |
| Rusty Key obtained | 1 point |

### Candle Thresholds

- **0-3 points**: 1 candle
- **4-7 points**: 2 candles
- **8-11 points**: 3 candles
- **12+ points**: 4 candles

You need **4 candles (12+ points)** to receive the Statue of Perfection.

### Re-evaluation

If you do not receive 4 candles on your first evaluation, you can be re-evaluated:
- Place a **Diamond** on Grandpa's Shrine (the shrine on the northwest corner of your farm).
- Grandpa will re-evaluate your progress the next day.
- There is no limit to re-evaluations.

## Daily Production

The Statue of Perfection produces **2-8 Iridium Ore** every day.

| Item | Quantity | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| Iridium Ore | 2-8 per day | Random amount within this range |

## Uses for Iridium Ore

Iridium Ore is one of the most valuable resources in the game:
- **Smelting**: 5 Iridium Ore + 1 Coal = 1 Iridium Bar in a Furnace.
- **Crafting**: Used in high-end crafting recipes (e.g., Iridium Sprinkler, Crystalarium).
- **Upgrading Tools**: The final tool upgrade (Iridium level) requires Iridium Bars.
- **Building**: Used in some late-game construction projects.
- **Gifting**: Iridium Bars are universally liked gifts.

## Placement

- Place the Statue of Perfection anywhere on your farm or in buildings.
- It produces Iridium Ore every day automatically.
- Consider placing it near a Furnace for convenient smelting.

## Tips

- Focus on earning money and building friendships early to ensure 4 candles.
- The Statue of Perfection is the most reliable source of Iridium Ore in the game.
- Combine with the Statue of Endless Fortune for a powerful daily resource generation setup.
- If you miss 4 candles, prioritize earning more money and completing the Community Center before re-evaluation.
