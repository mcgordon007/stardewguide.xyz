# Stardew Valley Golden Animal Crackers Guide - Double Animal Produce

**Golden Animal Crackers** are a special item introduced in Stardew Valley 1.6 that can double the production of farm animals and fish ponds. They are a powerful endgame farming tool that requires Farming Mastery to unlock.

## How to Unlock Golden Animal Crackers

### Farming Mastery Requirement

Golden Animal Crackers become available after unlocking **Farming Mastery**:

1. Reach Level 10 in all five core skills (Farming, Mining, Foraging, Fishing, Combat)
2. Complete the Farming Mastery Quest (ship a large quantity of crops)
3. Claim the Farming Mastery perk from the Mastery Cave

Once Farming Mastery is unlocked, Golden Animal Crackers can be found through various means.

## How to Obtain Golden Animal Crackers

Golden Animal Crackers can be obtained from the following sources:

| Source | Drop Rate |
|--------|-----------|
| Fishing Chests | 7% |
| Golden Fishing Chests | 9% |
| Skull Cavern Treasure Rooms | 2% |
| Golden Mystery Boxes | 0.5% |
| Artifact Spots | ~0.9% |
| Shaking Trees | ~0.2% |
| Chopping Trees | ~0.033% |
| Breaking Crates (mines/dungeons) | ~0.15% |
| Killing Monsters | ~0.15% |

### Best Farming Methods

The most reliable ways to obtain Golden Animal Crackers:

1. **Fishing with Treasure Hunter tackle** - Maximizes chest spawn rate
2. **Deep Skull Cavern runs** - Treasure rooms have a 2% chance
3. **Daily tree shaking** - Low chance but easy to do
4. **Artifact Spot digging** - Use the Tracker profession to spot them easily

## What Do Golden Animal Crackers Do?

### Double Animal Production

When used on a farm animal, Golden Animal Crackers **double the amount of products** that animal produces:

- **Cows:** Produce 2 Milk instead of 1
- **Chickens:** Produce 2 Eggs instead of 1
- **Ducks:** Produce 2 Duck Eggs instead of 1
- **Goats:** Produce 2 Goat Milk instead of 1
- **Sheep:** Produce 2 Wool instead of 1
- **Rabbits:** Produce 2 Rabbit''s Foot instead of 1
- **Dinosaurs:** Produce 2 Dinosaur Eggs instead of 1

### Important Exception: Pigs

**Pigs do NOT benefit from Golden Animal Crackers.** Pigs find Truffles by digging outside, which is not considered a "produced item" in the same way. Using a Golden Animal Cracker on a pig is a waste.

### Fish Pond Bonus

Golden Animal Crackers can also be used on **Fish Ponds** to double the roe/production output of the pond.

## How to Use Golden Animal Crackers

1. Select the Golden Animal Cracker from your inventory
2. Right-click on the target animal or Fish Pond
3. The cracker is consumed and the animal/pond now has a golden sparkle effect
4. Production is permanently doubled for that animal/pond

## Sell Price

| Item | Price |
|------|-------|
| Golden Animal Cracker | 1,000g |

While they can be sold, it is almost always better to use them on your animals for long-term profit.

## Important Warning

### Permanent Loss

**If you sell an animal or empty a Fish Pond that has been fed a Golden Animal Cracker, the cracker is permanently lost.** You will not get it back.

### Best Practices

- Only use Golden Animal Crackers on animals you plan to keep long-term
- Do NOT use on animals you might sell
- Prioritize your highest-value animals (Pigs are excluded, so focus on Cows, Goats, and Chickens)
- For Fish Ponds, only use on ponds with valuable fish that you plan to maintain

Using Golden Animal Crackers on a full Deluxe Barn or Coop:

- **Deluxe Barn (12 animals):** Doubles production for all animals
- **Deluxe Coop (12 animals):** Doubles egg and other product output
- **Fish Pond:** Doubles roe output for valuable fish like Sturgeon or Lava Eel

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I remove a Golden Animal Cracker from an animal?**
No, once used, the effect is permanent until the animal is sold or the pond is emptied.

**Does the effect stack if I use multiple crackers?**
No, using multiple crackers on the same animal has no additional effect.

**Can I use Golden Animal Crackers on baby animals?**
Yes, but they will only start producing double items once they reach adulthood.

**Do Golden Animal Crackers affect truffle quality?**
No, pigs are not affected by Golden Animal Crackers at all.

**Can I get Golden Animal Crackers before Farming Mastery?**
No, they only begin appearing after you unlock Farming Mastery.

**Is it worth using on Fish Ponds?**
Yes, especially for high-value fish like Sturgeon (produces Roe for Caviar) or Lava Eel.

## Summary

Golden Animal Crackers are one of the most powerful farming tools in Stardew Valley 1.6. By doubling animal and fish pond production, they can dramatically increase your farm''s daily output. Focus on obtaining them through fishing and Skull Cavern runs, and use them wisely on your highest-value animals. Remember: never sell an animal or empty a pond that has been fed a Golden Animal Cracker, as the bonus will be lost forever.
