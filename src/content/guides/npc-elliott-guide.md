# Elliott Complete Guide

Elliott is a marriageable male NPC in Stardew Valley, living in a cabin on the south side of the beach (Elliott's Cabin). He is a writer, dreaming of one day writing a great novel. He is a sentimental romantic who likes strong drinks.

## Basic Information

- **Birthday**: Fall 5
- **Address**: Elliott's Cabin (south of beach, near Willy's Fish Shop)
- **Family**: None (lives alone)
- **Marriage**: Yes
- **Clinic Check Day**: Summer 9

## Schedule

### Spring
- **Regular Schedule**: 8:00 AM at home; 12:00 PM leaves cabin to stand at south beach; 1:30 PM leaves beach to return to cabin; 3:00 PM leaves cabin to stand at north beach bridge; 6:00 PM leaves bridge to return home.
- **Rainy Day**: Stays home all day.
- **Thursday/Friday (any player has 6 hearts with Elliott)**: 8:00 AM at home; 11:30 AM leaves home for Pierre's General Store; 5:30 PM leaves Pierre's to return home.
- **Friday and Sunday (no player has 6 hearts with Leah)**: 8:00 AM at home; 11:00 AM leaves cabin to stand at dock next to Willy's Fish Shop; 5:00 PM leaves dock for Stardrop Saloon; 11:40 PM leaves Saloon to return home.

### Summer
- **Regular Schedule**: 8:00 AM at home; 11:30 AM leaves home for forest south of Leah's Cottage; 6:00 PM leaves forest to return home; 7:40 PM arrives home, sleeps.
- **Summer 9 (Clinic Check)**: 10:30 AM leaves home for Clinic; 1:30 PM moves from waiting room to examination room; 4:00 PM leaves Clinic to return home.
- **Rainy Day**: Stays home all day.
- **Other Schedule**: Same as Spring.

### Fall
- **Regular Schedule**: 8:00 AM at home; 11:30 AM leaves home for Library (Museum); 5:30 PM leaves Library to return home; 6:40 PM arrives home standing by bonsai tree; 9:00 PM leaves bonsai tree to stand by desk.
- **Rainy Day**: Stays home all day.
- **Other Schedule**: Same as Spring.

### Winter
- **Regular Schedule**: 8:00 AM at home; 11:30 AM leaves home for Library (Museum); 5:30 PM leaves Library to return home.
- **Winter 12 and 13**: 8:00 AM leaves cabin to stand at dock next to Willy's Fish Shop; 5:00 PM leaves dock for Stardrop Saloon; 12:00 AM leaves Saloon to return home.
- **Winter 17 (Night Market)**: 8:00 AM at home; 11:30 AM leaves home for Library; 4:50 PM leaves Library to go to beach for Night Market; 1:00 AM leaves Night Market to return home.
- **Other Schedule**: Same as Spring.

### Post-Marriage Schedule
- **Monday**: 8:30 AM leaves farmhouse for beach; 5:00 PM leaves beach to return home.
- **Other times**: Usually stays in farmhouse writing.

## Heart Events

### 2 Heart Event
Enter Elliott's Cabin, you will see him writing at his desk. He will talk to you about his writing and his confusion about the future.

### 4 Heart Event
Enter Elliott's Cabin between 11:00 AM and 4:00 PM, you will see him writing. He will show you the novel he is working on and ask for your opinion.

### 6 Heart Event
Enter the beach between 4:00 PM and evening, you will see Elliott standing by the dock. He will open up to you, talking about his love for writing and his fear of failure.

### 8 Heart Event
Enter Elliott's Cabin between 10:00 AM and 4:00 PM, you will see him writing. He will show you his completed novel and thank you for your continuous support.

### 10 Heart Event
Go to the beach in the evening (8:00 PM to midnight), Elliott will invite you to go out on a boat. On the boat, he will confess to you and give you a special gift.

### 14 Heart Event
Post-marriage event. Elliott will invite you to participate in a special event, showing how much he values your relationship.

## Marriage

After marrying Elliott, he will move into your farmhouse. He will set up a writing workstation in the farmhouse, sometimes help fill the pet's water bowl. He will also occasionally give you gifts, such as Wine or Duck Feather.

## Relationships

- **Willy**: Elliott's friend, lives nearby at the Fish Shop.
- **Leah**: Elliott's friend, lives in Leah's Cottage in the forest. If the player doesn't invite them to the Flower Dance, Elliott will dance with Leah.

## Tips

- Among Elliott's favorite gifts, Duck Feather can be obtained from ducks (need to build a Coop and raise ducks).
- Lobster can be caught with Crab Pots.
- Pomegranate can be harvested from Pomegranate Trees (need to plant fruit trees).
- Squid Ink can be obtained from Squid Kid (monster in Mines) or Fish Ponds.
- Tom Kha Soup requires learning the cooking recipe (from Sandy at 7 hearts friendship).
- Crab Cakes requires learning the cooking recipe (from The Queen of Sauce).
- His birthday is Fall 5, gift effects are doubled on this day (8x friendship).
- In Spring, Summer, and Fall, players need to reach 2 hearts friendship to enter Elliott's Cabin. In Winter, any friendship level can enter between 10:00 AM and 6:00 PM.
