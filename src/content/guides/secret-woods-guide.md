# Complete Secret Woods Guide

![Secret Woods](/images/location-secret-woods.jpg)

The Secret Woods is a hidden area in Stardew Valley that offers valuable resources, unique forageables, and one of the elusive Stardrops. Accessible only after obtaining a Steel Axe, this mystical forest is an essential location for any serious farmer. This comprehensive guide covers everything you need to know about accessing and maximizing the Secret Woods.

## Accessing the Secret Woods

The Secret Woods is located in the northwest corner of Cindersap Forest, but it is blocked by a Large Stump that requires specific tools to remove.

### Requirements to Enter

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Tool Required** | Steel Axe (or better: Gold Axe, Iridium Axe) |
| **Location** | Northwest corner of Cindersap Forest |
| **Obstacle** | Large Stump blocking the entrance |
| **Alternative Access** | None - must chop the stump |

### How to Get a Steel Axe

**Cost:** 5 Iron Bars + 5,000g
**Location:** Blacksmith (Clint)
**Time:** 2 days to upgrade

**Steps:**
1. Mine Iron Ore from levels 40-79 of the Mines
2. Smelt Iron Ore into Iron Bars (5 ore + 1 coal = 1 bar)
3. Collect 5 Iron Bars
4. Save 5,000g
5. Visit Clint at the Blacksmith (9:00 AM - 4:00 PM, Monday-Thursday)
6. Select "Upgrade Tools" and choose your Axe
7. Wait 2 days and collect your upgraded axe

**Important:** Plan your upgrade timing carefully. You will not have access to your axe for 2 days, which can impact farm work.

### Alternative: Gold Axe

If you already have a Gold Axe, you can also chop the Large Stump. The Gold Axe requires:
- 5 Gold Bars + 10,000g
- Can also chop Large Logs (which Steel Axe cannot do)

## Secret Woods Layout

The Secret Woods is a relatively small but resource-rich area divided into several sections:

| Area | Features |
|------|----------|
| **Entrance Area** | Large Stump removal point, immediate access to hardwood stumps |
| **Central Clearing** | 6 Large Stumps, Slime spawn area |
| **Northern Path** | Leads to Old Master Cannoli statue |
| **Pond Area** | Small fishing pond, unique fish |
| **Hidden Nook** | Secret forageable spawns |

## Large Stumps and Hardwood Farming

The primary reason to visit the Secret Woods daily is the Hardwood. Six Large Stumps spawn here every day, providing a renewable source of this valuable material.

### Large Stump Mechanics

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Spawn Rate** | Daily respawn |
| **Quantity** | 6 stumps |
| **Hardwood per Stump** | 2 Hardwood |
| **Daily Total** | 12 Hardwood |
| **Energy to Chop** | 12 energy (with Steel Axe) |
| **Hits Required** | 6 hits (Steel Axe), 3 hits (Gold Axe), 2 hits (Iridium Axe) |

### Hardwood Uses

Hardwood is required for many important crafting recipes and buildings:

| Recipe/Building | Hardwood Required | Notes |
|-----------------|-------------------|-------|
| **Hardwood Fence** | 1 per fence | Most durable fence |
| **Cheese Press** | 10 | For processing milk |
| **Oil Maker** | 20 | For truffle oil and cooking oil |
| **Keg** | 30 | For wine, beer, juice, pale ale |
| **Loom** | 60 | For processing wool |
| **Obelisk (Earth)** | 10 | Warp to mountains |
| **Obelisk (Water)** | 10 | Warp to beach |
| **Obelisk (Island)** | 10 | Warp to Ginger Island |
| **Gold Clock** | 0 | Does not require hardwood |
| **Horse Stable** | 100 | From Robin's shop |
| **House Upgrade 2** | 150 | Nursery addition |

### Daily Hardwood Farming Routine

**Recommended Daily Visit:**
1. Enter Secret Woods through Cindersap Forest
2. Chop all 6 Large Stumps (12 Hardwood)
3. Kill any slimes for combat XP and drops
4. Collect any forageables
5. Check the Old Master Cannoli statue if you have a Sweet Gem Berry
6. Exit through the same entrance

**Time Required:** 5-10 minutes per day
**Monthly Yield:** ~360 Hardwood (if visited daily)

The Secret Woods is one of the few locations outside the Mines where slimes spawn naturally.

### Slime Mechanics

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Spawn Rate** | Daily |
| **Quantity** | 3-6 slimes |
| **Types** | Green Slimes, occasionally Blue Slimes |
| **Respawn** | Daily |

### Slime Drops

| Item | Drop Rate | Use |
|------|-----------|-----|
| **Slime** | 100% | Crafting (Oil Maker, Cork Bobber, etc.) |
| **Sap** | 50% | Crafting, tree fertilizer |
| **Spring Onion** | Rare | Early game food |
| **Solomon's Seal** | Very Rare | Not in base game |

### Combat Tips

- Slimes are relatively weak enemies
- Use your sword for quick kills
- Slimes can drop valuable Slime resource
- Kill them for Combat XP
- Be careful not to get surrounded by multiple slimes

## Old Master Cannoli and the Stardrop

The most valuable secret in the Secret Woods is the Old Master Cannoli statue, which rewards you with a Stardrop.

### Location

**Where:** Northern area of the Secret Woods
**Appearance:** A statue of a man with a top hat
**Access:** Walk north from the entrance, past the Large Stumps

### Obtaining the Stardrop

**Requirement:** Give the statue a Sweet Gem Berry
**Reward:** 1 Stardrop (permanently increases maximum energy by 34 points)

### Sweet Gem Berry

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Seed Source** | Rare Seed from Sandy's Oasis Shop (Calico Desert) |
| **Seed Cost** | 1,000g |
| **Growth Time** | 24 days |
| **Season** | Fall only |
| **Sell Price** | 3,000g - 6,000g (depending on quality) |
| **Base Value** | 3,000g |

**Important Notes:**
- Sweet Gem Berry cannot be processed in Kegs or Preserves Jars
- It is not considered a fruit or vegetable for gifting purposes
- The only use besides selling is giving to Old Master Cannoli
- One Sweet Gem Berry = One Stardrop

### Sweet Gem Berry Strategy

1. **Buy Rare Seeds:** Purchase from Sandy whenever available
2. **Plant in Fall:** 24-day growth time requires early planting
3. **Use Speed-Gro:** Deluxe Speed-Gro reduces growth to 18 days
4. **Save for Stardrop:** Do not sell your first Sweet Gem Berry
5. **Subsequent Berries:** Sell additional berries for profit (3,000g+ each)

- Fishing here is peaceful and usually free of distractions

## The Traveling Cart

### Location

**Where:** South of the Secret Woods entrance, in Cindersap Forest

**Hours:** 6:00 AM - 8:00 PM

### What It Sells

## Secret Woods Strategy and Tips

### Daily Routine Integration

**Optimal Route:**
1. Exit farm through southern entrance to Cindersap Forest
2. Collect any forageables in Cindersap Forest
3. Enter Secret Woods (Steel Axe required)
4. Chop all 6 Large Stumps (12 Hardwood)
5. Kill all slimes for drops and XP
6. Collect Secret Woods forageables
7. Check Traveling Cart (if Friday/Sunday)
8. Return to farm or continue to town

**Time Investment:** 10-15 minutes
**Daily Rewards:** 12 Hardwood, Slime drops, forageables, Combat XP

### Hardwood Storage Strategy

| Storage Method | Recommendation |
|----------------|----------------|
| **Chest** | Keep a chest near the Secret Woods entrance |
| **Inventory** | Bring empty slots for Hardwood collection |
| **Farm** | Store in organized chests by use |
| **Shipping** | Do not sell Hardwood - too valuable for crafting |

### Early Game Priority

1. **Unlock Steel Axe:** Priority upgrade after Watering Can
2. **Daily Visits:** Make Secret Woods part of your daily routine
3. **Save Hardwood:** Do not use frivolously - plan for buildings
4. **Buy Rare Seeds:** When Traveling Cart sells them
5. **Prepare for Fall:** Plant Sweet Gem Berry as soon as possible

### Late Game Uses

Even in late game, the Secret Woods remains valuable:
- **Daily Hardwood:** For crafting and building
- **Slime Farming:** For Slime resource
- **Forageables:** For cooking and gifting
- **Woodskip Fishing:** For completing fish collection

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Selling First Sweet Gem Berry:** Always give the first one to Old Master Cannoli
2. **Not Visiting Daily:** Missing days means missing 12 Hardwood
3. **Using Hardwood Early:** Save for important buildings (Kegs, House Upgrade)
4. **Forgetting the Traveling Cart:** Check Fridays and Sundays for Rare Seeds
5. **Not Upgrading Axe:** Steel Axe should be an early priority
6. **Ignoring Slimes:** They provide valuable Slime resource
7. **Not Planning Fall Planting:** Sweet Gem Berry needs 24 days to grow

The Secret Woods is an essential location for any Stardew Valley player. The daily renewable Hardwood alone makes it worth visiting every day, while the Stardrop from Old Master Cannoli provides a permanent energy boost that benefits all aspects of gameplay. Make the Secret Woods part of your daily routine, and you will have a steady supply of one of the game's most valuable resources.
