# Stardew Valley Marnie Guide: Complete NPC Overview

Marnie is the owner of Marnie's Ranch, the local animal supply shop in Stardew Valley. She provides animals, feed, and animal care supplies essential for ranching. This comprehensive guide covers everything about Marnie, from her schedule to her shop inventory to her gift preferences.

## Basic Information

### Character Overview

**Name**: Marnie
**Role**: Ranch Owner, Animal Supplier
**Location**: Cindersap Forest (Marnie's Ranch)
**Family**: Lewis (relationship), Shane (nephew), Jas (niece)
**Birthday**: Fall 18
**Marriageable**: No

### Personality

Marnie is a warm, friendly woman who loves animals and ranching. She's caring and maternal, especially toward her niece Jas. Marnie has a secret romantic relationship with Mayor Lewis, though they keep it private. She's knowledgeable about animal care and always willing to help with ranching questions. Marnie is a beloved member of the community.

## Animal Care Tips from Marnie

### Basic Animal Care

1. **Feed daily**: Animals need hay or grass
2. **Let outside**: Animals enjoy grazing
3. **Pet daily**: Increases friendship
4. **Keep warm**: Heater for winter

### Friendship Levels

Animal friendship affects:
- Product quality
- Production frequency
- Special products (duck feathers, rabbit's foot)

### Winter Care

- Keep animals inside
- Use heater in buildings
- Feed hay daily
- Pet animals daily

## Heart Events

### 3 Hearts

**Trigger**: Enter Marnie's Ranch during business hours

**Event**: Marnie discusses her ranch and animal care tips. She shares her love for animals and her family.

### 6 Hearts

**Trigger**: Enter Marnie's Ranch or see her with Lewis

**Event**: Marnie's relationship with Lewis becomes apparent. She may express frustration about keeping it secret.

### Lewis's Shorts Quest

**Trigger**: Find Lewis's purple shorts in Marnie's bedroom

**Event**: Discovering the shorts reveals their relationship. Return shorts to Lewis for a reward.

**Reward**: 750g and special dialogue

## Family Relationships

### Lewis (Partner)

Marnie has a secret romantic relationship with Mayor Lewis. They keep it private, but evidence (Lewis's shorts in her room) reveals it.

### Shane (Nephew)

Shane lives at Marnie's ranch. He struggles with depression and alcoholism. Marnie cares for him despite his difficulties.

### Jas (Niece)

Jas is Marnie's young niece who lives at the ranch. Marnie is maternal and caring toward Jas, providing a stable home.

## Conclusion

Marnie is an essential NPC for animal ranching in Stardew Valley. Her ranch shop provides animals, feed, and supplies for your livestock operation. Visit her shop for animal purchases and care supplies.

Gift Marnie diamonds, pumpkin pie, or animal products to build friendship. Her secret relationship with Lewis adds depth to her character, and her care for Shane and Jas shows her maternal nature. Marnie's Ranch is your gateway to successful animal farming in Stardew Valley.
