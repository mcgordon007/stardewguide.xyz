# Stardew Valley Boots and Shoes Guide: Complete Overview

Boots and shoes are equipment items in Stardew Valley that provide defensive stats and immunity bonuses. Unlike hats, boots actually affect gameplay by reducing damage and preventing negative effects. This comprehensive guide covers all boots available and where to find them.

## Overview of Boots

### What are Boots

Boots are equipment items that:
- Provide defense stat (reduce damage)
- Provide immunity stat (prevent debuffs)
- Are worn in the boots slot
- Affect gameplay performance

### Boot Stats

**Defense**:
- Reduces damage taken from monsters
- Higher defense = less damage
- Essential for mine exploration
- Ranges from 0 to 8

**Immunity**:
- Prevents debuff effects
- Higher immunity = less debuff chance
- Protects against monster abilities
- Ranges from 0 to 8

### Boot Slot

The boot slot is:
- Separate from hat slot
- Separate from ring slots
- Can only wear one pair
- Replaces previous boots when changed

## Boot Stats Explained

### Defense Stat

**How Defense Works**:
- Reduces damage from monster attacks
- Formula: Damage - Defense = Actual Damage
- Example: 20 damage attack with Defense 5 = 15 damage

**Defense Benefits**:
- Survive longer in mines
- Less health loss per hit
- Better survivability
- Essential for combat

### Immunity Stat

**How Immunity Works**:
- Reduces chance of debuffs
- Debuffs include: slow, burn, poison
- Higher immunity = less debuff chance
- Maximum immunity prevents most debuffs

**Immunity Benefits**:
- Avoid movement speed debuffs
- Prevent burn damage
- Avoid poison effects
- Better combat performance
