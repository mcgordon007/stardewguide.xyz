# Stardew Valley Oil Maker Guide

## Overview

The **Oil Maker** is an artisan equipment that produces **Oil** from various crops and **Truffle Oil** from truffles found by pigs. Truffle Oil is one of the most valuable artisan goods in the game, making the Oil Maker essential for late-game profit.

## Unlock Requirements

- **Farming Level 8** required to unlock the crafting recipe

## Crafting Materials

To craft an Oil Maker, you will need:

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Slime | 100 |
| Hardwood | 50 |
| Gold Bar | 1 |

## How to Use

1. Place the Oil Maker on your farm or inside a shed
2. Interact with it while holding a valid input item
3. Processing time varies by input material

## Output Products

### Truffle Oil (from Truffle)
- **Truffle** → **Truffle Oil** (1,065g)
- Processing time: **6 hours** (360 minutes)
- One of the highest-value artisan goods in the game

### Oil (from Crops)
- **Corn** → **Oil** (100g) - Processing time: 48 hours (2,880 minutes)
- **Sunflower** → **Oil** (100g) - Processing time: 48 hours
- **Sunflower Seeds** → **Oil** (100g) - Processing time: 48 hours

## Tips & Strategy

- **Truffle Oil** with the Artisan profession sells for **1,491g**, making it extremely profitable
- Pigs find truffles only during **Spring, Summer, and Fall** when allowed outside
- Truffles are also affected by the Gatherer and Botanist foraging professions
- Place Oil Makers near your barn for quick processing of truffles
- Oil from crops is generally not very profitable; focus on truffle oil production
