# Stardew Valley Robin Guide: Complete NPC Overview

Robin is the carpenter of Pelican Town and owner of the Carpenter's Shop. She provides all building construction and house upgrade services in Stardew Valley. This comprehensive guide covers everything about Robin, from her schedule to her services to her gift preferences.

## Basic Information

### Character Overview

**Name**: Robin
**Role**: Carpenter, Carpenter's Shop Owner
**Location**: Mountains (Carpenter's Shop)
**Family**: Demetrius (husband), Sebastian (son), Maru (daughter)
**Birthday**: Summer 21
**Marriageable**: No

### Personality

Robin is a hardworking, skilled carpenter who takes pride in her work. She's friendly and helpful, always willing to assist with building projects. She's a devoted mother and wife, though she sometimes struggles with her relationship with Sebastian (her son from a previous relationship). Robin is passionate about construction and enjoys seeing her buildings enhance the community.

## Carpenter's Shop

### Location

Robin's Carpenter's Shop is located in the Mountains, at the base of the path leading to the mines. It's accessible from Pelican Town via the mountain path.

### Shop Hours

| Day | Hours |
|-----|-------|
| Monday | Closed |
| Tuesday | 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM |
| Wednesday | 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM |
| Thursday | 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM |
| Friday | 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM |
| Saturday | 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM |
| Sunday | 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM |

**Note**: The shop is closed on Mondays. Plan building purchases around this schedule.

### Services Available

Robin provides several services:

1. **Farm Buildings**: Purchase and construct farm buildings
2. **House Upgrades**: Upgrade your farmhouse
3. **Building Demolition**: Remove existing buildings
4. **Building Relocation**: Move buildings to new locations
5. **Furniture Sales**: Purchase furniture items
6. **Furniture Catalog**: Buy catalog for unlimited furniture

## Building Services

### Demolition

Robin can demolish any farm building:
- Free service
- Building and materials lost
- Animals in building are lost
- Space becomes available

### Relocation

Robin can move existing buildings:
- Free service
- Building moves instantly
- Animals stay in building
- Perfect for reorganizing

## Heart Events

### 3 Hearts

**Trigger**: Visit Robin's home when she's present

**Event**: Robin discusses her carpentry work and her family. She may share stories about her projects or family dynamics.

### 6 Hearts

**Trigger**: Enter the Carpenter's Shop or Robin's home

**Event**: Robin reveals more about her relationship with Sebastian and her family history. She may discuss her previous marriage and current family situation.

### Notes on Heart Events

- Robin's events focus on her work and family
- They reveal family dynamics
- Sebastian relationship is explored
- No major gameplay rewards

## Family Relationships

### Demetrius (Husband)

Demetrius is Robin's current husband. He's a scientist who studies the local environment. They have a loving relationship.

### Sebastian (Son)

Sebastian is Robin's son from a previous relationship. He's somewhat distant from Robin and prefers spending time alone or with friends. Their relationship is sometimes strained.

### Maru (Daughter)

Maru is Robin and Demetrius's daughter. She's close to both parents and shares her father's interest in science. Robin is proud of Maru's abilities.

## Conclusion

Robin is an essential NPC for all building and construction needs in Stardew Valley. Her Carpenter's Shop provides farm buildings, house upgrades, and furniture. Visit her shop Tuesday through Sunday for construction services.
