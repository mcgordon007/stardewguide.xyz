# Mastery Cave Guide

## Overview

The **Mastery Cave** is a new feature introduced in **Stardew Valley 1.6**. It becomes accessible after the player reaches **level 10 in all five skills** (Farming, Mining, Foraging, Fishing, and Combat). Inside, players can choose from five distinct mastery paths, each providing powerful permanent bonuses.

## How to Unlock

1. Reach **level 10** in all five skills:
   - Farming
   - Mining
   - Foraging
   - Fishing
   - Combat
2. Once all skills are maxed, a notification will appear.
3. The Mastery Cave is located in the **Cindersap Forest** (southeast area).

## The Five Mastery Paths

After unlocking the cave, players earn **Mastery Points** by performing skill-related activities. These points can be spent on the following mastery paths:

### 1. Farming Mastery
- Increases crop quality chances.
- Unlocks the ability to use **Golden Animal Crackers**.
- Provides bonuses to animal product quality.

### 2. Mining Mastery
- Increases ore yield from nodes.
- Unlocks the ability to find **Gem Nodes** more frequently.
- Provides bonuses to geode contents.

### 3. Foraging Mastery
- Increases forageable spawn rates.
- Unlocks the ability to find **Moss** more easily.
- Provides bonuses to tree product quality.

### 4. Fishing Mastery
- Increases fish quality chances.
- Unlocks the ability to find **Treasure Chests** more frequently.
- Provides bonuses to crab pot yields.

### 5. Combat Mastery
- Increases damage dealt.
- Unlocks special combat abilities and bonuses.
- Provides bonuses to monster loot drops.

## Tips

- Focus on leveling all skills evenly to unlock the cave sooner.
- Choose mastery paths that complement your playstyle.
- Mastery Points continue to accumulate after maxing skills, so keep playing.
- The bonuses are permanent and significantly impact late-game efficiency.
