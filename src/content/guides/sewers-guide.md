# Stardew Valley Sewers Guide: Complete Overview

The Sewers are an underground location in Stardew Valley that house Krobus's shop and various shadow creatures. Accessible after donating 60 items to the Museum, the Sewers offer unique items, combat opportunities, and access to Krobus's services. This comprehensive guide covers everything about the Sewers.

## Sewers Overview

### What are the Sewers

The Sewers are an underground system beneath Pelican Town. They contain:
- Krobus's shop
- Shadow brute spawning areas
- Warp statue
- Various passages and rooms

### Purpose

The Sewers serve several purposes:
1. **Krobus's Shop**: Unique item purchases
2. **Combat Area**: Shadow brute fights
3. **Warp Point**: Fast travel location
4. **Story Location**: Shadow people lore

---

## Accessing the Sewers

### Unlock Requirement

**Rusty Key**: Donate 60 items to the Museum

**Process**:
1. Collect various items (minerals, artifacts, etc.)
2. Donate to Gunther at the Museum
3. Reach 60 total donations
4. Gunther rewards you with the Rusty Key
5. Use key to unlock sewer grate

### Sewer Entrance

The sewer entrance is located:
- Pelican Town
- Near the river/bridge area
- Visible grate on ground
- South side of town

### Using the Rusty Key

**Unlock Process**:
1. Approach sewer grate
2. Interact with grate
3. Key automatically used
4. Entrance permanently unlocked
5. Can enter anytime after
