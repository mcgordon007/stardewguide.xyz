# Stardew Valley Traveling Cart Guide: Complete Overview

The Traveling Cart is a mysterious merchant that appears periodically in Cindersap Forest, selling rare and unusual items at varying prices. It's one of the best sources for hard-to-find items and seasonal seeds outside their normal seasons. This comprehensive guide covers everything about the Traveling Cart.

## Cart Overview

### What is the Traveling Cart

The Traveling Cart is a merchant wagon operated by a mysterious pig. It appears in Cindersap Forest on certain days and sells a rotating inventory of rare items, seeds, and goods.

### Location

The Traveling Cart appears:
- Cindersap Forest
- South of your farm
- Near the path to the Wizard's Tower
- West side of the forest

### Appearance Schedule

| Day | Time | Location |
|-----|------|----------|
| Friday | 6:00 AM - 7:00 PM | Cindersap Forest |
| Saturday | 6:00 AM - 7:00 PM | Cindersap Forest |
| Sunday (sometimes) | 6:00 AM - 7:00 PM | Cindersap Forest |

**Note**: The cart appears every Friday and Saturday, and sometimes on Sunday. It leaves at 7:00 PM.

## Coffee Bean Details

### Coffee Bean at Cart

**Benefits**:

- Coffee sells for 15g each
- Make coffee for speed buff

- Daily value: ~30g (after established)
- Long-term profitable
