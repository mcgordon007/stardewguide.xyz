# Mango Tree Guide

Mango Trees are tropical fruit trees introduced in Stardew Valley 1.5. They produce large, sweet mangoes that are loved by Leo and can be used in cooking and crafting. This guide covers everything you need to know about growing and using Mango Trees.

## What Are Mango Trees?

Mango Trees are fruit trees that produce **Mangoes** daily during their harvest season. They are one of the tropical trees added with Ginger Island content.

## How to Get Mango Saplings

### Sources

| Source | Price/Requirement | Notes |
|--------|-------------------|-------|
| Island Trader | 75 Mussels | Available on Ginger Island |
| Golden Coconuts | Random drop | Crack open Golden Coconuts at the Blacksmith |

**Note:** Mango Saplings cannot be purchased from Pierre's shop or the Oasis.

## Growing Mango Trees

### Requirements

- **Growth Time:** 28 days to mature
- **Harvest Season:** Summer (in Stardew Valley) or All Seasons (on Ginger Island or in Greenhouse)
- **Spacing:** Requires clear 3x3 area around the sapling (no objects, flooring, or other trees)
- **Location:** Can be planted on the Farm, Ginger Island, or in the Greenhouse

### Growth Stages

Mango Trees go through 5 growth stages before producing fruit. Once mature, they produce one Mango every day during the harvest season.

### Gifting

- **Love:** Leo
- **Like:** Demetrius, Elliott, Harvey, Jodi, Kent, Leah, Linus, Pam, Robin, Sandy, Shane
- **Neutral:** Most other villagers
- **Dislike:** Abigail, Haley, Jas, Vincent

### Other Uses

- **Tailoring:** Used with Cloth to create Relaxed Fit Pants
- **Dyeing:** Yellow dye for clothing
- **Fish Pond:** Lionfish may request 1 Mango to increase pond capacity from 5 to 10
- **Trading:** Island Trader exchanges 1 'Volcano' Photo for 5 Mangoes on Thursdays

## Tips for Mango Farming

1. **Leo's Favorite:** Mangoes are Leo's loved gift - perfect for befriending him
2. **Ginger Island:** Plant on Ginger Island for year-round production
3. **Greenhouse:** Alternative for all-season harvesting
4. **Tiller Profession:** Increases sell price by 10%
5. **Artisan Profession:** Significantly boosts wine and jelly profits
