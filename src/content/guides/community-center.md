# Community Center Bundles Guide

## TL;DR

The Community Center is the main storyline quest in Stardew Valley, unlocked on Summer 5 of Year 1. Completing all 6 rooms (Crafts Room, Pantry, Fish Tank, Boiler Room, Bulletin Board, Vault) restores the building and unlocks rewards including the Greenhouse, Minecarts, and Bus Stop. The Vault requires 42,500g total gold donations. The Joja Mart alternative costs 275,000g total but provides no bonus rewards. The Community Center route is recommended for most players due to lower cost and valuable rewards like free seeds, recipes, and friendship boosts.

The Community Center is the main storyline quest in Stardew Valley. On Summer 5 of your first year, entering Pelican Town from the eastern path between 9:00 AM and 3:00 PM triggers a cutscene where you discover the abandoned Community Center and meet the Junimos. Completing all bundles restores the building and unlocks various rewards.

## How Do Bundles Work?

Each bundle requires specific items to be donated. Once all items in a bundle are complete, the Junimos grant a reward. Completing all bundles in a room unlocks a larger room reward. You can check bundle progress on the bulletin board inside the Community Center.

Items must be the **correct quality or type** specified. Most bundles accept any quality, but the Quality Crops Bundle specifically requires gold-quality crops. You can donate items in any order.

## What is the Joja Mart Alternative?

If you prefer a simpler (but more expensive) path, you can purchase a Joja Mart membership for 5,000g after the Community Center cutscene. This replaces the bundles with monetary costs:

| Project | Cost | Equivalent Bundle |
|---------|------|-----------------|
| Bus Stop | 40,000g | Vault 2,500g Bundle |
| Minecarts | 15,000g | Boiler Room completion |
| Bridge | 25,000g | Vault 25,000g Bundle |
| Greenhouse | 35,000g | Vault 10,000g Bundle |
| Pantry (Community Center) | 35,000g | Pantry completion |
| Boiler Room | 40,000g | Boiler Room bundles |
| Craft Room | 25,000g | Crafts Room bundles |
| Fish Tank | 40,000g | Fish Tank bundles |
| Bulletin Board | 20,000g | Bulletin Board bundles |
| **Total** | **275,000g** | All bundles |

### Should I Choose Community Center or Joja Mart?

| Factor | Community Center | Joja Mart |
|---------|-----------------|-----------|
| Total Cost | ~42,500g (vault) + items | 275,000g + 5,000g membership |
| Time Required | Longer (need to collect items) | Shorter (just gold) |
| Rewards | Seeds, recipes, friendship boost | None beyond project completion |
| Junimo Connection | Yes (Junimo help on farm later) | No |
| Aesthetics | Restored Community Center | Joja Mart warehouse |
| Recommendation | Better for most players | Only if you have excess gold |

The Community Center route is generally recommended because it provides valuable rewards (free seeds, recipes, friendship boosts) and costs significantly less gold.
