# Stardew Valley Luau Guide

## Overview

The **Luau** is a summer festival in *Stardew Valley* where the entire town gathers at the beach for a communal soup tasting event hosted by the Governor.

## Date and Time

- **Date:** Summer 11
- **Time:** 9:00 AM – 2:00 PM
- **Entry:** Enter The Beach between 9:00 AM and 2:00 PM to participate

## Location

**The Beach** — The sandy shores east of Pelican Town are decorated with tropical-themed decorations and a large soup pot.

## How to Participate

1. Travel to The Beach during festival hours.
2. Talk to villagers to enjoy the atmosphere.
3. Add an ingredient to the communal soup pot for the Governor to taste.

## Soup Ingredient Effects

The ingredient you add to the soup affects the Governor's reaction and your friendship with villagers:

### Best Response (+120 friendship with all villagers)
- **Silver or Gold quality:** Krobus's favorite (if modded), or high-quality loved items
- **Silver/Gold quality:** Ancient Fruit, Purple Mushroom, Starfruit, or other universally loved items of good quality

### Good Response (+60 friendship with all villagers)
- Adding a **liked item** of normal or better quality
- Examples: Most fruits, most vegetables, most cooked dishes

### Neutral Response (no friendship change)
- Adding a **neutral item**
- Examples: Wild Horseradish, Daffodil, Leek, Dandelion, most foraged items

### Bad Response (-50 friendship with all villagers)
- Adding a **disliked or hated item**
- Examples: Holly, Stone, Sap, Torch, most fish, most artisan goods (except wine/cheese)

### Worst Response (-100 friendship with all villagers)
- Adding a **universally hated item** or something toxic
- Examples: Rotten Plant, Sea Urchin (if hated), or deliberately bad items

## Governor's Reactions

| Reaction | Ingredient Type | Friendship Impact |
|----------|----------------|-------------------|
| Best | High-quality loved item | +120 with all villagers |
| Good | Liked item | +60 with all villagers |
| Neutral | Neutral item | No change |
| Bad | Disliked item | -50 with all villagers |
| Worst | Hated item | -100 with all villagers |

## Activities

- Socialize with villagers
- Add an ingredient to the soup pot
- Watch the Governor's reaction
- Enjoy the tropical beach atmosphere

## Rewards

- The main reward is the **friendship boost (or penalty)** with all villagers based on the soup quality.
- There is no special shop or unique items available at this festival.

## Tips

- **Bring your best:** Use a high-quality, universally loved item for the best results.
- **Avoid disasters:** Never add trash, hated items, or low-quality disliked items.
- **Safe choices:** Silver or Gold quality fruits like Starfruit, Ancient Fruit, or Melon are excellent choices.
- **Check the wiki:** If unsure, verify whether an item is loved, liked, neutral, or hated before adding it.

## Notes

- The festival ends automatically at 2:00 PM, and you return to the farm at 10:00 PM.
- Time is paused during the festival.
- You can only add one ingredient to the soup pot.
- The friendship change applies to **all villagers**, making this a high-impact event.
