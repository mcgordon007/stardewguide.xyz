# Stardew Valley Rings Guide

Rings are equippable accessories that provide various beneficial effects. Players can wear two rings at a time, and certain rings can be combined at the Volcano Forge on Ginger Island.

## Craftable Rings

### Sturdy Ring
- **Recipe:** 10 Copper Bars, 5 Refine Quartz, 25 Coal
- **Effect:** Reduces duration of negative status effects by 50%
- **Source:** Combat Level 1

### Warrior Ring
- **Recipe:** 10 Iron Bars, 25 Coal
- **Effect:** Occasionally infuses the wearer with Warrior Energy after killing a monster (+10 Attack for 5 seconds)
- **Source:** Combat Level 4

### Ring of Yoba
- **Recipe:** 5 Gold Bars, 5 Iridium Bars
- **Effect:** Occasionally shields the wearer from damage
- **Source:** Combat Level 7

### Thorns Ring
- **Recipe:** 50 Bone Fragments, 50 Stone
- **Effect:** Monsters take damage equal to the unmitigated damage they deal to you
- **Source:** Combat Level 7

### Glow Ring
- **Recipe:** Cannot be crafted (found in mines)
- **Effect:** Emits a small radius of light
- **Source:** Mines levels 40+

### Magnet Ring
- **Recipe:** Cannot be crafted (found in mines)
- **Effect:** Increases magnetism by 2 tiles
- **Source:** Mines levels 40+

### Slime Charmer Ring
- **Recipe:** Cannot be crafted (reward from Gil)
- **Effect:** Prevents damage from slimes
- **Source:** Monster Eradication Goal (slimes)

### Savage Ring
- **Recipe:** Cannot be crafted (reward from Gil)
- **Effect:** Gain a short speed boost after killing a monster
- **Source:** Monster Eradication Goal (skeletons)

### Vampire Ring
- **Recipe:** Cannot be crafted (reward from Gil)
- **Effect:** Gain 2 health after killing a monster
- **Source:** Monster Eradication Goal (bats)

### Burglar's Ring
- **Recipe:** Cannot be crafted (reward from Gil)
- **Effect:** Monsters have a doubled chance of dropping loot
- **Source:** Monster Eradication Goal (dust sprites)

### Iridium Band
- **Recipe:** 5 Iridium Bars, 50 Solar Essence, 50 Void Essence
- **Effect:** Glow, magnetism, and +10% Attack Damage
- **Source:** Combat Level 9

### Napalm Ring
- **Recipe:** Cannot be crafted (reward from Gil)
- **Effect:** Monsters explode when killed, dealing damage to nearby enemies
- **Source:** Monster Eradication Goal (serpents)

## Non-Craftable / Special Rings

### Small Glow Ring / Glow Ring
- **Effect:** Emit light radius (small and medium)
- **Source:** Mines, fishing chests

### Small Magnet Ring / Magnet Ring
- **Effect:** Increase item pickup radius (+1 and +2 tiles)
- **Source:** Mines, fishing chests

### Hot Java Ring
- **Effect:** Greatly increases chance to find coffee drinks when killing monsters
- **Source:** Prize from the Museum

### Lucky Ring
- **Effect:** +1 Luck
- **Source:** Panning, Skull Cavern treasure rooms

### Protection Ring
- **Effect:** Increases invincibility duration by 0.4 seconds after taking damage
- **Source:** Volcano Dungeon chests

### Soul Sapper Ring
- **Effect:** Gain 4 energy after killing a monster
- **Source:** Volcano Dungeon chests

### Phoenix Ring
- **Effect:** Once per day, restores health to 50% when it drops to 0
- **Source:** Volcano Dungeon chests

### Immunity Band
- **Effect:** +4 Immunity
- **Source:** Volcano Dungeon chests

### Glowstone Ring
- **Effect:** Combines Glow Ring and Magnet Ring effects
- **Source:** Mining (random), crafting recipe at Mining Level 4

### Jade Ring
- **Effect:** Increases critical strike chance by 10%
- **Source:** Crafting at Combat Level 6

### Topaz Ring
- **Effect:** Increases weapon precision by 10%
- **Source:** Crafting at Combat Level 4

### Amethyst Ring
- **Effect:** Increases knockback by 10%
- **Source:** Crafting at Combat Level 2

### Aquamarine Ring
- **Effect:** Increases critical strike power by 10%
- **Source:** Crafting at Combat Level 4

### Emerald Ring
- **Effect:** Increases weapon speed by 10%
- **Source:** Crafting at Combat Level 6

### Ruby Ring
- **Effect:** Increases attack by 10%
- **Source:** Crafting at Combat Level 6

## Ring Combinations at the Volcano Forge

At the Volcano Forge, you can combine two rings into one using 20 Cinder Shards. The combined ring retains both effects.

### Best Ring Combinations

1. **Iridium Band + Burglar's Ring**
   - Attack boost, glow, magnetism, and doubled monster drops

2. **Iridium Band + Lucky Ring**
   - Attack boost, glow, magnetism, and +1 Luck

3. **Napalm Ring + Burglar's Ring**
   - Exploding monsters with doubled drops

4. **Vampire Ring + Soul Sapper Ring**
   - Massive sustain in combat (health and energy on kill)

5. **Slime Charmer Ring + Thorns Ring**
   - Immunity to slimes with reflective damage

6. **Two Lucky Rings (combined)**
   - +2 Luck total

### Forging Rules
- Two different rings can be combined into one
- Combined rings take up only one ring slot
- You can wear two combined rings (4 effects total)
- Rings cannot be un-combined once forged
- The order of combination does not matter

## Tips

- Always prioritize rings that complement your playstyle
- Burglar's Ring is one of the best for general farming
- Iridium Band is essentially three rings in one
- Combine utility rings (glow/magnet) with combat rings to save slots
- Lucky Ring is extremely rare but very powerful
