# Stardew Valley Golden Walnuts Guide

![Ginger Island](/images/location-ginger-island.jpg)

Golden Walnuts are the currency of Ginger Island, introduced in Stardew Valley version 1.5. There are 130 Golden Walnuts total to collect, and they're required to unlock various island features, including Qi's Walnut Room (100 walnuts needed). This comprehensive guide covers every Golden Walnut location organized by area.

## Unlocking Ginger Island

Before collecting Golden Walnuts, you must unlock Ginger Island:

1. Complete the Community Center or Joja Warehouse
2. Receive Willy's letter about his boat
3. Visit Willy's Fish Shop and enter the back room
4. Repair the boat (requires 200 Hardwood, 5 Iridium Bars, 5 Battery Packs)
5. Pay 1,000g per trip to travel to the island

## Puzzle Solutions for Golden Walnuts

### Island East - Gem Bird Puzzle (5 Walnuts)

**Location:** The shrine in the eastern jungle

**Solution:**
1. Wait for a rainy day on Ginger Island
2. Find the four gem-colored birds (one in each cardinal direction)
3. Note which gem each bird drops
4. Place the gems on the corresponding pedestals at the shrine

**Bird Locations:**
- North Island: Amethyst or Topaz bird
- South Island: Aquamarine or Jade bird
- East Island: Emerald or Ruby bird
- West Island: Diamond bird

**Reward:** 5 Golden Walnuts

### Island South - Mermaid Puzzle (5 Walnuts + Pearl)

**Location:** The beach area on rainy days

**Solution:**
1. Wait for a rainy day
2. Go to the mermaid area on the south beach
3. Place Flute Blocks on the pedestals
4. Tune them to match the notes from Secret Note #23: 1-10-8-4-2

**How to Tune Flute Blocks:**
- Right-click to increase pitch
- Left-click to decrease pitch
- Numbers represent how many clicks from the base note

**Reward:** 5 Golden Walnuts + 1 Pearl

### Island North - Dig Site Puzzle (Multiple Walnuts)

The dig site contains many artifact spots that can yield Golden Walnuts when dug up. Check daily as they respawn.

### Volcano Dungeon - Level 10 Forge

Reaching the Forge at level 10 rewards Golden Walnuts through:
- Monster kills
- Chest openings
- Cinder Shard mining

## Golden Coconut Locations

Golden Coconuts can be cracked open at Clint's shop for a chance at Golden Walnuts.

**Where to Find Golden Coconuts:**
- Shake palm trees on Ginger Island (rare)
- Artifact spots on the beach
- Fishing in island waters
- Volcano Dungeon chests

**Golden Coconut Contents:**
- Chance for Golden Walnuts
- Rare minerals
- Artifacts
- Furniture items

## Efficient Golden Walnut Farming

### Daily Routine

**Morning:**
1. Check all artifact spots on the island
2. Cut any respawned jungle grass
3. Harvest crops on the island farm
4. Kill Tiger Slimes in the west

**Afternoon:**
1. Enter the Volcano Dungeon
2. Kill Magma Sprites
3. Open all chests
4. Mine Cinder Shards

**Evening:**
1. Fish in island waters
2. Check for rainy day (Mermaid/Gem Bird puzzles)
3. Crack open Golden Coconuts

### Priority Order

**First Priority (Easy Walnuts):**
- Dig all visible artifact spots
- Cut all jungle grass
- Mine all mussel nodes

**Second Priority (Medium Effort):**
- Kill Tiger Slimes (20 walnuts)
- Harvest crops (32 walnuts)
- Fish in island waters

**Third Priority (Dungeon):**
- Volcano Dungeon exploration
- Magma Sprite farming
- Chest hunting

**Fourth Priority (Puzzles):**
- Gem Bird puzzle (requires rainy days)
- Mermaid puzzle (requires rainy days)
- Pirate Cove (requires rainy days)

### Using the Parrot for Hints

Once you have access to Qi's Walnut Room (or the parrot in the farmhouse), you can spend 1 Golden Walnut per hint to locate unfound walnuts.

**When to Use Hints:**
- When you've found most walnuts naturally
- When you're stuck on the final few
- When you want to speed up the process

## Tips and Tricks

### General Tips

1. **Check Daily:** Artifact spots and jungle grass respawn
2. **Plant Crops:** The island farm can yield many walnuts through harvesting
3. **Kill Everything:** Tiger Slimes and Magma Sprites drop walnuts
4. **Fish Often:** Island fishing yields walnuts
5. **Wait for Rain:** Some puzzles require rainy days

### Time-Saving Strategies

1. **Use the Parrot Express:** Once unlocked, fast travel between areas
2. **Bring Staircases:** For quick Volcano Dungeon runs
3. **Equip Proper Gear:** Use weapons and rings for efficient monster killing
4. **Eat Buff Food:** Speed and luck buffs help

### Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Not checking the entire island daily** - Artifact spots respawn everywhere
2. **Ignoring the farm** - Harvesting crops gives many walnuts
3. **Not killing slimes** - Tiger Slimes give 20 walnuts total
4. **Missing rainy days** - Some content only available in rain
5. **Not using hints** - The parrot can save hours of searching
