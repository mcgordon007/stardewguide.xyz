# Backwoods Guide

## Overview

The **Backwoods** is a small outdoor area located directly **north of the player's farm**. It serves as a connector between the Farm and the **Railroad** area, which is unlocked after completing either the Boiler Room bundles or the Joja Community Development Form.

## Geography

- The Backwoods lies immediately north of the farm.
- To the north, it connects to the **Railroad** (after unlocking).
- It is a relatively small map with trees, stumps, and a small pond.

## Activities

### Foraging
- Various seasonal forageables can spawn here.
- Trees and stumps provide wood and hardwood.
- Check regularly for seasonal wild plants.

### Fishing
- The pond in the Backwoods contains fish.
- Available fish depend on the season and time of day.
- Common catches include basic river/lake fish.

### Hardwood
- Stumps in the Backwoods respawn and provide **hardwood**.
- Useful early game before access to the Secret Woods.

## Railroad Access

- The Railroad is blocked by a landslide initially.
- Complete the **Boiler Room** bundles (Community Center) or the Joja equivalent to unlock it.
- The Railroad contains unique forageables, the Spa, and the train passing event.

## Tips

- Collect hardwood from stumps daily.
- The pond is a convenient early fishing spot close to home.
- Check the Railroad area after unlocking for extra foraging opportunities.
