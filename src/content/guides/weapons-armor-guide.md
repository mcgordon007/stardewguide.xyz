# Complete Weapons and Armor Guide

![Weapons Collection](/images/weapons-collection.jpg)

Combat is an essential part of Stardew Valley, whether you are exploring the mines, delving into the Skull Cavern, or fighting monsters on your farm. This comprehensive guide covers every weapon type, the complete progression path, armor and boots, and strategies for obtaining the most powerful weapons in the game.

## Understanding Combat Stats

Before diving into specific weapons, it is important to understand what each stat means:

| Stat | Description | Impact |
|------|-------------|--------|
| **Damage** | Range of damage per hit (e.g., 20-30) | Higher is better; determines how quickly you defeat enemies |
| **Speed** | How fast you can swing the weapon | Higher speed = more attacks per second |
| **Crit Chance** | Percentage chance for critical hits | Critical hits deal significantly more damage |
| **Crit Power** | Multiplier for critical hit damage | Base is 3x damage; higher crit power increases this |
| **Weight** | Knockback effect on enemies | Higher weight pushes enemies back further |
| **Defense** | Damage reduction | Each point reduces damage taken by 1 |
| **Immunity** | Debuff resistance | Reduces chance of being affected by negative status effects |

## Weapon Types

Stardew Valley features four distinct weapon categories, each with unique characteristics:

### Swords

Swords are the most balanced weapon type, offering good damage, decent speed, and a defensive special move. They are the recommended choice for most players.

**Special Move:** Block - Hold right-click to block incoming attacks, reducing damage taken.

**Characteristics:**
- Moderate speed
- Good damage range
- Defensive capabilities
- Reliable and consistent

### Daggers

Daggers are the fastest weapon type but sacrifice raw damage and range. They excel at dealing rapid damage to single targets.

**Special Move:** Rapid Stab - Perform a quick series of stabs dealing massive damage to a single target.

**Characteristics:**
- Very fast attack speed
- Lower damage per hit
- Short range
- Excellent for focused damage

### Clubs

Clubs are slow but powerful weapons that deal massive damage and have excellent knockback. They are ideal for crowd control.

**Special Move:** Smash - A powerful ground slam that damages all enemies in an area and can destroy rocks.

**Characteristics:**
- Slow attack speed
- Very high damage
- Strong knockback
- Area damage special move

### Slingshots

Slingshots are ranged weapons that use ammunition. They are unique and situational, requiring practice to master.

**Ammunition Types:**
- Stones (1 damage)
- Explosive Ammo (powerful area damage)
- Various other items

**Characteristics:**
- Ranged attacks
- Requires ammunition
- Difficult to aim
- Situational usefulness

## The Galaxy Sword: How to Obtain

The Galaxy Sword is the gateway to the most powerful weapons in the game. Here is how to get it:

### Step 1: Obtain a Prismatic Shard

Prismatic Shards are extremely rare items. Here are the ways to obtain them:

| Method | Chance | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Omni Geode | 1/250 (0.4%) | Best method - crack open Omni Geodes at the Blacksmith |
| Iridium Node | 4% | Mine Iridium Nodes in Skull Cavern |
| Mystic Stone | 25% | Rare stones in Skull Cavern (Level 100+) |
| Monster Drops | Varies | Serpents, Mummies, Purple Slimes (rare) |
| Fishing Treasure | Very rare | Treasure chests while fishing |
| Rainbow Trout Fish Pond | 0.1% daily | With 9+ Rainbow Trout in pond |

### Step 2: Unlock the Calico Desert

Complete the Vault Bundle at the Community Center or purchase the Bus Repair from Joja for 40,000g. This unlocks access to the Calico Desert.

### Step 3: Visit the Three Pillars

1. Travel to the Calico Desert via the bus (Pam drives on days after you have paid for the repair)
2. Walk to the northwest corner of the desert
3. Find the three stone pillars arranged in a triangle
4. Stand in the center of the three pillars
5. Hold the Prismatic Shard and right-click on the center pillar
6. The Prismatic Shard will be consumed and you will receive the Galaxy Sword

**Important:** Make sure you do not have your sword slot full when doing this. The Galaxy Sword will replace whatever is in your sword slot.

## Weapon Forging and Enchanting

At the Volcano Forge on Ginger Island, you can enhance your weapons beyond their base stats.

### Weapon Upgrades

You can upgrade a weapon's base damage up to 3 times using various gems:

| Gem | Effect | Best For |
|-----|--------|----------|
| Ruby | +10% Damage | All weapons (general damage boost) |
| Aquamarine | +10% Crit Chance | Daggers (already high crit) |
| Amethyst | +10% Knockback | Clubs (maximize crowd control) |
| Emerald | +10% Speed | Slow weapons (Clubs) |
| Jade | +10% Crit Power | High-damage weapons |
| Topaz | +1 Defense | Survival builds |

### Weapon Enchantments

After fully upgrading a weapon, you can apply one enchantment:

| Enchantment | Effect | Best For |
|-------------|--------|----------|
| Artful | Special move cooldown reduced by 50% | All weapons |
| Bug Killer | Double damage to bugs; bugs always drop | Mines/General |
| Crusader | Double damage to mummies and skeletons; prevents mummy revival | Skull Cavern |
| Vampiric | 9% chance to regain some health on kill | Survival/Deep dives |
| Haymaker | 50% more hay from cutting weeds with sword | Farming utility |
| Powerful | +5 additional damage | Early game weapons |

## Best Weapons for Each Situation

### Early Game (Year 1, Mine Levels 1-40)

**Recommended:** Steel Smallsword or Forest Sword
- Easy to obtain from chests
- Decent damage for early monsters
- Good speed for learning combat

### Mid Game (Year 1, Mine Levels 40-80)

**Recommended:** Cutlass or Pirate's Sword
- Available from Adventurer's Guild
- Good balance of damage and speed
- Reliable for most encounters

### Late Game Pre-Galaxy (Mine Levels 80-120)

**Recommended:** Obsidian Edge or Bone Sword
- High damage output
- Bone Sword's speed is excellent
- Obsidian Edge hits hard despite slow speed

### Skull Cavern Exploration

**Recommended:** Galaxy Sword or Infinity Blade
- Essential for deep Skull Cavern runs
- High damage needed for tough monsters
- Speed helps with Serpents

### General Farming/Monster Hunting

**Recommended:** Galaxy Sword with Artful enchantment
- Balanced stats
- Reduced special cooldown for blocking
- Reliable for all situations

### Boss Fights (Modded/Special)

**Recommended:** Infinity Gavel or Infinity Dagger
- Gavel for area damage
- Dagger for focused single-target damage
- Maximum upgraded stats

## Combat Tips and Strategies

### General Combat Advice

1. **Always carry food:** Bring plenty of healing items when exploring mines
2. **Use the sword block:** Hold right-click to block incoming attacks
3. **Prioritize flying enemies:** Bats and Serpents can hit you from any angle
4. **Watch your health:** Retreat when low; dying costs money and items
5. **Upgrade weapons regularly:** Visit the Adventurer's Guild often

### Mine-Specific Strategies

| Mine Level Range | Strategy |
|-----------------|----------|
| 1-40 | Focus on slimes and bugs; learn attack patterns |
| 40-80 | Watch out for skeletons; use knockback to your advantage |
| 80-120 | Bring bombs for mummies; use high-damage weapons |
| Skull Cavern | Speed is key; bring Staircases and healing food |
