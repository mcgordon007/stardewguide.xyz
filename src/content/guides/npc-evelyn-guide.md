# Stardew Valley Evelyn Guide: Complete NPC Overview

Evelyn is the beloved grandmother of Pelican Town, known for her warmth, wisdom, and legendary cookies. As the wife of George and a central figure in the community, she represents the caring matriarch who keeps the town's spirit alive. This comprehensive guide covers everything about Evelyn, from her daily schedule to her gift preferences to her heartwarming cookie quest.

## Basic Information

### Character Overview

**Name**: Evelyn
**Role**: Town Elder / Grandmother
**Location**: 1 Willow Lane (next to the Stardrop Saloon)
**Family**: George (husband)
**Birthday**: Winter 20
**Marriageable**: No

### Personality

Evelyn is a kind, gentle, and nurturing woman who treats everyone in Pelican Town like family. She is an excellent baker, particularly known for her cookies, and she loves sharing her creations with the community. Despite her age, Evelyn is active and maintains a positive outlook on life. She is patient with her husband George, even when he is grumpy, and she serves as a moral anchor for the town. Her wisdom and warmth make her one of the most universally loved characters in the game.

## Gift Preferences

Evelyn appreciates gifts related to cooking, gardening, and nature. Her preferences reflect her background as a baker and homemaker.

### Loved Gifts (80 points)

These gifts give the maximum friendship boost:

- **Fiddlehead Fern** - A seasonal foraged item available only during Summer in the Secret Woods. Evelyn loves this rare and nutritious plant.
- **Tomato** - A common Summer crop that Evelyn uses in her cooking. Easy to grow and give.
- **Yam** - A Fall crop that Evelyn particularly enjoys. Plant Yams in Fall for a steady supply of loved gifts.
- **Cookies** - Fittingly, Evelyn loves receiving cookies even though she is known for baking them herself. Cook Cookies using 1 Sugar and 1 Wheat Flour.

### Liked Gifts (45 points)

Good alternatives when loved gifts are unavailable:

- **All cooked foods** (except disliked ones)
- **All fruits** (except disliked ones)
- **All vegetables** (except disliked ones)
- **Milk** (any quality)
- **Flowers** (except Poppy and Tulip)
- **Chocolate Cake**
- **Pumpkin Pie**
- **Spaghetti**
- **Bread**
- **Farmer's Lunch**

### Neutral Gifts (20 points)

- **Most basic items**
- **Eggs**

### Disliked Gifts (-20 points)

Avoid giving these items to Evelyn:

- **All gems**
- **All minerals and ores**
- **Monster Loot**
- **Mayonnaise**
- **Duck Mayonnaise**
- **Truffle**
- **Truffle Oil**
- **Honey**
- **Coffee**
- **Tea**

### Hated Gifts (-40 points)

Never give these items to Evelyn:

- **Clay**
- **Coal**
- **Dried Spices**
- **Fiber**
- **Garlic**
- **Green Tea**
- **Holly**
- **Nautilus Shell**
- **Seaweed**

### Birthday Gift (Winter 20)

Evelyn's birthday is Winter 20. Giving a loved gift on this day provides 640 friendship points. Cookies are the most thematic choice, but Yam or Tomato (if preserved) also work well. Since her birthday is in Winter, you may need to use preserved or stored crops from previous seasons.

## Heart Events

Evelyn's heart events are gentle and heartwarming, reflecting her role as the town's grandmother.

### Two Hearts - The Garden

This event triggers when you visit Evelyn's house. She is in the kitchen or standing near her garden. She talks about her love for gardening and how growing things gives her a sense of purpose. She mentions that George used to help her in the garden before his legs got bad. This event establishes Evelyn's connection to nature and her nurturing personality.

### Four Hearts - The Cookies

Visit Evelyn's house between 9:00 AM and 4:00 PM. Evelyn is baking cookies and offers you some fresh from the oven. She talks about how baking has always been her way of showing love to the people around her. She mentions that she bakes cookies for George every week and that it is one of the few things that makes him smile. This event introduces Evelyn's signature cookies and her role as a caregiver.

### Six Hearts - Memories

Visit Evelyn's house in the evening. She is sitting in the living room looking at old photo albums. She talks about her life in Pelican Town, how she and George met, and how the town has changed over the decades. She shares stories about the other villagers when they were younger, including Lewis, Marnie, and others. This event provides rich lore about the town's history and shows Evelyn's deep roots in the community.

### Eight Hearts - George's Health

Visit Evelyn's house when George is present. Evelyn is helping George with his wheelchair and making sure he is comfortable. She talks about the challenges of caring for George as he ages but emphasizes that she would not have it any other way. She mentions that love means taking care of someone even when it is difficult. This event showcases Evelyn's devotion to her husband and her strength of character.

### Ten Hearts - The Town's Heart

Visit the town square between 10:00 AM and 2:00 PM. Evelyn is handing out cookies to various villagers. She talks to the player about how the town is like one big family and how everyone looks out for each other. She expresses gratitude for the player's presence in the valley and says that new faces bring new life to Pelican Town. This event is a beautiful celebration of community and friendship.

## Cookie Quest

One of Evelyn's most notable interactions is her cookie-related quest, which is tied to her character and provides a fun gameplay objective.

### The Quest

At some point during your friendship with Evelyn, she may mention that she wants to share her cookies with more people in town. While there is no formal quest log entry for this, the spirit of the quest is reflected in her dialogue and events where she distributes cookies to the community.

### Giving Evelyn Cookies

When you give Evelyn Cookies as a gift, she reacts with particular delight. She may comment on how it feels strange but wonderful to receive cookies instead of making them. This is one of the most charming gift interactions in the game.

### Cookie Recipe

To make Cookies, you will need:
- **1 Sugar** (can be purchased from Pierre's or made from Sugar Cane)
- **1 Wheat Flour** (can be purchased from Pierre's or milled from Wheat)

The recipe is learned by default and can be cooked in any kitchen upgrade.

## Relationship with George

Evelyn's relationship with George is one of the most enduring marriages in Pelican Town. Despite George's grumpy exterior and declining health, their love for each other is evident in every interaction.

### Caring for George

Evelyn takes care of George's daily needs, including preparing his meals, helping him with his wheelchair, and keeping him company. She never complains about this responsibility and sees it as an expression of her love.

### Balancing Act

Evelyn sometimes mediates between George and other villagers, particularly when George's grumpiness offends someone. She gently encourages George to be more sociable while also respecting his need for space and rest.

### Mutual Affection

Despite George's complaints, he clearly loves Evelyn deeply. He often mentions her cooking and becomes visibly upset when she is unwell or upset. Their relationship is a testament to the enduring power of love and commitment.

## Friendship Tips and Strategies

### Maximizing Friendship with Evelyn

1. **Grow loved crops**: Plant Beets in Fall and Tulips in Spring for loved gifts. Fairy Roses bloom in Fall and are also loved.
2. **Make Raisins**: Plant Sunflowers in Summer and use a Dehydrator to make Raisins, a loved gift for Evelyn.
3. **Birthday priority**: Winter 20 is Evelyn's birthday. Save your best gifts for this day.
4. **Daily conversations**: Evelyn is almost always at home, making her one of the easiest NPCs to talk to daily.
5. **Fairy Rose**: Plant Fairy Rose seeds in Fall for beautiful loved gifts. They bloom late in the season.

### Seasonal Gift Planning

- **Spring**: Plant Tulips for loved gifts. They are easy to grow and bloom in Spring.
- **Summer**: Plant Sunflowers for Raisins later. Use saved loved items from Spring.
- **Fall**: Beets and Fairy Roses are the primary loved gifts. Also make Stuffing with Bread and Cranberry Sauce.
- **Winter**: Use Chocolate Cake, preserved flowers, or stored items.

### Cost-Effective Strategy

The most cost-effective approach to befriending Evelyn is to grow Tulips in Spring and Beets in Fall. Tulip seeds cost 20g each from Pierre's and produce one harvest. Beet seeds cost 20g each and produce one harvest. Combined with Fairy Roses in Fall, you can maintain a steady supply of loved gifts for very little gold.
