# Stardew Valley Egg Festival Guide

## Overview

The **Egg Festival** is a lively spring festival in *Stardew Valley* celebrating the season with an exciting egg hunt competition, festive decorations, and the exclusive sale of Strawberry Seeds.

## Date and Time

- **Date:** Spring 13
- **Time:** 9:00 AM – 2:00 PM
- **Entry:** Enter Pelican Town between 9:00 AM and 2:00 PM to participate

## Location

**Pelican Town Square** — The town center is decorated with colorful spring banners, egg decorations, and a festive atmosphere.

## How to Participate

1. Travel to Pelican Town during festival hours.
2. Talk to Mayor Lewis to begin the egg hunt.
3. Search the town for hidden colored eggs.
4. After the egg hunt, visit Pierre's booth to purchase Strawberry Seeds.

## Egg Hunt Minigame

### Rules
- You have **50 seconds** to find as many eggs as possible.
- Eggs are hidden throughout Pelican Town Square and surrounding areas.
- You compete against Abigail, Sam, and other villagers.

### How to Win
- Find **at least 9 eggs** to win the competition.
- The optimal route involves checking behind buildings, bushes, and fences.

### Rewards
- **First place:** A **Straw Hat** (cosmetic item, awarded only once)
- **Subsequent wins:** 1,000g
- **Participation:** Friendship boost with villagers

## Strawberry Seeds

One of the most important features of the Egg Festival is Pierre's exclusive shop:

| Item | Price | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| Strawberry Seeds | 100g each | The most profitable spring crop |

### Why Strawberry Seeds Are Essential
- Strawberries are the **most profitable crop in Spring**.
- They regrow after harvesting (produce multiple harvests per plant).
- If planted on Spring 13 and watered consistently, you can get up to **2-3 harvests** before Summer.
- With proper planning (using Speed-Gro or the Agriculturalist profession), you can maximize yields.

### Tips for Strawberry Seeds
- **Buy as many as you can afford** — this is your only chance to get them in Spring.
- **Save money early in Spring** specifically for this festival.
- **Plant immediately** after the festival ends (you return to the farm at 10:00 PM).
- **Use Speed-Gro** (Basic or Deluxe) to get an extra harvest before Summer.

## Activities

- Compete in the egg hunt
- Purchase Strawberry Seeds from Pierre
- Socialize with villagers
- Enjoy the spring decorations

## Friendship Boost

- Talking to villagers during the festival provides standard friendship gains.
- Winning the egg hunt may provide a small additional friendship boost with participants.

## Tips

- **Practice the route:** Learn the egg locations to consistently win the hunt.
- **Save early, spend big:** Don't waste money on non-essentials before Spring 13.
- **Strawberry strategy:** Prioritize Strawberry Seeds over other purchases — they pay for themselves many times over.
- **Speed-Gro synergy:** If you have access to Speed-Gro, use it on Strawberry Seeds for maximum profit.
- **First year focus:** In your first year, buy as many Strawberry Seeds as possible to build capital for future seasons.

## Notes

- The festival ends automatically at 2:00 PM, and you return to the farm at 10:00 PM.
- Time is paused during the festival.
- You can only buy Strawberry Seeds during this festival — they are not available at Pierre's shop at any other time.
- The Straw Hat reward is a unique cosmetic item that can only be obtained by winning the egg hunt for the first time.
