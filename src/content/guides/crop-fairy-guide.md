# Stardew Valley Crop Fairy Guide

## Overview

The **Crop Fairy** is a rare and beneficial random event in Stardew Valley. During the night, a magical fairy may visit your farm and instantly grow a patch of your crops, saving you days of waiting time.

## How to Trigger the Event

### Spawn Conditions

- **Farm Type**: Can occur on any farm type.
- **Season**: Can happen during **Spring, Summer, or Fall** (not in Winter, as crops do not grow).
- **Chance**: There is a **1% chance** each night for the event to occur.
- **Crop Requirement**: You must have **crops planted** that are not yet fully grown.

### What Happens

When the event triggers, you will receive the following message in the morning:

> "The crop fairy visited your farm last night!"

A small area of your crops (typically a 5x5 area) will be **instantly advanced to the next growth stage**. If the crops were close to maturity, they may become fully grown and ready to harvest immediately.

## Event Mechanics

### Affected Area

- The Crop Fairy selects a **random 5x5 area** on your farm.
- Only crops within this area are affected.
- Crops that are already fully grown are ignored.
- If there are no valid crops, the event still occurs but has no effect.

### Growth Effect

- Crops in the affected area advance by **one full growth stage**.
- For multi-harvest crops (like Blueberries, Cranberries, or Tomatoes), this brings them closer to their first harvest.
- If a crop only had one stage remaining, it becomes fully mature.

## Maximizing the Event's Value

### Strategic Planting

1. **Plant Large Fields**: The more crops you have, the higher the chance the fairy affects something valuable.
2. **Focus on High-Value Crops**: If the fairy grows your Starfruit or Ancient Fruit, you save significant time.
3. **Avoid Empty Space in Crop Areas**: Keep your crop fields dense so the 5x5 area hits maximum plants.

### Best Crops to Benefit

| Crop | Why It's Good |
|------|---------------|
| Starfruit | High value, long growth time |
| Ancient Fruit | Long growth time, multi-harvest |
| Pumpkin | High value, takes 13 days |
| Melon | High value, takes 12 days |
| Cauliflower | High value, takes 12 days |
| Sweet Gem Berry | Extremely high value, long growth |

## Comparison with Other Growth Methods

| Method | Effect | Cost |
|--------|--------|------|
| Crop Fairy | Free, 1% chance nightly | Free |
| Speed-Gro Fertilizer | 10% or 25% faster growth | Purchase or craft |
| Deluxe Speed-Gro | 33% faster growth | Purchase or craft |
| Agriculturist Profession | 10% faster growth | Level 10 Farming choice |

The Crop Fairy is unique because it is **completely free** and can stack with other methods for even faster results.

## Tips for Players

1. **Plant Every Season**: Since the fairy only appears when crops are growing, always keep fields active.
2. **Don't Rely on It**: With only a 1% chance, this is a bonus, not a strategy. Use fertilizers for consistent results.
3. **Check in the Morning**: If you see the message, head straight to your fields to harvest any newly mature crops.
4. **Multiplayer Note**: In multiplayer, the event affects the host's farm and is shared among all players.

## Trivia

- The Crop Fairy is one of the few random events that provides a direct gameplay benefit.
- The fairy's appearance is accompanied by a gentle, magical sound effect.
- Unlike the Strange Capsule or Stone Owl, the Crop Fairy does not leave a physical item behind.
