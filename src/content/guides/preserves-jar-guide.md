# Stardew Valley Preserves Jar Guide

## Overview

The **Preserves Jar** is an essential artisan equipment in Stardew Valley that allows players to process fruits into **Jelly** and vegetables into **Pickles**. It is one of the most profitable early-game crafting items and a cornerstone of any successful artisan goods operation.

## Unlock Requirements

- **Farming Level 4** required to unlock the crafting recipe

## Crafting Materials

To craft a Preserves Jar, you will need:

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Wood | 50 |
| Stone | 40 |
| Coal | 8 |

## How to Use

1. Place the Preserves Jar on your farm or inside a shed
2. Interact with it while holding a **fruit** or **vegetable**
3. The processing time varies based on the item type:
   - **Fruits** → **Jelly** (takes 4,000 minutes of in-game time, approximately 2-3 days)
   - **Vegetables** → **Pickles** (takes 4,000 minutes of in-game time, approximately 2-3 days)

## Output Products

### Jelly (from Fruits)
- Base fruit value × 2 + 50g
- Example: Strawberry (120g) → Jelly (290g)

### Pickles (from Vegetables)
- Base vegetable value × 2 + 50g
- Example: Pumpkin (320g) → Pickles (690g)

## Tips & Strategy

- Preserves Jars are particularly profitable with low-value, high-yield crops like Corn or Tomatoes
- The Artisan profession (+40% sell price) applies to both Jelly and Pickles
- Place Preserves Jars inside a Shed to save farm space
- Cannot process: Foraged items, flowers, or tree fruits (use Keg for tree fruits)
