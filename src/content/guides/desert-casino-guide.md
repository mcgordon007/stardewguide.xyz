# Stardew Valley Desert Casino Guide

The **Desert Casino** is a secret gambling establishment located in the back room of the **Oasis** shop in the **Calico Desert**. It offers two casino games, a shop with rare items, and a unique currency system using **Qi Coins**.

## How to Unlock the Desert Casino

To enter the Desert Casino, you must complete **The Mysterious Qi** quest chain to obtain the **Club Card**. This multi-step quest involves placing specific items in hidden locations around Stardew Valley.

### The Mysterious Qi Quest Steps

1. **Place 1 Battery Pack** in the encrypted box at the tunnel next to the Bus Stop.
2. **Place 1 Rainbow Shell** in the box at the Train Station.
3. **Place 10 Beets** in Mayor Lewis's refrigerator.
4. **Place 1 Solar Essence** in the mouth of the Sand Dragon in the Calico Desert.
5. **Check the wood pile** next to your house.

**Final Reward:** Club Card (allows entry to the Desert Casino)

### Important Note About Beets

Beets can only be purchased from the **Traveling Cart** during **Winter** at 100g each. It is highly recommended to stock up on Beets in advance, as they are not always available and are required to progress through the quest chain.

## Casino Games

### CalicoJack (Blackjack Variant)

CalicoJack is a card game similar to Blackjack and is the most reliable way to earn Qi Coins.

#### Tables and Stakes

- **Low Stakes Table:** 100 Qi Coins per game
- **High Stakes Table:** 1,000 Qi Coins per game

#### How to Play

- **Objective:** Score higher than the dealer without exceeding 21.
- Both the player and dealer are dealt two cards. The first card is valued 1-11, and the second is valued 1-9.
- The dealer's first card is hidden.
- The player can choose to **Stay** or **Hit** (draw a card valued 1-9).
- **Bust:** If your total exceeds 21, you lose immediately.
- **CalicoJack:** If your total is exactly 21, you win immediately.
- If you choose to stay, the dealer reveals their hidden card and draws until they either bust or reach a total of 18 or higher that matches or exceeds your total.

#### Dealer Behavior

- The dealer usually stands on 18 but may draw in specific cases.
- If the player has 18 or higher, the dealer has a chance to instantly bust:
  - **10% chance** at 18
  - **25% chance** at 19
  - **50% chance** at 20
- There is a small chance the dealer draws a **Qi Fruit** image, which results in an automatic dealer bust and your bet is **tripled**.

#### Odds and Strategy

- The odds **slightly favor the player** (not the house).
- **Luck does NOT affect** CalicoJack results.
- Weighted odds mean hitting with 16 or higher increases your chance to get 20 or 21.
- **Optimal Strategy:** Stand on 18 or higher, except when the dealer shows a 9, in which case stand on 19 or higher.
- **Double Or Nothing** is available after winning a hand.

### Slot Machine

The Slot Machine is another way to try your luck at the Desert Casino.

- **Bet:** 1 to 100 Qi Coins per spin.
- **Objective:** Match symbols to win.
- **Luck does NOT affect** slot machine results.

## Qi Coins

Qi Coins are the exclusive currency used in the Desert Casino. They are stored separately from your regular gold.

### Purchasing Qi Coins

- Speak to the NPC near the slot machines.
- **Exchange Rate:** 1,000g = 100 Qi Coins (10g per Qi Coin)
- **Note:** Qi Coins cannot be converted back to gold.

### Earning Qi Coins

- Buy them with gold.
- Win at **CalicoJack** (most reliable method).
- Win at the **Slot Machine**.

## Tips

- **CalicoJack is the most reliable way to earn Qi Coins.** Learn the optimal strategy and stick to it.
- The odds slightly favor the player, making it a consistent source of Qi Coins over time.
- Prioritize purchasing the **Rarecrow #3** if you are working toward the Deluxe Scarecrow.
- **Warp Totem: Farm** is useful for quick travel back to your farm.
- Stock up on **Beets** from the Traveling Cart during Winter before starting The Mysterious Qi quest chain.
- Press **Y** on keyboard to quickly answer "yes" at the Qi coin vending machine.
