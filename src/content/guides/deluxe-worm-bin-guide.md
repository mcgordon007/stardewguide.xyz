# Stardew Valley Deluxe Worm Bin Guide

## Introduction

**Deluxe Worm Bin** is an advanced device added in Stardew Valley 1.6, an upgraded version of Worm Bin that can automatically produce more Bait daily.

## Unlock Condition

- Recipe unlocked after reaching **Fishing Level 8**

## Crafting Materials

- Worm Bin x1
- Refined Quartz x1
- Moss x30

## Function

- Produces **4-5 Bait** every morning
- No need to put in any raw materials, automatic production
- Produced Bait is regular Bait, usable on all rods

## Comparison with Worm Bin

| Attribute | Worm Bin | Deluxe Worm Bin |
|-----------|----------|-----------------|
| Fishing Level | Level 8 | Level 8 (needs Worm Bin unlocked first) |
| Daily Output | 2-3 Bait | 4-5 Bait |
| Crafting Materials | Hardwood x25, Gold Bar x1, Iron Bar x1, Fiber x50 | Worm Bin x1, Refined Quartz x1, Moss x30 |

## Usage Tips

1. Place Deluxe Worm Bin in a convenient location on the farm
2. Multiple Deluxe Worm Bins can stack output to meet large fishing needs
3. Produced Bait can be used for Sea Jelly/River Jelly crafting (version 1.6)
