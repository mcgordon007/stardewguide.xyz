# Stardew Valley Adventurer's Guild Guide

![Monster Eradication](/images/monster-eradication.jpg)

The Adventurer's Guild is located east of the Mines entrance and serves as the hub for all combat-related activities in Stardew Valley. Run by Marlon and Gil, the guild offers monster eradication goals, equipment purchases, and a convenient way to sell monster loot. This comprehensive guide covers everything you need to know about the Adventurer's Guild.

## Unlocking the Adventurer's Guild

### Requirements

To unlock the Adventurer's Guild, you must:
1. Enter the Mines (unlocked on Spring 5, Year 1)
2. Reach Mine Level 5
3. Kill at least 10 Green Slimes
4. Receive a mail invitation from Marlon

### The Initiation Quest

After entering the Mines and encountering slimes, you'll receive a letter from Marlon inviting you to the guild. Once you kill 10 slimes (tracked automatically), the guild will be permanently unlocked.

**Tip:** Green Slimes appear on Mine Levels 5-29. They're slow and easy to kill, making this quest straightforward.

## Adventurer's Guild Layout

### Main Hall
- **Marlon:** Sells weapons, armor, and rings
- **Gil:** Awards Monster Eradication rewards
- **Adventurer's Board:** Tracks your monster kill progress
- **Sell Box:** Sell monster loot directly

### Operating Hours
- **Open:** 2:00 PM - 10:00 PM
- **Closed:** During festivals
- **Members Only:** Must complete initiation quest

### Monster Details and Strategies

#### Slimes (Green/Blue) - 1,000 Kills

| Type | Location | HP | Damage | Tips |
|------|----------|-----|--------|------|
| Green Slime | Mines 5-29 | 24 | 5 | Basic enemy, very easy |
| Blue Slime | Mines 40-79 | 106 | 7 | Slightly tougher |

**Strategy:** The Mines levels 5-29 are perfect for farming Green Slimes. They respawn when you re-enter a level. With the Slime Charmer Ring, slimes will no longer damage you.

**Slime Charmer Ring Effect:** Prevents all damage from slimes.

#### Void Spirits - 150 Kills

| Type | Location | HP | Damage | Tips |
|------|----------|-----|--------|------|
| Shadow Shaman | Skull Cavern 100+ | 80 | 17 | Casts healing spells |
| Shadow Brute | Skull Cavern 100+ | 150 | 20 | High damage |

**Strategy:** These only appear in Skull Cavern levels 100+. Bring strong weapons and healing items. They're dangerous but the Savage Ring is worth it.

**Savage Ring Effect:** Gain a short speed boost after killing a monster.

#### Bats - 200 Kills

| Type | Location | HP | Damage | Tips |
|------|----------|-----|--------|------|
| Bat | Mines 30-119 | 24 | 6 | Basic flying enemy |
| Frost Bat | Mines 40-79 | 36 | 7 | Ice damage |
| Lava Bat | Mines 80-119 | 80 | 15 | Fire damage |
| Iridium Bat | Skull Cavern | 300 | 30 | Very dangerous |

**Strategy:** Bats are common throughout the Mines. You'll likely complete this naturally while exploring. The Vampire Ring provides health regeneration.

**Vampire Ring Effect:** Gain 2 health after killing a monster.

#### Skeletons - 50 Kills

| Type | Location | HP | Damage | Tips |
|------|----------|-----|--------|------|
| Skeleton | Mines 70-79 | 100 | 10 | Throws bones |

**Strategy:** Farm Mines levels 70-79. Skeletons are slow but can be annoying with their bone throws. The Skeleton Mask is a cosmetic hat.

#### Cave Insects - 125 Kills

| Type | Location | HP | Damage | Tips |
|------|----------|-----|--------|------|
| Bug | Mines 1-29 | 1 | 8 | Very weak |
| Cave Fly | Mines 10-29 | 22 | 10 | Flying |
| Grub | Mines 10-29 | 20 | 4 | Burrows underground |

**Strategy:** Early mine levels (10-29) are best. These enemies are weak and easy to kill. The Insect Head is a decent early-game weapon.

**Insect Head Stats:** Damage 10-20, Speed +2, Crit Power +10

#### Duggies - 30 Kills

| Type | Location | HP | Damage | Tips |
|------|----------|-----|--------|------|
| Duggy | Mines 10-29 | 40 | 6 | Burrows underground |

**Strategy:** Duggies only appear in dirt patches. Wait for them to surface, then attack. The Hard Hat is a cosmetic item.

#### Dust Sprites - 500 Kills

| Type | Location | HP | Damage | Tips |
|------|----------|-----|--------|------|
| Dust Sprite | Mines 40-79 | 40 | 8 | Drops coal frequently |

**Strategy:** This is one of the most valuable goals. Dust Sprites drop coal (50% chance), making this excellent for coal farming. Mines 55 is particularly good.

**Burglar's Ring Effect:** Monsters have a higher chance of dropping loot.

#### Rock Crabs - 60 Kills

| Type | Location | HP | Damage | Tips |
|------|----------|-----|--------|------|
| Rock Crab | Mines 1-29 | 60 | 5 | Disguised as rocks |
| Lava Crab | Mines 80-119 | 120 | 15 | Fire damage |
| Iridium Crab | Skull Cavern | 240 | 15 | Very tough |

**Strategy:** Look for rocks that move slightly. Hit them to reveal the crab. The Crab Cakes recipe is valuable for speed boosts.

**Crab Cakes Effect:** +1 Speed, +1 Defense for 16m 47s

#### Mummies - 100 Kills

| Type | Location | HP | Damage | Tips |
|------|----------|-----|--------|------|
| Mummy | Skull Cavern | 260 | 30 | Revives after death |

**Strategy:** Mummies revive after being killed unless finished with an explosive (bomb or explosive ammo). Use bombs efficiently. The Arcane Hat is cosmetic.

#### Pepper Rex - 50 Kills

| Type | Location | HP | Damage | Tips |
|------|----------|-----|--------|------|
| Pepper Rex | Skull Cavern | 300 | 15 | Fire breath attack |

**Strategy:** These are rare spawns in Skull Cavern. They can drop Dinosaur Eggs. Be patient; this goal takes time. The Knight's Helmet is cosmetic.

#### Serpents - 250 Kills

| Type | Location | HP | Damage | Tips |
|------|----------|-----|--------|------|
| Serpent | Skull Cavern | 150 | 12-24 | Fast and aggressive |
| Royal Serpent | Skull Cavern (dangerous) | 300 | 24-48 | Very dangerous |

**Strategy:** Serpents are very common in Skull Cavern. You'll complete this naturally while exploring. The Napalm Ring is powerful but dangerous.

**Napalm Ring Effect:** Monsters explode on death, dealing damage to nearby enemies (and you!).

#### Magma Sprites - 150 Kills

| Type | Location | HP | Damage | Tips |
|------|----------|-----|--------|------|
| Magma Sprite | Volcano Dungeon | 220 | 15 | Fire element |
| Magma Sparker | Volcano Dungeon | 310 | 15 | Stronger version |

**Strategy:** Found only in the Volcano Dungeon on Ginger Island (version 1.5+). They drop Cinder Shards and Omni Geodes. The Hot Java Ring provides a speed buff.

**Hot Java Ring Effect:** +1 Speed after killing a monster.

## Tips for Efficient Monster Farming

### General Strategies

1. **Use the Right Weapon:** Galaxy Sword (from Prismatic Shard) is best-in-slot
2. **Equip Useful Rings:** Iridium Band + Burglar's Ring is a great combo
3. **Eat Buff Food:** Spicy Eel (Speed + Luck) or Crab Cakes (Speed + Defense)
4. **Bring Healing Items:** Life Elixirs or high-healing food
5. **Use Staircases:** Skip levels in Skull Cavern to find target monsters faster

### Specific Monster Farming Locations

| Monster | Best Location | Strategy |
|---------|---------------|----------|
| Slimes | Mines 5-29 | Re-enter level repeatedly |
| Dust Sprites | Mines 55 | Excellent coal farming spot |
| Bats | Mines 30-50 | Natural progression |
| Skeletons | Mines 70-79 | Farm these specific levels |
| Serpents | Skull Cavern 1-50 | Very common |
| Void Spirits | Skull Cavern 100+ | Use staircases to reach deep levels |
| Mummies | Skull Cavern 100+ | Bring bombs |
| Pepper Rex | Skull Cavern | Rare spawn, be patient |
| Magma Sprites | Volcano Dungeon 1-5 | First levels have many |

### Daily Routine for Eradication Goals

**Early Game:**
- Visit Mines 5-29 for slimes
- Progress naturally through levels for other monsters

**Mid Game:**
- Farm Dust Sprites for coal and Burglar's Ring
- Delve deep in Skull Cavern for Void Spirits

**Late Game:**
- Focus on remaining goals (usually Pepper Rex, Mummies)
- Use Staircases aggressively in Skull Cavern

## Adventurer's Guild and Perfection

Completing all Monster Eradication Goals is required for 100% Perfection. This includes:

- All 12 monster types listed above
- The Volcano Dungeon monsters (Magma Sprites) added in 1.5

**Tips for Perfection:**
- Start working on goals early
- The Burglar's Ring helps with other collection goals
- Don't neglect the Volcano Dungeon after unlocking Ginger Island

## Conclusion

The Adventurer's Guild is essential for any combat-focused player in Stardew Valley. The Monster Eradication Goals provide long-term objectives with valuable rewards, while Marlon's shop offers crucial equipment upgrades. Whether you're working toward Perfection or just want to become the ultimate monster hunter, the Adventurer's Guild has everything you need.

Remember: The journey to completing all eradication goals is long, but the rewards - especially the rings - are well worth the effort. Happy hunting!
