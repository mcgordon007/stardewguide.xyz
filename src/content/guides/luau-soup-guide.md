# Stardew Valley Luau Soup Guide

## Overview

The **Luau** is a summer festival held on **Summer 11** at the beach. The highlight of the event is the communal soup, where each villager contributes an ingredient. Most importantly, **you** get to add an ingredient to the pot, and the **Governor's reaction** determines the outcome for everyone.

## How the Luau Soup Works

1. Travel to the **Beach** between **9:00 AM and 2:00 PM** on Summer 11.
2. Walk up to the large soup pot in the center.
3. You will be prompted to add **one ingredient** from your inventory.
4. The Governor tastes the soup and reacts based on the quality and type of ingredient you added.
5. Your friendship with villagers may be affected by his reaction.

## Governor's Reactions

The Governor has four possible reactions, ranging from disgust to delight:

### Best Reaction — "This is the best soup I've ever tasted!"

**Effect**: All villagers gain **120 friendship points** with you.

**Best Ingredients**:
- **Silver, Gold, or Iridium quality** items from these categories:
  - **Fruits**: Starfruit, Melon, Ancient Fruit, Pomegranate, Peach
  - **Vegetables**: Pumpkin, Cauliflower, Red Cabbage
  - **Animal Products**: Large Milk (any quality), Large Egg (any quality), Duck Mayonnaise, Truffle
  - **Artisan Goods**: Wine, Jelly, Honey, Cheese (high quality)
  - **Fish**: Legendary fish, high-quality fish like Lava Eel, Ice Pip
  - **Mushrooms**: Purple Mushroom, Red Mushroom (high quality)

**Note**: The ingredient must be of **Silver quality or higher** to trigger the best reaction, unless it is a universally loved high-value item.

### Good Reaction — "That's a very nice soup."

**Effect**: All villagers gain **60 friendship points** with you.

**Acceptable Ingredients**:
- Normal quality fruits and vegetables
- Normal quality fish
- Normal quality milk or eggs
- Most foraged items
- Standard cooking ingredients

### Neutral/Bad Reaction — "Hmm... it's a bit bland."

**Effect**: No friendship points gained or lost.

**Bland Ingredients**:
- Low-value items like Wheat, Hops, or common foraged items
- Normal quality items with low base value

### Worst Reaction — "This is disgusting!"

**Effect**: All villagers **lose 50 friendship points** with you.

**Worst Ingredients** (NEVER add these):
- **Holly** (poisonous)
- **Pufferfish** (poisonous)
- **Red Mushroom** (low quality)
- **Sea Urchin**
- **Sap**
- **Trash items** (Broken Glasses, Broken CD, Soggy Newspaper, etc.)
- **Strange Bun**
- Any item with negative health/energy effects

## Best Ingredient Recommendations by Game Stage

### Early Game (Year 1, low resources)
- **Silver Quality Melon** (if available)
- **Any quality Large Milk**
- **Any quality Large Egg**
- **Silver quality Blueberry or Hot Pepper**

### Mid Game (Year 2+, some artisan equipment)
- **Gold/Iridium quality Starfruit Wine**
- **Gold quality Cheese**
- **Gold quality Truffle**
- **Gold quality Pumpkin or Melon**

### Late Game (Established farm)
- **Iridium quality Starfruit**
- **Iridium quality Ancient Fruit Wine**
- **Legendary fish** (Crimsonfish, Angler, Legend, Glacierfish, Mutant Carp)
- **Iridium quality Rabbit's Foot**

## Tips for the Luau

1. **Save a High-Quality Item**: Set aside a gold or iridium quality crop or artisan good before Summer 11.
2. **Avoid Poisonous Items**: Holly and Pufferfish will ruin the soup and tank your friendships.
3. **Quality Matters**: Higher quality almost always leads to a better reaction.
4. **Check Your Inventory**: Make sure you have something good before heading to the beach.
5. **Artisan Goods Are Safe Bets**: Wine, Cheese, and Jelly are consistently well-received.
