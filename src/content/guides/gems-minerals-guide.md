# Gems & Minerals Guide

Stardew Valley features 53 donatable minerals divided into four categories: Foraged Minerals, Gems, Geode Minerals, and Geodes. This guide covers every mineral, where to find it, its sell price, and the best strategies for completing your Museum collection.

## TL;DR

There are 8 gems (Diamond is 750g, Prismatic Shard is 2,000g), 4 foraged minerals (Quartz to Fire Quartz), and 33 geode minerals (extracted from Geodes, Frozen Geodes, and Magma Geodes). The **Gemologist** profession (Mining Lv 10, Geologist branch) adds 30% to all mineral sell prices. Break geodes at Clint's (25g each) or build a **Geode Crusher** (free processing). All gems can be used for **weapon forging** at the Volcano Forge.

### Omni Geode Sources

Omni Geodes are the most versatile source and can be obtained from:
- Mining any floor in the Mines or Skull Cavern
- Carbon Ghost kills (Skull Cavern)
- Panning in rivers and lakes
- Krobus shop (Tuesdays, 300g)
- Oasis shop (Wednesdays, 1,500g for 5)
- Ginger Island artifact spots
- Fishing treasure chests

## The Gemologist Profession

At Mining Level 10, choosing the **Geologist branch** gives you the **Gemologist** profession:

| Effect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Sell Price Bonus** | +30% on all minerals and gems |
| **Stacks with** | Tiller? No (separate skill). Crafts? No. Only mineral sell prices |

### Gemologist vs Excavator

| Profession | Effect | Best For |
|------------|--------|----------|
| **Gemologist** | +30% mineral sell prices | Players who sell many minerals |
| **Excavator** | Double geode yield chance | Players who want more geodes to crack |

If you primarily process geodes for the Museum, Excavator is better. If you sell most minerals for gold, Gemologist earns more over time. Both are viable.

## Museum Completion Tips

1. **Build a Geode Crusher** ASAP (2 Coal + 50 Stone + 1 Gold Bar) -- saves 25g per geode processed
2. **Save Omni Geodes** for minerals you're missing -- they can contain anything
3. **Check Gunther's missing list** frequently by visiting the Museum
4. **Mine specific floors** for missing minerals (e.g., floors 1-39 for Geode minerals)
5. **Use the "track quests" feature** to see which minerals you still need
6. **Geode minerals are mostly disliked as gifts** -- the Wizard likes all of them, but everyone else dislikes them

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I get a Prismatic Shard?

Prismatic Shards can be obtained from: Iridium Nodes (4% chance), Mystic Stones in Skull Cavern (25% chance), Omni Geodes (0.4%), killing Shadow Brutes/Shamans (0.05%), killing Serpents and Mummies (0.1%), or purchased rarely from the Traveling Cart.

### Should I donate or sell my first Diamond?

Donate your first Diamond to the Museum for the reward. After that, sell extras -- Diamonds sell for 750g (974g with Gemologist) and are loved gifts for 8 NPCs.

### Is the Geode Crusher worth building?

Yes. It costs 2 Coal + 50 Stone + 1 Gold Bar to build, and it processes geodes for free. If you process more than 20 geodes, it has already paid for itself compared to paying Clint 25g each.

### What happens when I complete the mineral collection?

Completing all mineral donations earns you the **Rusty Key** from Gunther, which unlocks the Sewer where Krobus lives. This is required for certain quests and achievements.
