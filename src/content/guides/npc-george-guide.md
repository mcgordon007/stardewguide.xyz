# Stardew Valley George Guide: Complete NPC Overview

George is the self-proclaimed grouch of Pelican Town, but beneath his abrasive exterior lies a man with a rich history and a deep love for his family. Confined to a wheelchair, George spends most of his days at home watching television, but his story is one of resilience and hidden warmth. This comprehensive guide covers everything about George, from his daily schedule to his gift preferences to his surprisingly touching heart events.

## Basic Information

### Character Overview

**Name**: George
**Role**: Retired Townsman
**Location**: 1 Willow Lane (next to the Stardrop Saloon)
**Family**: Evelyn (wife)
**Birthday**: Winter 7
**Marriageable**: No

### Personality

George is known throughout Pelican Town for his grumpy demeanor and sharp tongue. He uses a wheelchair due to leg problems and spends most of his time at home watching TV, particularly the "Love of Cooking" channel. Despite his constant complaints and tendency to push people away, George has a good heart that reveals itself in his heart events and interactions with his wife Evelyn. He is a man who has experienced much in his long life and has become somewhat bitter about his physical limitations, but he is not without warmth or humor.

## Daily Schedule

George has one of the simplest and most predictable schedules in the game. Due to his wheelchair, he rarely leaves his home, making him very easy to find.

### Standard Schedule

| Time | Location | Activity |
|------|----------|----------|
| 8:00 AM | 1 Willow Lane (Living Room) | Waking up |
| 9:00 AM | 1 Willow Lane (Living Room) | Watching TV |
| 12:00 PM | 1 Willow Lane (Kitchen) | Lunch |
| 1:00 PM | 1 Willow Lane (Living Room) | Watching TV |
| 5:00 PM | 1 Willow Lane (Kitchen) | Dinner |
| 6:00 PM | 1 Willow Lane (Living Room) | Evening TV |
| 10:00 PM | 1 Willow Lane (Bedroom) | Going to sleep |

### Rainy Day Schedule

George's schedule does not change on rainy days since he already spends most of his time indoors. He will still be in the living room watching TV.

### Special Schedule Notes

- **George almost never leaves his house**, making him one of the easiest NPCs to locate.
- **He is always in the living room** during the day, watching television.
- **Festival Days**: George rarely attends festivals due to his mobility limitations.
- **His location never changes**, which means you can always find him at home.

## Gift Preferences

George's gift preferences are somewhat unique and reflect his personal tastes. He appreciates specific foods and foraged items.

### Loved Gifts (80 points)

These gifts give the maximum friendship boost:

- **Fried Mushroom** - George's absolute favorite dish. Requires 1 Common Mushroom to cook. Common Mushrooms can be foraged in the Secret Woods during Fall.

### Liked Gifts (45 points)

Good alternatives when loved gifts are unavailable:

- **Ginger**
- **Horse Radish**
- **Wild Horseradish**
- **Snow Yam**

- **Oyster**

Avoid giving these items to George:

### Birthday Gift (Winter 7)

George's birthday is Winter 7. Giving a loved gift on this day provides 640 friendship points. Fried Mushroom is the best choice. Since his birthday is early in Winter, you may need to use preserved mushrooms from Fall.

## Heart Events

George's heart events are surprisingly emotional and reveal the man behind the grumpy facade. They deal with themes of aging, regret, and the enduring power of love.

### Two Hearts - The TV

This event triggers when you enter George's house. George is sitting in his wheelchair watching TV and grumbles about the programs. He tells you to leave him alone and says he does not need any company. However, if you persist in talking to him, he reluctantly admits that having someone visit is not entirely unwelcome. This event establishes George's grumpy personality while hinting at his loneliness.

### Four Hearts - The Old Days

Visit George's house between 9:00 AM and 4:00 PM. George is looking at an old photo album. He talks about his younger days when he was strong and active. He mentions how he used to work on the farm and how much he loved being outdoors. He becomes wistful and a bit sad, but then catches himself and tells you not to feel sorry for him. This event reveals George's sense of loss and his struggle with his physical limitations.

### Six Hearts - The Argument

Visit George's house when Evelyn is also home. You will witness George snapping at Evelyn about something trivial. After Evelyn leaves the room, George looks remorseful and talks to the player about how he hates being mean to Evelyn but does not know how to stop. He admits that his frustration with his situation sometimes comes out as anger toward the people he loves most. This event is a raw and honest portrayal of how physical limitations can affect emotional well-being.

### Eight Hearts - Evelyn's Cookies

Visit George's house in the afternoon. Evelyn brings George a plate of fresh cookies, and his demeanor softens immediately. He talks about how Evelyn has been taking care of him for decades and how he does not deserve her patience and kindness. He asks the player not to tell Evelyn that he said anything nice about her, as it would ruin his reputation. This event is both funny and touching, showing the deep love George has for his wife beneath his grumpy exterior.

### Ten Hearts - Acceptance

Visit George's house in the evening. George is sitting by the window looking at the sunset. He talks to the player about how he has finally made peace with his situation. He says that while he cannot change his circumstances, he can choose how he faces them. He thanks the player for being persistent in visiting him despite his grumpiness and says that having a friend has made a real difference in his life. This event is a beautiful conclusion to George's arc and shows genuine growth.

## Relationship with Evelyn

George's relationship with Evelyn is central to his character. Their decades-long marriage is built on a foundation of love, patience, and mutual understanding.

### The Dynamic

George is grumpy and often irritable, while Evelyn is patient and nurturing. Despite their contrasting personalities, they clearly love each other deeply. George's grumpiness is never directed at Evelyn with true malice, and Evelyn understands that his frustration is with his situation, not with her.

### Hidden Affection

Throughout the game, you can see glimpses of George's true feelings for Evelyn. He defends her when others criticize his behavior, he becomes worried when she is upset, and he secretly loves her cookies more than he lets on.

### Growing Old Together

George and Evelyn represent the beauty of a long-term relationship. They have weathered many storms together, and their bond has only grown stronger over time. Their relationship is one of the most realistic and touching in the game.

## Friendship Tips and Strategies

### Maximizing Friendship with George

1. **Forage mushrooms**: Collect Common Mushrooms in the Secret Woods during Fall for loved gifts.
2. **Fried Mushroom is easy**: Cook Fried Mushroom using just 1 Common Mushroom. Forage mushrooms in Fall for free ingredients.
3. **Birthday priority**: Winter 7 is George's birthday. Save your best gifts for this day.
4. **Visit daily**: Since George never leaves his house, it is very easy to visit him every day for conversation points.
5. **Plan ahead**: Save Common Mushrooms from Fall for Winter gifts.

### Gift Acquisition Tips

- **Fried Mushroom**: Forage Common Mushrooms in the Secret Woods during Fall. They can also be grown in a Mushroom Cave.
- **Common Mushroom**: Found in the Secret Woods during Fall, or grown in a Mushroom Cave year-round.
- **Mushroom Cave**: If you chose the Mushroom Cave, you can get Common Mushrooms year-round.


### Cost-Effective Strategy

The most cost-effective way to befriend George is to forage Common Mushrooms in the Secret Woods during Fall and cook Fried Mushroom. If you have a Mushroom Cave, you can produce Common Mushrooms year-round. George only has one loved gift, so focus on stocking up during Fall.

## Conclusion

George is a beautifully written character who proves that first impressions can be deceiving. Behind his grumpy exterior lies a man who has lived a full life, faced significant challenges, and come out the other side with a deeper understanding of what truly matters. His relationship with Evelyn is one of the most touching in the game, and his heart events offer some of the most emotionally resonant moments in Stardew Valley. By persistently visiting him, giving him Fried Mushroom, and experiencing his story, you will discover that George is not just the town grouch, but a beloved grandfather figure with a heart of gold.
