# Stardew Valley Perfection Guide

![Money Making](/images/money-guide-illustration.jpg)

Perfection is the ultimate end-game goal in Stardew Valley, representing 100% completion of virtually everything the game has to offer. This comprehensive guide will walk you through every requirement, strategy, and reward associated with achieving Perfection.

## What is Perfection?

Perfection is a completion metric introduced in Stardew Valley version 1.5 that tracks your progress across all major aspects of the game. It is measured as a percentage, with 100% representing complete mastery of Stardew Valley.

### Grandpa's Evaluation

The concept of evaluation began with Grandpa's ghost, who returns to evaluate your progress at the beginning of Year 3 (and can be re-evaluated anytime after by placing a Diamond on his shrine). While Grandpa's evaluation awards candles (1-4), Perfection is a separate, more comprehensive system.

### Qi's Walnut Room

After unlocking Ginger Island and entering Qi's Walnut Room (requires 100 Golden Walnuts), you'll gain access to the **Perfection Tracker** on the right side of the room. This display shows your current completion percentage and breaks down each category.

## Detailed Requirements

### 1. Golden Walnuts (5%)

Collect all 130 Golden Walnuts scattered across Ginger Island. These are found through:
- Exploring all island areas (North, South, East, West)
- Solving puzzles
- Fishing in specific spots
- Mining in the Volcano Dungeon
- Killing monsters
- Harvesting Golden Coconuts

**Tip:** Use the Parrot in Qi's Walnut Room to hint at unfound walnuts (costs 1 Golden Walnut per hint).

### 2. Farmer Level (5%)

Reach level 10 in all five skills (Farming, Mining, Foraging, Fishing, Combat). This requires:
- Harvesting crops
- Caring for animals
- Collecting animal products
- Using the hoe and watering can

### 3. Produce & Forage Shipped (15%)

Ship at least one of every item type in the game. This includes:
- All crops and forage items
- All animal products
- All fish and fish products
- All artisan goods
- All minerals and gems
- All foraged items

**Tip:** Check the Collections tab periodically to track your shipping progress.

### 4. Great Friends (11%)

Reach maximum hearts with all befriendable NPCs:

**Bachelors/Bachelorettes (12):**
- Abigail, Alex, Elliott, Emily, Haley, Harvey
- Leah, Maru, Penny, Sam, Sebastian, Shane

**Other Villagers (22):**
- Caroline, Clint, Demetrius, Dwarf, Evelyn, George
- Gus, Jas, Jodi, Kent, Krobus, Leo
- Lewis, Linus, Marnie, Pam, Pierre, Robin
- Sandy, Vincent, Willy, Wizard

**Friendship Tips:**
- Give loved gifts (2x per week)
- Complete quests
- Talk daily (+20 points)
- Gift on birthdays (8x multiplier)
- Complete special orders

### 5. Obelisks on Farm (4%)

Place all 4 Obelisks on your farm:
- **Earth Obelix** - Teleports to the Mountains
- **Water Obelix** - Teleports to the Beach
- **Desert Obelix** - Teleports to the Desert
- **Island Obelix** - Teleports to Ginger Island

Each obelix requires specific materials and gold to craft. The Island Obelix is purchased from the Qi Walnut Room for 10 Golden Walnuts.

## Perfection Rewards

### Statue of True Perfection

Upon reaching 100% Perfection, a special shrine appears on the western side of Ginger Island (near the Tiger Slime grove). Interacting with it grants the **Statue of True Perfection**, which produces one Prismatic Shard per day.

### Golden Chicken

After achieving Perfection, Golden Chickens become available:
- Purchase from Marnie for 100,000g
- Lay Golden Eggs (sell for 500g, 625g with Rancher)
- Golden Eggs can be used in Mayonnaise Machine to make 3 Gold-Quality Mayonnaise
- Can also be incubated to hatch more Golden Chickens

### Other Benefits

- Access to the summit of the mountain (cutscene)
- Special dialogue from villagers
- Sense of ultimate accomplishment

## Tips for Efficient Perfection Grinding

### Early Game Priorities

1. **Unlock the Greenhouse** (Community Center or Joja) for year-round crop growth
2. **Build friendships early** - Talk to everyone daily, give loved gifts
3. **Watch Queen of Sauce** every Monday and Sunday
4. **Donate to Museum** as you find items
5. **Complete Adventurer's Guild** goals passively while mining

### Mid Game Focus

1. **Unlock Ginger Island** as soon as possible (repair Willy's boat)
2. **Collect Golden Walnuts** systematically (use parrot hints)
3. **Maximize all skills** through efficient training
4. **Cook all recipes** - Keep track of what you're missing
5. **Craft everything** - Check crafting menu for missing items

### Late Game Optimization

1. **Farm gold efficiently** - Ancient Fruit wine, Starfruit with Casks
2. **Catch missing fish** - Check schedules and locations carefully
3. **Complete Monster Eradication** - Focus on remaining goals
4. **Purchase Golden Clock** - Save 10,000,000g
5. **Check Perfection Tracker** regularly in Qi's Walnut Room

### Time-Saving Strategies

| Strategy | Benefit |
|----------|---------|
| Use Warp Totems | Save time traveling |
| Eat speed food | Move faster |
| Use Staircases in Skull Cavern | Reach lower levels quickly |
| Check TV daily | Never miss Queen of Sauce |
| Use Statue of Uncertainty | Change professions if needed |
| Build multiple barns/coops | More friendship opportunities |

### Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Forgetting to check the Traveling Cart for rare items
- Not watching Queen of Sauce reruns (Wednesday)
- Missing legendary fish windows
- Not befriending the Dwarf (need to donate 4 items to Museum first)
- Forgetting to check the Secret Woods for the Sweet Gem Berry Stardrop
- Not completing special orders for unique recipes
