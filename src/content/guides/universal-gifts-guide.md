# Complete Guide to Universal Gift Preferences in Stardew Valley

## TL;DR

Gift-giving is essential for building NPC relationships in Stardew Valley. Loved gifts give +80 friendship points, liked gifts give +45, and birthday gifts have an 8x multiplier. The Rabbit's Foot is the closest universal loved gift, loved by all 12 marriage candidates and most NPCs. Universal liked gifts include Cookies, Chocolate Cake, Pizza, Bread, and most fruits/vegetables. Never give trash items, algae, or bug meat - these are universally disliked or hated. Each NPC can receive 2 gifts per week and 1 per day. The best strategy is giving loved gifts on birthdays for maximum friendship boost (640 points for loved birthday gift).

One of the most important aspects of Stardew Valley is building relationships with NPCs through gift-giving. While each NPC has unique preferences, there are certain gifts that have universal reactions across all (or most) characters. This guide provides a complete reference of universal gift preferences, helping you choose safe and effective gifts for any NPC.

## What Are the Gift Reaction Tiers?

### How Much Friendship Does Each Gift Give?

When you give a gift to an NPC, their reaction falls into one of six tiers:

| Tier | Friendship Points | Reaction |
|------|------------------|----------|
| Loved | +80 points | Hearts and sparkle animation |
| Liked | +45 points | Small hearts animation |
| Neutral | +20 points | Nod or slight smile |
| Disliked | -20 points | Frown or head shake |
| Hated | -40 points | Angry animation, green tint |
| Birthday Bonus | 8x multiplier | Applied to the base reaction |

### What Are the Gift Rules?

- **One gift per NPC per day**: You can give each NPC one gift per day.
- **Two gifts per week**: Each villager can receive a maximum of 2 gifts per week (best gift counts fully, second gift gives reduced points).
- **No duplicates**: Giving the same item twice in a week (to the same NPC) reduces the friendship bonus after the first gift.
- **Birthday bonus**: Giving a gift on an NPC's birthday multiplies the friendship points by 8.
- **No gifting during events**: You cannot give gifts during certain festival events.

## What is the Best Gift-Giving Strategy?

### What Should I Do Daily for Gifts?

For optimal friendship building:

1. **Give One Loved Gift Per Day**: Focus on one NPC and give them their loved gift every day.
2. **Rotate NPCs**: Give loved gifts to different NPCs on different days to build broad friendships.
3. **Birthday Priority**: On an NPC's birthday, give them their most-loved gift for the 8x bonus.
4. **Avoid Dislikes**: Never give a disliked or hated gift to any NPC.

## What Gift-Giving Mistakes Should I Avoid?

### Common Beginner Mistakes

1. **Giving Hated Items**: Always check preferences before giving gifts. Hated items lose 40 friendship points.
2. **Giving Disliked Items**: Even disliked items lose 20 points. Avoid them.
3. **Forgetting Birthdays**: Missing a birthday means missing the 8x bonus.
4. **Giving the Same Gift Repeatedly**: While you can give the same type of gift daily, variety is appreciated.
5. **Ignoring Universal Gifts**: When in doubt, use universal safe gifts.

### Advanced Mistakes

1. **Not Stockpiling Birthday Gifts**: Prepare birthday gifts months in advance.
2. **Wasting Loved Gifts on Non-Birthdays**: Save your best gifts for birthdays.
3. **Giving Gifts to Jealous Spouses**: Avoid giving gifts to other marriage candidates after marriage.
4. **Not Tracking Preferences**: Use a reference guide or mod to track NPC preferences.
5. **Ignoring Seasonal Availability**: Plan gift-giving around seasonal item availability.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is there a gift that EVERYONE loves?**
No single gift is loved by literally every NPC. However, Rabbit's Foot comes closest, being loved by all marriage candidates and most other NPCs.

**What happens if I give a hated gift?**
You lose 40 friendship points with that NPC. Avoid hated gifts at all costs.

**Can I give the same gift every day?**
You can give the same type of gift every day, but giving the exact same item within a week provides reduced friendship points. It is best to rotate between loved gifts.

**Does the birthday bonus stack with loved gifts?**
Yes, a loved gift on a birthday gives 80 x 8 = 640 friendship points. This is the single largest friendship boost available in the game.

**Do universal gifts work on Krobus and the Wizard?**
Yes, but Krobus and the Wizard have unique preferences. Check their specific gift guides for the best results.
