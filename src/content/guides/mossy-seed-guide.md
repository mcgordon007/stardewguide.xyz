# Stardew Valley Mossy Seed Guide

## Overview

**Mossy Seeds** were introduced in **Stardew Valley 1.6**. When planted, they grow into trees that are permanently covered in **Moss**, providing a reliable and renewable source of this crafting material.

## How to Obtain Mossy Seeds

Mossy Seeds can be obtained from the following sources:

- **Moss**: Harvesting Moss from trees in the wild or on your farm has a chance to yield a Mossy Seed.
- **Green Rain Event**: During the rare "Green Rain" weather event, many trees become covered in Moss, providing abundant harvesting opportunities and potential seeds.
- **Tree Stumps**: Moss-covered tree stumps may occasionally produce Mossy Seeds.

## How to Grow a Moss-Covered Tree

1. Select the Mossy Seed in your inventory.
2. Plant it on your farm in an empty tile, similar to planting regular tree seeds.
3. The tree will grow through **5 stages** over time.
4. Once fully grown, the tree will be permanently covered in **Moss**.
5. Moss can be harvested from the tree repeatedly without chopping it down.

## Moss-Covered Tree Behavior

- **Seasons**: Moss-covered trees grow in **all seasons**, including Winter.
- **Moss Harvesting**: Once fully grown, the tree produces Moss that can be harvested. The Moss regrows over time.
- **No Spreading**: Unlike Mushroom Trees, Moss-covered trees do **not** spread to adjacent tiles.
- **Tappers**: You can place a Tapper on a Moss-covered tree, but it will produce the same products as the base tree type (e.g., Maple Syrup for Maple Trees).

## Uses for Moss

Moss is used in the following crafting recipes and activities:

| Recipe | Materials | Source |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| Moss Soup | 20 Moss | Cooking (TV) |
| Speed-Gro | 1 Moss, 1 Pine Tar | Pierre's Shop or Crafting |
| Deluxe Speed-Gro | 1 Oak Resin, 1 Moss, 1 Coral | Pierre's Shop or Crafting |
| Tree Fertilizer | 5 Fiber, 5 Stone | Foraging Level 7 |
| Various 1.6 Crafting Recipes | Moss + other materials | Various |

## Tips

- Plant multiple Mossy Seeds to create a dedicated Moss farm.
- Moss is needed in significant quantities for new 1.6 crafting recipes.
- During Green Rain, prioritize harvesting Moss from wild trees for a large one-time bounty.
- Moss-covered trees are an excellent long-term investment for passive Moss generation.

## Version History

- **Moss** and **Mossy Seeds** were introduced in **Stardew Valley 1.6** (March 2024).
