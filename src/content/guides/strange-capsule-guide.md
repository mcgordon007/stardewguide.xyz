# Stardew Valley Strange Capsule Guide

## Overview

The **Strange Capsule** is one of the rarest random events in Stardew Valley. It is an eerie, alien-like artifact that can appear on your farm under very specific conditions, adding a touch of mystery to your farming life.

## How to Trigger the Event

### Spawn Conditions

- **Farm Type**: Can occur on any farm type.
- **Year Requirement**: The event can only happen **after Year 1** (starting from Year 2, Spring 1).
- **Chance**: There is a **1% chance** each night for the event to occur, provided the conditions are met.
- **Tile Requirement**: The game will attempt to place the Strange Capsule on a valid empty tile on your farm. If no valid tile is found, the event will not occur.

### What Happens

When the event triggers, you will receive the following message in the morning:

> "A strange sound was heard in the night..."

Upon investigating your farm, you will find a large, pulsating **Strange Capsule** somewhere on your property.

## The Strange Capsule Item

- The capsule is a **decorative item** that can be picked up using a tool (like a pickaxe or axe).
- It can be placed on your farm as a unique decoration.
- The capsule emits a faint glow and has an otherworldly appearance.

## Transformation: Broken Glass Hoe

After approximately **3 days**, the Strange Capsule will undergo a transformation:

- The capsule will break open.
- It becomes the **Broken Glass Hoe**, a unique decorative item.
- The Broken Glass Hoe resembles a shattered glass version of the basic hoe and serves as a rare trophy of this event.

## Tips for Players

1. **Patience is Key**: With only a 1% chance per night, this event is extremely rare. Do not expect it to happen immediately.
2. **Clear Your Farm**: Keep empty tiles available on your farm to increase the chance that the game can place the capsule.
3. **Year 2+ Focus**: Since it cannot happen in Year 1, focus on other goals in your first year.
4. **Check Every Morning**: After Year 1, make a habit of checking your farm in the morning for any unusual items.

## Trivia

- The Strange Capsule is one of several rare random events in Stardew Valley, alongside the Stone Owl and Crop Fairy.
- The event is purely cosmetic and does not affect gameplay mechanics or farm productivity.
- The Broken Glass Hoe cannot be used as a tool; it is strictly a decorative item.
