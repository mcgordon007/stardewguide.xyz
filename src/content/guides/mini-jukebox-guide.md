# Stardew Valley Mini-Jukebox Guide

The Mini-Jukebox is a furniture item in Stardew Valley that players can use to play in-game music.

## Basic Information

- **Type**: Furniture
- **Source**: Event reward

## How to Obtain

After reaching 5 hearts friendship with Gus, exit the farmhouse door between 6:00 AM and 11:30 AM on a non-rainy day to trigger a related event, where Gus will gift you the Mini-Jukebox and its crafting recipe.

## How to Use

- Place the Mini-Jukebox indoors
- Interact with it to play various in-game music tracks
- Can switch or stop playback at any time

## Features

- Can play various background music from Stardew Valley
- Adds atmosphere to the farmhouse or other buildings
- Purely decorative function, does not affect gameplay

## Tips

- Increasing friendship with Gus is the key to obtaining the Mini-Jukebox
- Remember to exit the farmhouse during the morning time slot on non-rainy days to trigger the event
- The Mini-Jukebox can be placed inside any building on the farm
