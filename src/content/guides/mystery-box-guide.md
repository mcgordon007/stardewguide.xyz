# Stardew Valley Mystery Box Guide

Mystery Boxes are a new loot system introduced in **version 1.6**, dropped by Mr. Qi. They function similarly to Geodes but contain a wide variety of items including rare and valuable rewards.

---

## How to Unlock Mystery Boxes

Mystery Boxes become available after **one of these conditions** is met:
- Spend 50 days in the valley
- Collect the second Prize Ticket from the billboard

After meeting either condition, you'll see a cutscene with Mr. Qi and receive a popup notification that Mystery Boxes can now be found.

---

## How to Obtain Mystery Boxes

### Regular Sources (Before Foraging Mastery)

| Activity | Chance |
|----------|--------|
| Regular Fishing Treasure Chests | 9-17% (based on daily luck) |
| Golden Fishing Treasure Chests | 12-21% (based on daily luck) |
| Panning | 5% |
| Shaking trees with seeds | 3% |
| Monster drops | 0.01 + (Daily Luck / 10) + (Luck Buffs / 125) |
| Breaking containers in Mines/Skull Cavern/Volcano | 0.0081 + (Daily Luck / 15) |
| Chopping trees (each axe hit) | 0.5% |
| Present train cars in Winter | 2% |
| Digging Artifact Spots | 0-11.25% (based on daily luck) |

### Special Sources (Always Available)

- Purchasing from the Calico Egg Merchant at the Desert Festival
- Trout Derby and SquidFest festival rewards
- Trading a Book of Mysteries with the Bookseller
- Opening Skull Cavern treasure chests (3.6% chance)
- Completing Raccoon requests beyond the fifth one
- Completing the Winter Star Bundle (Remixed)
- Using the prize machine in Mayor's Manor
- Chopping a giant Qi Fruit (when Qi's Crop quest is NOT active)
- Placing Milk or Cookies on a table during Winter 24th night

### Golden Mystery Boxes

After claiming **Foraging Mastery**, regular Mystery Box attempts have a chance to become **Golden Mystery Boxes** instead:
- 66% success rate without Book of Mysteries power
- 88% success rate with Book of Mysteries power

Golden Mystery Boxes contain all the same items as regular boxes plus exclusive rewards.

---

## How to Open Mystery Boxes

Take Mystery Boxes to **Clint at the Blacksmith** to open them for **25g each**, just like Geodes. 

**Note:** Unlike Geodes, Mystery Boxes **cannot** be opened with a Geode Crusher.

## Key Tips & Strategies

1. **Open 10 Boxes First** - Many rare rewards are locked until you've opened more than 10 boxes total
2. **Get the Book of Mysteries** - Increases your Mystery Box spawn rate from 66% to 88%
3. **High Luck Days** - Fishing treasure chests and artifact spots have better rates on high-luck days
4. **Trade Up** - After completing the 6th Raccoon request and claiming Foraging Mastery, trade 3 Mystery Boxes for 1 Golden Mystery Box at the Giant Stump
5. **Trade for Magic Rock Candy** - 20 Golden Mystery Boxes can be traded for 1 Magic Rock Candy at the Giant Stump
6. **Bundle Requirement** - 5 Mystery Boxes are needed for the Helper's Bundle (Remixed Bulletin Board)
