# Spring Season Guide

Spring is your first season on the farm and the foundation for everything that follows. This guide covers all aspects of Spring: foraging, fishing, festivals, crop strategy, social priorities, and a week-by-week plan to maximize your first season.

## TL;DR

Spring priorities: plant parsnips on Day 1, buy **Strawberry Seeds** at the Egg Festival (Spring 13) for the best seasonal profit, forage Wild Horseradish for the Community Center bundle, fish whenever possible, and socialize with villagers. Two festivals: **Egg Festival** (Spring 13) for strawberry seeds, **Flower Dance** (Spring 24) for friendship with your dance partner. Key forageables: Leek (60g), Morel (150g), Wild Horseradish (50g).

### Best Foraging Strategy

- **Days 1-14**: Collect Wild Horseradish (for CC bundle), Leeks (good gold), and Dandelions daily
- **Days 15-18**: Salmonberry Season -- grab every berry for free foraging XP and energy
- **After Earthquake (Day 3, Year 1)**: Railroad unlocks, adding more forage spawn points
- **Secret Woods (needs steel axe)**: Morel (150g) and Common Mushroom are abundant here

### Fishing Tips for Spring

- **Rain days are golden**: Catfish (200g), Eel (85g), Shad (60g), and the legendary **Legend** (5,000g) only appear in rain
- **Ocean is easiest**: Anchovy, Herring, and Sardine are great for quick fishing XP
- **Save Rain Totems**: Use them on Spring to catch rare rain-only fish
- **Fishing Level 2**: Buy the Fiberglass Rod from Willy (1,800g) to unlock bait usage
- **Community Center**: Ocean Fish bundle needs Sardine + Tuna (wait for Summer) + Pufferfish (wait for Summer); River Fish needs Catfish + Shad + Sunfish (Walleye is Fall/Winter)

## Spring Festivals

### Egg Festival (Spring 13)

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Time** | 9:00 AM - 2:00 PM |
| **Location** | Pelican Town Square |
| **Activities** | Social + Egg Hunt |

**Egg Hunt Rules:**
- 50 seconds to find at least 9 colored eggs (fewer in multiplayer)
- **First win reward**: Straw Hat
- **Repeat win reward**: Prize Ticket (changed in 1.6)
- Default winner if you lose: Abigail always wins

**Strawberry Seeds -- THE most important purchase:**

| Item | Price | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| **Strawberry Seeds** | **100g each** | **Buy as many as possible!** Only available here and Traveling Cart |

Strawberry Seeds planted on Spring 13 yield 2 harvests (Day 21 and Day 25), making them the best gold/day crop at 11.67g/day. Save seeds from Year 1 harvests for Year 2 -- planting on Day 1 with saved seeds yields 5 harvests for 20.83g/day.

### Flower Dance (Spring 24)

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Time** | 9:00 AM - 2:00 PM |
| **Location** | Far west of Cindersap Forest (festival-only area) |
| **Requirement** | 4 hearts (1,000 friendship) with dance partner |

**Dance Mechanics:**
- Talk to a bachelor/bachelorette 2 times until the dance invitation appears
- Dancing gives **+250 friendship** (1 heart)
- If you don't dance, default pairs form (Abigail + Sebastian, Haley + Alex, etc.)
- Available at Pierre's shop: **Rarecrow #5** (2,500g)

## Week-by-Week Spring Plan

### Week 1 (Spring 1-7)

- **Day 1**: Clear some farm space, plant 15 Parsnip Seeds (free gift), introduce yourself to villagers
- **Day 2**: Water parsnips, forage, fish at the ocean for easy XP
- **Day 3**: Earthquake unlocks the Railroad. Keep foraging and fishing
- **Day 4**: Kent's birthday. Water crops, continue clearing farm
- **Day 5-6**: Buy Potatoes (50g, 240g sell, 6 days) -- best single-harvest early profit. Plant more parsnips if you have space
- **Day 7**: Lewis's birthday. Parsnips harvest. Plant more crops

### Week 2 (Spring 8-14)

- **Day 8-10**: Plant Cauliflower (150g seed, 525g sell, 12 days) if you can afford it. Continue foraging
- **Day 11-12**: Prepare for Egg Festival. Ensure you have enough gold for strawberry seeds
- **Day 13**: **Egg Festival**. Buy as many Strawberry Seeds as you can afford. Plant them immediately after the festival
- **Day 14**: Haley's birthday. Water strawberries and remaining crops

### Week 3 (Spring 15-21)

- **Day 15-18**: **Salmonberry Season**. Pick berries from every bush for free XP and energy
- **Day 16-17**: Plant a second round of Potatoes if desired
- **Day 18**: Pam's birthday. Parsnip/Potato harvest if planted. Plant new crops
- **Day 20**: Shane's birthday
- **Day 21**: First Strawberry harvest! Save seeds from at least some berries

### Week 4 (Spring 22-28)

- **Day 24**: **Flower Dance**. Dance with whoever has 4+ hearts for +250 friendship
- **Day 25**: Second Strawberry harvest
- **Day 26**: Pierre's birthday
- **Day 27**: Emily's birthday
- **Day 28**: Last day of Spring. Prepare for Summer: buy Melon and Blueberry seeds if available

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Should I plant potatoes or cauliflower in Spring?

Potatoes are safer for early game (50g seed, 240g sell, 6 days). Cauliflower has higher per-harvest profit (150g seed, 525g sell, 12 days) but more risk if crops fail. Plant potatoes early, cauliflower mid-season.

### How many strawberry seeds should I buy?

Buy as many as you can afford. At minimum, buy 15-20 for a good return. If you have 5,000g+, buy 50. Save some harvested strawberries to turn into seeds with the Seed Maker for Year 2.

### When does it rain in Spring?

Rain is semi-random. Check the TV weather channel each morning. If rain is forecast, plan to fish for Catfish, Eel, and Shad. Use Rain Totems if you have them.

### Can I complete the Spring Foraging Bundle in Year 1?

Yes. Wild Horseradish spawns commonly in Cindersap Forest, Backwoods, and the Secret Woods (once unlocked). You only need one for the bundle.
