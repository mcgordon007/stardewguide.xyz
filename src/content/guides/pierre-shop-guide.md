# Stardew Valley Pierre's General Store Guide: Complete Inventory

Pierre's General Store is the primary shop for farming supplies in Stardew Valley. From seeds to fertilizers to backpack upgrades, Pierre provides essential items for your farming journey. This comprehensive guide covers everything available at Pierre's store, organized by category and season.

- Across from the Saloon
- Easy to access

## Fertilizers

### Basic Fertilizer

**Cost**: 100g
**Crafting**: Sap (2)
**Effect**: Increases quality chance by 1 level

**Quality Improvement**:
- Normal crops have chance to be silver quality
- Silver crops have chance to be gold quality
- Must be applied before planting

### Quality Fertilizer

**Cost**: 150g (Year 2+)
**Crafting**: Sap (4), Fish (1)
**Effect**: Increases quality chance by 2 levels

**Quality Improvement**:
- Better quality chances than Basic
- More likely to get gold quality
- Year 2+ purchase available

### Deluxe Fertilizer

**Cost**: 200g (Year 2+)
**Crafting**: Sap (6), Fish (2), Iridium Ore (1)
**Effect**: Maximum quality improvement

**Quality Improvement**:
- Best quality chances
- Can produce iridium quality crops
- Year 2+ purchase available

### Speed-Gro

**Cost**: 100g
**Crafting**: Pine Tar (1), Oak Resin (1), Clay (1)
**Effect**: 10% faster crop growth

**Growth Reduction**:
- Reduces growth time by 10%
- Example: 10-day crop becomes 9 days
- Useful for quick harvests

### Deluxe Speed-Gro

**Cost**: 150g (Year 2+)
**Crafting**: Pine Tar (1), Oak Resin (1), Clay (1), Slime (1)
**Effect**: 25% faster crop growth

**Growth Reduction**:
- Reduces growth time by 25%
- Example: 10-day crop becomes 7-8 days
- Year 2+ purchase available

### Hyper Speed-Gro

**Cost**: 200g (Year 2+)
**Crafting**: Pine Tar (1), Oak Resin (1), Clay (1), Slime (2), Iridium Ore (1)
**Effect**: 33% faster crop growth

**Growth Reduction**:
- Reduces growth time by 33%
- Maximum speed boost
- Year 2+ purchase available

## Backpack Upgrades

### First Upgrade

**Cost**: 2,000g
**Effect**: Increases inventory from 12 to 24 slots

**Benefits**:
- Double inventory space
- Carry more items
- Essential early purchase
- Recommended first purchase

### Second Upgrade

**Cost**: 10,000g
**Effect**: Increases inventory from 24 to 36 slots

**Benefits**:
- Triple inventory space
- Maximum capacity
- Essential for late game
- Recommended second purchase

### Upgrade Priority

**Recommended Order**:
1. First backpack upgrade (2,000g) - Essential
2. Second backpack upgrade (10,000g) - Important
3. Seeds and supplies - Ongoing

## Conclusion

Pierre's General Store is your primary source for farming supplies in Stardew Valley. From seasonal seeds to fertilizers to backpack upgrades, Pierre provides everything you need for successful farming.

Prioritize backpack upgrades early, then focus on seeds and fertilizers for your crops. Pierre's prices are generally better than Joja Mart, and supporting his store helps the Community Center storyline. Visit Pierre regularly for supplies and check his special stock on Fridays and Saturdays for rare items.
