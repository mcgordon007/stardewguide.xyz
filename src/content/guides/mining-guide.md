# Complete Mining Guide

![Mining](/images/mining-illustration.jpg)

## TL;DR

Mining is essential for gathering ores, gems, and resources in Stardew Valley. The Mines have 120 levels divided into three themes: Earth (1-39, Copper), Frozen (40-79, Iron), and Lava (80-119, Gold). The Skull Cavern in the Calico Desert has infinite levels and is the primary source of Iridium Ore. Level 120 contains the Skull Key, which unlocks Skull Cavern access. The Miner -> Blacksmith profession path provides +1 ore per vein and 50% bonus to bar sell prices. Staircases (99 Stone each) and bombs are essential for deep Skull Cavern runs. Daily luck affects ladder spawn rates significantly.

Mining is essential for gathering ores, gems, and other resources used in crafting, building, and gifting. This guide covers The Mines (levels 1-120), the Skull Cavern, the Volcano Dungeon, geodes, ore distribution, and strategies for efficient mining.

## What is The Mines Overview?

The Mines are located in the northeast corner of the map, north of the mountain lake. They consist of 120 levels divided into distinct themes. You can enter the mines at any time and leave through the ladder on any floor you have previously reached.

### What is at Level 120?

Level 120 is the bottom of the mines. There is **no boss fight**. Instead, you will find a chest containing the **Skull Key**, which unlocks the entrance to the Skull Cavern in the Calico Desert. Simply open the chest to obtain it.

## How Do Ladders Work in The Mines?

To descend through the mines, you need to find ladders. Ladders appear when you:

1. **Break rocks** - Each rock has a small chance to reveal a ladder (approximately 2%)
2. **Kill monsters** - Each monster killed has a chance to spawn a ladder (approximately 15%)
3. **Clear all rocks on a floor** - If you break every rock, the next rock or monster kill guarantees a ladder
4. **Use a Staircase** - Crafted from 99 stone (Mining Level 2), instantly descends one level
5. **Use a Warp Totem: Mine** - Teleports you to the mine entrance

### What Affects Ladder Chance?

| Modifier | Effect |
|----------|--------|
| Daily Luck (high) | Increases ladder chance |
| Daily Luck (low) | Decreases ladder chance |
| Food buffs | Some foods increase luck |
| Ladder Forming Talisman | Guarantees ladder after killing a monster |

## How to Navigate Skull Cavern Effectively?

The Skull Cavern is located in the Calico Desert, accessible after repairing the bus stop and obtaining the Skull Key from mine level 120. Unlike The Mines, the Skull Cavern has no fixed bottom.

### What Are the Skull Cavern Key Features?

- **Levels are infinite** and randomly generated
- **Difficulty increases with depth** - deeper levels have tougher monsters and better rewards
- **Iridium Ore** is the primary target, found in nodes at all levels of the Skull Cavern
- **Prismatic Shards** can be found in Omni Geodes and as rare drops
- **Time-limited** - you can only stay until 2:00 AM, then you pass out
- **Skull Cavern does not reset** - you keep your progress each day

### What is the Best Skull Cavern Strategy?

1. **Bring 50-100 Staircases** (crafted from 99 stone each) to skip unproductive floors
2. **Use bombs** (Cherry Bomb, Mega Bomb) to quickly clear rock-filled floors and find ladders
3. **Eat food that boosts luck** - Lucky Lunch, Special Charm, or high-luck TV forecast
4. **Use the Iridium Band ring** for its chance to instantly kill monsters
5. **Use the Galaxy Sword** or other powerful weapons for combat
6. **Focus on descending** rather than exploring each floor thoroughly
7. **Bring food** - Spicy Eel (speed + defense), Crab Cakes (fishing + defense), or Espresso (speed)
8. **Check daily luck** on the TV before going. Higher luck means more ladders and better ore
9. **Kill Serpents quickly** - they can poison you and deal significant damage
10. **Save stairs for floors with few rocks** - use bombs and combat on rock-heavy floors

### What Rewards Can I Find in Skull Cavern?

## What is the Volcano Dungeon?

The Volcano Dungeon is found on Ginger Island (accessible after completing the Community Center or Joja route and repairing Willy's boat with 5 Iridium Bars). It consists of 10 levels of fire-themed challenges.

### What Are the Volcano Dungeon Key Features?

- **Heat meter** - You have a heat gauge that rises over time. Stand near water pools or use the Dwarvish Heat Earring to cool down
- **Dragon Tooth** - Used to craft the Dragon Tooth Ring and other items
- **Magma Duggy** - A unique monster that tunnels underground and attacks from below
- **Reward** - Access to the Forge at level 10, where you can combine rings and enchant weapons

### What is the Best Volcano Dungeon Strategy?

- Bring the Dwarvish Heat Earring (found in a chest on level 5) to reduce heat buildup
- Use water pools to cool down when the heat meter gets high
- Bring food for energy and health restoration
- Dragon Teeth are used to craft the Dragon Tooth Ring (increases damage by 10%)

## What Geodes Can I Find?

Geodes are mineral-containing rocks found while mining or from breaking rocks at the quarry. They can be processed at the Blacksmith for 25g each.

| Geode Type | Location | Contents |
|-----------|----------|----------|
| Geode | Mines (any level) | Minerals (Quartz, Calcite, etc.), Earth Minerals, Artifacts |
| Frozen Geode | Mines (levels 40-79) | Minerals, Frozen Minerals (Frozen Tear, etc.), Artifacts |
| Magma Geode | Mines (levels 80-119) | Minerals, Magma Minerals (Fire Quartz, etc.), Artifacts |
| Omni Geode | Anywhere (rare) | Any mineral or artifact in the game |
| Artifact Trove | Dig spots, monster drops | Artifacts only |
| Golden Coconut | Ginger Island (field office) | Volcano items, Golden Animal Crackers, etc. |

### What is the Recommended Mining Profession Path?

**Miner -> Blacksmith** is generally considered the best path for players focused on ore and bar production. The extra ore per vein significantly increases your mining output. **Geologist -> Gemologist** is excellent for players who want to sell gems for profit.

## What is the Best Mining Strategy for Each Stage?

### Early Game (Levels 1-40)

- Bring food (foraged items) for energy
- Clear rocks systematically, working left to right
- Kill monsters for combat XP and potential ladder drops
- Collect copper ore for your first tool upgrades
- Leave when energy drops below 50

### Mid Game (Levels 40-80)

- Bring better food (cooked dishes) for energy
- Use the elevator to return to specific floors
- Focus on iron ore for steel tool upgrades
- Start collecting geodes for minerals and artifacts
- Consider using bombs for faster clearing

### Late Game (Skull Cavern)

- Bring 50-100 staircases per run
- Use the best food available (Spicy Eel, Lucky Lunch)
- Equip the Iridium Band ring
- Use bombs and Galaxy Sword for combat
- Focus on descending to level 100+ for iridium
- Check daily luck before each run
