# Spirit's Eve Guide

## Overview

**Spirit's Eve** is a **Halloween-themed festival** that takes place every year on **Fall 27** in Pelican Town. The festival features a haunted maze, a decorative town square, and the opportunity to obtain the rare **Golden Pumpkin**.

## Festival Details

- **Date**: Fall 27
- **Time**: 10:00 PM to 11:50 PM
- **Location**: Pelican Town Square
- **Entry**: Enter Pelican Town between 10:00 PM and 11:50 PM to participate.

## The Haunted Maze

The main attraction of Spirit's Eve is the **haunted maze**:

### Layout
- The maze is set up in the town square with spooky decorations.
- It features dead ends, hidden paths, and atmospheric lighting.

### Golden Pumpkin
- At the end of the maze, players can find a **Golden Pumpkin**.
- The Golden Pumpkin is a valuable item that can be:
  - Sold for **2,500g**
  - Gifted to villagers (most love it)
  - Used as a decorative item

### Navigating the Maze
- Take the path to the **northwest** area of the maze.
- Look for a hidden passage through the hedges.
- Follow the path to the treasure chest containing the Golden Pumpkin.

## Other Festival Features

- **Decorations**: The town is decorated with pumpkins, cobwebs, and spooky lights.
- **NPCs**: Villagers are present in costume and have unique dialogue.
- **Photo Opportunity**: The decorated town makes for great screenshots.

## Tips

- The maze can be completed quickly once you know the path.
- Only **one Golden Pumpkin** can be obtained per year.
- The Golden Pumpkin is a universal loved gift (except for a few exceptions).
- If you miss the festival, you must wait until next Fall 27.
- The festival ends at midnight; leave before then or you will pass out.
