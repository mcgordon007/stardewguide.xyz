# Complete Pelican Town Guide

![Pelican Town](/images/location-pelican-town.jpg)

Pelican Town is the heart of Stardew Valley, a charming rural community where you will spend most of your time building relationships, shopping for supplies, and participating in local events. This comprehensive guide covers every building, shop, NPC home, and secret the town has to offer.

## Town Layout Overview

Pelican Town is divided into several distinct areas, each serving different purposes in your daily life:

| Area | Location | Key Features |
|------|----------|--------------|
| **Town Center** | Central area | Pierre's, Community Center, Mayor's House, Museum |
| **Eastern Side** | Right side of town | Blacksmith, JojaMart, Trailer, Bus Stop |
| **Western Side** | Left side of town | Beach path, Wizard's Tower access |
| **Northern Side** | Top of town | Carpenter's Shop, Mountain Lake, Mine entrance |
| **Southern Side** | Bottom of town | Path to your farm, Cindersap Forest |

## All Shops and Buildings

### Pierre's General Store

**Location:** Town Center (north of the town square)
**Hours:** 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM (closed Wednesdays)
**Owner:** Pierre

### Blacksmith

**Location:** Eastern side of town
**Hours:** 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM (closed Fridays and Saturdays)
**Owner:** Clint

The Blacksmith is essential for tool upgrades, geode processing, and ore refining.

| Service | Cost | Description |
|---------|------|-------------|
| **Tool Upgrade** | 2,000g - 25,000g + materials | Upgrade tools to Copper, Steel, Gold, or Iridium |
| **Geode Cracking** | 25g per geode | Open geodes to find minerals and artifacts |
| **Ore Smelting** | 20g + coal | Smelt ore into bars (if you lack a furnace) |
| **Coal Purchase** | 150g each | Buy coal for crafting and smelting |
| **Copper Ore** | 75g each | Purchase copper ore |
| **Iron Ore** | 150g each | Purchase iron ore |
| **Gold Ore** | 400g each | Purchase gold ore |

**Geode Types Processed:**
- Regular Geode (found in mines levels 1-39)
- Frozen Geode (found in mines levels 40-79)
- Magma Geode (found in mines levels 80-120)
- Omni Geode (found everywhere, contains rare items)

### Mayor's Manor

**Location:** Town Center (southeast, next to the Museum)
**Hours:** 8:30 AM - 12:00 PM, 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM (open most days)
**Resident:** Mayor Lewis

Mayor's Manor serves several important functions:

**Lost and Found Box:**
- Retrieves items left behind at festivals
- Recovers items from divorced spouses
- Holds items from abandoned quests

**Statue of Uncertainty:**
- Cost: 10,000g
- Effect: Allows you to change your chosen professions
- Can only change one skill's professions per use
- Available after completing the Community Center or Joja Warehouse

**Marriage Records:**
- View your current relationship status
- Access to divorce papers (50,000g)

## Hidden Areas and Secrets

### The Sewers

**Access:** Donate 60 items to the Museum to receive the Rusty Key from Gunther
**Resident:** Krobus
**Features:**
- **Krobus's Shop:** Sells rare items including Void Eggs, Stardrops, and unique furniture
- **Mutant Carp:** Legendary fish can be caught here
- **Statue of Uncertainty:** Change professions (also available at Mayor's Manor after completion)

### Secret Woods

**Access:** Chop the large log blocking the entrance with a Steel Axe or better
**Location:** Northwest corner of Cindersap Forest
**Features:**
- 6 Large Stumps that respawn daily (12 Hardwood per day)
- Unique forageables: Fiddlehead Fern, Morel
- Slime spawning
- Old Master Cannoli statue (gives Stardrop when given Sweet Gem Berry)

### The Witch's Swamp

**Access:** Complete the "Dark Talisman" quest (triggered after completing the Community Center and reaching Railroad)
**Features:**
- Witch's Hut with three shrines
- Henchman guarding the hut (requires Void Mayonnaise to pass)
- Legendary fish: Mutant Carp (different from Sewers)

### The Summit

**Access:** Achieve Perfection (complete all perfection criteria)
**Location:** Top of the mountain, past the Railroad
**Features:**
- Cutscene with Grandpa
- Rock with perfect statue
- Beautiful view of the valley

### Hidden Path to the Beach

**Location:** South of town, requires 300 Wood to repair the bridge on your farm
**Shortcut:** After repairing the bridge on your farm, you can access the beach quickly from the farm rather than walking through town.

Completing the Community Center bundles transforms Pelican Town in several ways:

### Immediate Effects

|-------------|-------------------|

After completing all bundles, the Community Center is fully restored:

| Feature | Effect |
|---------|--------|

| **JojaMart** | Closes down (if not completed Joja route) |

## Tips for Navigating Pelican Town

1. **Learn Shop Hours:** Pierre's is closed Wednesdays; Blacksmith is closed Fridays and Saturdays
2. **Check the Saloon:** Most villagers visit the Saloon in the evening - great for socializing
3. **Use the Minecarts:** After completing the Boiler Room bundles, minecarts provide fast travel
4. **Visit on Sunny Days:** More villagers are outside on sunny days, making them easier to find
5. **Bring Gifts:** Keep a stack of loved gifts when visiting town to maximize friendship gains
6. **Watch for Events:** Town events change schedules and provide unique opportunities
7. **Check the Bulletin Board:** New quests appear regularly outside Pierre's store
