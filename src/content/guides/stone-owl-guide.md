# Stardew Valley Stone Owl Guide

## Overview

The **Stone Owl** is a rare random event in Stardew Valley that places a mysterious stone statue of an owl on your farm. It is a purely decorative item and one of the most sought-after rare occurrences by collectors.

## How to Trigger the Event

### Spawn Conditions

- **Farm Type**: Can occur on any farm type.
- **Chance**: There is a **1% chance** each night for the event to trigger.
- **Tile Requirement**: The game selects a random valid tile on your farm. If the tile is occupied or invalid, the event will not occur.

### What Happens

When the event triggers, you will see the following message in the morning:

> "A strange sound was heard in the night..."

You will find a **Stone Owl** statue placed somewhere on your farm.

## The Stone Owl Item

- The Stone Owl is a **decorative furniture item**.
- It can be picked up and placed anywhere on your farm or in your house.
- It has a unique, mystical appearance that makes it a great addition to any farm aesthetic.

## Differences from the Stone Owl Statue (Night Market)

Do not confuse the random event Stone Owl with the **Stone Owl Statue** sold at the **Night Market** for 500g. They are different items:

| Feature | Random Event Stone Owl | Night Market Stone Owl Statue |
|---------|------------------------|-------------------------------|
| Acquisition | Random 1% night event | Purchased at Night Market |
| Appearance | Slightly different pose | Standard statue pose |
| Rarity | Very rare | Available annually |

## Tips for Players

1. **Leave Space**: Ensure there are empty tiles on your farm for the owl to spawn.
2. **Daily Checks**: After waking up, scan your farm for any new items.
3. **Multiple Owls**: It is possible to receive multiple Stone Owls over the course of multiple years.
4. **Display It**: Place the Stone Owl in a prominent location to show off this rare find to friends in multiplayer.

## Trivia

- The Stone Owl event was added in Stardew Valley version 1.5.
- Like the Strange Capsule, the Stone Owl is purely cosmetic and has no functional gameplay benefits.
- The owl's glowing eyes at night add a spooky atmosphere to your farm.
