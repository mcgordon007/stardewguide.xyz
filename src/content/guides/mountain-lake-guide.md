# Mountain Lake Guide

## Overview

The **Mountain Lake** area is located **north of Pelican Town** and is one of the most important regions in Stardew Valley. It contains key locations including the entrance to the Mines, the Quarry, Robin's Carpenter Shop, and Linus's tent.

## Key Locations

### The Mines
- Located in the northeast part of the mountain.
- Contains 120 floors with increasing difficulty.
- Source of ores (copper, iron, gold, iridium), gems, and geodes.
- Reaching floor 120 unlocks the Skull Cavern in the Calico Desert.
- The Mines reset daily, allowing repeated farming of early floors.

### Quarry
- Unlocked after completing the **Bridge Repair** bundle (Crafts Room) or Joja equivalent.
- Contains large rocks, ore nodes, and gem nodes.
- Rocks and nodes respawn daily.
- A great source of stone, ore, and coal.

### Robin's Carpenter Shop
- Open most days from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM (closed Tuesdays).
- Robin sells building upgrades, farm buildings, and furniture.
- Key purchases include:
  - House upgrades (kitchen, nursery, cellar)
  - Farm buildings (barn, coop, silo, well, etc.)
  - Wood and stone (at a markup)

### Linus's Tent
- Home of **Linus**, a homeless villager who lives in a tent.
- Linus is a forager who loves nature.
- Befriending him unlocks unique recipes and dialogue.
- He teaches the player the Sashimi recipe at 3 hearts.

### The Adventurer's Guild
- Located east of the Mines entrance.
- Run by Marlon and Gil.
- Sells weapons, boots, and rings.
- Monster eradication goals reward unique items.

## Fishing

The Mountain Lake contains a variety of fish:
- **Largemouth Bass** (common)
- **Carp**
- **Sturgeon** (winter only, valuable for caviar)
- **Legend** (spring, rainy days only - one of the five legendary fish)

## Tips

- Visit the Mines as soon as possible to gather ore and upgrade tools.
- The Quarry is excellent for passive resource gathering.
- Bring gifts for Linus (he loves coconuts, cactus fruit, and yams).
- Check the Mountain Lake on rainy spring days to catch the Legend fish.
