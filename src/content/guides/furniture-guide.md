# Stardew Valley Furniture Guide: Complete Overview

Furniture in Stardew Valley allows you to decorate your farmhouse and create a personalized living space. From chairs and tables to lamps and rugs, furniture adds aesthetic appeal to your home. This comprehensive guide covers the furniture system, sources, and decoration tips.

## Overview of Furniture

### What is Furniture

Furniture items are:
- Decorative items for your farmhouse
- Cosmetic only (no gameplay benefits)
- Placeable in your home
- Customizable appearance

### Furniture Types

**Seating**:
- Chairs, armchairs, couches, stools
- Benches, dining chairs

**Tables**:
- Dining tables, coffee tables, end tables

**Storage**:
- Bookshelves, dressers, cabinets

**Lighting**:
- Floor lamps, table lamps, torches

**Decorations**:
- Rugs, house plants, paintings, posters
- Clocks, trophies, statues

**Wall Items**:
- Wallpaper, paintings, posters

**Floor Items**:
- Flooring, rugs

## Furniture Catalog Details

### What is the Catalog

The Furniture Catalog is a special item that:
- Provides unlimited furniture access
- Costs 200,000g one-time
- Available at Robin's shop
- Best furniture investment

### How to Use

**Using the Catalog**:
1. Purchase from Robin (200,000g)
2. Place catalog in your house
3. Interact with catalog
4. Select furniture to place
5. Furniture appears for free

### Catalog Benefits

**Advantages**:
- Unlimited furniture placement
- No ongoing costs
- All furniture available
- Easy decorating

**Value Analysis**:
- 200,000g investment
- Unlimited furniture
- Worth buying for extensive decorating
- Saves thousands in individual purchases

## Furniture Placement

### How to Place Furniture

**Placement Process**:
1. Select furniture in inventory
2. Approach desired location
3. Click to place
4. Furniture appears

### Placement Rules

**Valid Locations**:
- Inside farmhouse
- Inside upgraded house areas
- Cellar area
- Specific furniture slots

**Invalid Locations**:
- Outside farmhouse
- In other buildings
- On occupied tiles
- Blocking pathways

### Furniture Slots

Some furniture has designated slots:
- TV slot (fixed position)
- Bed slot (fixed position)
- Kitchen area (fixed)
- Free placement areas

### Moving Furniture

**How to Move**:
1. Click on placed furniture
2. Pick up furniture
3. Place in new location
4. Or store in inventory
