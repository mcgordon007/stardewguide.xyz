# Bus Stop Guide

## Overview

The **Bus Stop** is a location in the northeast corner of Stardew Valley, east of the Farm. Initially, the bus is broken down and cannot be used. Repairing it unlocks access to the **Calico Desert**, a late-game area containing the Skull Cavern, Oasis shop, and other unique resources.

## How to Unlock

There are two ways to repair the bus:

### 1. Community Center Route (Recommended)
- Complete the **Vault** room bundles in the Community Center.
- The Vault requires four money bundles:
  - 2,500g bundle
  - 5,000g bundle
  - 10,000g bundle
  - 25,000g bundle
- Total cost: **42,500g**
- Once completed, the Junimos repair the bus overnight.

### 2. Joja Community Development Form
- Purchase a **JojaMart Membership** for 5,000g from Morris.
- After completing all other Joja Community Development projects, pay **40,000g** for the Bus repair.
- The bus is repaired the next day.

## Using the Bus

- After repair, the bus operates from **10:00 AM to 5:00 PM**.
- Each trip to the Calico Desert costs **500g**.
- Pam is the bus driver (after her house is built in 1.4+).

## Calico Desert

Once unlocked, the desert provides access to:
- **Skull Cavern**: A dangerous mine with iridium ore and prismatic shards.
- **Oasis**: Sells rare seeds (like Rhubarb, Starfruit, and Beet seeds).
- **Sandy**: A villager who sells unique items.
- **Three Pillars**: Used to obtain the Galaxy Sword.
- **Desert Trader**: Trades goods for Omni Geodes and other items.

## Tips

- Save up gold early to complete the Vault bundles.
- The Skull Cavern is extremely dangerous; bring plenty of food and bombs.
- Starfruit seeds from the Oasis are highly profitable for wine-making.
