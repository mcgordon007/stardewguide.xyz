# Green Rain Guide

## Overview

**Green Rain** is a rare weather event introduced in **Stardew Valley 1.6**. It is a special type of rainfall that has unique effects on the environment, crops, and foraging. It can only occur after the player has unlocked certain late-game content.

## How It Occurs

- Green Rain is a **random rare weather event**.
- It can occur in any season after the player has progressed sufficiently in the game.
- The chance increases slightly in later years.

## Effects on Crops

- Crops affected by Green Rain may experience accelerated growth.
- Some crops may produce higher quality yields after the event.
- Wild seeds and forageables in the area may change.

## Moss Growth

- One of the primary features of Green Rain is the growth of **Moss**.
- Moss appears on trees and rocks throughout the valley.
- Moss is a new crafting material introduced in 1.6, used in various recipes and crafting items.
- Collect moss during and after the event for crafting purposes.

## Foraging Effects

- Unique forageables may appear during Green Rain.
- Mushroom spawn rates may increase in the forest areas.
- Check the Secret Woods and Cindersap Forest for special spawns.

## Tips

- Green Rain is rare, so take advantage of it when it occurs.
- Collect as much moss as possible during the event.
- Check all outdoor areas for special foraging opportunities.
- The event is safe and does not damage crops or structures.
