# Stardew Valley Oasis Shop Guide: Sandy's Desert Store

The Oasis is a shop located in the Calico Desert, operated by Sandy. It sells unique items unavailable elsewhere, including Starfruit Seeds and desert-specific goods. This comprehensive guide covers everything about the Oasis shop, from location to inventory to access requirements.

## Shop Overview

### What is the Oasis

The Oasis is a shop in the Calico Desert that sells unique seeds, furniture, and desert items. It's operated by Sandy, a friendly merchant who provides goods unavailable in Pelican Town.

### Location

The Oasis is located:
- Calico Desert
- North of the bus stop
- Near the entrance to Skull Cavern
- West side of desert

## Accessing the Desert

### Bus Repair Requirement

To access the desert, you need:

**Community Center Route**:
- Complete Vault Bundle (4,250g total)
- Pam becomes bus driver
- Bus operates daily

## Starfruit Guide

### Starfruit Value

**Base Value**: 750g
**Wine Value**: 2,250g (3x base)
**Iridium Wine**: 4,500g (6x base)

### Starfruit Strategy

**Best Practices**:
1. Plant in Summer (outdoors)
2. Plant in Greenhouse (year-round)
3. Plant on Ginger Island (year-round)
4. Process into wine for maximum profit

### Profit Calculation

**Raw Starfruit**:
- Seed cost: 400g
- Fruit value: 750g
- Profit: 350g per fruit

**Starfruit Wine**:
- Seed cost: 400g
- Wine value: 2,250g
- Profit: 1,850g per wine

**Iridium Starfruit Wine**:
- Seed cost: 400g
- Iridium wine: 4,500g
- Profit: 4,100g per wine

### Comparison with Other Crops

| Crop | Base Value | Wine Value | Iridium Wine |
|------|------------|------------|--------------|
| Starfruit | 750g | 2,250g | 4,500g |
| Ancient Fruit | 750g | 2,250g | 4,500g |
| Melon | 250g | 750g | 1,500g |
| Pumpkin | 320g | 960g | 1,920g |

**Conclusion**: Starfruit and Ancient Fruit are the most valuable crops.

## Rhubarb Guide

**Best Practices**:
- Buy seeds at Oasis in Spring
- Plant early in Spring
- Harvest before season ends
- Process or sell for profit

## Conclusion

The Oasis shop in the Calico Desert is your primary source for Starfruit Seeds, Rhubarb Seeds, and desert-themed items. Access the desert by repairing the bus through the Community Center or Joja route, then visit Sandy's shop for unique purchases.

Starfruit is the highest-value crop in Stardew Valley, making Oasis visits essential for profit maximization. Buy Starfruit Seeds for Summer planting or greenhouse use, and purchase Rhubarb Seeds for Spring crops unavailable at Pierre's. The Oasis provides items you can't find anywhere else, making desert trips worthwhile for serious farmers.
