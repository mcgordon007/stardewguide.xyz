# Stardew Valley Feast of the Winter Star Guide

## Overview

The **Feast of the Winter Star** is a heartwarming winter festival in *Stardew Valley* where the townsfolk gather in Pelican Town for a secret gift exchange, celebrating community and generosity.

## Date and Time

- **Date:** Winter 25
- **Time:** 9:00 AM – 2:00 PM
- **Entry:** Enter Pelican Town between 9:00 AM and 2:00 PM to participate

## Location

**Pelican Town Square** — The center of town is decorated with festive winter decorations, lights, and a large tree.

## How to Participate

1. Travel to Pelican Town during festival hours.
2. One week before the festival (Winter 18), you will receive a letter telling you who your secret gift recipient is.
3. Bring a gift that your recipient will love.
4. During the festival, give your gift to your assigned recipient.
5. Another villager will also give you a gift in return!

## Secret Gift Exchange Mechanics

### Before the Festival
- On **Winter 18**, check your mailbox for a letter revealing your secret gift recipient.
- The recipient is randomly selected from villagers you have met.
- Choose a gift that your recipient **loves** for maximum friendship gain.

### During the Festival
- Find your secret recipient in the town square.
- Give them the gift you prepared.
- You will receive a gift from another random villager.

## Friendship Boost

- Giving a **loved gift** to your secret recipient: **+500 friendship points**
- Giving a **liked gift**: +300 friendship points
- Giving a **neutral gift**: +100 friendship points
- Giving a **disliked/hated gift**: No bonus or penalty (but don't be a Grinch!)

## Possible Gifts You Can Receive

The gift you receive depends on who is assigned as your secret giver. Common gifts include:

- **Universal gifts:** Coal, Wood, Stone, or basic resources (from less wealthy villagers)
- **Quality items:** Artisan goods, cooked dishes, or foraged items
- **Rare items:** Sometimes you may receive something valuable or unique

## Activities

- Participate in the secret gift exchange
- Socialize with all villagers
- Enjoy the festive winter decorations
- Admire the large decorated tree in the town square

## Tips

- **Check the wiki:** Look up your recipient's loved gifts before the festival.
- **Plan ahead:** Stock up on universally loved items (like Prismatic Shard, Rabbit's Foot, or Pearl) as emergency gifts.
- **Easy loved gifts:** Most villagers love **Prismatic Shards**, **Rabbit's Foot**, **Pearl**, **Magic Rock Candy**, or **Golden Pumpkin**.
- **Personalized gifts:** If you know your recipient well, give them a specific loved item for extra thoughtfulness.
- **Talk to everyone:** Don't forget to chat with all villagers for standard friendship gains.

## Notes

- The festival ends automatically at 2:00 PM, and you return to the farm at 10:00 PM.
- Time is paused during the festival.
- You cannot participate in the gift exchange if you do not attend the festival.
- If you are married, your spouse may have special dialogue during this festival.
- The Feast of the Winter Star is the last major festival of the year before the New Year.
