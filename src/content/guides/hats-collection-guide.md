# Stardew Valley Hats Collection Guide: Complete Overview

Hats are cosmetic items in Stardew Valley that can be worn to customize your character's appearance. There are numerous hats available, obtained through achievements, festivals, shops, and special events. This comprehensive guide covers every hat in the game and how to obtain them.

## Overview of Hats

### What are Hats

Hats are wearable items that:
- Change your character's appearance
- Provide no gameplay benefits (cosmetic only)
- Can be worn with other equipment
- Are stored in the hat slot

### Hat Slot

The hat slot is separate from:
- Ring slots
- Boots slot
- Equipment slots
- Does not affect stats

### Hat Inventory

Hats are stored:
- In your inventory
- Can be placed in chests
- Can be sold (except unique ones)
- Can be worn anytime

## How to Collect All Hats

### Achievement Strategy

**Wealth Hats**:
1. Focus on earning gold
2. Sell crops, artisan goods, minerals
3. Progress through wealth achievements
4. Hats unlock automatically

**Museum Hat**:
1. Donate all artifacts and minerals
2. Check museum completion
3. Visit Gunther regularly
4. Hat unlocks at completion

**Farming Hats**:
1. Ship variety of crops (Polyculture)
2. Ship large quantities (Monoculture)
3. Both give Crop Hat variants

**Quest Hats**:
1. Complete quests from board
2. Help villagers with requests
3. Track quest completion
4. Hats unlock at milestones

**Combat Hats**:
1. Kill monsters in mines
2. Combat in Skull Cavern
3. Track kill count
4. Hats unlock at milestones

**Fishing Hats**:
1. Catch all fish types
2. Find treasures while fishing
3. Complete fish collection
4. Hats unlock at completion

**Mining Hat**:
1. Reach bottom of mines (level 120)
2. Complete mine exploration
3. Hat unlocks at bottom

**Summit Hat**:
1. Complete all achievements
2. Reach summit (100% completion)
3. Ultimate hat reward

### Festival Strategy

**Token Collection**:
1. Participate in festival games
2. Win competitions
3. Earn star tokens
4. Purchase hats at festival shops

**Participation Hats**:
1. Attend all festivals
2. Participate in events
3. Some hats are free
4. Collect participation rewards

### Shop Strategy

**Mouse Shop**:
1. Access Sewers (60 museum items)
2. Visit Mouse Shop
3. Purchase hats with gold
4. Collect sewer-exclusive hats

## Conclusion

Hats in Stardew Valley are cosmetic items that showcase your achievements and participation in events. Collect all hats by completing achievements, attending festivals, and visiting special shops. Each hat represents a milestone in your Stardew Valley journey.

Start with easy achievement hats like the Straw Hat and Cowboy Hat, then progress to harder achievements for unique hats. Visit festivals for special event hats, and check the Mouse Shop for sewer-exclusive options. Hat collection adds a fun cosmetic element to your gameplay experience.
