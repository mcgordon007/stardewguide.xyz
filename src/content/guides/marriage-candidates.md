# Marriage Candidates Guide

![NPC Portraits](/images/npc-portraits.jpg)

## TL;DR

Stardew Valley features 12 marriage candidates: 6 bachelors (Alex, Elliott, Harvey, Sam, Sebastian, Shane) and 6 bachelorettes (Abigail, Emily, Haley, Leah, Maru, Penny). Marriage requires 10 hearts friendship, all 5 heart events, a Bouquet (200g from Pierre), and a Mermaid's Pendant (5,000g from Old Mariner). Each spouse has unique loved gifts, daily schedules, and spouse perks like cooking breakfast or helping with farm chores. The Rabbit's Foot is the closest universal loved gift for all candidates. After marriage, spouses move into the farmhouse and can have up to two children.

Stardew Valley features 12 marriage candidates: 6 bachelors and 6 bachelorettes. Each has a unique personality, story, preferences, and daily schedule. This guide covers each candidate in extreme detail, including their complete gift preferences, heart event summaries, and what they do after marriage.

## What Are the Marriage Requirements?

Before diving into individual candidates, here are the universal requirements for marriage:

1. Reach **10 hearts** of friendship
2. See all **5 heart events** (2, 4, 6, 8, and 10 hearts)
3. Give them a **Bouquet** (purchased from Pierre's for 200g after reaching 8 hearts)
4. Give them a **Mermaid's Pendant** (purchased from the Old Mariner on the beach for 5,000g, available after 10 hearts and the beach bridge is repaired)

After marriage, your spouse moves into your farmhouse. You can have up to two children.

## What Happens After Marriage?

After marriage, your spouse will:

- **Move into your farmhouse** and add their personal room
- **Help with chores** - watering crops, feeding animals, repairing fences
- **Cook breakfast occasionally** - each spouse has a unique dish they may make
- **Give you a Stardrop** at 12.5 hearts (only once)
- **Allow you to have children** - ask "Do you want to have/try for a baby?" after the nursery upgrade
- **Stay indoors on rainy days** and go to bed at a reasonable hour
- **Get jealous** if you give a bouquet to another marriage candidate (lose 30 friendship)
- **Go back to their old room** if friendship drops to 0 hearts (divorce is possible)

### How to Get a Divorce?

If you want to end a marriage, you can use the small booklet on the nightstand in Mayor Lewis's bedroom at the Mayor's Manor. This costs 50,000g. The divorce takes effect the next morning, and your ex-spouse's friendship resets to 0 hearts.
