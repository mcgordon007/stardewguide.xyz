# Stardew Valley Loom Guide

## Overview

The **Loom** is an artisan equipment that converts **Wool** into **Cloth**. Cloth is a valuable artisan good used in several crafting recipes, clothing creation, and as a loved gift for certain villagers.

## Unlock Requirements

- **Farming Level 7** required to unlock the crafting recipe

## Crafting Materials

To craft a Loom, you will need:

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Wood | 60 |
| Fiber | 30 |

## How to Use

1. Place the Loom on your farm or inside a shed
2. Interact with it while holding **Wool**
3. Processing time: **4 hours** of in-game time

## Output Products

### Cloth (from Wool)
- **Wool** → **Cloth** (470g)
- There is a chance to produce **2 Cloth** from a single Wool if you have the **Rancher** profession

## Alternative Sources of Cloth

- Recycling **Soggy Newspaper** in a Recycling Machine has a 10% chance to produce Cloth
- Occasionally found in trash cans
- Can be purchased from the Desert Trader on Wednesdays for 3 Aquamarine

## Uses for Cloth

Cloth is used in various crafting recipes including:
- Dressed Spinner (fishing tackle)
- Mill (farm building)
- Several clothing items at the Sewing Machine
- Dye Packs (Emily's house)

## Tips & Strategy

- The Artisan profession (+40% sell price) boosts Cloth value to 658g
- Rabbits produce Wool more frequently than Sheep, making them ideal for Cloth production
- With the Rancher profession, the chance for double Cloth makes this highly profitable
- Cloth is a **loved gift** for Emily
