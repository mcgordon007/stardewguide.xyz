# Summer Season Guide

Summer is the most profitable crop season and when your farm truly begins to take off. With high-value crops like Blueberry and Starfruit, rare summer fish, and two memorable festivals, this guide covers everything you need for a successful summer.

## TL;DR

Summer priorities: plant **Blueberries** (best multi-harvest profit at 20.8g/day) and **Melon** (250g single harvest) on Day 1, unlock the Desert for **Starfruit** (750g, 26.92g/day), catch rare rain fish and the legendary Crimsonfish, and prepare for the Luau (Summer 11). Don't miss **Moonlight Jellies** (Summer 28). Key forageables: Grape (80g), Spice Berry (80g), Fiddlehead Fern (90g).

### Best Foraging Strategy

- **Secret Woods**: Fiddlehead Fern (90g) is the most valuable regular forageable. Requires a steel axe to access
- **Rainbow Shell**: Beach forageable, cannot be sold but is a **universally loved gift** -- give to any villager for +80 friendship
- **Grape and Spice Berry**: 80g base value, good for wine/jelly processing
- **Days 12-14**: Extra beach forageables spawn

### Fishing Tips for Summer

- **Sturgeon** is incredibly valuable: 200g base AND produces **Caviar** (500g) in a Fish Pond -- one of the best passive income sources
- **Crimsonfish** requires Fishing Level 5 minimum. Use Cork Bobber + Trap Bobber for the best chance
- **Octopus** (95 difficulty, 150g) is one of the hardest regular fish -- great for XP
- **Rain days**: Catfish (200g), Red Snapper (50g), Shad (60g)
- **Sturgeon** spawns only in Mountain Lake, 6am-7pm -- fish here when possible
- **Ocean Fish Bundle**: Tuna + Red Snapper + Tilapia (all catchable in Summer)

## Week-by-Week Summer Plan

### Week 1 (Summer 1-7)

- **Day 1**: Plant Blueberries (80g, 13 days to first harvest, then every 4 days) and Melons (80g, 12 days)
- **Day 2-3**: Water crops, forage Grapes and Spice Berries, fish at the ocean
- **Day 3 (Year 1)**: Earthquake unlocks the Railroad
- **Day 4**: Jas's birthday
- **Day 5-6**: Unlock the Desert bus (400g, Vault bundle) and buy Starfruit Seeds (400g each from Oasis Shop)
- **Day 7**: If you planted Starfruit Day 1, water them. Continue fishing

### Week 2 (Summer 8-14)

- **Day 8**: Gus's birthday. Harvest Melons if planted Day 1 (12 days)
- **Day 10**: Maru's birthday
- **Day 11**: **Luau**. Bring a high-quality Melon or Starfruit for the soup
- **Day 12-13**: Starfruit harvest if planted early. Process into wine if you have Kegs
- **Day 13**: Alex's birthday. Blueberries first harvest if planted Day 1 (13 days)
- **Day 14**: Continue Blueberry harvesting (regrows every 4 days: Day 17, 21, 25)

### Week 3 (Summer 15-21)

- **Day 15-18**: Harvest Blueberries, plant Hops (60g, 11 days, daily regrow) for Pale Ale production
- **Day 17**: Sam's birthday. Second Blueberry harvest
- **Day 19**: Demetrius's birthday
- **Day 20-21**: **Trout Derby**. Catch and submit fish for points and prizes
- **Day 21**: Third Blueberry harvest

### Week 4 (Summer 22-28)

- **Day 22**: Dwarf's birthday
- **Day 24**: Willy's birthday. Fourth Blueberry harvest
- **Day 25**: Fifth Blueberry harvest. Hot Pepper (40g, every 3 days) should have multiple harvests by now
- **Day 26**: Leo's birthday
- **Day 27**: Plant crops that carry into Fall if you have a Greenhouse
- **Day 28**: **Moonlight Jellies** festival (evening). Last day of Summer

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Should I buy Starfruit Seeds from the Oasis?

Yes, if you can afford them. Starfruit (400g seed, 750g sell, 13 days = 26.92g/day) is the most profitable crop per day. Turn into wine (1,500g with Artisan) for massive profit. Plant in the Greenhouse for year-round income.

### What should I bring to the Luau?

A Gold or Iridium quality Melon or Starfruit. Gold Starfruit guarantees the best reaction (+120 friendship with everyone). Even a normal Melon will get "Good" (+60 friendship). Avoid bringing Seaweed or low-value items.

### When should I plant Hops?

Plant Hops in Week 2 if you want Pale Ale. They take 11 days to mature then regrow daily. Best paired with Kegs: Pale Ale sells for 300g (400g with Artisan). One hop harvest produces multiple hops.

### How do I catch the Crimsonfish?

The Crimsonfish appears at the East Pier on the Beach (east side, requires Fishing Level 5). It has 95 difficulty -- use Cork Bobber + Trap Bobber with the Iridium Rod. Eat Seafoam Pudding or Dish o' The Sea for +Fishing buff. It only appears once per save file.
