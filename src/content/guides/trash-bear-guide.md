# Stardew Valley Trash Bear Guide

The **Trash Bear** is a special NPC that appears in Cindersap Forest starting in Year 3. By fulfilling its requests, you can trigger a heartwarming cutscene that cleans up trash around the valley and rewards you with visual improvements to the town.

---

## How to Unlock

- Trash Bear appears on **non-rainy, non-festival days** beginning in **Year 3**
- Located at the bottom of Cindersap Forest, near the Spring Onion patch and the entrance to The Sewers

---

## How It Works

Trash Bear will ask for **4 items total**, in this order:
1. **First request:** 1 random seasonal fish or foraged item
2. **Second request:** 1 random seasonal fish or foraged item
3. **Third request:** 1 cooked dish
4. **Fourth request:** 1 cooked dish

There is **no time limit** to complete these requests. However, Trash Bear does **not appear on rainy days**, so you cannot fulfill requests on those days.

### Request Rules
- Requests will **never change** until completed
- If the **season changes** and the previous request was seasonal, the request will update to match the new season
- You can check Trash Bear's current request by interacting with it

### Cooked Dishes (Any Season)

For the 3rd and 4th requests, Trash Bear may ask for any cooked dish in the game. Common requests include:
- Fried Egg
- Omelet
- Salad
- Baked Fish
- Vegetable Medley
- Complete Breakfast
- And many more!

---

## Rewards

After giving Trash Bear all 4 requested items, a cutscene will play:

1. Trash Bear plays pan-pipes
2. Flies up on an umbrella made of leaves
3. **Cleans up trash** in the unreachable area near the water in Cindersap Forest, replacing it with bushes and trees
4. Flies to Pelican Town and removes the trash in **Pam's front yard**
5. Upgrades **Dusty's dog pen** with a proper dog house and fence

### Visual Changes
- Cindersap Forest river area becomes cleaner and greener
- Pam's trailer area looks tidier
- Dusty's dog pen gets a major visual upgrade

---

## Tips

1. **Stock Up on Cooked Dishes** - Since the 3rd and 4th requests can be any cooked dish, having a variety prepared helps
2. **Check Every Sunny Day** - Trash Bear only appears on non-rainy days
3. **Seasonal Planning** - If you're close to a season change and the request is seasonal, it will update automatically
4. **No Rush** - There's no time limit, so take your time gathering the requested items
5. **Cooking Skill Helps** - Having a well-stocked kitchen makes the cooked dish requests much easier
