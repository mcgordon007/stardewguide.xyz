# Stardew Valley Krobus Guide: Complete NPC Overview

Krobus is a unique NPC in Stardew Valley - a shadow person who lives in the sewers and operates a secret shop. He's the only non-human NPC you can befriend and potentially live with as a roommate. This comprehensive guide covers everything about Krobus, from his schedule to his shop inventory to the roommate option.

## Basic Information

### Character Overview

**Name**: Krobus
**Role**: Shadow Person, Sewers Shop Owner
**Location**: Sewers (under Pelican Town)
**Family**: None (shadow people community)
**Birthday**: Winter 1
**Marriageable**: No, but can become roommate

### Personality

Krobus is a peaceful shadow person who has chosen to live apart from his aggressive kin. He's friendly, curious about humans, and runs a shop selling rare items. Krobus is intelligent and philosophical, often discussing the nature of shadow people and their relationship with humans. He's grateful for friendship and offers unique items unavailable elsewhere.

## Accessing the Sewers

### Sewer Entrance

The sewers are located:
- Under Pelican Town
- Entrance near the river in town
- Requires Rusty Key to enter

### Getting the Rusty Key

**Unlock Requirement**: Donate 60 items to the Museum

**Process**:
1. Donate 60 items to Gunther at the Museum
2. Gunther gives you the Rusty Key
3. Use key to unlock sewer grate
4. Enter sewers anytime after

### Sewer Layout

The sewers contain:
- Krobus's shop area
- Shadow brute spawning area
- Warp statue
- Various passages

## Stardrop Purchase

### The Stardrop

Krobus sells one Stardrop for 20,000g:

- One of 7 Stardrops available
- Worth purchasing early
- Significant gameplay benefit

### Stardrop Benefits

- Base energy: 270
- After Stardrop: 304
- Makes daily tasks easier
- More stamina for activities

## Becoming Roommate

### Requirements

To invite Krobus as roommate:
- Reach 10 hearts friendship
- Empty bedroom (no spouse)
- Obtain Void Ghost Pendant (trade Rabbit's Foot with Desert Trader on Fridays)
- Invite Krobus to move in

### Process

1. **Build Friendship**: Gift Krobus regularly to 10 hearts
2. **Invite**: Ask Krobus to move in
3. **Roommate**: Krobus lives in your farmhouse

### Roommate Benefits

**Living with Krobus**:
- He stays in your house
- Provides friendship bonus
- Unique dialogue
- Shadow person perspective

### Roommate vs. Spouse

| Feature | Krobus Roommate | Spouse |
|---------|-----------------|--------|
| Marriage | No | Yes |
| Children | No | Yes |
| Help on farm | No | Yes |
| Unique dialogue | Yes | Yes |
| Story depth | Yes | Yes |

### Roommate Events

Living with Krobus provides:
- Morning greetings
- Evening conversations
- Shadow person insights
- Unique interactions

## Heart Events

### 3 Hearts

**Trigger**: Visit Krobus in the sewers

**Event**: Krobus discusses his nature as a shadow person and his decision to live peacefully. He shares his philosophy about shadow people and humans.

### 14 Hearts - Roommate Event

**Trigger**: Invite Krobus to move in (requires Void Ghost Pendant)

**Event**: Krobus accepts your invitation and moves into your farmhouse. He becomes your roommate.

Krobus is a unique and fascinating NPC in Stardew Valley. As a peaceful shadow person, he offers rare items, friendship, and the option to become your roommate. Visit his sewer shop for essential items like the Stardrop and Return Scepter.
