# Stardew Valley Robin's Carpenter Shop Guide

Robin's Carpenter Shop is the central hub for all construction and building services in Stardew Valley. From farm buildings to house upgrades to furniture purchases, Robin provides essential services throughout your farming journey. This comprehensive guide covers everything you need to know about Robin's shop and services.

## Shop Location and Hours

### Location
Robin's Carpenter Shop is located in the Mountains area, northeast of Pelican Town. It's situated at the base of the mountain path, next to Robin and Demetrius's house. The shop is easily accessible from the town or via the mountain path.

| Friday | 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM |
| Saturday | 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM |

**Notes**:
- Shop is closed on Mondays (Robin works on other projects)
- Robin leaves the counter at 5:00 PM but remains in the shop briefly
- Shop is open during festivals that occur on regular days
- Robin may be absent during certain heart events or quests

### Special Schedule Notes

- **Summer 18**: Robin is at the shop for Sebastian's birthday
- **Winter 21**: Robin may be absent for family events
- **Festival Days**: Shop may have modified hours

## House Upgrades

Robin provides all farmhouse upgrades, expanding your living space and adding new features.

### First Upgrade - Kitchen

**Cost**: 10,000g
**Materials**: Wood (450), Stone (200)
**Construction Time**: 3 days

**Features Added**:
- Kitchen with cooking functionality
- Double bed (allows marriage)
- Expanded living room
- Refrigerator for ingredient storage

**Unlocks**:
- Cooking recipes
- Marriage capability
- Kitchen access

### Second Upgrade - Bedroom

**Cost**: 50,000g
**Materials**: Hardwood (150)
**Construction Time**: 3 days

**Features Added**:
- Second bedroom (for children)
- Nursery with crib
- Expanded house size
- Two cribs for two children

**Unlocks**:
- Ability to have children (after marriage)
- More storage space
- Larger living area

### Third Upgrade - Cellar

**Cost**: 100,000g
**Materials**: Hardwood (30)
**Construction Time**: 3 days

**Features Added**:
- Cellar below house
- 33 Cask slots for aging wine/cheese
- Access via staircase

**Unlocks**:

- Quality improvement for artisan goods
- Iridium quality products possible

| Wine | 3x fruit value | 6x fruit value | 2 seasons |

## Building Services

### Construction Process

1. **Purchase**: Buy building at Robin's counter
2. **Placement**: Select location on farm
3. **Materials**: Ensure you have required materials
4. **Construction**: Robin builds over 2-3 days
5. **Completion**: Building appears on farm

### Building Demolition

Robin can demolish any farm building for a fee:

**Cost**: Free (but you lose the building and materials)

**Process**:
1. Select building to demolish
2. Confirm demolition
3. Building disappears immediately
4. Space becomes available

**Note**: Animals in demolished buildings are lost. Remove animals before demolishing.

### Building Relocation

Robin can move existing buildings to new locations:

**Cost**: Free

**Process**:
1. Select "Move Building" option
2. Choose building to move
3. Select new location
4. Building moves instantly

**Benefits**:
- Reorganize farm layout
- No reconstruction needed
- Animals stay in building
- Perfect for layout optimization

## Tips for Using Robin's Shop

### Planning Your Purchases

**Early Game**:
- Silo first (before winter)
- Coop or Barn based on preference
- Well if needed

**Mid Game**:
- House upgrades (kitchen first)
- Stable for horse
- Upgrade animal buildings

**Late Game**:
- Junimo Huts
- Obelisks
- Gold Clock
- Furniture Catalog

### Material Preparation

Before purchasing buildings:
1. Check required materials
2. Gather materials beforehand
3. Have gold ready
4. Plan placement location

### Construction Timing

- Buildings complete overnight
- Plan around seasons
- Consider festival days
- Account for Robin's schedule

### Space Planning

- Reserve space for future buildings
- Consider building sizes
- Plan for obelisks near house
- Leave room for expansion
