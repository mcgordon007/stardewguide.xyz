# Skills and Professions Guide

## TL;DR

Stardew Valley has 5 skills (Farming, Mining, Fishing, Foraging, Combat) that level from 1 to 10 with profession choices at levels 5 and 10. The most profitable profession paths are: Tiller -> Artisan (+40% to artisan goods), Miner -> Blacksmith (+1 ore per vein, +50% bar prices), Fisher -> Angler (+75% total fish value), Gatherer -> Botanist (all forage iridium quality), Fighter -> Brute (+30% total damage). The Artisan profession is the single most impactful money multiplier in the game. Level 10 in all skills unlocks the Mastery system for additional rewards including Stardrops.

Stardew Valley features 5 skills: Farming, Mining, Fishing, Foraging, and Combat. Each skill levels from 1 to 10, with profession choices at levels 5 and 10. At level 10, you can also unlock a Mastery system. This guide covers every skill in complete detail with profession analysis.

## What is the Farming Skill?

### How to Gain Farming XP?

| Action | XP Gained |
|--------|----------|
| Harvest a crop | 3-8 XP (varies by crop) |
| Harvest a tree fruit | 5 XP |
| Pet an animal | 5 XP |
| Milk a cow/goat | 5 XP |
| Shear a sheep | 5 XP |
| Collect an egg | 5 XP |
| Collect a truffle | 7 XP |

### What Are the Farming Level-Up Rewards?

| Level | XP Required | Reward |
|-------|------------|--------|
| 1 | 0 | Basic farming knowledge |
| 2 | 100 | - |
| 3 | 380 | Quality Fertilizer recipe |
| 4 | 770 | Basic Fertilizer recipe |
| 5 | 1,300 | Choose: Tiller or Rancher |
| 6 | 2,000 | Speed-Gro recipe |
| 7 | 2,800 | Deluxe Speed-Gro recipe |
| 8 | 3,900 | Iridium Sprinkler recipe |
| 9 | 5,200 | Quality Retaining Soil recipe |
| 10 | 7,000 | Choose: Artisan or Agriculturist |

### What is the Best Farming Profession at Level 5?

| Profession | Effect | Best For |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Tiller | Crops sell for 10% more | Crop-focused farmers |
| Rancher | Animal products sell for 20% more | Animal-focused farmers |

**Recommendation:** Tiller is generally better because crop profits scale much higher than animal product profits, especially with wine and jelly processing. The 10% bonus applies to all crops, and when combined with Artisan at level 10, the total bonus becomes very significant.

### What is the Best Farming Profession at Level 10 (Tiller Branch)?

| Profession | Effect | Best For |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Artisan | Artisan goods (wine, jelly, cheese, etc.) sell for 40% more | Keg/preserves jar users |
| Agriculturist | Crops grow 10% faster | Players who want faster harvests |

**Recommendation:** Artisan is almost universally considered the best farming profession. The 40% bonus to artisan goods dramatically increases profits. Starfruit wine with Artisan sells for 3,150g (vs 2,250g without). This profession is the foundation of the most profitable farming strategies.

### What is the Best Farming Profession at Level 10 (Rancher Branch)?

| Profession | Effect | Best For |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Shepherd | Sheep produce wool faster | Sheep farmers |
| Coopmaster | Chickens produce eggs faster, incubator ready sooner | Chicken farmers |

**Recommendation:** Neither rancher branch profession is as profitable as the Tiller/Artisan path. If you chose Rancher, Shepherd is slightly better due to wool's value.

| Action | XP Gained |
|--------|----------|
| Break a rock | 1-2 XP |
| Break an ore node | 3-5 XP |
| Kill a monster | Varies (5-20 XP) |

### What Are the Mining Level-Up Rewards?

| Level | XP Required | Reward |
|-------|------------|--------|
| 1 | 0 | Pickaxe knowledge |
| 2 | 30 | Copper ore nodes appear |
| 3 | 70 | Staircase recipe |
| 4 | 120 | Cherry Bomb recipe |

| 6 | 250 | Transmute (Fe) recipe |
| 7 | 350 | Transmute (Au) recipe |
| 8 | 500 | Mega Bomb recipe |
| 9 | 750 | Transmute (Ir) recipe |
| 10 | 1,000 | Choose: Blacksmith/Excavator or Gemologist/Excavator |

### What is the Best Mining Profession at Level 5?

| Profession | Effect | Best For |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Miner | +1 ore per vein | Ore-focused miners |
| Geologist | Chance for gems to appear in pairs | Gem-focused miners |

**Recommendation:** Miner is generally better because extra ore directly translates to more bars for tool upgrades and selling. The extra ore per vein is consistent and reliable.

### What is the Best Mining Profession at Level 10 (Miner Branch)?

| Profession | Effect | Best For |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Blacksmith | Bars sell for 50% more | Bar sellers |
| Excavator | Chance to find extra geodes | Geode collectors |

**Recommendation:** Blacksmith is better for direct profit. Iridium bars sell for 600g normally, 900g with Blacksmith. This adds up significantly over time.

### What is the Best Mining Profession at Level 10 (Geologist Branch)?

| Profession | Effect | Best For |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Gemologist | Gems sell for 30% more | Gem sellers |
| Excavator | Chance to find extra geodes | Geode collectors |

**Recommendation:** Gemologist is better for players who want to sell gems. The 30% bonus applies to all gems, including valuable ones like Prismatic Shards (2,000g -> 2,600g).

| Action | XP Gained |
|--------|----------|
| Catch a fish | 3 + (difficulty x 3) XP |
| Perfect catch | 2.2x multiplier |
| Crab pot item | 5 XP |
| Trash | 3 XP |

### What Are the Fishing Level-Up Rewards?

| Level | XP Required | Reward |
|-------|------------|--------|
| 1 | 0 | Bamboo Pole |
| 2 | 100 | Fiberglass Rod available |
| 3 | 300 | Crab Pot recipe |
| 4 | 600 | Barbed Hook recipe |
| 5 | 1,000 | Choose: Fisher or Trapper |
| 6 | 2,000 | Iridium Rod available |
| 7 | 3,000 | Spinners recipe |
| 8 | 5,000 | Trap Bobber recipe |
| 9 | 8,000 | Curiosity Lure recipe |
| 10 | 12,000 | Choose: Angler/Pirate or Mariner/Luremaster |

### What is the Best Fishing Profession at Level 5?

| Profession | Effect | Best For |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Fisher | Fish sell for 25% more | Active fishers |
| Trapper | Crab pots require no bait | Passive crab potters |

**Recommendation:** Fisher is better for most players. The 25% bonus to fish sales is significant and applies to all fish. Trapper is only worthwhile if you plan to place many crab pots.

### What is the Best Fishing Profession at Level 10 (Fisher Branch)?

| Profession | Effect | Best For |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Angler | Fish sell for 50% more (total +75%) | Fish sellers |
| Pirate | Chance to find double treasure | Treasure hunters |

**Recommendation:** Angler is the best fishing profession. Combined with Fisher, you get 75% more gold from all fish sales. This makes fishing extremely profitable.

### What is the Best Fishing Profession at Level 10 (Trapper Branch)?

| Profession | Effect | Best For |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Mariner | Crab pots never produce trash | Crab pot users |
| Luremaster | Crab pots do not require bait | Crab pot users |

**Recommendation:** Mariner is better because it eliminates trash from crab pots, making them a reliable source of income. Luremaster saves bait costs but Mariner increases actual income.

## What is the Mastery System?

After reaching level 10 in all 5 skills, you unlock the Mastery system. Mastery is earned by completing tasks:

### What Are the Mastery Tasks?

| Skill | Task | Mastery Points |
|-------|------|---------------|
| Farming | Harvest 15 crops of each quality (normal, silver, gold, iridium) | 1 point |
| Mining | Reach mine level 120 and donate every mineral | 1 point |
| Fishing | Catch every fish | 1 point |
| Foraging | Forage every seasonal item | 1 point |
| Combat | Kill every monster type | 1 point |

### What Are the Mastery Rewards?

| Mastery Level | Points Required | Reward |
|---------------|----------------|--------|
| 1 | 1 point | 5 Stardrop Fragments |
| 2 | 2 points | 5 Stardrop Fragments |
| 3 | 3 points | 5 Stardrop Fragments |
| 4 | 4 points | 5 Stardrop Fragments |
| 5 | 5 points | 1 Stardrop (permanent max energy +50) |

Collecting all 25 Stardrop Fragments and combining them at the Master Forge creates a Stardrop, which permanently increases your maximum energy by 34 points.

## What Skill-Based Titles Can I Unlock?

Reaching level 10 in each skill unlocks a title that appears in your character info:

| Skill | Title |
|-------|-------|
| Farming | Farmhand -> Rancher -> Agriculturist -> Agricultural Master |
| Mining | Prospector -> Excavator -> Blacksmith -> Excavation Master |
| Fishing | Fisher -> Angler -> Pirate -> Master Angler |
| Foraging | Forager -> Botanist -> Tracker -> Nature Master |
| Combat | Fighter -> Brute -> Acrobat | Combat Master |
