# NPC Friendship Guide

![NPC Portraits](/images/npc-portraits.jpg)

Building friendships with Pelican Town's residents is a core part of Stardew Valley. Friendship unlocks heart events, recipes, discounts, and eventually marriage. This guide explains how the friendship system works in complete detail and how to maximize your relationships with every villager.

## Heart Levels and Friendship Points

Friendship is measured in points, displayed as hearts on the social menu. Each heart represents 250 friendship points, for a maximum of 10 hearts (2,500 points) for regular villagers and 14 hearts (3,500 points) for your spouse after marriage.

### Complete Heart Level Table

| Hearts | Friendship Points | Milestone | Notes |
|--------|------------------|-----------|-------|
| 0 Hearts | 0 | Starting point | New villager |
| 1 Heart | 250 | First tier | Basic friendship |
| 2 Hearts | 500 | First heart event (marriage candidates) | Cutscene triggers |
| 3 Hearts | 750 | Second tier | - |
| 4 Hearts | 1,000 | Second heart event (marriage candidates) | Deeper story |
| 5 Hearts | 1,250 | Third tier | - |
| 6 Hearts | 1,500 | Third heart event (marriage candidates) | Conflict/story |
| 7 Hearts | 1,750 | Fourth tier | - |
| 8 Hearts | 2,000 | Fourth heart event (marriage candidates) | Pre-romantic |
| 9 Hearts | 2,250 | Fifth tier | - |
| 10 Hearts | 2,500 | Maximum friendship | Marriage possible |
| 12 Hearts | 3,000 | Spouse (post-marriage) | After marriage |
| 14 Hearts | 3,500 | Maximum spouse friendship | Stardrop gift at 12.5 hearts |

## Gift Giving Mechanics

You can give each villager one gift per day (two per day if your spouse). Gifts are the primary way to build friendship. The reaction depends on the villager's preferences.

### Gift Reaction Points

| Reaction | Points | Description | Visual Indicator |
|----------|--------|-------------|-----------------|
| Loved | +80 | The villager's absolute favorite items | Heart particles |
| Liked | +45 | Items the villager enjoys | Small smile |
| Neutral | +20 | Items the villager has no strong opinion about | No special reaction |
| Disliked | -20 | Items the villager does not like | Frown |
| Hated | -40 | Items the villager strongly dislikes | Green sick face |

### Gift Limits

| Relationship | Gifts Per Day | Weekly Limit | Notes |
|------------|--------------|-------------|-------|
| Regular villager | 1 gift per day | 2 gifts per week | Only the best gift each week counts fully |
| Spouse | 1 gift per day | No weekly limit | Friendship gains are halved |
| Any villager | Maximum 1 gift per birthday | Birthday gift counts as daily gift | 8x multiplier applies |

## Birthday Bonuses (8x Multiplier)

Giving a gift on a villager's birthday multiplies the friendship points by 8:

| Reaction | Normal Points | Birthday Points |
|----------|--------------|----------------|
| Loved | +80 | +640 |
| Liked | +45 | +360 |
| Neutral | +20 | +160 |
| Disliked | -20 | -160 |
| Hated | -40 | -320 |

**Always save your best gifts for birthdays.** A single loved birthday gift (+640) is worth more than 8 days of daily liked gifts (+360). This is the single most important friendship optimization in the game.

## How to Find Birthday Information

1. **Check the calendar** in your farmhouse - shows all birthdays
2. **Talk to villagers** - they may mention other villagers' birthdays
3. **Look at the notice board** outside Pierre's store - sometimes has birthday announcements
4. **Watch TV** - the "Ranch" channel sometimes mentions birthdays

## Heart Event Triggers

Heart events are cutscenes that trigger when you reach certain friendship levels with a villager. For marriage candidates, these events tell their personal story and are required before you can propose.

### Heart Event Requirements

| Event | Hearts Required | Conditions |
|-------|----------------|-----------|
| First event | 2 hearts | Non-rainy day, enter correct location |
| Second event | 4 hearts | Non-rainy day, enter correct location |
| Third event | 6 hearts | Non-rainy day, enter correct location |
| Fourth event | 8 hearts | Non-rainy day, enter correct location |
| Fifth event | 10 hearts | Non-rainy day, enter correct location |

### Important Heart Event Notes

- Heart events only trigger on **non-rainy days**
- You must **enter the correct location** at the right time
- If conditions are not met, the event triggers the next eligible day
- Some events have **choice branches** that affect dialogue but not the overall story

## Friendship Decay Mechanics

Friendship points decay by 2 points per day for each villager you have not spoken to. This decay is very slow and only matters if you completely ignore a villager for extended periods.

### Decay Details

| Action | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| Not speaking to a villager | -2 points per day |
| Speaking to a villager | +20 points, stops decay for that day |
| Giving a disliked gift | -20 points |
| Giving a hated gift | -40 points |
| Giving a loved birthday gift | +640 points |

### Decay Mitigation

- Speaking to a villager daily prevents decay entirely
- Even a brief conversation (just clicking on them) counts as "speaking"
- Marriage candidates do not decay faster than regular villagers
- After marriage, friendship decay continues if you do not talk to your spouse

## Marriage Requirements

To marry a villager, you must:

1. Reach **10 hearts** of friendship
2. See all **5 heart events** for that villager
3. Give them a **Bouquet** (purchased from Pierre's for 200g after 8 hearts)
4. Give them a **Mermaid's Pendant** (purchased from the Old Mariner on the beach for 5,000g, available after 10 hearts and the beach bridge is repaired)

After marriage, your spouse moves into your farmhouse and helps with chores. You can have children after marriage by selecting the option from the spouse's dialogue.

## Tips for Efficient Friendship

1. **Talk to everyone daily** - 20 points per day adds up to 7,300 points per year
2. **Check birthdays** on the calendar and plan gifts in advance
3. **Give loved gifts** whenever possible - one loved gift per week makes a huge difference
4. **Attend festivals** for bonus friendship with participants
5. **Complete quests** for villagers when they appear on the help board outside Pierre's store
6. **Do not give hated gifts** - a single hated gift erases 4 days of talking
7. **Prioritize marriage candidates** if you plan to marry, focus on one candidate at a time
8. **Use universal loves** when you do not know a villager's specific preferences
9. **Save Rabbit's Foot** for birthday gifts - the 8x multiplier makes loved birthday gifts incredibly powerful
