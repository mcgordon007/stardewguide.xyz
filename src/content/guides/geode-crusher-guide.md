# Stardew Valley Geode Crusher Guide

## Introduction

The **Geode Crusher** is a device added in Stardew Valley 1.5 that can automatically open geodes without needing to pay Clint at the Blacksmith, making it a convenient tool for mid-to-late game miners.

## Unlock Requirements

- Obtain the recipe after completing **Clint's Special Order** "Cave Patrol"

## Crafting Materials

- Gold Bar x2
- Stone x50
- Diamond x1

## Functionality

- Place **1 geode** + **1 coal** to automatically open it
- Opening time is about **1 hour** (game time)
- Can open all types of geodes:
  - Geode
  - Frozen Geode
  - Magma Geode
  - Omni Geode

## Comparison with Blacksmith

| Attribute | Blacksmith Clint | Geode Crusher |
|-----------|------------------|---------------|
| Cost | 25g per geode | 1 coal |
| Time | Instant | About 1 hour |
| Batch Processing | Can process multiple | 1 at a time |
| Convenience | Must travel to town | Can be placed on farm |

## Usage Tips

1. Place the Geode Crusher near storage on your farm for easy access
2. When opening geodes in bulk, build multiple Geode Crushers to work in parallel
3. Coal cost is usually lower than Clint's fee, more economical in the long run
4. When opening Omni Geodes, pair with Crystalariums copying diamonds for better value

## Notes

- Cannot open Artifact Troves
- Opening speed is slow, not suitable when you need contents urgently
- Coal may be scarce in late game, plan accordingly
