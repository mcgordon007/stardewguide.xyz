# Meowmere Guide

The Meowmere is a secret crossover weapon introduced in Stardew Valley 1.6 as part of a collaboration with Terraria, another beloved indie game developed by Re-Logic. This whimsical sword features a unique rainbow sparkle effect and is a fun collectible for fans of both games.

## TL;DR

The Meowmere is a Stardew Valley x Terraria crossover sword (Level 4, 20-20 damage, +4 speed). Obtain the Far Away Stone by holding an Ancient Doll near the lava pool on floor 100 of the Mines or the Volcano Caldera, then place it on the plant pedestal in the Wizard's Tower basement. The sword makes enemies explode into rainbow sparkles. It is a novelty weapon, not endgame gear.

## How to Obtain

### Step 1: Get an Ancient Doll

You need one **Ancient Doll** to trigger the Far Away Stone event. Ancient Dolls can be obtained from:

- **Artifact Spots** (worms) in Pelican Town and Cindersap Forest
- **Fishing Treasure Chests** (random chance)
- **Omni Geodes** (when cracked open by Clint)
- **Artifact Troves**

### Step 2: Obtain the Far Away Stone

Once you have an Ancient Doll:

1. Travel to **Floor 100 of the Mines** OR the **Volcano Caldera** (top of the Volcano Dungeon on Ginger Island)
2. **Equip the Ancient Doll** in your active hand slot
3. **Right-click (or interact with) the lava pool** while holding the Ancient Doll
4. The Ancient Doll is consumed, and a **Far Away Stone** appears in its place

The Far Away Stone can be obtained multiple times by repeating this process with additional Ancient Dolls.

### Step 3: Visit the Wizard's Tower Basement

1. Travel to the **Wizard's Tower** in Cindersap Forest (southwest of Pelican Town)
2. Enter the tower and proceed to the **basement** (accessible via stairs)
3. In the basement, you will find a **plant pedestal** with a glowing purple crystal
4. **Equip the Far Away Stone** and interact with the pedestal
5. The Far Away Stone is consumed, and the **Meowmere** appears in its place

### Combat Performance

The Meowmere is a **low-to-mid level weapon** with the following characteristics:

- **20-20 damage** is fixed - every swing deals exactly the same damage
- **+4 Speed** makes it one of the fastest swords, excellent for rapid strikes
- **Weight +2** provides solid knockback to keep enemies at a distance
- The **rainbow sparkle effect** on kills is purely cosmetic but visually delightful
- It is outperformed by any weapon above level 4 (e.g., Bone Sword, Templar's Blade)

**Important:** The Meowmere is a **collector's item and novelty weapon**, not an endgame blade. It is significantly weaker than the Galaxy Sword (60-80 damage) or Lava Katana (55-64 damage).

## The Terraria Connection

The Meowmere is a direct reference to Terraria's iconic **Meowmere** weapon:

| Feature | Stardew Valley | Terraria |
|---------|---------------|----------|
| **Name** | Meowmere | Meowmere |
| **Theme** | Cat-inspired rainbow sword | Cat-inspired rainbow sword |
| **Effect** | Rainbow sparkles on enemy kill | Shoots bouncing cat projectiles |
| **Obtaining** | Far Away Stone + Wizard's basement | Moon Lord drop (endgame boss) |
| **Role** | Low-level secret/novelty | Endgame legendary weapon |

The collaboration was a mutual celebration between ConcernedApe (Stardew Valley) and Re-Logic (Terraria), two iconic indie game developers. Stardew Valley also includes other Terraria references, such as the Junimo Kart arcade game.

## Tips and Tricks

1. **Get Multiple Far Away Stones** - You can repeat the Ancient Doll + lava pool event to obtain multiple Far Away Stones
2. **Not a Combat Upgrade** - The Meowmere is weaker than weapons you can find in the mid-Mines. Use it for fun, not for serious combat
3. **Screenshot the Event** - The transformation animation in the Wizard's basement is a cool secret worth capturing
4. **Forge Enchantments** - You can still enchant the Meowmere at the Volcano Forge, though it won't make it competitive with endgame weapons
5. **No Missable Content** - This secret can be completed at any time, there are no time limits

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is the Meowmere a good weapon?

No, the Meowmere is a novelty weapon. With only 20-20 damage, it is significantly weaker than weapons commonly found in the lower Mines (floor 80+). It is a collector's item and fun Easter egg, not a practical combat tool.

### Can I get the Meowmere without an Ancient Doll?

No, you must have an Ancient Doll to trigger the Far Away Stone event. There is no alternative method.

### Can I get multiple Meowmeres?

Yes, you can obtain multiple Far Away Stones (by using multiple Ancient Dolls at the lava pool) and multiple Meowmeres (by placing each Far Away Stone on the pedestal).

### Can I sell the Meowmere?

Yes, the Meowmere can be sold for 200g at the Adventurer's Guild or through the Shipping Bin. However, as a unique secret item, you may want to keep it for your collection.

### Does the Meowmere count toward the "Complete Collection" achievement?

The Meowmere is not required for any achievements, museum collections, or shipping goals. It is purely a secret Easter egg.

### Is there a Terraria reference other than the Meowmere?

Yes, Stardew Valley 1.6 also includes a plushie of Terraria's Guide NPC, which can be obtained from the Crane Game at the Movie Theater.
