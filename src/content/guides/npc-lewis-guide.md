# Stardew Valley Lewis Guide: Complete NPC Overview

Lewis is the mayor of Pelican Town and a central figure in Stardew Valley's community. He manages town affairs, organizes festivals, and has a secret relationship with Marnie. This comprehensive guide covers everything about Lewis, from his schedule to his mayoral duties to his hidden secrets.

## Basic Information

### Character Overview

**Name**: Lewis
**Role**: Mayor of Pelican Town
**Location**: Pelican Town (Mayor's House)
**Family**: None (single publicly, relationship with Marnie)
**Birthday**: Spring 7
**Marriageable**: No

### Personality

Lewis is a friendly, somewhat traditional mayor who takes his responsibilities seriously. He's been mayor for many years and cares about the town's wellbeing. However, he has a secret romantic relationship with Marnie that he keeps hidden to maintain his public image. Lewis can be somewhat bureaucratic but genuinely wants the town to thrive.

## Lewis's Shorts Quest

### Quest Overview

**Quest Name**: Mayor's Shorts
**Trigger**: Find purple shorts in Marnie's bedroom
**Reward**: 750g and special dialogue

### Quest Steps

1. **Enter Marnie's Ranch**: Visit during business hours
2. **Find Shorts**: Enter Marnie's bedroom (when she's not there)
3. **Pick Up Shorts**: Collect purple shorts
4. **Return to Lewis**: Give shorts to Lewis at his house

### Quest Details

**Shorts Location**:
- Marnie's bedroom
- Visible on floor
- Reveals secret relationship

**When Available**:
- After reaching certain friendship
- Can enter bedroom when Marnie absent
- Quest appears naturally

### Quest Rewards

- 750g gold reward
- Special dialogue from Lewis
- Understanding of relationship
- Secret revealed

### Hidden Meaning

The shorts reveal:
- Lewis and Marnie's relationship
- Why Lewis keeps it secret
- His concern for public image
- Personal vulnerability

## Heart Events

### 3 Hearts

**Trigger**: Visit Lewis at his house or see him in town

### 6 Hearts

**Trigger**: Enter Marnie's Ranch when Lewis is there

**Event**: Lewis's relationship with Marnie becomes apparent. He may express his conflicted feelings about keeping it secret.

### Notes on Heart Events
