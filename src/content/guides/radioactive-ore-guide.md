# Radioactive Ore & Dangerous Mines Guide

Radioactive Ore is a rare and valuable resource introduced in Stardew Valley 1.5, found only in the dangerous versions of the Mines and Skull Cavern. It is essential for crafting two of the most powerful items in the game: **Hyper Speed-Gro** and **Magic Bait**.

## TL;DR

Radioactive Ore (300g sell) spawns from **Radioactive Nodes** found exclusively in **Dangerous Mines** or **Dangerous Skull Cavern**. Activate the **Shrine of Challenge** on Mines floor 120 or the **Skull Cavern entrance statue** to permanently enable dangerous mode. Smelt 5 ore + 1 coal into a Radioactive Bar. Used for crafting **Hyper Speed-Gro** (+33% growth speed) and **Magic Bait** (catch any season/time/weather fish). Daily Luck is the biggest factor for finding nodes.

## How to Find Radioactive Ore

Radioactive Ore comes from **Radioactive Nodes**, which only appear in dangerous/monster-enriched versions of mine areas.

### Source 1: Dangerous Mines

| Method | How to Activate | Details |
|--------|-----------------|---------|
| **Danger In The Deep quest** | Complete the 1.6 storyline quest | Temporarily activates dangerous mode during the quest |
| **Shrine of Challenge** | Interact with the shrine on Mines floor 120 | Permanently toggles dangerous mode on/off |

In dangerous mode, the Mines spawn more monsters and **Radioactive Nodes** appear from floor 80+ onward.

### Source 2: Dangerous Skull Cavern

| Method | How to Activate | Details |
|--------|-----------------|---------|
| **Skull Cavern Invasion quest** | Complete the 1.6 storyline quest | Temporarily activates dangerous mode |
| **Entrance Statue** | Interact with the statue at the Skull Cavern entrance | Permanently toggles dangerous mode on/off |

In dangerous Skull Cavern, Radioactive Nodes can appear at any depth.

### Factors That Affect Spawn Rate

| Factor | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| **Daily Luck** | **Strongest factor** -- high luck days massively increase Radioactive Node frequency |
| **Depth** | Slight increase deeper in the Mines/Skull Cavern |
| **Luck Buffs** | Small increase (Special Charm, Lucky Lunch, etc.) |
| **Number of Monsters** | More monsters killed = slightly more nodes revealed |

### Best Strategy for Farming Radioactive Ore

1. **Check your Daily Luck** on TV each morning. Farm only on high luck days (labeled "The spirits are very happy today")
2. **Eat luck-boosting food**: Lucky Lunch (+3 Luck), Spicy Eel (+2 Luck + Speed), or Special Charms
3. **Activate dangerous mode** via the Shrine of Challenge or Skull Cavern statue
4. **Bring bombs** (Cherry Bomb, Mega Bomb) to clear stone quickly and reveal nodes
5. **Go as deep as possible** in the Skull Cavern -- nodes are more common on deeper floors
6. **Use the minecart shortcut** to quickly exit and re-enter if a run is unlucky

## What to Make with Radioactive Ore

### Hyper Speed-Gro

| Property | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Recipe** | 1 Radioactive Ore + 3 Bone Fragment + 1 Solar Essence |
| **Recipe Cost** | 30 Qi Gems (from Mr. Qi's Walnut Room) |
| **Effect** | Increases crop growth rate by **at least 33%** |
| **Quantity** | Craft 5 per recipe |
| **Best Use** | Starfruit, Ancient Fruit, Pumpkin, and any high-value crop |

Hyper Speed-Gro is one of the most valuable fertilizers in the game. Applied to soil before planting, it guarantees at least 33% faster growth. Combined with Deluxe Speed-Gro or basic Speed-Gro effects, it dramatically increases harvest cycles.

**Profit comparison with Starfruit:**

| Method | Growth Time | Gold/Day (with Artisan) |
|--------|-------------|----------------------|
| No fertilizer | 13 days | 22.68g/day |
| Hyper Speed-Gro | ~9 days | 32.78g/day |
| Hyper + Agriculturist | ~8 days | 36.88g/day |

### Magic Bait

| Property | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Recipe** | 1 Radioactive Ore + 3 Bug Meat |
| **Recipe Cost** | 20 Qi Gems (from Mr. Qi's Walnut Room) |
| **Effect** | Catches fish from **any season, time, or weather** |
| **Quantity** | Craft 5 per recipe |

Magic Bait is essential for completing the fishing collection. When attached to a fishing rod with bait, it overrides seasonal/time/weather restrictions, allowing you to catch any fish at any time. This is the only way to catch certain fish outside their normal windows.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Radioactive Ore worth farming in Year 1?

Probably not. You need to reach floor 120 of the Mines to access the Shrine of Challenge, which takes significant time. Most players will start farming Radioactive Ore in Year 2 or later when they have better equipment and skills.

### Does the Shrine of Challenge affect my existing mine progress?

Yes. Activating dangerous mode permanently changes the Mines to spawn more monsters and Radioactive Nodes. You can toggle it off again by interacting with the shrine. Your elevator access and mine progress are preserved.

### What's the best way to get Bone Fragments for Hyper Speed-Gro?

Bone Fragments drop from Shadow Brutes (Mines 80+), Shadow Shamans, and Serpents in the Skull Cavern. They also drop from killing any monster in the Dangerous Mines. Stockpile them during your mining runs.

### Can I buy Radioactive Ore?

No, Radioactive Ore cannot be purchased from any shop. It must be mined from Radioactive Nodes in dangerous mine areas. The Traveling Cart very rarely sells Radioactive Bars, but this is unreliable.
