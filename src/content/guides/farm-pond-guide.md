# Stardew Valley Farm Pond Guide

The Fish Pond is a farm building that allows players to raise fish and harvest various fish-related products. It provides a steady source of Roe, fish-specific items, and even some rare materials.

## Building Requirements

- **Cost:** 5,000g
- **Materials:**
  - 200 Stone
  - 5 Seaweed
  - 5 Green Algae
- **Builder:** Robin at the Carpenter's Shop
- **Build Time:** 3 days
- **Size:** 5x5 tiles

## Fish Pond Basics

### Capacity
- Each Fish Pond can hold a maximum of **10 fish** of the same type
- Fish will reproduce over time up to the capacity limit
- Only one type of fish can be placed in each pond

### Fish Population Growth
- Fish reproduce naturally over time
- Completing pond quests (item requests) increases the maximum population
- Population starts at 1-3 fish and can grow to 10 through quest completion
- Higher population = more and better products

## Fish Types and Pond Products

### Common Fish and Their Products

#### Salmon / Tuna / Red Snapper / Tilapia / Halibut
- **Products:** Roe
- **Roe Value:** Varies by fish type
- **Notes:** Standard ocean fish, produce basic Roe

#### Sturgeon
- **Products:** Sturgeon Roe (makes Caviar when placed in a Preserves Jar)
- **Special Value:** Caviar sells for 500g (base price)
- **Notes:** One of the most profitable pond fish

#### Lava Eel
- **Products:** Roe, Magma Geodes, Gold Ore, Spicy Eel
- **Notes:** Very profitable; Spicy Eel is a valuable food buff item

#### Ice Pip
- **Products:** Roe, Frozen Geodes, Iron Ore, Diamond
- **Notes:** Good source of gems and geodes

#### Blobfish
- **Products:** Roe, Pearl, Warp Totem: Farm
- **Notes:** Chance to produce rare Pearls

#### Octopus
- **Products:** Roe, Omni Geode, Magma Geode
- **Notes:** Produces geodes consistently

#### Super Cucumber
- **Products:** Roe, Iridium Ore, Amethyst
- **Notes:** Source of valuable Iridium Ore

#### Rainbow Trout
- **Products:** Roe, Rainbow Shell, Prismatic Shard (very rare)
- **Notes:** Small chance for Prismatic Shards

#### Slimejack
- **Products:** Roe, Green Slime Egg, Algae
- **Notes:** Useful for Slime Hutch restocking

#### Woodskip
- **Products:** Roe, Hardwood, Acorn, Maple Seed, Pine Cone
- **Notes:** Good source of Hardwood and tree seeds

#### Void Salmon
- **Products:** Roe, Void Essence, Void Egg
- **Notes:** Source of Void Eggs for cooking

#### Tiger Trout
- **Products:** Roe, Tiger Slime Egg
- **Notes:** Source of Tiger Slime Eggs

### Legendary Fish

## Pond Quests

As your fish population grows, the pond will request specific items:
- **Population 3:** Requests 1 item
- **Population 5:** Requests 1-2 items
- **Population 7:** Requests 2-3 items
- **Population 10:** Final quest

### Common Quest Items
- Seaweed, Algae
- Specific fish types
- Gems and minerals
- Foraged items
- Artisan goods

Completing quests increases the pond's capacity and improves product quality.

## Roe and Caviar

### Roe Production
- All fish produce Roe (except Coral, Sea Urchin, and some others)
- Roe color varies by fish type
- Roe sells for 30g base price (varies by fish)

### Making Caviar
- Place **Sturgeon Roe** in a Preserves Jar
- Processing time: 4 days
- Caviar sells for 500g (base price)
- Caviar is loved by certain villagers as a gift

### Aged Roe
- Placing any Roe in a Preserves Jar creates Aged Roe
- Aged Roe sells for 1.5x the base Roe price
- Not as valuable as Caviar but still profitable

## Decoration Options

While the Fish Pond itself cannot be decorated, you can enhance the surrounding area:

### Pathing and Flooring
- Use Stone Path, Wood Path, or Crystal Path around the pond
- Flooring prevents weed growth and looks organized

### Fences
- Place fences around the pond area for a contained look
- Hardwood Fences last the longest

### Lighting
- Place Torches or Lamp Posts nearby for nighttime visibility
- Jack-O-Lanterns provide seasonal decoration

### Surrounding Decorations
- Plant flowers nearby for color
- Use seasonal crops as border decoration
- Place statues or unique furniture items nearby
- Trees (Maple, Oak, Pine) provide natural framing

### Multi-Pond Layouts
- Many players create "fish farm" areas with multiple ponds
- Group ponds of the same fish type together
- Create themed areas (ocean fish, river fish, specialty fish)

## Profitability Tips

### Most Profitable Fish
1. **Lava Eel** - Roe + valuable items (Spicy Eel, Gold Ore)
2. **Sturgeon** - Caviar production (500g each)
3. **Blobfish** - Chance for Pearls
4. **Ice Pip** - Diamonds and geodes
5. **Super Cucumber** - Iridium Ore

### Tips for Maximum Profit
- Build multiple ponds for different fish types
- Focus on Sturgeon for consistent Caviar income
- Keep ponds at maximum population (10 fish)
- Complete all pond quests promptly
- Process Roe in Preserves Jars
- Use the Fish Pond as passive income alongside crops and animals

## Related Buildings and Items

- **Preserves Jar:** Processes Roe into Aged Roe or Caviar
- **Fish Smoker:** Smokes fish to increase value
- **Recycling Machine:** Processes Trash into useful items
- **Rain Totem:** Ensures rain (some fish prefer rainy weather)
