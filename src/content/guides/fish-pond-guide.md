# Stardew Valley Fish Pond Guide: Complete Overview

The Fish Pond is a farm building that allows you to raise fish and produce roe for profit. It's an excellent way to generate passive income and produce valuable artisan goods. This comprehensive guide covers everything about Fish Ponds, from construction to fish selection to roe production.

## Fish Pond Overview

### What is a Fish Pond

A Fish Pond is a farm building that:
- Houses fish populations
- Produces roe regularly
- Can be upgraded for capacity
- Generates passive income

### Purpose

Fish Ponds serve several purposes:
1. **Roe Production**: Regular roe harvest
2. **Fish Breeding**: Population growth
3. **Passive Income**: Automated production
4. **Artisan Goods**: Roe for processing

---

## Construction

### Building Requirements

**Cost**: 5,000g
**Materials**: Stone (200), Seaweed (5), Green Algae (5)
**Size**: 5x5 tiles
**Source**: Robin's Carpenter's Shop
**Construction Time**: 2 days

### Materials Sources

| Material | Source |
|----------|--------|
| Stone | Mining, rocks on farm |
| Seaweed | Beach foraging, fishing |
| Green Algae | Fishing, mines |

### Multiple Ponds

You can build multiple Fish Ponds:
- No limit on quantity
- Different fish in each
- Maximize production
- Strategic placement

## Fish Pond Capacity

### Initial Capacity

**Starting Capacity**: 3 fish (most fish)
**Rare Fish Starting Capacity**: 1 fish (Sturgeon, Lava Eel, Ice Pip, Blobfish, Slimejack)

### Upgrading Capacity

**Upgrade Process**:
1. Add fish to pond
2. Wait for population growth
3. Complete quests (fish requests items)
4. Capacity increases

### Maximum Capacity

**Maximum**: 10 fish per pond

**Capacity Progression**:
| Level | Capacity | Quest Required |
|-------|----------|----------------|
| 1 | 3 fish | None |
| 2 | 5 fish | Quest 1 |
| 3 | 7 fish | Quest 2 |
| 4 | 10 fish | Quest 3 |

## Adding Fish to Pond

### How to Add Fish

1. **Catch Fish**: Use fishing rod
2. **Approach Pond**: Stand near Fish Pond
3. **Throw Fish**: Click on pond with fish selected
4. **Fish Added**: Population increases

### Fish Selection

**Important Rules**:
- Only one fish type per pond
- Cannot mix fish species
- Choose carefully
- Cannot change fish type easily

### Removing Fish

To remove fish:
- Use fishing rod on pond
- Catch fish from pond
- Fish removed from population
- Can relocate to another pond

## Roe Production

### Production Rate

Roe production depends on fish population. Each day there is a chance to produce roe based on the formula:

`base_chance = (population * 0.08) + 0.15`

| Population | Daily Roe Chance |
|------------|-----------------|
| 1 fish | 23% |
| 3 fish | 39% |
| 5 fish | 55% |
| 7 fish | 71% |
| 10 fish | 95% |

### Roe Quality

Roe quality can vary:
- Normal quality (base value)
- Silver quality (+25%)
- Gold quality (+50%)
- Iridium quality (+100%)

**Quality Factors**:
- Fish friendship (pond level)
- Random chance
- Higher population = better quality

### Roe Processing

Roe can be processed into:
- **Preserves Jar**: Aged Roe (2x value)
- **Mayonnaise Machine**: Fish Roe Mayonnaise (for certain fish)

**Aged Roe Value**:
| Roe Type | Base | Aged Value |
|----------|------|------------|
| Sturgeon Roe | 130g | 300g (Caviar 500g) |
| Blobfish Roe | 130g | 300g |
| Salmon Roe | 60g | 120g |

## Special Fish Products

### Sturgeon - Caviar

Sturgeon Roe can be made into Caviar:

- Sturgeon Roe base value: 130g
- Caviar value: 500g (base)

### Blobfish - Blobfish Roe

Blobfish produces valuable roe:
- Blobfish Roe: 130g
- Aged Roe: 300g
- Iridium Aged Roe: 600g

### Lava Eel - Spicy Eel

Lava Eels occasionally produce:
- Spicy Eel (rare drop)
- Value: 200g
- Buff food: Speed + Luck

### Super Cucumber - Purple Roe

Super Cucumber produces:
- Purple Roe (unique color)
- Value: 120g
- Aged: 240g

## Conclusion

Fish Ponds in Stardew Valley provide excellent passive income through roe production. Build Fish Ponds at Robin's shop, populate them with high-value fish like Sturgeon or Blobfish, and process roe into aged products for maximum profit.

Sturgeon ponds are the most profitable for Caviar production (500g), while Blobfish (130g roe) and Lava Eels (380g roe) offer excellent alternatives. Maintain maximum fish population (10 fish) for daily roe production, and process roe in Preserves Jars for doubled value. Fish Ponds represent a smart investment for long-term profit and sustainable income in Stardew Valley.
