## How to Access the Adventurer's Guild

Before you can meet Marlon, you must first unlock the Adventurer's Guild.

### Unlocking Requirements

The Adventurer's Guild becomes accessible after you reach **Level 5 in any combat-related skill** (Fighting, which is later renamed Combat). Once you reach this milestone, you will receive a letter from Marlon inviting you to visit the guild.

### Location

The Adventurer's Guild is located east of Pelican Town, across the river near the entrance to the mines. It is a small building that is easy to miss if you are not looking for it.

### Guild Hours

The guild is open from 2:00 PM to 10:00 PM. Marlon is always present during these hours.

## Adventurer's Guild Services

Marlon provides several essential services through the Adventurer's Guild.

### Monster Eradication Goals

Marlon posts monster eradication goals on the board in the guild. Completing these goals rewards the player with unique items and money.

#### Notable Monster Goals

| Goal | Requirement | Reward |
|------|-------------|--------|
| Slimes | Kill 1,000 Slimes | Master Slime Ring |
| Bugs | Kill 1,000 Bugs | Bug Steak |
| Bats | kill 200 Bats | Bat Wing Recipe |
| Serpents | kill 500 Serpents | Snake Milk Recipe |
| Dust Sprites | kill 500 Dust Sprites | Fairy Dust Recipe |
| Mummies | kill 100 Mummies | Iridium Ore (30) |
| Lava Lurks | kill 50 Lava Lurks | Iridium Ore (50) |
| Pepper Rex | kill 30 Pepper Rex | Iridium Ore (50) |
| Magma Duggy | kill 40 Magma Duggy | Iridium Ore (30) |
| Shadow Brute | kill 150 Shadow Brutes | Dwarf Scroll IV |
| Shadow Shaman | kill 80 Shadow Shamans | Dwarf Gadget |

### Monster Slayer's Badge

As you complete monster eradication goals, Marlon awards you Monster Slayer's Badges. These badges can be exchanged for powerful items:

- **1 Badge**: Slime Charmer Ring (prevents slime damage)
- **5 Badges**: Burglar's Ring (increases monster drop chance)
- **10 Badges**: Savage Ring (increases critical hit chance)
- **20 Badges**: Ring of Yoba (provides passive healing)
- **30 Badges**: Iridium Band (combines effects of multiple rings)

### Weapon and Armor Shop

Marlon sells basic weapons and armor for adventurers who are just starting out:

- **Forest Sword** - A basic sword
- **Steel Sword** - An upgrade from the Forest Sword
- **Steel Smallsword** - A fast but weaker sword
- **Wood Club** - A basic club
- **Hero's Ring** - A ring that provides combat bonuses
