# Complete NPC Birthday Calendar for Stardew Valley

Birthdays are the most important days for building friendships in Stardew Valley. Giving a gift on an NPC's birthday provides an **8x friendship multiplier**, making it the single most efficient way to boost relationships. This guide provides a complete calendar of all NPC birthdays organized by season, along with the best gift recommendations for each character.

## The Birthday Gift Bonus

### How the 8x Multiplier Works

When you give a gift on an NPC's birthday, the friendship points are multiplied by 8:

| Gift Tier | Normal Points | Birthday Points |
|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| Loved | +80 | +640 |
| Liked | +45 | +360 |
| Neutral | +20 | +160 |
| Disliked | -20 | -160 |
| Hated | -40 | -320 |

### Why Birthdays Matter

A single loved gift on a birthday provides **640 friendship points**, which is equivalent to 8 days of daily loved gifting. Missing a birthday means missing the biggest friendship opportunity of the year.

### Birthday Rules

- **One Gift Only**: You can still only give one gift per day, even on birthdays.
- **Multiplier Applies Once**: The 8x multiplier applies to the single gift you give.
- **No Stacking**: You cannot give multiple gifts on a birthday for multiple bonuses.
- **Gift Quality Matters**: Always give a loved gift on birthdays for maximum benefit.

## Advanced Birthday Strategies

### Year 1 Birthday Planning

In Year 1, resources are limited. Focus on:

1. **Spring Birthdays**: Use foraged items and simple cooked dishes.
2. **Summer Birthdays**: Cook with early crops.
3. **Fall Birthdays**: Use fall crops and foraged items.
4. **Winter Birthdays**: Use saved items from previous seasons.

### Multi-Year Optimization

Over multiple years, you can optimize your birthday gift-giving:

- **Rabbit's Foot Production**: Set up a Deluxe Coop with rabbits for consistent Rabbit's Foot production.
- **Pumpkin Storage**: Grow extra pumpkins in Fall and store them for year-round birthday gifts.
- **Diamond Mining**: Mine diamonds in the Skull Cavern for high-value birthday gifts.
- **Cooked Dishes**: Prepare loved dishes in bulk and store them for birthday use.

### Tracking Birthdays

Use this guide or an external tracker to remember upcoming birthdays:

- **TV Forecast**: The TV does not announce birthdays, so you need to track them yourself.
- **Calendar Mod**: Consider using a calendar mod if you frequently forget birthdays.
- **Save Scumming**: If you forget a birthday, you can reload a previous day's save to give the gift.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What happens if I miss an NPC's birthday?**
You simply miss the 8x bonus for that year. The NPC does not get upset about missing their birthday, but you lose a significant friendship opportunity.

**Can I give a birthday gift at any time during the day?**
Yes, you can give a birthday gift at any time during the day, as long as the NPC is accessible. However, some NPCs may be hard to find on their birthdays due to special schedules.

**Does the Secret Santa gift on Winter 25 count as a birthday gift?**
No, the Secret Santa gift is a separate event and does not provide the birthday bonus. It follows normal gift-giving rules.

**Do children (Jas, Vincent) have the same birthday bonus?**
Yes, children have the same 8x birthday multiplier as adult NPCs. However, their gift preferences are more limited.

**Can I give a birthday gift during a festival?**
If an NPC's birthday falls on a festival day, you can give them a gift at the festival. The birthday bonus still applies.
