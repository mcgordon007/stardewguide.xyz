# Stardew Valley Forge Enchantments Guide

The Volcano Forge on Ginger Island allows players to enchant tools and weapons using Cinder Shards. Each enchantment costs 1 Prismatic Shard and 20 Cinder Shards.

## Tool Enchantments

### Powerful
- **Effect:** Increases tool power by 1 tier (e.g., Steel Axe becomes Gold Axe strength)
- **Applies to:** Axe, Pickaxe, Hoe, Watering Can
- **Notes:** Stacks with tool upgrades, making even basic tools highly effective

### Reaching
- **Effect:** Increases tool reach by 1 tile in all directions
- **Applies to:** Axe, Pickaxe, Hoe, Watering Can, Scythe
- **Notes:** Allows you to affect a 5x5 area from the center tile with upgraded tools

### Generous
- **Effect:** 50% chance to get double the items when digging or panning
- **Applies to:** Hoe, Pan
- **Notes:** Does not apply to artifact spots or panning

### Archaeologist
- **Effect:** Doubles the chance of finding artifacts and bone fragments
- **Applies to:** Hoe
- **Notes:** Extremely useful for completing the Museum collection

### Efficient
- **Effect:** Tool use costs no energy
- **Applies to:** Axe, Pickaxe, Hoe, Watering Can
- **Notes:** One of the most practical enchantments for daily farm work

### Swift
- **Effect:** Charge-up time for the Hoe and Watering Can is 33% faster
- **Applies to:** Hoe, Watering Can
- **Notes:** Saves significant time when watering or tilling large areas

## Weapon Enchantments

### Vampiric
- **Effect:** 9% chance to recover some health on kill
- **Applies to:** Any weapon
- **Notes:** Amount healed scales with monster difficulty

### Crusader
- **Effect:** 50% more damage to mummies and slimes; prevents mummy revival
- **Applies to:** Any weapon
- **Notes:** Essential for Skull Cavern exploration

### Bug Killer
- **Effect:** Doubles damage to armored bugs and grubs
- **Applies to:** Any weapon
- **Notes:** Makes dangerous mine enemies much easier to handle

### Haymaker
- **Effect:** More fiber and hay drops from weeds; extra chance to get more produce from crops
- **Applies to:** Any weapon
- **Notes:** Useful when clearing large amounts of weeds or harvesting crops with a Scythe

### Magic
- **Effect:** 10% chance to recover some energy on kill
- **Applies to:** Any weapon
- **Notes:** Helpful for extended mining or combat sessions

### Artful
- **Effect:** Special move cooldown reduced by 50%
- **Applies to:** Any weapon
- **Notes:** Allows frequent use of powerful special attacks

## Fishing Enchantments

### Master
- **Effect:** Adds 1 level to fishing skill when using the rod
- **Applies to:** Fishing Rod
- **Notes:** Makes catching difficult fish significantly easier

### Auto-Hook
- **Effect:** Automatically hooks fish when they bite
- **Applies to:** Fishing Rod
- **Notes:** Perfect for players who struggle with reaction time

### Preserving
- **Effect:** 50% chance that tackle and bait are not consumed on cast
- **Applies to:** Fishing Rod
- **Notes:** Saves money on expensive tackle over time

## Forging Tips

- You can replace an existing enchantment by applying a new one
- The old enchantment is lost when overwritten
- Each tool/weapon can only have one enchantment at a time
- Enchantments are permanent unless replaced
- Save scumming (reloading the day) can be used to get desired enchantments
