# Stardew Valley SquidFest Guide - Winter Fishing Festival

**SquidFest** is a new fishing festival introduced in Stardew Valley 1.6. Held during winter on the beach, this festival challenges players to catch as many squid as possible over two days to earn valuable prizes.

## Festival Overview

### When and Where

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Date** | Winter 12-13 |
| **Time** | 6:10 AM - 2:00 AM |
| **Location** | Beach |
| **Season** | Winter |

### Special Feature

During SquidFest, **squid can be caught all day long**. Normally, squid only appear at night (after 6:00 PM), making this festival the best opportunity to catch them in large quantities.

## Prize Tiers

Prizes are awarded based on the number of squid caught each day. There are four prize tiers: Copper, Iron, Gold, and Iridium.

### Day 1 Prizes (Winter 12)

| Tier | Squid Caught | Prize | Additional Reward |
|------|-------------|-------|-------------------|
| Copper | 1 | Deluxe Bait (20) | - |
| Iron | 3 | Winter Seeds or Mystery Box | Dish O'' The Sea |
| Gold | 5 | Pearl | Coffee |
| Iridium | 8 | Squid Kid or The Art O'' Crabbing | - |

### Day 2 Prizes (Winter 13)

| Tier | Squid Caught | Prize | Additional Reward |
|------|-------------|-------|-------------------|
| Copper | 2 | Trap Bobber | - |
| Iron | 5 | Winter Seeds or Mystery Box | Dish O'' The Sea |
| Gold | 7 | Treasure Chest | Triple Shot Espresso |
| Iridium | 10 | Squid Hat or The Art O'' Crabbing | - |

### Prize Details

- **Deluxe Bait (20):** High-quality bait that reduces time until fish bite
- **Winter Seeds:** Seeds that grow winter forage items
- **Mystery Box:** Contains a random surprise item
- **Dish O'' The Sea:** Food buff that increases fishing skill (+3)
- **Pearl:** A rare and valuable item (2,500g sell price)
- **Coffee:** Speed buff for faster movement
- **Squid Kid:** A decorative furniture item
- **The Art O'' Crabbing:** A decorative furniture item/book
- **Trap Bobber:** Fishing tackle that reduces escape progress when fish isn''t in the bar
- **Treasure Chest:** Contains valuable random items
- **Triple Shot Espresso:** Stronger speed buff
- **Squid Hat:** A wearable cosmetic hat

## Squid Fishing Tips

### Using Squid Bait

**Squid Bait** is specially designed to attract squid more effectively:

- Crafted using a **Bait Maker**
- Place squid in the Bait Maker to create Squid Bait
- Squid Bait increases the chance of catching squid

### Optimal Setup

For maximum squid catches during SquidFest:

1. **Iridium Rod** - Equipped with bait and tackle
2. **Squid Bait** - Use in the Bait Maker before the festival
3. **Trap Bobber** - Reduces escape progress for easier catches
4. **Dish O'' The Sea** - Eat before fishing for +3 fishing buff
5. **Coffee** - For faster movement between fishing spots

### Fishing Strategy

- Fish from the beach docks or shoreline
- Squid are relatively easy to catch (difficulty: 45)
- Cast as frequently as possible to maximize catches
- Use the fishing mini-game practice to improve your skills before the festival

## Day-by-Day Strategy

### Day 1 (Winter 12)

- Goal: Catch at least 8 squid for Iridium tier
- Focus on speed and quantity
- Use Deluxe Bait if available
- Eat Dish O'' The Sea for fishing buff

### Day 2 (Winter 13)

- Goal: Catch at least 10 squid for Iridium tier
- Requirements are higher than Day 1
- Use Trap Bobber from Day 1 prize if earned
- Continue using Squid Bait for best results

## Reward Priorities

### Most Valuable Rewards

1. **Pearl** (Day 1 Gold) - 2,500g sell price, very rare
2. **Treasure Chest** (Day 2 Gold) - Contains valuable items
3. **Squid Hat** (Day 2 Iridium) - Unique cosmetic item
4. **Deluxe Bait** (Day 1 Copper) - Useful for future fishing

### Tips for Maximizing Rewards

- Participate on both days to earn the full set of prizes
- Prepare Squid Bait in advance using the Bait Maker
- Stock up on food buffs before the festival
- Clear your inventory to make room for all the squid you''ll catch

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I need to keep the squid I catch?**
The festival tracks your catches automatically. You can sell or discard the squid afterward.

**Can I catch squid outside of SquidFest?**
Yes, but only at night (6:00 PM - 2:00 AM) during winter. SquidFest is special because squid are available all day.

**What is the Bait Maker?**
The Bait Maker is a crafting station that converts fish into specialized bait. It becomes available after reaching a certain fishing skill level.

**Do I need to stay at the beach all day?**
You can come and go, but to maximize catches, it''s best to spend as much time fishing as possible.

**Are the prizes the same every year?**
Yes, SquidFest returns every winter with the same prize structure.
