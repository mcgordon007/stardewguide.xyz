# Stardew Valley Enricher Guide

## Introduction

**Enricher** is an automatic fertilization device added in Stardew Valley 1.6. When placed on farmland, it automatically applies fertilizer to planted crops, saving the trouble of manual fertilization.

## Acquisition

Enricher can be obtained in the following ways:

- **Mr. Qi's Walnut Room**: Purchase with Qi Gems
- **Unlock Condition**: Need to complete Ginger Island exploration and collect enough Golden Walnuts to enter Mr. Qi's Walnut Room

## Usage

1. Place Enricher on tilled farmland
2. Plant crop seeds on the tile where Enricher is located
3. Enricher automatically applies fertilizer effect to crops

## How It Works

- Enricher automatically applies **Quality Fertilizer** effect
- This means crops have a higher probability of producing Silver, Gold, or even Iridium quality produce
- No need to manually use fertilizer, saving time and inventory space

## Notes

- Enricher can only affect the single tile of farmland it is on
- Needs to be used with tilled farmland
- Does not affect crops on surrounding tiles
- Can be used on both Ginger Island and main farm

## Strategy Tips

- Use Enricher when planting high-value crops (such as Ancient Fruit, Sweet Gem Berry, Starfruit) to maximize profits
- Suitable for large-scale farm automation layouts
- Works best when combined with Sprinklers and Pressure Nozzles

## Related Items

- **Quality Fertilizer**: Fertilizer effect automatically applied by Enricher
- **Deluxe Fertilizer**: Higher quality fertilizer, but needs to be manually applied
- **Speed-Gro**: Enricher does not apply Speed-Gro effect

## Version History

- **Version 1.6**: Added item
