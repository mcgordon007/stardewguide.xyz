# Stardew Valley Flower Dance Guide

## Overview

The **Flower Dance** is a spring festival in *Stardew Valley* that celebrates the beauty of the season with music, dancing, and socializing.

## Date and Time

- **Date:** Spring 24
- **Time:** 9:00 AM – 2:00 PM
- **Entry:** Enter Cindersap Forest between 9:00 AM and 2:00 PM to participate

## Location

**Cindersap Forest** — The forest west of Pelican Town transforms into a festive dance floor with a decorated pavilion.

## How to Participate

1. Travel to Cindersap Forest during festival hours.
2. Talk to villagers and enjoy the atmosphere.
3. You may invite a bachelor or bachelorette to dance **if you have at least 4 hearts of friendship** with them.

## Dancing Mechanics

- You can ask any marriage candidate to dance, provided your friendship level is **4 hearts or higher**.
- Dancing with a villager grants a **+250 friendship points boost** with your dance partner.
- If no eligible partner is available, you can still watch the dance and enjoy the festival.

## Activities

- Socialize with villagers
- Watch the flower dance ceremony
- Dance with a marriage candidate (requires 4+ hearts)

## Friendship Boost

- Dancing with a partner: **+250 friendship points**
- Talking to villagers during the festival provides the standard friendship gain for the day.

## Tips

- **Plan ahead:** Focus on building friendships with your desired dance partner before Spring 24.
- **Gifts help:** Give loved gifts twice a week to quickly raise hearts.
- **No shop:** Unlike some festivals, there is no special shop at the Flower Dance.
- **Single players only:** You cannot dance with non-marriage-candidate villagers.

## Notes

- The festival ends automatically at 2:00 PM, and you return to the farm at 10:00 PM.
- Time is paused during the festival, so you won't lose any farm work hours.
- The Flower Dance is a great opportunity to significantly boost friendship with a potential spouse.
