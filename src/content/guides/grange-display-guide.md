# Stardew Valley Fair Grange Display Guide

## Overview

The **Grange Display** is a competition held during the **Stardew Valley Fair** on **Tuesday, Fall 16**. Players submit up to 9 items to be judged by Governor. Winning first place rewards **1,000 Star Tokens**, which can be exchanged for rare prizes.

## Scoring System

Your Grange Display is scored on a point-based system. To win **first place**, you need **90 or more points**.

### Base Points

- You start with **14 base points**.

### Category Bonuses

You gain points by placing items from diverse categories. Each unique category represented gives bonus points:

- **8 or more categories**: +9 points
- **7 categories**: +7 points
- **6 categories**: +5 points
- **5 categories**: +3 points
- **4 categories**: +1 point

### Item Quality Points

Each item is scored individually based on its quality and type:

| Quality | Points |
|---------|--------|
| Iridium | +8 points |
| Gold | +6 points |
| Silver | +4 points |
| Normal | +2 points |

### Category Points per Item

Certain high-value items give bonus points in their categories:

- **Animal Products** (Milk, Eggs, Wool, etc.): Up to +5 points for high-quality large products
- **Artisan Goods** (Wine, Jelly, Honey, Cheese, etc.): Up to +5 points for high-value goods
- **Cooking**: Up to +5 points for high-quality dishes
- **Fish**: Up to +5 points for rare/legendary fish
- **Foraging**: Up to +5 points for high-quality foraged items
- **Fruits**: Up to +5 points
- **Vegetables**: Up to +5 points
- **Minerals**: Up to +5 points for rare gems and minerals

### Penalty

- **Empty slot**: -1 point per empty slot (you should always fill all 9 slots)

## Winning Strategy (90+ Points)

### Recommended Items for Maximum Score

To reliably score 90+ points, include a diverse selection of high-quality items:

1. **Iridium Quality Legendary Fish** (e.g., Crimsonfish, Angler, Legend, Glacierfish, Mutant Carp) — +8 points each
2. **Iridium Quality Animal Products** (Large Milk, Large Egg, Rabbit's Foot, Duck Feather) — +8 points
3. **High-Value Artisan Goods** (Starfruit Wine, Ancient Fruit Wine, Truffle Oil) — category bonus + quality
4. **Iridium Quality Crops** (Pumpkin, Melon, Cauliflower, Starfruit) — +8 points
5. **Rare Minerals** (Prismatic Shard, Diamond, Ruby) — category bonus
6. **Cooked Dishes** (Strange Bun, Seafoam Pudding, or high-quality recipes)
7. **Foraged Items** (Purple Mushroom, Crystal Fruit in Fall)

### Optimal 9-Item Setup Example

| Slot | Item | Category |
|------|------|----------|
| 1 | Iridium Starfruit Wine | Artisan Goods |
| 2 | Iridium Large Goat Milk | Animal Products |
| 3 | Legend Fish | Fish |
| 4 | Iridium Pumpkin | Vegetables |
| 5 | Prismatic Shard | Minerals |
| 6 | Rabbit's Foot | Animal Products |
| 7 | Iridium Crystal Fruit | Foraging |
| 8 | Strange Bun | Cooking |
| 9 | Iridium Melon | Fruits |

This setup covers 8 categories with mostly iridium quality items, easily exceeding 90 points.

## Tips for Success

1. **Plan Ahead**: Start saving high-quality items throughout the year.
2. **Diversify Categories**: Cover as many categories as possible.
3. **Quality Over Quantity**: Iridium and Gold quality items make a huge difference.
4. **Artisan Goods**: Processed goods like Wine and Jelly often score higher than raw crops.
5. **Legendary Fish**: If you have caught any legendary fish, they are excellent display items.
6. **Don't Leave Slots Empty**: Always fill all 9 slots to avoid penalties.

## Rewards

- **First Place (90+ points)**: 1,000 Star Tokens
- **Second Place**: 500 Star Tokens
- **Third Place**: 200 Star Tokens
- **Fourth Place**: 50 Star Tokens

Star Tokens can be exchanged for prizes at the Fair's token shop, including the rare **Stardrop** (2,000 tokens) and exclusive furniture.
