# Books Guide

The Books system was introduced in Stardew Valley 1.6, adding a new layer of permanent character progression. Books grant permanent advantages, skill experience, and quality-of-life upgrades when read.

## TL;DR

Books are permanent upgrade items introduced in 1.6. There are 19 Power Books that grant permanent abilities and 7 Skill Books that give experience points. The Bookseller visits Pelican Town twice per season. Most Power Books can be bought from the Bookseller starting Year 3 for 20,000g each.

## What are Books?

Books are items that, when read, provide permanent benefits to your character. They can be obtained from the Bookseller, found in the world, or earned as rewards.

### How to Read Books

1. Select the book in the top row of your inventory
2. Right-click (PC) or press the use button
3. The book is consumed and its effect is applied permanently

**Note:** In multiplayer, each player must read their own books to receive effects.

## The Bookseller

The Bookseller is a traveling merchant who visits Pelican Town twice per season on random days. His shop is located near the town square.

| Visit Frequency | Location |
|----------------|----------|
| 2 times per season | Near town square |

## Power Books (19 Total)

Power Books grant permanent abilities. Reading all 19 unlocks the "Well-Read" achievement.

### Utility Books

| Book | Effect | How to Obtain |
|------|--------|---------------|
| **Price Catalogue** | See item sell prices | Bookseller (3,000g) |
| **Animal Catalogue** | Access Marnie's shop anytime | Marnie (5,000g, Year 2+) |
| **Mapping Cave Systems** | 50% discount on Marlon's item retrieval | Adventurer's Guild box (kill 1,000 monsters) or Bookseller (20,000g, Year 3+) |
| **Friendship 101** | Friendship increases slightly faster | Lewis' house prize machine or Bookseller (20,000g, Year 3+) |

### Movement Books

| Book | Effect | How to Obtain |
|------|--------|---------------|
| **Way Of The Wind pt. 1** | Run slightly faster | Bookseller (15,000g) |
| **Way Of The Wind pt. 2** | Run even faster (stacks with pt. 1) | Bookseller (35,000g, after reading pt. 1) |
| **Ol' Slitherlegs** | Run much faster through grass and crops | Bookseller (25,000g) |
| **Horse: The Book** | Extra speed when riding horses | Bookseller (25,000g) |

### Combat Books

| Book | Effect | How to Obtain |
|------|--------|---------------|
| **Monster Compendium** | Small chance for monsters to drop double loot | Monster drops or Bookseller (20,000g, Year 3+) |
| **Jack Be Nimble, Jack Be Thick** | +1 Defense | Artifact Spots or Bookseller (20,000g, Year 3+) |
| **Dwarvish Safety Manual** | Bombs deal 25% less damage to you | Dwarf (4,000g) or Bookseller (20,000g, Year 3+) |

## Skill Books (7 Total)

Skill Books grant experience points. Unlike Power Books, they do not count toward achievements.

| Book | Effect | How to Obtain |
|------|--------|---------------|
| **Bait And Bobber** | 250 Fishing XP | Bookseller (5,000g - 10,000g), Mystery Boxes, various locations |
| **Combat Quarterly** | 250 Combat XP | Bookseller (5,000g - 10,000g), Mystery Boxes, various locations |
| **Mining Monthly** | 250 Mining XP | Bookseller (5,000g - 10,000g), Mystery Boxes, various locations |
| **Stardew Valley Almanac** | 250 Farming XP | Bookseller (5,000g - 10,000g), Mystery Boxes, various locations |
| **Woodcutter's Weekly** | 250 Foraging XP | Bookseller (5,000g - 10,000g), Desert Festival, Mystery Boxes, various locations |
| **Book Of Stars** | 250 XP in all skills | Bookseller (15,000g), Mystery Boxes, raccoon rewards |
| **Queen Of Sauce Cookbook** | Learn all Queen of Sauce recipes | Bookseller (50,000g, after 100 Golden Walnuts) |

### Skill Book Locations

Skill Books can be found in many places:
- Bookseller (various prices)
- Mystery Boxes and Golden Mystery Boxes
- Fishing Treasure Chests
- Artifact Spots
- Shaking trees with seeds
- Chopping trees
- Slaying monsters in mines
- Lewis' house prize machine
- Traveling Cart

## Re-reading Books

After reading a Power Book once, you can read it again for additional benefits:

| Book Type | Re-read Benefit |
|-----------|----------------|
| Most Power Books | 100 XP in relevant skill |
| Way Of The Wind / Friendship 101 / Horse Book / Ol' Slitherlegs | 20 XP in each skill |
| Price Catalogue / Animal Catalogue | No additional benefit |
| Skill Books | Same XP reward again |

## Tips

- **Priority books:** Price Catalogue (early game), Animal Catalogue (convenience), Way Of The Wind (speed)
- **Save money:** Many books can be found for free before buying from the Bookseller
- **Year 3 strategy:** Most Power Books become available at the Bookseller in Year 3 for 20,000g each
- **Penny loves books:** All books are loved gifts for Penny
