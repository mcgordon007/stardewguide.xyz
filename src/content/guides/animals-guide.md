# Complete Animal Guide

Animals are a key part of any successful farm in Stardew Valley. They provide a steady stream of products that can be sold raw or processed into artisan goods for significantly higher profits. This guide covers every animal type in detail, including purchase prices, housing requirements, product types, happiness mechanics, and artisan processing strategies.

## Overview: Coops vs Barns

There are two types of animal housing: coops and barns. Each can be upgraded to house more animals and unlock new animal types.

### Coop Upgrades

| Level | Animals | Cost | Materials | Animals Available |
|-------|---------|------|-----------|-----------------|
| Coop | 4 animals | 4,000g | 300 Wood, 100 Stone | Chickens |
| Big Coop | 8 animals | 10,000g | 400 Wood, 150 Stone | + Ducks, Dinosaurs |
| Deluxe Coop | 12 animals | 20,000g | 500 Wood, 200 Stone | + Rabbits |

## Happiness Mechanics

Animal happiness affects product quality and production rate. Happy animals produce higher quality products more frequently.

### Happiness Factors

| Factor | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| Fed today | +4 happiness |
| Petted today | +4 happiness |
| Outside today (if coop/barn has door open) | +4 happiness |
| Not fed today | -20 happiness |
| Not petted today | -4 happiness |
| No hay available | -10 happiness |

### Happiness Levels

| Happiness | Hearts | Effect |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| 0-39 | 0 | Unhappy, reduced production |
| 40-79 | 1 | Below average |
| 80-119 | 2 | Average |
| 120-159 | 3 | Above average |
| 160-199 | 4 | Happy |
| 200-255 | 5 | Max happiness, best production |

### Product Quality by Friendship

## Animal Pregnancy

### Mammal Pregnancy (Cows, Goats, Sheep, Pigs)

- Requires a male and female of the same species in the same barn
- Pregnancy lasts 14 days
- Results in a baby animal
- Baby animals take 5-10 days to mature into adults
- Can be disabled by closing the barn door at night

### Incubation (Chickens, Ducks)

- Place an egg in an incubator (available in Big Coop)
- Incubation takes approximately 5-6 days
- Results in a baby animal of the same species
- No pregnancy mechanic needed

## Tips for Animal Management

1. **Build Deluxe Barns first** for pigs (highest profit)
2. **Use Auto-Grabbers and Auto-Petters** to automate animal care
3. **Let animals outside** on sunny days for happiness bonus
4. **Close barn/coop doors at night** during winter to prevent pregnancy
5. **Fill hay troughs daily** to ensure animals are always fed
6. **Process all products** into artisan goods for maximum profit
7. **Upgrade coops and barns** as soon as you can afford to house more animals
8. **Buy animals in spring** so they have maximum time to produce before winter
9. **Keep hay stocked** - buy hay from Marnie or grow grass and cut it
10. **Check animal happiness daily** - happy animals produce better products
