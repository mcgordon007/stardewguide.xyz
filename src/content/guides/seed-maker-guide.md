# Seed Maker Guide

## Unlock Requirements

The Seed Maker is unlocked at **Farming Level 9**.

## Crafting Materials

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Wood | 25 |
| Gold Bar | 1 |
| Coal | 10 |

## Function

The Seed Maker can convert mature crops into seeds:

- Place any harvestable crop into the Seed Maker, and after approximately **20 minutes** of game time, it will produce seeds.
- Output quantity: 1-3 seeds (average of about 2).
- Small chance to produce **Mixed Seeds** or an **Ancient Seed**.
- Ancient Seeds are extremely rare and are the key to obtaining Ancient Fruit.

## Usage Tips

- Best used for seed duplication of high-value crops, such as Ancient Fruit and Starfruit.
- Use in conjunction with the Greenhouse for year-round planting of high-value crops.
- Crafting multiple Seed Makers can improve seed production efficiency.
- Note: The quality of the input crop does not affect the number of seeds produced.

## Applicable Crops

Almost all harvestable crops can be placed in the Seed Maker, including:
- Spring crops: Parsnip, Cauliflower, Potato, etc.
- Summer crops: Melon, Blueberry, Starfruit, etc.
- Fall crops: Pumpkin, Corn, Eggplant, etc.
- Special crops: Ancient Fruit, Sweet Gem Berry (produces Mixed Seeds)
