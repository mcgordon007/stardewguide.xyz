# Stardew Valley Dance of the Moonlight Jellies Guide

## Overview

The **Dance of the Moonlight Jellies** is a serene and beautiful summer festival in *Stardew Valley* where the townsfolk gather at the beach to watch glowing jellyfish migrate along the coast.

## Date and Time

- **Date:** Summer 28
- **Time:** 10:00 PM – 12:00 AM
- **Entry:** Enter The Beach between 10:00 PM and 12:00 AM to participate

## Location

**The Beach** — The eastern shore of Pelican Town becomes a magical viewing area for the jellyfish migration.

## How to Participate

1. Travel to The Beach between 10:00 PM and midnight.
2. Talk to villagers who have gathered to watch the spectacle.
3. Stand on the pier or beach to watch the glowing jellyfish float by.

## Activities

- Socialize with villagers before and during the event
- Watch the mesmerizing jellyfish migration
- Enjoy the peaceful nighttime atmosphere
- Take in the unique seasonal experience

## Friendship Boost

- Talking to villagers during the festival provides the standard friendship gain for the day.
- While there is no specific dance or activity that grants bonus friendship like the Flower Dance, the shared experience strengthens community bonds.

## What Happens

- At the festival, Mayor Lewis gives a short speech.
- The townsfolk light lanterns and release them onto the water.
- Glowing moonlight jellies drift past the shore, creating a breathtaking visual display.
- The event is purely observational and social — no minigames or competitions.

## Tips

- **End of Summer:** This festival marks the final day of Summer, so make sure to complete any summer-specific tasks beforehand.
- **Crops:** Harvest all summer crops before attending, as the next day is Fall 1.
- **Socialize:** Talk to every villager for maximum friendship gains.
- **No shop:** There are no vendors or special items for sale at this festival.
- **Atmosphere:** This is one of the most visually stunning events in the game — enjoy the moment!

## Notes

- The festival ends at midnight, and you return to the farm at 12:00 AM.
- Time progresses normally during this festival (unlike daytime festivals where time is paused).
- The transition from Summer to Fall happens immediately after the festival.
- Make sure to plant any fall crops and prepare your farm for the new season the next morning.
