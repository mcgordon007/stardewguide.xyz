# Stardew Valley Winter Crops Guide - Complete Overview

Winter is the only season in Stardew Valley where standard outdoor crops cannot grow. However, that does not mean farming stops entirely. This guide covers everything you can grow and forage during winter, including Winter Seeds, greenhouse farming, and Ginger Island year-round crops.

## Winter Forageables

During Winter, four unique forageable items appear across Stardew Valley. These items are essential for crafting Winter Seeds and completing bundles.

### Winter Forageable Items

| Item | Sell Price | Energy Restored | Health Restored | Location |
|------|-----------|-----------------|-----------------|----------|
| Winter Root | 70g | 25 | 11 | Dig with Hoe in dirt/snow areas |
| Crystal Fruit | 150g | 63 | 28 | Found on the ground |
| Snow Yam | 100g | 30 | 13 | Dig with Hoe in dirt/snow areas |
| Crocus | 60g | 0 | 0 | Found on the ground |

### Where to Find Winter Forageables

| Location | Items Found | Method |
|----------|-------------|--------|
| Pelican Town | Crocus, Crystal Fruit | Ground forage |
| Cindersap Forest | Crocus, Crystal Fruit, Holly | Ground forage |
| Mountain | Winter Root, Snow Yam | Hoe dirt patches |
| Railroad | Winter Root, Snow Yam | Hoe dirt patches |
| Backwoods | Winter Root, Snow Yam | Hoe dirt patches |
| Beach | Crocus, Crystal Fruit | Ground forage |

**Tip:** Winter Roots and Snow Yams are found by using the Hoe on tillable dirt or snow patches in outdoor areas. They do not spawn on the ground like other forageables.

## Winter Seeds

Winter Seeds are the primary way to grow crops outdoors during winter. They produce a random winter forageable when harvested.

### Crafting Winter Seeds

| Recipe | Ingredients | Unlocked By |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| Winter Seeds | Winter Root (1), Crystal Fruit (1), Snow Yam (1), Crocus (1) | Foraging Level 7 |

### Winter Seed Stats

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Growth Time | 7 days |
| Harvest | Random winter forageable |
| Regrowth | No |
| Sell Price (crafted) | Free (foraged ingredients) |
| Average Harvest Value | ~95g |

### Winter Seed Strategy

Winter Seeds are an excellent low-effort income source during winter:

- Plant as many Winter Seeds as you can craft
- Harvest every 7 days
- Replant immediately after harvest
- Use Deluxe Speed-Gro to reduce growth to 5 days
- Process Crystal Fruits into jelly or wine for extra profit

**Profit Example:** With 100 Winter Seed plants, you can harvest approximately 4 times during winter, yielding ~38,000g in raw forageables or significantly more if processed.

## Greenhouse Crops in Winter

The Greenhouse is the most important structure for winter farming. Once unlocked, you can grow any season's crops year-round inside.

### Unlocking the Greenhouse

| Route | Requirement | Cost |
|-------|-------------|------|
| Community Center | Complete all Pantry bundles | Free |
| Joja Mart | Purchase Greenhouse development | 35,000g |

### Best Greenhouse Crops for Winter

Since outdoor farming stops in winter, the Greenhouse becomes your primary crop income source.

| Crop | Seed Cost | Growth Time | Regrowth | Base Sell | Best Processing |
|------|-----------|-------------|----------|-----------|-----------------|
| Ancient Fruit | Ancient Seed | 28 days | 7 days | 550g | Wine: 1,650g (2,310g Artisan) |
| Starfruit | 400g (Oasis) | 13 days | None | 750g | Wine: 2,250g (3,150g Artisan) |
| Cranberry | 240g | 7 days | 5 days | 130g (x2) | Jelly: 200g (280g Artisan) |
| Blueberry | 80g | 13 days | 4 days | 50g (x3) | Wine: 150g (210g Artisan) |
| Hops | 60g | 11 days | 1 day | 25g | Pale Ale: 300g (420g Artisan) |

### Greenhouse Winter Strategy

1. **Ancient Fruit (Recommended):** Fill the Greenhouse with Ancient Fruit for passive income every 7 days
2. **Starfruit Rotation:** Plant Starfruit for maximum wine value, replanting after each harvest
3. **Mixed Setup:** Combine Ancient Fruit (for steady income) with Starfruit (for high-value bursts)

For a complete Greenhouse layout guide, see the [Greenhouse Guide](/guides/greenhouse-guide).

## Ginger Island Year-Round Farming

Ginger Island, introduced in version 1.5, allows year-round crop farming regardless of the season in the main valley.

### Unlocking Ginger Island

1. Complete the Community Center or Joja Warehouse
2. Receive Willy's letter the next day
3. Visit Willy's Fish Shop back room
4. Repair the boat:
   - 200 Hardwood
   - 5 Battery Packs
   - 5 Iridium Bars

### Ginger Island Winter Crops

| Crop | Seed Source | Growth Time | Sell Price | Notes |
|------|-------------|-------------|------------|-------|
| Pineapple | Island Trader (1 Golden Walnut) | 14 days | 300g | Can become giant crop |
| Taro Root | Island Trader (1 Golden Walnut) | 10 days (watered) | 100g | Grows faster near water |
| Ginger | Forage from dig spots | Instant | 60g | Found by hoeing dig spots |
| Banana | Banana Sapling (Golden Coconut) | 28 days (tree) | 150g | Tree produces year-round |
| Mango | Mango Sapling (Golden Coconut) | 28 days (tree) | 130g | Tree produces year-round |

### Ginger Island Farm Features

- **Year-round growing:** Any crop from any season can grow
- **No crows:** Scarecrows are not needed
- **Sprinklers work:** Standard sprinkler mechanics apply
- **Expandable:** Unlock more land with Golden Walnuts
- **No seasonal death:** Crops never die from season changes

### Best Ginger Island Strategy for Winter

While your main farm is frozen, Ginger Island provides excellent farming opportunities:

1. **Plant Ancient Fruit:** The island farm is perfect for Ancient Fruit since it grows forever
2. **Grow Starfruit:** Best profit per tile when processed into wine
3. **Plant Trees:** Banana and Mango trees produce fruit year-round on the island
4. **Taro Root:** Fast-growing crop for quick turnover

## Powdermelon (Version 1.6+)

Powdermelon is a new winter crop added in Stardew Valley 1.6.

### Powdermelon Stats

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Seed Cost | Free (dig up seeds) |
| Growth Time | 7 days |
| Sell Price | 60g |
| Giant Crop | Yes (3x3 pattern) |
| Location | Found by digging in winter |

### Powdermelon Seeds

Powdermelon Seeds can be obtained through various methods (available from Fall 21 to Winter 20):
- Digging up Seed Spots
- Completing Raccoon requests at the Giant Stump
- Trading 2 Pine Cones at the Raccoon Wife's Shop
- Catching Golden Fishing Treasure Chests (7% chance)
- Using the Seed Maker
- Prize Machine at Mayor's Manor
- Opening Mystery Boxes or Golden Mystery Boxes
- Breaking crates/barrels in the Mines, Skull Cavern, or Volcano Dungeon (2% chance)
- Slaying Iridium Golems (50% chance)

## Winter Farming Strategies

### Strategy 1: Greenhouse Focus

**Setup:**
- Fill Greenhouse with Ancient Fruit or Starfruit
- Place kegs/preserves jars nearby for processing
- Harvest every 7 days (Ancient Fruit) or replant every 13 days (Starfruit)

**Expected Income:**
- 120 Ancient Fruit plants = ~277,200g per harvest cycle (with Artisan)
- 120 Starfruit plants = ~378,000g per harvest cycle (with Artisan, replanting)

### Strategy 2: Winter Seeds + Foraging

**Setup:**
- Craft Winter Seeds from foraged items
- Plant large patches on your farm
- Hoe dirt patches daily for Winter Roots and Snow Yams
- Collect ground spawns for Crystal Fruits and Crocuses

**Expected Income:**
- 100 Winter Seeds = ~9,500g per week raw
- Additional ~2,000g/day from active foraging

### Strategy 3: Ginger Island Expansion

**Setup:**
- Unlock and expand the island farm (20+ Golden Walnuts)
- Plant high-value crops (Starfruit, Ancient Fruit)
- Set up processing on the island or ship back to main farm

**Expected Income:**
- Varies based on farm size, but can exceed 500,000g per season with full setup

### Strategy 4: Processing Stockpile

**Setup:**
- Use winter downtime to process stored crops from fall
- Fill kegs with Pumpkin Wine, Cranberry Wine, etc.
- Fill preserves jars with fall crops
- Age wine in casks (cellar)

**Expected Income:**
- Depends on stockpile size, but can generate massive passive income

## Winter Crop FAQ

### Can I grow regular crops outside in winter?

No. All standard outdoor crops die at the transition from Fall 28 to Winter 1. The only exceptions are Winter Seeds and Powdermelon Seeds.

### What is the best winter crop?

Ancient Fruit in the Greenhouse is the best overall winter "crop" since it produces indefinitely every 7 days. For outdoor farming, Winter Seeds are your only option besides Powdermelon.

### Should I bother with Winter Seeds?

Yes. Winter Seeds are essentially free income since the ingredients are foraged. They require minimal effort and provide steady gold during a season when other farming stops.

### How do I get enough Winter Seeds?

- Forage daily for all four winter forageables
- Use the Seed Maker on Crystal Fruits (highest value forageable)
- Hoe every dirt patch you see for Winter Roots and Snow Yams

### Is the Greenhouse worth unlocking before winter?

Absolutely. Unlocking the Greenhouse before winter ensures you have a steady crop income during the coldest season. Prioritize completing the Pantry bundles in the Community Center.

### Can fruit trees grow in winter?

Fruit trees planted outdoors will not produce fruit in winter. However, fruit trees planted inside the Greenhouse or on Ginger Island will produce fruit year-round.
