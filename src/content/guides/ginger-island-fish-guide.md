# Stardew Valley Ginger Island Fish Guide

Ginger Island is a tropical paradise introduced in version 1.5, accessible after repairing Willy's boat. Unlike the main valley, **season does not affect which fish can be caught on Ginger Island** - all island fish are available year-round!

---

## How to Access Ginger Island

1. Complete the Community Center or Joja Warehouse
2. Receive Willy's letter about the boat repair
3. Gather materials: 200 Hardwood, 5 Battery Packs, 5 Iridium Bars
4. Wait for the boat repair cutscene
5. Pay 1,000g per trip to the island

---

## Ginger Island Exclusive Fish

These fish can **only** be caught on Ginger Island:

### 1. Blue Discus

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Location** | Island North and Island West (freshwater) |
| **Season** | All seasons |
| **Time** | Any |
| **Weather** | Any |
| **Difficulty** | 60 (Dart behavior) |
| **Sell Price** | 120g |

A brightly colored tropical fish popular in aquariums. Can be used to trade for an Oceanic Rug at the Island Trader (3 Blue Discus on Saturdays).

**Fish Pond:**
- Initial capacity: 3, max: 10
- Reproduces every 3 days
- Quests: 3 Taro Root, then 10 Taro Root
- Special outputs: Banana, Golden Coconut

---

### 2. Lionfish

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Location** | Island West (ocean), South, and Southeast |
| **Season** | All seasons |
| **Time** | Any |
| **Weather** | Any |
| **Difficulty** | 50 (Smooth behavior) |
| **Sell Price** | 100g |

An aggressive, predatory fish with venomous spines. Can be traded for a Tropical Chair at the Island Trader (1 Lionfish on even days).

**Fish Pond:**
- Initial capacity: 3, max: 10
- Reproduces every 3 days
- Quests: 3 Ginger or 1 Pineapple, then 1 Mango
- Special outputs: Taro Tuber, Tiger Slime Egg

---

### 3. Stingray

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Location** | Pirate Cove (Island Southeast) |
| **Season** | All seasons |
| **Time** | Any |
| **Weather** | Any |
| **Difficulty** | 80 (Sinker behavior) |
| **Sell Price** | 180g |

Despite having a toxic stinger, these fish are shy and prefer to avoid humans. The Pirate Cove is the **only location** in the game where Stingrays can be caught. Can be traded for Mahogany Seeds at the Island Trader.

**How to access Pirate Cove:**
- Build the Island Beach Resort (20 Golden Walnuts)
- Enter through the hidden entrance east of the star-shaped tide pool

**Fish Pond:**
- Initial capacity: 3, max: 10
- Reproduces every 4 days
- Quests: 7 Cinder Shards, then 1 Dragon Tooth
- Special outputs: Magma Cap, Cinder Shard, Dragon Tooth, Battery Pack

## Non-Exclusive Fish Available on Ginger Island

These fish from the main valley can also be caught on Ginger Island year-round:

### Ocean Fish (Island West, South, Southeast)
- **Tuna** - Any season, 6am-7pm
- **Pufferfish** - Any season, 12pm-4pm (Sunny only)
- **Red Snapper** - Any season, 6am-7pm (Rain only)
- **Squid** - Any season, 6pm-2am
- **Sea Cucumber** - Any season, 6am-7pm
- **Super Cucumber** - Any season, 6pm-2am
- **Octopus** - Any season, 6am-1pm
- **Flounder** - Any season, 6am-8pm
- **Halibut** - Any season, 6am-11am, 7pm-2am
- **Tilapia** - Any season, 6am-2pm

### Freshwater Fish (Island North, West)
- **Tilapia** - Any season, 6am-2pm
- **Blue Discus** - Any season, any time

## Pirate Cove Special Catches

In addition to Stingrays, the Pirate Cove allows you to catch:
- **Gourmand Statue** - Appears as a present box when caught
- All ocean fish listed above

### Pirate Cove Events
- Pirates visit after 8pm on non-rainy even-numbered days
- Play darts to win Golden Walnuts (rewards for first 3 wins)
- Wear a Pirate Hat, Eye Patch, Deluxe Pirate Hat, or Parrot Egg trinket to get free Mead at the bar

---

## Ginger Island Fishing Tips

1. **Season Doesn't Matter** - All fish are available year-round
2. **Bring Bait** - The island has many fishing spots, so come prepared
3. **Unlock Pirate Cove First** - Stingrays are exclusive there and valuable
4. **Check the Island Trader** - Lionfish and Blue Discus can be traded for unique furniture
5. **Fish Pond Opportunities** - Island fish have unique pond outputs like Golden Coconuts and Battery Packs
6. **Tropical Fish Special Order** - Willy may request 5 of each island fish for a special order quest
