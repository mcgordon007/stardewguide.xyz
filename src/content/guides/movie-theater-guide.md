# Stardew Valley Movie Theater Guide: Complete Overview

The Movie Theater is a wonderful late-game feature in Stardew Valley that allows you to watch movies with NPCs, boosting friendship and unlocking unique dialogue. Located in the old abandoned Joja Mart building, the theater becomes a community hub after you complete certain game milestones. This comprehensive guide covers everything about the Movie Theater, from unlocking it to maximizing friendship through movie outings.

## How to Unlock the Movie Theater

The Movie Theater becomes available after completing one of two main storyline paths.

### Community Center Route

1. **Complete all Community Center bundles** including the Boiler Room, Crafts Room, Pantry, Fish Tank, Bulletin Board, and Vault.
2. **Attend the Community Center completion ceremony** where the Junimos restore the building.
3. **The Movie Theater will automatically unlock** in the old Joja Mart location.

### Joja Mart Route

1. **Purchase all Joja Mart community development projects** from Morris, including the Movie Theater.
2. **The Movie Theater costs 500,000g** through the Joja route, making it significantly more expensive.
3. **Once purchased**, the theater becomes available immediately.

### After Unlocking

Once the theater is unlocked, you will receive a letter from Gunther announcing the grand opening. The theater is located in the building that previously housed Joja Mart (or was abandoned if you chose the Community Center route).

## Theater Basics

### Location and Hours

- **Location**: The old Joja Mart building in Pelican Town
- **Hours**: The theater is open from 2:00 PM to 10:00 PM
- **Days**: The theater operates on specific days depending on the current movie schedule
- **Closed**: Festival days and certain holidays

### Ticket Prices

| Ticket Type | Price | Notes |
|-------------|-------|-------|
| Single Ticket | 250g | Standard admission |
| Group Ticket | 1,000g | Covers you and one NPC friend |

### How to Attend

1. **Enter the theater** during operating hours.
2. **Purchase a ticket** from the ticket booth.
3. **Choose a movie** from the current selection.
4. **Optionally invite an NPC** to watch with you (requires a Group Ticket).
5. **Select your seats** and enjoy the show.

## Watching Movies with Friends

One of the best features of the Movie Theater is the ability to invite NPCs to watch movies with you.

### How to Invite an NPC

1. **Talk to an NPC** who is available on the day the theater is open.
2. **Select the "Movie" dialogue option** when it appears.
3. **The NPC will agree or decline** depending on their mood and friendship level.
4. **Purchase a Group Ticket** (1,000g) and meet the NPC at the theater.

### Friendship Boosts

Watching a movie with an NPC provides a significant friendship boost:

- **Standard movie**: +50 friendship points
- **Movie the NPC loves**: +80 friendship points
- **Movie the NPC hates**: +10 friendship points (reduced boost)
- **Concession gift**: Additional friendship points based on the item

## Movie Theater Tips and Strategies

### Maximizing Friendship

1. **Watch movies weekly**: The theater typically has movies showing once per week. Attend every showing for consistent friendship gains.
2. **Choose the right movie**: Select movies that your target NPC loves for maximum friendship boosts.
3. **Bring the right concession**: Purchase the concession that your NPC companion loves for additional friendship points.
4. **Rotate NPCs**: Take different NPCs to the theater each week to build friendship with multiple characters simultaneously.
5. **Birthday synergy**: If possible, time movie outings with NPC birthdays for maximum impact.

### Cost Management

- **Group Tickets cost 1,000g each**, which can add up over time.
- **Concessions add additional costs** (75g to 400g per item).
- **Budget for movie outings** as part of your weekly expenses.
- **Prioritize NPCs** who are harder to befriend through other means.

### Seasonal Planning

Plan your movie outings around seasonal movie availability:

- **Spring**: Focus on nature-loving NPCs who enjoy Spring films.
- **Summer**: Take adventure-loving NPCs to see action films.
- **Fall**: Invite romance-loving NPCs to dramatic films.
- **Winter**: Focus on cozy films for homebody NPCs.

## Advanced Movie Strategies

### Optimal Movie Scheduling

Planning your movie outings strategically can maximize friendship gains across multiple NPCs:

1. **Create a movie calendar**: Map out which movies show each week and which NPCs prefer each film.
2. **Group similar NPCs**: Take NPCs with similar movie preferences together when possible.
3. **Save group tickets for favorites**: Use your group tickets on movies that multiple NPCs love.
4. **Track NPC reactions**: Note which movies each NPC reacts positively to for future reference.

### Friendship Maximization Per Outing

Each movie outing can yield significant friendship when optimized:

- **Base friendship from movie**: +50 points
- **Loved movie bonus**: +30 additional points (80 total)
- **Loved concession bonus**: +80 additional points
- **Combined maximum per outing**: +160 friendship points

This means that a single well-planned movie outing can nearly match the effect of giving a loved birthday gift.

## Movie Theater as a Social Hub

The Movie Theater serves as more than just a place to watch films. It functions as a social hub where you can observe NPC interactions and discover new aspects of their personalities.

### NPC Interactions at the Theater

When you attend the theater without an NPC companion, you may observe NPCs interacting with each other:

- **Couples on dates**: Married NPCs may attend movies together, providing cute dialogue.
- **Friend groups**: Groups of friends attend together, revealing social dynamics.
- **Solo visitors**: Some NPCs attend alone, providing insight into their personalities.

### Theater Decorations

The Movie Theater features decorations that change with the seasons and current movie showings:

- **Movie posters**: Displayed in the lobby, advertising current and upcoming films.
- **Seasonal decorations**: The theater is decorated for each season and special holidays.
- **Concession stand displays**: The concession area changes based on available items.

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

- **NPC declines invitation**: Some NPCs may decline if they are busy, in a bad mood, or if their friendship is too low. Try again on a different day.
- **Theater appears closed**: Check that it is not a festival day. The theater closes on festival days.
- **Cannot afford tickets**: Save gold throughout the week specifically for movie outings. Budget 1,000g per week for group tickets.
- **Movie not showing**: Movies rotate on a schedule. Check with Gunther or the ticket booth for the current schedule.
