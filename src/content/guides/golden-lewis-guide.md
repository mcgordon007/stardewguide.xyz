# Stardew Valley Golden Lewis Guide

## Overview

The **Golden Lewis** is a secret decorative statue in Stardew Valley. It is a golden version of the town's mayor, Lewis, and is hidden in a discreet location in Pelican Town. Finding it is a fun Easter egg for observant players.

## Location

The Golden Lewis statue is located **behind Mayor Lewis's house** in Pelican Town.

### How to Find It

1. Travel to **Pelican Town**.
2. Go to **Mayor Lewis's house**, located north of the town square and east of the clinic.
3. Walk to the **back of his house** (the northern side).
4. You will find the **Golden Lewis** statue hidden behind a fence/bush.
5. Interact with it to pick it up.

## Using the Golden Lewis

- The Golden Lewis is a **furniture/decorative item**.
- It can be placed anywhere on your farm or inside your house.
- It serves no functional purpose but is a humorous and prestigious decoration.

## A Secret Risk

Be careful where you place the Golden Lewis:

- If you place the Golden Lewis statue **in Pelican Town** where villagers can see it, **Mayor Lewis** will send you an **angry letter** the next day.
- He will include **750g** as "hush money" to keep the statue's location secret.
- The statue will be returned to your inventory or mailbox.

This makes it a fun prank item, but if you want to keep it on display, place it on your farm instead!

## Trivia

- The Golden Lewis is one of several secret items hidden throughout Stardew Valley.
- The statue references Mayor Lewis's pride in his position and his secretive nature.
- It is a popular item to display in multiplayer games to show off your knowledge of secrets.
