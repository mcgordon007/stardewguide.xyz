# Stardew Valley Weapons Guide

Weapons are essential for combat in the Mines, Skull Cavern, and Volcano Dungeon. There are four weapon types: Swords, Clubs, Daggers, and Slingshots.

## Weapon Types

### Swords
- **Attack Speed:** Medium
- **Special Move:** Block - Reduces damage taken by 0.8x and reflects projectiles
- **Best For:** Balanced combat, defensive play
- **Average Stats:** Moderate damage, moderate speed

### Clubs
- **Attack Speed:** Slow
- **Special Move:** Smash - Deals heavy damage in a wide area and can knock back enemies
- **Best For:** High damage, crowd control
- **Average Stats:** High damage, slow speed, high knockback

### Daggers
- **Attack Speed:** Fast
- **Special Move:** Rapid Stab - Quickly strikes multiple times in succession
- **Best For:** Fast attacks, critical hits
- **Average Stats:** Low damage, very fast speed, high critical chance

### Slingshots
- **Attack Speed:** Varies by ammo
- **Special Move:** None
- **Best For:** Ranged combat
- **Average Stats:** Depends on ammo type

## Weapon Stats Explained

- **Damage:** The range of damage dealt per hit (min-max)
- **Speed:** How fast the weapon attacks (negative = slower, positive = faster)
- **Crit. Chance:** Base percentage chance for a critical hit
- **Crit. Power:** Multiplier applied to critical hit damage
- **Weight:** Affects knockback (higher = more knockback)
- **Defense:** Reduces damage taken (only on some weapons)
- **Immunity:** Reduces debuff duration (only on some weapons)

## Best Weapons by Type

### Best Swords

#### Galaxy Sword
- **Damage:** 60-80
- **Speed:** +4
- **Source:** Bring a Prismatic Shard to the Three Pillars in the Calico Desert
- **Notes:** One of the best all-around weapons; required to obtain the other Galaxy weapons

#### Infinity Blade
- **Damage:** 80-100
- **Speed:** +4
- **Source:** Forge the Galaxy Sword at the Volcano Forge with 3 Galaxy Souls and 60 Cinder Shards
- **Notes:** The ultimate sword; highest damage and excellent speed

#### Dwarf Sword
- **Damage:** 65-75
- **Speed:** +2
- **Source:** Volcano Dungeon chests
- **Notes:** Excellent mid-to-late game option

#### Lava Katana
- **Damage:** 55-64
- **Speed:** +3
- **Defense:** +3
- **Crit. Chance:** +25%
- **Source:** Adventurer's Guild (25,000g) after reaching Mine level 120
- **Notes:** Great defensive sword with high crit chance

#### Obsidian Edge
- **Damage:** 30-45
- **Speed:** -1
- **Crit. Chance:** +10%
- **Source:** Mine level 90 reward chest
- **Notes:** Excellent early-game sword

### Best Clubs

#### Slingshot
- **Damage:** Depends on ammo (1-3x base)
- **Source:** Mine level 40 reward chest
- **Ammo:** Stones (1x), Explosive Ammo (varies), various fruits/vegetables

#### Master Slingshot
- **Damage:** Depends on ammo (1-3x base, higher than basic)
- **Source:** Mine level 70 reward chest
- **Notes:** More powerful than basic slingshot

## Special Weapon Effects

### Enchantments (Volcano Forge)
Weapons can be enchanted with various effects:
- **Vampiric:** Chance to heal on kill
- **Crusader:** Bonus damage to mummies/slimes, prevents mummy revival
- **Bug Killer:** Double damage to bugs
- **Haymaker:** Extra fiber/hay from weeds
- **Magic:** Chance to restore energy on kill
- **Artful:** 50% cooldown reduction on special moves

### Forging
At the Volcano Forge, you can:
- Enchant weapons (1 Prismatic Shard + 20 Cinder Shards)
- Combine rings (20 Cinder Shards)
- Upgrade Galaxy weapons to Infinity weapons (Galaxy Souls + Cinder Shards)

## Combat Tips

- Swords are the most versatile weapon type for beginners
- Daggers excel against single tough enemies due to high crit potential
- Clubs are best for crowd control in dense monster areas
- Always bring food for health/energy recovery in combat
- Use the block ability (right-click) with swords to avoid damage
- Enchant your endgame weapons for additional benefits
- The Infinity weapons are the absolute best in each category
