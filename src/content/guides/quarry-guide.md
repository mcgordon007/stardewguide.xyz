# Stardew Valley Quarry Guide

The Quarry is a large mining area located in the northeast part of the Mountain, east of the Mines and Adventurer's Guild. It provides a renewable source of rocks, ore, gems, and geodes.

## Unlock Requirements

The Quarry is unlocked by **completing the Crafts Room bundle** at the Community Center.

## Quarry Mechanics

### Daily Spawns
- New rocks, ore nodes, and gem nodes spawn every day
- The entire quarry area respawns resources over time
- Trees and stumps also spawn regularly
- There is no limit to how many days the quarry produces resources

### Resource Types

#### Rocks and Ore
- **Stone:** Common, drops 1-3 Stone per rock
- **Copper Ore:** Copper-colored nodes, drops 1-3 Copper Ore
- **Iron Ore:** Silver-colored nodes, drops 1-3 Iron Ore
- **Gold Ore:** Gold-colored nodes, drops 1-3 Gold Ore
- **Iridium Ore:** Purple-colored nodes, drops 1-3 Iridium Ore (rare)

#### Gems and Minerals
- **Gem Nodes:** Colored nodes that drop specific gems
  - Amethyst Node (purple)
  - Aquamarine Node (blue)
  - Emerald Node (green)
  - Ruby Node (red)
  - Topaz Node (yellow)
  - Diamond Node (clear/white, very rare)

- **Mystic Stones:** Rare stones that can drop:
  - Prismatic Shards (very rare)
  - Gold Ore
  - Iridium Ore

#### Geode Nodes
- **Geode Node:** Drops regular Geodes
- **Frozen Geode Node:** Drops Frozen Geodes
- **Magma Geode Node:** Drops Magma Geodes
- **Omni Geode Node:** Drops Omni Geodes (rare)

### Trees and Wood
- Regular trees (Oak, Maple, Pine) spawn regularly
- Stumps and hardwood stumps also appear
- A good secondary source of Wood and Hardwood
- Mahogany Trees can also spawn (rare)

## Quarry Layout

- The Quarry is a large open area with multiple levels and cliffs
- A bridge connects the main mountain area to the quarry
- The area is affected by seasonal changes (snow in winter)
- There is a small pond in the quarry area
- The quarry is separate from the Mines and does not require a pickaxe to enter

## Strategic Uses

### Early Game
- Supplemental source of Stone and Ore
- Alternative to deep mining when energy is low
- Source of Geodes for Museum donations
- Wood and Hardwood from spawning trees

### Mid to Late Game
- Passive resource generation while focusing on other tasks
- Source of Iridium Ore without going to Skull Cavern
- Gem farming for gifting and crafting
- Geode farming for minerals and artifacts

### Quarry vs. Mines
| Feature | Quarry | Mines |
|---------|--------|-------|
| Danger | No monsters | Monsters present |
| Energy Cost | Lower | Higher |
| Resource Variety | Fixed spawns | More varied |
| Iridium Chance | Low | Higher in Skull Cavern |
| Convenience | Daily check | Requires dedicated trip |

## Tips and Tricks

### Daily Routine
- Check the Quarry every morning for new spawns
- Prioritize rare nodes (Iridium, Diamond, Omni Geodes)
- Clear all nodes to maximize next-day spawns
- Bring bombs to clear large areas quickly

### Tool Upgrades
- A Gold or Iridium Pickaxe makes clearing the Quarry much faster
- Consider using Explosive Ammo with a Slingshot for clustered rocks

### Seasonal Considerations
- Winter: Quarry is covered in snow but still spawns resources
- Trees do not grow in winter, but stumps remain
- Rain does not affect quarry spawns

### Quarry Bridge Repair
After unlocking the Quarry, the broken bridge to the east of the Quarry can be repaired:
- **Cost:** 500 Wood
- **Leads to:** The Quarry Mine (a small additional mining area)
- The Quarry Mine contains unique monsters and resources

## The Quarry Mine

After repairing the bridge east of the Quarry (500 Wood):
- A small dungeon-like area with multiple levels
- Contains unique enemies
- Features a special Golden Coconut spawn point
- Additional mining opportunities beyond the main Quarry

## Related Locations and Buildings

- **The Mines:** Main dungeon for mining (levels 1-120)
- **Skull Cavern:** Advanced mining area in the Calico Desert
- **Volcano Dungeon:** Ginger Island endgame mining
- **Blacksmith:** Clint processes Geodes and upgrades tools
- **Adventurer's Guild:** Buy weapons and combat items

## Summary

The Quarry is an excellent passive resource generation area that becomes available mid-game through Community Center progression. While it won't replace the Mines or Skull Cavern for dedicated mining sessions, it provides a safe, monster-free environment for daily resource collection. Check it every day, clear all nodes, and enjoy the steady stream of Stone, Ore, Gems, and Geodes.
