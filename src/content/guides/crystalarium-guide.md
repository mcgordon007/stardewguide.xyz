# Stardew Valley Crystalarium Guide

## Introduction

**Crystalarium** is an advanced device in Stardew Valley that can infinitely replicate placed gems or minerals, making it one of the important sources of stable income in the mid-to-late game.

## Unlock Condition

- Recipe unlocked after reaching **Mining Level 9**

## Crafting Materials

- Stone x99
- Gold Bar x5
- Iridium Bar x2
- Battery Pack x1

## Function

- After placing **1 replicable gem/mineral**, the machine automatically replicates that item
- After replication is complete, the product can be removed, and the machine continues to replicate the same item
- Replication time varies for different items

## Best Profit Strategy

1. **Diamond Strategy**: Highest single sell price (750g), suitable for long-term stable income
2. **Jade Strategy**: Shorter replication time, can be exchanged for Staircases for use in Skull Cavern
3. **Batch Construction**: Build 20+ Crystalariums, fill with Diamonds, weekly income is considerable

## Usage Tips

1. Place Crystalariums in Farmhouse or Shed for easy daily collection
2. Replicating Diamonds is the most reliable profit method
3. Replicating Jade for Skull Cavern exploration is more cost-effective than buying Staircases directly
4. Cannot replicate Prismatic Shards or artifacts

## Notes

- Removing the original item during replication resets replication progress
- Crystalarium itself is not consumed, can be used infinitely
- Requires Battery Pack for crafting, recommended to coordinate with Solar Panels or Lightning Rods
