# Stardew Valley Meteorite Guide

The **Meteorite** is a rare random farm event that can occur overnight. It drops valuable resources including Iridium Ore and has a chance to contain a Prismatic Shard.

---

## How It Spawns

### Chance
- There is a **1% chance** each night that the Meteorite event is selected
- The event is chosen from a pool of random overnight farm events

### Spawn Requirements
When the event is selected, the game:
1. Chooses a random 3x3 area on your farm
2. Checks if the area contains:
   - Water
   - Placed objects that block movement
   - Buildings
   - Resource clumps
3. If any of these are present, the event does nothing (no meteorite spawns)
4. If the area is clear, the meteorite spawns in the lower-left 2x2 square of the selected area

**Note:** The meteorite will destroy any crops or paths in the spawn area.

---

## Overnight Notification

When a meteorite lands, you'll see this message before the shipping screen:

> "An explosion was heard in the night..."

A unique meteorite sound effect plays when this happens.

---

## Mining the Meteorite

### Requirements
- **Gold Pickaxe or better** required to mine
- The meteorite **does not despawn** naturally
- It will remain on your farm until mined

### Hits Required
- Gold Pickaxe: **7 hits**
- Iridium Pickaxe: **6 hits**

### Drops

| Item | Amount | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| Iridium Ore | 10 | Primary resource |
| Stone | 8 | |
| Omni Geode | 2 | |
| Prismatic Shard | 1 | **25% chance** (as of v1.6.3) |

**Historical Note:** In v1.6, Prismatic Shard drop rate was 100%, but was reduced to 25% in v1.6.3.

---

## Visual Appearance

The meteorite is a large, dark rock with spiky projections. It has a distinctive appearance that makes it easy to spot on your farm.

**Trivia:** From v1.0 to v1.04 (February 26 to March 1, 2016), the meteorite was gray with no spiky projections.

---

## Strategy and Tips

### Protecting Your Farm
- The meteorite destroys crops and paths in its spawn area
- This can be counter-productive if the destroyed crops are more valuable than the meteorite drops
- There's no way to prevent meteorites from spawning

### When to Mine
- Mine it as soon as you have a Gold Pickaxe
- The Iridium Ore is extremely valuable early-to-mid game
- The Prismatic Shard chance makes it worth checking every meteorite

### Prismatic Shard Uses
If you get lucky with a Prismatic Shard drop:
- Donate 1 to the Museum (required for completion)
- Use in the Desert Obelisk or Island Obelisk crafting
- Gift to villagers (universal love, except Haley who hates it)
- Craft Wedding Ring (multiplayer)
- Trade for Galaxy weapons at the Desert

### Meteorite vs. Other Random Events
Other random overnight events include:
- **Crop Fairy** (1% chance) - Grows crops in a 5x5 area
- **Witch** (1% chance) - Leaves a Void Egg or Golden Egg
- **Stone Owl** (0.5% chance) - Decorative item appears
- **Strange Capsule** (0.8% chance after Year 1) - UFO event

The Meteorite is one of the more beneficial random events due to its valuable drops.
