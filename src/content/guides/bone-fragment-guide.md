# Stardew Valley Bone Fragment Guide

## Overview

**Bone Fragments** are a resource item introduced in Stardew Valley 1.5, primarily found on **Ginger Island**. They are used in various crafting recipes and are essential for players exploring the late-game content.

## Where to Find Bone Fragments

Bone Fragments can be obtained from the following sources:

### Ginger Island
- **Digging up Artifact Spots** on Ginger Island (the diggable worms).
- **Killing Skeletons** in the Volcano Dungeon.
- **Breaking Bone Nodes** in the Dig Site on Ginger Island West.
- **Fishing Treasure Chests** on Ginger Island.

### Other Sources
- **The Mines**: Skeletons on regular mine levels (not exclusive to Ginger Island).
- **Skull Cavern**: Skeleton enemies may also drop Bone Fragments.

## Uses

Bone Fragments are used in the following crafting recipes:

| Recipe | Materials | Source |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| Bone Mill | 10 Bone Fragments, 3 Clay, 20 Stone, 1 Coal | Gunther (after finding first Bone Fragment) |
| Thorns Ring | 50 Bone Fragments, 50 Stone | Combat Level 7 |
| Solid Gold Lewis | 20 Bone Fragments, 3 Gold Bars | Secret (after finding Secret Note #19) |
| Ostrich Incubator | 50 Bone Fragments, 50 Hardwood, 20 Cinder Shards | Professor Snail (after completing his collection) |

## Tips

- Bring a good weapon when farming Skeletons in the Volcano Dungeon.
- Check the Dig Site regularly for Bone Nodes, which respawn over time.
- Save Bone Fragments for crafting the Ostrich Incubator, as Ostriches are highly profitable.

## Version History

- Introduced in **Stardew Valley 1.5** (December 2020).
